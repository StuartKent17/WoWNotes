Mechagon Isle

Bots Gone Wild - Rustfeather's Hill
Discs of Norgannon - Rustbolt Visitor Daily

This daily allows you to summon a Data Anomaly near Junkwatt Depot, summoning a hologram of a random boss from classic WoW. This hologram has a chance to drop a unique blueprint as well as some of the other rare items from the island.

While this quest is available, you can also loot Scorched Data Disc (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169595-scorched-data-disc), Rust Covered Disc (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169594-rust-covered-disc), and Large Storage Fragment (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169593-large-storage-fragment).

These items let you summon an elite Data Anomaly.

These have a chance to drop the unique blueprint Blueprint: Holographic Digitalization Relay (https://www.wowdb.com/items/168906-blueprint-holographic-digitalization-relay) - Blueprint to create Holographic Digitalization Hearthstone (https://www.wowdb.com/items/168907-holographic-digitalization-hearthstone).
Other items on the loot table include Blueprint: Blue Spraybot (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169169-blueprint-blue-spraybot), Vinyl: Battle of Gnomeregan (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169690-vinyl-battle-of-gnomeregan), and Metal Detector (https://www.wowdb.com/items/168631-metal-detector). Some classic items are also dropping such as Earthfury Epaulets (https://www.wowdb.com/items/16844-earthfury-epaulets).


You can farm these data anomalies as much as you want. You don't have to be the one to summon it to get loot.
