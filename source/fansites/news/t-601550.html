Brewfest
As Eloderung pointed out in this post, the 2.2 files have a few files/spells related to a "Brew fest". This includes a few song (check the post if you want to listen to them), and tons of spells.

This will more likely be a end-september event, during the Oktoberfest (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oktoberfest) (Pure speculation, of course), and this could also confirms that Blizzard is updating the yearly events to make them more related to the Outlands zones.

I guess you'll be able to smash kegs ([spell=42393]), Dark Irons will be back ([spell=42448]), race with rams ([spell=42146]), and of course eat sausages ! ([spell=42248]). You can check the full spell list on Wowhead (http://www.wowhead.com/?search=brewfest)

Broom flying mount
Today is the official "let's look at the spells" day ! And we can confirm that the Broom will be a flying mount. It will be used for an event, probably Halloween (Just a few weeks after the Oktoberfest, what a coincidence). A few broom spells are available, all of them are good for only one ride. ([spell=42679] and [spell=42680]for example)
