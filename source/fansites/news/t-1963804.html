The difference in the two choices is that there are people who pronounce Dalaran as either Dalar-ahn (all the 'a' sounds sound the same, like the 'a' in 'bar') or Dala-ran (the final 'a' sounds like ae, as in 'cat', or to be exact, like the past tense of the verb 'to run', which is 'ran' as in I ran away from my enemies). There's also a southern US way to pronounce, where the first 'a' would also be ae. Just pretend you're from Texas, it sounds like Delahren.

The correct pronunciation is the first one, all three As are like bar. The questions was there just to see how people pronounce it, not to determine the correct one.

However, I'm afraid that because of the large international public that WoW enjoys that the two choices in the poll aren't clear enough for the average English as a second language player.
