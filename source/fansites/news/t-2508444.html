Free Character Moves Starting Wednesday at 11:00 a.m. PDT
Starting at 11:00 a.m. PDT (2:00 p.m. EDT) on Wednesday, Sept. 11, we will open the following free character moves to offer you a way to move your characters from the most high-population realms that have extended queues:

From -> To

Benediction -> Heartseeker
Bigglesworth -> Anathema
Blaumeux -> Anathema
Faerlina -> Heartseeker
Fairbanks -> Arcanite Reaper
Grobbulus -> Deviate Delight
Herod -> Earthfury
Incendius -> Heartseeker
Kirtonos -> Earthfury
Kromcrush -> Earthfury
Mankrik -> Windseeker
Pagle -> Windseeker
Rattlegore -> Anathema
Skeram -> Earthfury
Stalagg -> Heartseeker
Sulfuras -> Heartseeker
Thalnos -> Earthfury
Thunderfury -> Arcanite Reaper
Westfall -> Windseeker
Whitemane -> Arcanite Reaper


We expect these free character moves to remain open until 11:00 a.m. PDT (2:00 a.m. EDT) on Monday, September 16. Please note that restrictions apply:


A character cannot move if the character:

is a guild leader.
has active auction listings or bids.
has mail.

You may be required to change your character’s name once you’ve reached the destination realm.
You cannot move a character to a PvP realm where you already have a character belonging to the opposing faction.
This is a one-way, irreversible move. Blizzard Customer Support cannot monitor or modify a free character move.


During periods of high usage, free character moves may take up to several hours to complete. This service may be closed at any time, without warning, should a destination realm become full. We strongly encourage players who wish to use this service, especially in coordinated groups, to do so as soon as possible.

Thank you!
