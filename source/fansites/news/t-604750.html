Bosses
Note on Kil'jaeden loot : Nobody killed him yet, however he will most likely be the only one dropping ilvl 164 items. You can already check a few of them at the Sunwell vendor but I'll only list the 3 that really look like they drop from Kil'jaeden himself (based on their itemID mostly). The three additional items are the one you can get from the vendor with an additional Sunmote, and Apolyon was added because it's an ilvl 164 weapon and that's probably the only boss it could drop from. Same goes for Thori'dal, it's confirmed to be a drop and I don't really see how it could not drop from the last boss of the dungeon. (Be careful though, the stats of both items are based on the leak posted a few weeks ago)
