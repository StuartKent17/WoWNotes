With Legion approaching, we are going back and updating a few older previews. If you want to test out this mount, you can use the Ratstallion Harness (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/139421-ratstallion-harness) from the Crate of Ratstallion Harnesses (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/139431-crate-of-ratstallion-harnesses) sold by Raethan (http://beta.wowdb.com/npcs/97359-raethan) for 950 x Sightless Eye (http://beta.wowdb.com/currencies/1149-sightless-eye). If you want the real deal (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/215558-ratstallion), you will want to complete Underbelly Tycoon (http://beta.wowdb.com/achievements/11066-underbelly-tycoon) for Ratstallion (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/138387-ratstallion).
