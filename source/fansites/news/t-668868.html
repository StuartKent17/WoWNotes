A new build has been deployed on test realms.

Achievements

BB King (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4436/bb-king/) / BB King (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4437/bb-king/)


Spell Comparison
General
Basic Campfire (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/818/) cast time has been lowered from 10 sec to 2 sec.

Leatherworking
Drums of the Wild (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/69388/) now requires Heavy Borean Leather (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/38425/) x 8 (Up from 4), Jormungar Scale (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/38561/) x 8 (Down from 20).

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ideathknights.gif Death Knight
Blood
Heart Strike (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/55262/) tooltip clarified : Instantly strike the target and his nearest ally, causing 50% weapon damage plus 368 on the primary target, and 25% weapon damage plus 184 on the secondary target. Each target takes 10% additonal damage for each of your diseases active on that target.

Unholy
Bone Shield (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/49222/) now lasts 1 min. (Down from 5 min)

Glyphs
Glyph of Scourge Strike (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/58642/) - Your Scourge Strike increases the duration of your diseases on the target by 3 sec, up to a maximum of 9 additional seconds.
Glyph of Bone Shield (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/58673/) - Adds 1 additional charges to your Bone Shield. (Down from 2)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid
Feral
Infected Wounds (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/48485/) now reduces the movement speed of the target by 16/34/50% (up from 8/17/25%) and its attack speed by 6/14/20% (up from 3/7/10%).

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter
Beast Mastery
The Beast Within (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/34692/) now reduces the mana costs of all spells by 50% for 10sec. (Up from 20%)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin
Protection
Ardent Defender (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/31852/) now reduces damage that takes you below 35% health by 7/13/20%. (Down from 10/20/30%)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman
Elemental
Shamanism (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/62101/) now also affects Chain Lightning. Gains from bonus damage effects to Chain Lightning and Lightning Bolt increased to 3/6/9/12/15%. (Up from 2/4/6/8/10%)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior
Protection
Critical Block (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/47296/) now gives your successful blocks a 20/40/60% chance to block double the normal amount. (Up from 10/20/30%)
