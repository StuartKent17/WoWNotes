Warcraft Movie Teaser at Comic-Con
Fans that were at Comic-Con this weekend got a treat in the form of a Warcraft movie teaser. It was a short clip that featured a human in the desert with a sword who comes across the skeleton of a solider. He takes a drink and then picks up the shield, which has the Alliance insignia on it. An orc then shows up and charges the human, with the clip ending there.

Duncan Jones also mentioned that filming would begin early next year, so this was just a test shot for Comic-Con. Unfortunately, no recordings have shown up yet, and there are no plans to release the footage...

Blue Tweets
Classes
Do you ever get worried about classes being too similar or blurring the lines between what makes every class unique?
Constantly! It's one of the hardest problems for us to solve b/c nobody wants to feel weak in the name of class differentiation. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/356134052501262336))

Can you explain the move back to mana regen scaling with spi/gear? MoP took away mana regen scaling with int, why this then?
The problem with Int was it provided two bonuses for one stat. Spirit is only regen. It comes at the expense of other stats. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/357519567788060672)

Healing is becoming a spam/dps like activity instead of making choices. Changes coming next expansion? Water cooler topic?
It often comes down to mana IMO. The challenge is we get a lot of pressure to buff regen instead of healers having to make choices. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/357510089344679936))
As I've said before, the encounters are balanced around class output, not vice versa. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/357510262896607232))
But when someone struggles with a fight, the inclination is to ask for class buffs. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/357510359776628736))

in fact correct me if i'm wrong GC but didn't you say people have too many buttons to push already?
I highly suspect DS is already on your action bar. It's more about how big those action bars are. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/357380902411706368))
Not sure it's a triumph of game design that players feel like they need 4+ bars to bind all of their abilities. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/357381055990345729))

with the constant nerfs to prep/readiness/alter time, maybe it's time you stopped putting cd reset abilities into the game

Weekly News Recap

July 14

We looked at the Season 14 Mage Armor Set (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/3307-Season-14-Mage-Armor-Set-Patch-5-4-PTR-Notes-Update-Class-Blue-Tweets-News-Recap).

July 15

We looked at Proving Grounds, with a preview of the DPS (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=i94mzpQirfs&feature=player_embedded), Tanking (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PnMjT1_xSt0&feature=player_embedded), and Healing (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S3desG5HyFA&feature=player_embedded) version.

July 18

We took a very quick look at Timeless Isle (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/3312-Patch-5-4-Timeless-Isle-Patch-5-5-SoO-Raid-Testing-5-4-Notes-Update-Blue-Posts), which has no flying mounts.

Most NPCs, objects, and gathering nodes scattered around the isle drop Timeless Coins (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/777-timeless-coin), as well as the pet battle (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/33222-little-tommy-newcomer) and lore daily quests (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/33211-a-timeless-question).
Timeless Coins (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/777-timeless-coin) are used to purchase Timeless Caches (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items?filter-patch=3388&filter-search=cache&filter-ilvl-min=496&filter-ilvl-max=496&filter-quality=16) for 7500 x Timeless Coin (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/777-timeless-coin), which reward item level 496 gear (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/armor?filter-patch=3388&filter-ilvl-min=496&filter-ilvl-max=496&filter-quality=16), similar to Battlefield: Barrens. You can also buy Mogu Runes (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/752-mogu-rune-of-fate) for 2500 x Timeless Coin (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/777-timeless-coin).
You can also buy http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/101538.jpg Kukuru's Cache Key (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/101538-kukurus-cache-key) for 500 x Timeless Coin (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/777-timeless-coin), which will open any chest in Master Kukuru's cave. These chests contain Timeless Coins and other minor loot.

The official PTR notes were updated (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/3312-Patch-5-4-Timeless-Isle-Patch-5-5-SoO-Raid-Testing-5-4-Notes-Update-Blue-Posts) with lots of new glyphs.
Alts will be able to access Ordos as long as one character on your account has the new legendary cloaks (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items?filter-patch=3388&filter-quality=32).
