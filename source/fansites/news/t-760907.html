Blue Posts
Guild XP "Top 20 Contributors"
There is no concept of "Top 20 contributors" anymore. We changed that functionality many months ago. XP contributed to the guild comes from all members. Guild rep is always available no matter what amount you have contributed to the guild. (Source (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/t/26435397268/leveling-speed/))

Guild Rep/XP not awarded in dungeons on beta?
This data did not make the last patch. This functionality should be live in the next build. (Source (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/t/26859266660/guild-rep-xp-not-awarded-in-dungeons/))

Guild System in Patch 4.0.1
The new guild system UI will be active in the pre-patch but guild experience and guild achievements will be disabled until Cataclysm launch. (Source (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/t/26560868398/when-will-it-go-live/))

To resolve any confusion.

Tol Barad the actual zone is the PvP Battle zone like Wintergrasp, when your team wins, 2 guys spawn in Baradin Hold with 3 quests each, these can rotate and change every time you re-take but you can only get 6 of them a day (currently on beta).

To the north there is Tol Barad Peninsula where the horde and alliance both have encampments with their Reputation vendors and 5 additional dailies that you can do any time of the day. So 11 a day total and your faction has to win Tol Barad at least once to do the other 6.

So ways to get rep:
#Winning Tol Barad - 300-325rep per battle win, multiple times a day if your faction wins alot
#11 Daily quests a day - 250-300 rep (can never tell with rep bonus) for a total of 2750-3300rep a day.
#Baradins Wardens Commendation (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/63517/baradins-wardens-commendation/) - 250 rep for the cost of 10 Tol Barad Commendation (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/62898/tol-barad-commendation/) ( I think its 10 )

Total rep you can earn a day is:
-Lets say you can win Tol Barad 3 times a day- 900-975 rep
-11 dailies- 2750rep-3300rep a day
-turning in commendations (which means you dont want any of the amazing pre-raid gear) 250 per day
Total: 3900-4525 rep a day, low number before rep bonus ( i think )
