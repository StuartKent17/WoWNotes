PTR Testing - Northrend Beast
The european realms are currently running an unscheduled test of the Northrend Beast raid encounter of the Trial of the Crusader raid dungeon. This news will be updated shortly.

The Beasts of Northrend encounter require you to fight 3 encounters one after the other: Gormok, Acidmaw & Dreadscale, Icehowl.
