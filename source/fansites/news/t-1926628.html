Ghostcrawler Tweets
Ghostcrawler still occasionally talks about WoW. Remember that he no longer works for or speaks for Blizzard.
Do you think mmo's would profit from having less numerical feedback? (eg. combatlogs and tooltips)
MMOs can't generally afford sufficient combat feedback without all the numbers. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/679678048979910656))
Some fighting games have so much rich animation that it sells how you're doing with very little use of numbers. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/679678227472764928))
MMOs often have to emphasize quantity over quality, and that usually means a pretty simple basic combat model. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/679678390719262724))
By which I mean positioning, timing and so on have to be pretty simple. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/679678687084597249))
They dress that up with depth, usually in the form of numbers, e.g. resistances, speed, crit, dots, and so on. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/679678897588322304))
