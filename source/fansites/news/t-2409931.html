Dark Iron Dwarf Mole Machine Locations
The Secret Finding Discord and Thulnir, Rufaic, Halflan, and Thormil of the Mead Hall RP community have discovered 16 different locations that you can add to the Mole Machine (https://beta.wowdb.com/spells/265225-mole-machine) racial!

If you travel to each location and click on the Mole Machine located there, that destination will be added to your Mole Machine!


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/july/moleMachine.jpg (https://imgur.com/a/WqovpC9#ucNel1Z)


Dark Iron Dwarf Capital - Shadowforge City
Shadowforge City is now a teleport option when using the Dark Iron Dwarf racial ability, Mole Machine (https://beta.wowdb.com/spells/265225-mole-machine) in the Battle for Azeroth beta! Upon teleporting to Shadowforge City you will find yourself in an instanced friendly version of Blackrock Depths in the Hall of Crafting. Only a small portion of the instance is utilized for the city including the Grim Guzzler and portions of the upper city.

Moira Thaurissan (https://beta.wowdb.com/npcs/70279-moira-thaurissan) is located by the Black Anvil where Lord Incendius (https://beta.wowdb.com/npcs/9017-lord-incendius) is in the dungeon version. She has a quest called Fer the Alliance (https://beta.wowdb.com/quests/51486-fer-the-alliance), which is similar to the other Allied Race quests that give the racial tabards. There is also a mole machine near where you spawn in that can take you right to the Stormwind Embassy which also has a mole machine back to the capital city.
