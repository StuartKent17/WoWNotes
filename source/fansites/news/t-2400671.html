End of Legion Mythic+ Feedback
With Legion coming to a close we thought we might try something a little different and collect some specific feedback on things. While we normally are reading tons of posts you guys make and sharing the details internally, we want to try collecting focused feedback to ensure your opinions are being heard. We're still doing our usual methods but want to see if this can be something we use going forward.

For the first one we're going to try one focused on Mythic+! We want to hear everything you guys have on this feature we introduced in Legion. What you think it does well, what you think it doesn't, ways you'd like to see it go, how did it work throughout the course of the expansion, etc.

Also a friendly note that just because you see it here doesn't mean it will be changed or added - and this topic is not an indication of future feature development.

First of all: I did play some m+ keys but did not enjoy this content particularly often (2 chars per week +15 for the chest). Even with affixes the dungeons become somewhat repetitive and boring (especially if you focus on loot upgrades).

What I loved is the possibility to run dungeons even after heroic items to get improvements and the scaling of the items provided. You atleast got some rewards (while running Heroic raids can result in weeks of waiting for your set boni). Also the complementing 2-item boni added some nice flavour. You can easily upgrade a "fresh" (item level 880) character to 920 within a week. As catch up mechanic - good. As min-max mechanic - horrific (the rewards at higher keystones were neglectable to the effort put in so you stop at your weekly +15).

The biggest issue I had here is the "group requirement" to get into a m+ group (aka you run a dungeon giving you itemlevel 900 and the groups require 930). So often you are running lower keystones (atleast with randoms).

What I did not like is the "must have 3 chests" mentality of most groups. This is a result of mixing the Challenge mode timers with "more loot the faster you are". There are multiple ways of how to reduce this (e.g. by guaranteeing every member a certain itemlevel for a certain keystone level achieved and increasing those baseline by 5/10/15 itemlevel for every timer bet).

Also: Class balance within itself was very struggling during Legion as some classes needed different itemsets and legendaries for SingleTarget/AOE whilst others were good to go by design (comparing moonkin versus demon hunter). Some legendaries however where must have to dish out a certain amount of damage required (e.g. Raddon's Cascading Eyes as Demon Hunter).

What I am unsure about is the ability of decently gear tanks to run m+ solo (Mionee with +22 CoS is exceptional) due to their selfheal. 1 Tank + 4 DPS was (and still is) a decent strategy for most mythic plus dungeons. This can be fun to watch during MDI and might reduce the need for heal but is that still WoW and not Diablo?

Some things Blizzard should consider in the future:
a) Some affix combinations where really hard while some where considerable easier.
b) Class balance (e.g. does Blizzard balance classes on M+, raids or PVP) - now that BfA gets some unique class boni back, Blizzard should really take a look at the MDI class distribution regarding overall performance and cheese strategies (e.g. Court of Stars event with Demon Hunter).
c) Loot - in Legion you knew with dungeons you want to run for titanforged item upgrades and which for upgrading your key (I only had to run 3 dungeons for items over and over again). I guess that this will get worse with the Azerite upgrades (as you might only run dungeons for Helm, Shoulder, Chest improvements). This does not mean that Blizzard should redistribute all the loot and increase chances of "unwanted"/"dead" loot in every dungeon but rather define a rewarding systeme e.g. like the Titan Essences or Antorus upgrade stones (e.g. you get an item that upgrades your gear by 5 itemlevel and you only get one from each different m+ )
