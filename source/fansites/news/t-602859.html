Paid character name changes are here
Rejoice, you'll finally be able to change this "Leghollass", but it will cost you 10$. This feature is being tested on a few servers, see the post for a complete list.
We’re happy to announce that through the wonders of modern gnomish engineering, you are now able to change your characters’ names. Finally, you can turn the moniker that seemed like a good idea at the time into something more befitting your true standing in World of Warcraft.

Visit Account Management to begin the paid character-name-change process. The fee is $10 per name change. As with our recent addition of voice chat to the game, this feature is being rolled out across all realms in several phases. The first-phase realms are listed in this forum post. Enjoy this new feature and the subsequent effect that your bold new name will have on friends and foes alike.

List of concerned realms (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2512090701&postId=25734810929&sid=1#0)

Yesterday's recap
I'm getting used to patch notes-like compilations, so here is a small summary of all the new things added in yesterday's PTR build.
For all classes changes, check the patch notes update (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php#23changes) posted 3 days ago.

PvP
Arena
[Vengeful Gladiator's Battle Staff] (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/october/s3/battle_staff.jpg) (3750 AP),[Vengeful Gladiator's Rifle] (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/october/s3/rifle.jpg) (3750 AP),[Vengeful Gladiator's Longbow] (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/october/s3/longbow.jpg) and [Vengeful Gladiator's Shield Wall] (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/october/s3/shield_wall.jpg) (1875 AP) have been added to arena rewards.
Season 1 Gear is now buyable with honor (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/october/s3/s1honor.jpg) on PTR, rings and amulets (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/october/s3/s3_ringamu2.jpg) too. There isn't any update of cloaks for the moment. If you're looking for the belt/boots/bracers of each set you can find them with their respective season 3 set in the 2.3 compilation



Hunters
All ranged attacks now have a wonderful 1 yard minimum range (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/october/hunter1yard.jpg).



Mage
Ritual of Refreshment now requires 2 [item=17020]
The 4-piece bonus of Gladiator's set now reduces the cooldown of the Blink spell by 2 seconds instead of reducing the cast time of Polymorph by 0.15 second.



Paladins
The threat reduction on Fanaticism listed in the patch notes update (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php#23changes) has been modified to not apply when Righteous Fury is active.



Raids & Dungeons
Guards have been added to the area surrounding Zul'aman to prevent excessive ganking.



Professions
Alchemy
The side effects of the Mad Alchemist potion might be the same as the "***** Blood" debuffs from Zangarmarsh fireflies. (This is still very, very unconfirmed)
Engineering
[list] [item=20475] mats have been lowered, it now requires 1x Adamantite Bar, 4x Simple Wood, and 2x Handful of Fel Iron Bolts.



Factions
A new pet has been added (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/october/sporebatdr8.jpg) to Sporeggar rewards. (Exalted)
Potions buyable for 50 apexis shards at the Ogri'la Quartermasters have been upgraded to match "Super" potions instead of "Major" (Example : 1800-3000 Mana instead of 1350-2250)



World Environment
Zeppelins and Boats around the world now have an embedded crew and reagents/food vendors (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/october/zepvendor.jpg)
