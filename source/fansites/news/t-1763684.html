Patch 6.2 PTR - Build 19934
Build 19934 will be deployed to the PTR realms soon.


It seems you will be able to upgrade the legendary ring to item level 795 (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/187958-power-infusion).
Some new garrison missions (http://ptr.wowdb.com/garrison/missions?filter-build=19934) have been added, and older Patch 6.2 missions have been updated (http://ptr.wowdb.com/garrison/missions?filter-patch=19890).
Naval missions now reward the new Baleful gear (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items?filter-patch=19890&filter-search=Baleful).
Naval missions also reward the Hellfire Citadel raid chests (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items?filter-search=Iron+Fleet+Treasure+Chest).
You can now give Pepe some new costumes (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items?filter-build=19934&filter-search=tiny).
