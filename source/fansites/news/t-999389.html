Patch 4.3: Epic Gem Patterns
The epic gem pattern vendor appeared recently on the PTR, hiding inside some boxes in Orgrimmar. Along with the epic gem cuts (http://db.mmo-champion.com/items/3/?level=85&quality=4), he offers a Tome of Burning Jewels (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/71949/tome-of-burning-jewels/), which allows you to learn a random epic gem cut at the slightly discounted price of 4 Illustrious Jewelcrafter's Token (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/52446/illustrious-jewelcrafters-token/) each.

Don't forget to continue doing your Jewelcrafting daily if you want to get the epic gem cuts (http://db.mmo-champion.com/items/3/?level=85&quality=4) though, as they cost 5 Illustrious Jewelcrafter's Token (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/52446/illustrious-jewelcrafters-token/) each and there are already 51 recipes on the vendor. Keep in mind that Ghostcrawler warned (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/2459-Patch-4.3-Interview-with-Greg-Street-%28Ghostcrawler%29) that epic gems will only come from raids rarely, and maybe not even at all from the Looking for Raid difficulty level.

The hunter kill just shows why blizzard fails whenever they don't put an enrage timer on a boss :DBut it's still hilarious, well done!

To be fair, Atramedes has an enrage mechanism, it's just not timer-based. Normally a player would need to hit the gongs to avoid (1) being chased by fire during air phase and (2) to stun the dragon during searing breath (which does a lot of damage to the entire raid). Once the gongs are all gone, these two mechanics would normally wipe your raid.

He is circumventing these mechanics by (1) using feign death, which bugs his tracking fire out. And (2) by using deterrence during the Searing Breath, which nullifies the damage from that ability. So he practically never have to use the gongs and thus the enrage mechanism isn't of any concern.

I find it enjoyable with these sort of soft enrage mechanisms instead of just having a flat out timer where the boss goes berserk for no appearent reason. I mean, why didn't he just berserk from the start and wipe the raid? Mechanism like these makes a lot more sense and plays to the strength of learning the fight properly rather than just having more dps to finish the boss off.
