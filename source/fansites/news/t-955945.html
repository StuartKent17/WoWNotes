While I admit that I am at a loss for what a Transmogrifier will end up being in WoW terms, I am certain that it will NOT be a way to re-texture our armor.

There are two levels of gear status symbolism in WoW, models and textures. A new model lets you indicate to others that you are up to date with the current content (both PvP and PvE). Textures are Blizzard's way of letting you show off your raiding or PvP prowress.

There is an entire set of PvP gear each season available at high ratings in 3v3/5v5/RBGs that has no stat difference from the conquest gear available to everyone. It even uses the same model. The only difference is the texture applied to the model.

If we were allowed to re-color/re-texture our armor, Blizzard would need to then compensate by creating four sets of models each tier (2 each for pvp and pve) rather than simply using different textures to differentiate between normal/heroic raiding or regular/elite pvp.

I think it would be great if they did that, but I really doubt it will happen.
