WotLK Mounts Update - Green Proto-Drake
A new drop location has been added to the mounts list (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=824). The Green Proto-Drake (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/november/greenprotodrake.jpg) is actually a (very?) rare drop from the Mysterious Egg (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=39878) rewarded by The Oracles (http://www.wowwiki.com/Oracles) in Sholazar Basin. I guess the choice between them and the Frenzyheart Tribe (http://www.wowwiki.com/Frenzyheart_Tribe) will be much easier now. (Thanks to Dalan (http://www.wowarmory.com/character-sheet.xml?r=Korgath&n=Dalan) from US-Korgath for his forum post (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?topic=28130.msg463907#msg463907))

Spirit Beasts and additional pets
We wanted to have a difficult to get pet for those hunters who enjoyed the challenge of it in the old days. We wanted to make sure the Spirit Beast was not absolutely the best pet for that reason. We will probably add more over time. (Source (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/28/13129103918-blizz-loquenahakrepost-from-hunter-forum.html))

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/paladin.jpg Paladin (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=610) / Talent Calc. (9095) (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?paladin))
Retribution nerf and PvP
We said before that if toning down Ret burst made them no longer very good at Arenas that we would be willing to give them additional utility. We just don't think the proverbial writing on the wall is that clear yet. We'll know a lot more in a few weeks. (Source (http://))

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/priest.jpg Priest (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=640) / Talent Calc. (9095) (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?priest))
Mind Flay "bug"
If I had any more information, I would give it. We changed the way Mind Flay worked, which introduced the problem you referenced. It is not technically a bug, but it's still annoying and we'd like to get it fixed. We're not sure if rebuilding the spell is the right answer or if there are other things we can do.

It is going to be really hard for me to continually repost on the same topics just to say "We haven't forgotten." (Source (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/28/13129904697-no-responses--frustrating.html))
