Patch 6.1 - Iron Horde Scrap Meltdown
Soon there will finally be a use for all of those Iron Horde Scraps (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/113681-iron-horde-scraps) you have been collecting. The War Mill will allow you to do a Scrap Meltdown (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/38188-scrap-meltdown), turning scraps into Weapon Enhancement Token (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/120302-weapon-enhancement-token) or Armor Enhancement Token (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/120301-armor-enhancement-token). Keep in mind that this is a daily quest, so you can only get one per day.

Patch 6.1 - Garrison Vendor
Patch 6.1 adds a few more items to your garrison's vendor to allow you to spend excess resources:

Scouting Missives - These cost 200 x Garrison Resources (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/824-garrison-resources) each and grant access to an additional daily quest that rewards 800 x Apexis Crystal (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/823-apexis-crystal)
Rush Order (Garden (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/122496-rush-order-garden-shipment) / Mine (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/122502-rush-order-mine-shipment)) - These cost 300 x Garrison Resources (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/824-garrison-resources) each and instantly complete 5 of your active work orders.
Rush Order (Barn (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/122307-rush-order-barn) / Gladiator's Sanctum (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/122487-rush-order-gladiators-sanctum) / War Mill (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/122491-rush-order-war-mill) / Goblin Workshop (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/122501-rush-order-goblin-workshop)) - These cost 1000 x Garrison Resources (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/824-garrison-resources) each and instantly complete 5 of your active work orders.
Follower Trait Retraining Guide (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/122273-follower-trait-retraining-guide) - This costs 1500 x Garrison Resources (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/824-garrison-resources) and will reroll follower traits.
Follower Ability Retraining Guide (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/122272-follower-ability-retraining-guide) - This costs 1500 x Garrison Resources (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/824-garrison-resources) and will reroll follower abilities.



Patch 6.1 - Rush Orders
Patch 6.1 adds Rush Orders (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/misc/other?filter-search=Rush+Order&filter-reqlvl-min=90&filter-reqlvl-max=90) to your garrison, allowing you to instantly complete 5 active work orders. All of the profession rush orders are rewards of profession missions. Missions that reward 2 take 4 hours and require 2 followers, while missions that reward 3 take 6 hours and require 3 followers.
