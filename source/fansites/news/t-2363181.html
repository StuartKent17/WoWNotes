PTR Development Notes - Build 25807
Hi all!

Today's PTR update includes a new method for acquiring Legendary items: the Purified Titan Essence. This item can be purchased from Arcanomancer Vridiel in Dalaran in exchange for 175 Wakening Essences.

When used, the Purified Titan Essence will immediately grant a Legion Legendary item appropriate for your specialization. Note that this item is bind on pickup, and that the Legendary item granted by the Purified Titan Essence does not affect your chance to earn a Legendary through other methods.

Additionally, the Awoken Titan Essence (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/154879-awoken-titan-essence) used to upgrade Legendary items to item level 1000 is now sold by Arcanomancer Vridiel as a vendor item, rather than as a quest reward, for the same total cost (50 Wakening Essence (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/1533-wakening-essence)). The quest will no longer be offered, but can be turned in if your character already has it active.

Balance Druid and Assassination Rogue January 9th ChangesAssassination and Balance Rotation Adjustments on Tuesday
In recent weeks, we’ve seen the rise of a pair of unusual builds for Assassination Rogues and Balance Druids, both of which end up abandoning core abilities like Solar Wrath, Lunar Strike, and Mutilate in favor of Moonfire and Fan of Knives spam. While player ingenuity often devises interesting and unexpected ways of optimizing performance, in this case the resulting gameplay became one-dimensional, and we’ve heard feedback to that effect.

As a result, with the upcoming weekly resets in each region, starting on Tuesday, January 9, we’ll be making some targeted changes to reinforce the core rotational abilities of these two specializations, with only a minimal effect on overall balance:

This Week in WoW History
We're taking a look back at old MMO-Champion posts and World of Warcraft news that occurred during this time in previous years.

Patch 4.3.2
The test realms saw this patch pop up in 2012, and a few interesting additions were found. The popular 64 Bit Game Client was added in a build called “test x86”, and while this patch was only supposed to bring small bug fixes, it was also found that three new items made it into the game. The Feldrake, Eye of the Legion, and Demon Hunter’s Aspect were all found and speculated to become TCG items.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/january/thumb/feldrake.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/2609-Patch-4-3-2-Items-Blue-Posts-Diablo-Anniversary-Page)

Patch 5.2 The Thunder King
This content Patch brought a few additions to the game. A small update to the Transmog system allowed players to transmog similar weapon styles, such as Swords into Daggers. Warlock Island was also found, and speculated that it would be used during the Green Fire Warlock specific questline. This patch also added new Heirloom items which scaled to level 85, along with a new zone called Isle of Giants.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/january/thumb/ThroneOfThunder41.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/3070-Throne-of-Thunder-Raid-Preview-Patch-5-2-PTR-Live-Blue-Posts-Blue-Tweets-Fan-Art)

Time for an Item Level Squish
The Warlords of Draenor Item Level Squish was intended to bring the numbers of players health, damage, but especially that of bosses to a lower number. The last boss of Siege of Orgrimmar Mythic, was designed to heal to full health several times, because the game engine could not handle a number large enough to have his health progress as a regular boss would. It was also made very clear that this item level squish would not affect players clearing older content, such as raids for transmog, because everything will be scaled accordingly.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/january/thumb/squish.png (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/3668-Warlords-of-Draenor-Group-Finder-and-Item-Level-Squish-Blue-Tweets-Armory-Stats)

Warlords of Draenor Cinematic
A special event was held to reveal the cinematic trailer to the newest WoW expansion. Streamers, YouTubers, and Blizzard employees were all at this event, revealing the introduction cinematic, which gave us some insight into the Horde on the world of Draenor. This expansion would take place years in the past, when Gul’dan tried to corrupt the Orcs of Draenor with fel, however many resisted to preserve their freedom.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/january/thumb/cinematicreveal.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4615-Armory-Stats-Professions-Poll-Cinematic-Reveal-Event-Footage)

Warcraft in China
The Warcraft movie mall tour in China was the largest exhibition mounted for this new film. With over 150 props from the movie, visitors had the opportunity to see statues, Orc huts, and many more items during the tour. This was in an attempt to appeal to the Chinese audience, who ended up making up half of the gross total ($220.8 million) after the release.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/january/thumb/wcChina01.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/5391-Warcraft-Movie-in-China-This-Month-in-WoW-Blue-Tweets-Activision-Blizzard-and-MLG)

Activision Blizzard Acquires MLG Assets
Major League Gaming was once the largest esports organizers in North America. After years of innovating the esports scene, the emergence of other organizers such as ESL put MLG in a tough spot with multiple competitors. This caused some financial struggles causing them to sell all assets to Activision Blizzard for $46 million. The platform was, and is still used to host WoW tournaments, and the newly formed Overwatch League, among others.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/january/thumb/mlgstream.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/5404-Holy-Paladin-Artifact-Weapon-Preview-Tweets-Activision-Blizzard-and-MLG-Fan-Art)

Diablo 20th Anniversary
The Diablo franchise celebrated its 20th Anniversary early 2017. To celebrate this event, a new dungeon was added to Diablo III. This new area was designed in the style of Diablo I, with graphics, movement, and sound being filtered to capture the feel of the game. This event was also celebrated in WoW, where killing a Treasure Goblin would allow players to reach The Secret Cow Level, and kill the Cow King for some Diablo referenced loot.
