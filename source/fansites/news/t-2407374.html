Developer Interview - Battle for Azeroth
Forbes (https://www.forbes.com/sites/hnewman/2018/06/26/top-things-to-do-before-battle-for-azeroth-by-warcrafts-game-director/#1b1e2a4757b0) posted part two of its two part interview with Game Director Ion Hazzikostas about the top things to do in game before Battle for Azeroth launches.


The Chromie scenario will be unchanged and the rewards will continue to be available in Battle for Azeroth.
Class mounts aren't going anywhere.
There will be no change to mount and collectible drop rates from outdoor content in Broken Isles or Argus after Legion ends.
The tower challenge was tuned around ~905 ilvl and 50 artifact power levels so the addition of max artifact weapons should help lower the stress of the challenge.
Most of the challenges are about practicing mechanics more than gear, and should be doable with outdoor-Argus level gear.
Mythic Gul'dan and Argus mounts will become rare drops and the Violet Spellwing mount will be unavailable when Battle for Azeroth launches.
Legion Brawler's Guild achievements will be replaced with Battle for Azeroth Brawler's Guild achievements.
If you have 800 skill level in a profession before the patch, you will have all previous expansion's crafting maxed.
If you have 650 skill level in a profession, you'll have 100 in earlier expansions, 50/100 for Warlords of Draenor, and 0/100 for Legion.
Trinket procs and set bonuses will remain functional when the patch goes live.
There are no plans to have events that boost leveling experience in the pre-expansion patch such as the demon invasions.
The team is happy with the overall pace of leveling as of 7.3.5.
Battle for Azeroth should be a little more alt friendly than Legion.
Getting BiS legendaries for your spec and leveling each artifact weapon is no longer an issue, so it should be much easier to keep your other specs relevant.
Blizzard feels that Legion was a truly successful expansions and players in general have been more positive about it than other recent expansions.
The team is excited about the improvements to the gearing system which gives more control to the player and lessen the impact of randomness.
The pre-expansion patch will have the story events and world quests leading up to the burning of Teldrassil.
Catch up gear will be included in the Darkshore world quest rewards for the prepatch event.
The plan is to have the Battle for Lordaeron scenario available a week before launch of the expansion for players that have purchased Battle for Azeroth.

This Week in WoW History
We're taking a look back at old MMO-Champion posts and World of Warcraft news that occurred during this time in previous years.

Legendary Staff
Firelands brought a special item for caster DPS, Dragonwrath, Teracgosa’s Rest. To obtain this legendary item, players had to gain the base version, that was not much more than any other staff which dropped from the raid, however completing a series of quests, most of which were time gated based on collecting items from the raid, the staff became more powerful. After going through three upgrades, the staff reached its final phase, which made it sometimes duplicate harmful spells, and allowed the player to transform into Teracgosa’s Visage.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/june/thumb/dragonwrath4.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/2290-Dragonwrath-Teracgosa-s-Rest-Preview)

Fendral’s Flamescythe
Majordomo Staghelm, in the Firelands drops a weapon that was of particular interest to feral druids. For the first time, equipping this weapon completely changed the way druids look in the Cat form. This blazing form was sought by many druids, and later inspired a number of the Artifact Weapon appearances in Legion.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/june/thumb/fandralsflamescythe.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/2361-Patch-4-2-Rage-of-the-Firelands-on-Live-Servers)

Sky Golem
Patch 5.4 introduced a new mount that was craftable by Engineers, but most used by Herbalists. This mount allows players to gather herbs while mounted, making it a luxury item to have, especially at the beginning of new expansions when herb prices tend to be very high.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/june/thumb/skygolem.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/3295-Patch-5-4-Sky-Golem-Mount-Blue-Tweets-Sha-of-Fear-Solo-Kill-Weekly-News-Recap)

Legendary Rings
The Warlords of Draenor equivalent of the Legendary Cloak were the Rings. Healers, tanks, and DPS all got unique procs that empowered all of the specific roles if one person used it. While only a few players had access to these during the world first rance, the rings contributed to many other guilds defeating the Hellfire Citadel.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/june/thumb/healerLegendary.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4990-Patch-6-2-Legendary-Ring-Proc-Visuals-Blue-Tweets-Setup-of-the-Month-DLC-495)

Sun Darter Hatchling
The Sun Darter Hatchling was one of the few very intricate secrets discovered throughout Legion. Acquiring this pet requires players to solve a series of riddles in a hidden cave, and making use of many items from previous expansions. This secret was of course solved, by the Secret Finding Discord, and a full guide was published to navigate players through it.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/june/thumb/sundart.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/6744-New-PTR-Realm-Test-Sun-Darter-Hatchling-Tweets-Build-of-the-Month-WowCraft-37)
