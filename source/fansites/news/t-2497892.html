Glory of the Eternal Raider World First
Glory of the Eternal Raider (https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/13687-glory-of-the-eternal-raider) has been completed by Horde and Alliance groups from the Achievements Discord (https://discord.gg/achievements)! This achievement rewards the Azshari Bloatray (https://www.wowdb.com/items/167171-azshari-bloatray) mount, one of the four ray mounts added in Rise of Azshara.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2019/july/thumb/glorypalace01.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2019/july/glorypalace01.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2019/july/thumb/glorypalace02.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2019/july/glorypalace02.jpg)

The World First for this feat goes to a group from the Asian realms.
