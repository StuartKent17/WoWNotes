Blizzard Store - Armored Bloodwing Mount Now Available
The Armored Bloodwing (http://www.wowdb.com/items/95341-armored-bloodwing) mount is now available for 25 USD on the US store (http://us.blizzard.com/store/details.xml?id=1100002458) and 20 EUR on the EU store (http://eu.blizzard.com/store/details.xml?id=221005482).

It has been a while since our last giveaway, so we will be giving away 10 x Armored Bloodwing (http://www.wowdb.com/items/95341-armored-bloodwing) mounts!


The giveaway is for 10 x Armored Bloodwing (http://www.wowdb.com/items/95341-armored-bloodwing) mounts. You can obviously only win one.
To enter the giveaway, just reply to this post! Make sure to mention if you play on US or EU servers in your reply.
The giveaway will be closed on Friday at 11:59 PM EST and winners will be chosen randomly.
A note will be added to the top of the Friday news post when winners have been PMed.


Soar into battle atop the undisputed ruler of the night skies—the all-new Armored Bloodwing (http://www.wowdb.com/items/95341-armored-bloodwing) mount. This monstrous, flesh-eating bat is the perfect companion for trips that call for death and destruction. Once purchased from the Blizzard Store, the mount will be available to all present and future characters on a single Battle.net® account.
