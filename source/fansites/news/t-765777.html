Patch 4.0.1 - PTR Build 13082
A small PTR build is now available.

Spell Changes

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/270-Druid) / 3.3.5 Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?druid#,,12213) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..druid) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1117-Druid-Cataclysm))
Balance

Solar Beam (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/78675/) now affects targets within 5 yards, down from 10 yards.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/271-Hunter) / 3.3.5 Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?hunter#,,12213) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..hunter) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1118-Hunter-Cataclysm))
Beast Mastery

Mend Pet (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/136/) no longer heals for x health, now heals for 25% of the pet's total health over 10 sec.


Marksmanship

Flare (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/1543/) now has a 40 yards range, up from 30 yards.
Silencing Shot (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/34490/) interrupt effect now works on players.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/273-Paladin) / 3.3.5 Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?paladin#,,12213) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..paladin) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1670-Paladin-Cataclysm))
Retribution

Rule of Law (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/87461/) no longer increases the critical effect chance of Holy Shock, affects Hammer of the Righteous instead.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/274-Priest) / 3.3.5 Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?priest#,,12213) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..priest) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1123-Priest-Cataclysm))
Shadow

Mind Spike (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/73510/) now causes a "high amount of threat".


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / 3.3.5 Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?warlock#,,12213) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..warlock) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1125-Warlock-Cataclysm))
Demonology

Health Funnel (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/755/) now sacrifices 1% of your total health to restore 6% of your summoned Demon's total health every 1 sec.
(Metamorphosis) Demon Leap (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/54785/) has been changed back to his pre-Cataclysm version: Leap through the air and slam down on all enemies within 5 yards of the target area, causing 2213.67 to 2624.95 Shadow damage and stunning them for 2 sec.


Destruction

Searing Pain (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/5676/) now costs 12% of base mana, up from 8% of base mana. Base damage increased by 150%.
Immolate (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/348/) base damage increased by 55%.


Affliction

Soul Swap (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/86121/) now only removes your shadow damage-over-time effects from the target.
