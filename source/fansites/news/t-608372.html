Arena Tournament Qualifier 1 Winners
We posted about it yesterday (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?topic=8242.0), the results are now official !

American Realms
We’re glad to announce the winners of the 2008 World of Warcraft Arena Tournament's first qualification round. These three teams have demonstrated their Arena combat prowess against strong competition.
Dude I got it on FRAPS (http://armory.worldofwarcraft.com/team-info.xml?r=Arena+Tournament+2&ts=3&t=Dude+I+got+it+on+FRAPS&select=Dude+I+got+it+on+FRAPS)
duelists going for glad (http://armory.worldofwarcraft.com/team-info.xml?r=Arena+Tournament+2&ts=3&t=duelists+going+for+glad&select=duelists+going+for+glad&fl=1)
Frag Dominant (http://armory.worldofwarcraft.com/team-info.xml?r=Arena+Tournament+2&ts=3&t=Frag+Dominant&select=Frag+Dominant&fl=1)

There was a tie for the fourth-place team, and we will be holding a special playoff to determine the fourth-place winner. Each team will be invited to participate in the regional qualifier for a chance to further showcase their skills, win cash prizes, and compete for an invitation to the global finals. For more information about the tournament or details on signing up for the second qualification round, please see our Arena tournament information (http://www.worldofwarcraft.com/pvp/tournament/index.xml) page. Congratulations to all the winners!

European Realms
We're glad to announce the winners of the 2008 World of Warcraft Arena Tournament's first qualification round. These four teams have demonstrated their Arena combat prowess against strong competition.
HERCULES (http://eu.wowarmory.com/team-info.xml?r=Arena+Tournament+1&ts=3&t=HERCULES&select=HERCULES)

kata bestils anyweree (http://eu.wowarmory.com/team-info.xml?r=Arena+Tournament+1&ts=3&t=kata+bestils+anyweree&select=kata+bestils+anyweree)

Each team will be invited to participate in the regional qualifier for a chance to further showcase their skills, win cash prizes, and compete for an invitation to the global finals. For more information about the tournament or details on signing up for the second qualification round, please see our Arena tournament information page (http://www.wow-europe.com/en/info/basics/arena/tournament/). Congratulations to all the winners!
