Patch 4.3 Dragon Soul Raid Mounts
One of the latest PTR builds added 2 mounts to the Dragon Soul loot tables, the Life-Binder's Handmaiden (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/77069/life-binders-handmaiden/) drops from Madness of Deathwing (last encounter of the Dragon Soul raid) in Heroic Mode. People defeating Madness of Deathwing in normal mode will be rewarded with Reins of the Blazing Drake (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/77067/reins-of-the-blazing-drake/).
