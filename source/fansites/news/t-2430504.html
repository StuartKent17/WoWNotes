Mythic Uldir Progression
Mythic Uldir progress continues, with two guilds (Limit and Method) at 7/8. Method (https://www.wowprogress.com/guild/eu/tarren-mill/Method) is still doing coverage (https://www.method.gg/raidprogress) and streaming progression (https://www.method.gg/method-to-live-stream-mythic-uldir).


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/september/thumb/mythiculdirprog916.jpg (https://www.wowprogress.com/)


Horde in Control of Arathi Highlands Warfront Zonewarfronts
The Horde have now taken control of Arathi Highlands! With this comes several things:


Horde now have access to the Lion's Roar world boss until the Alliance retake control.
Horde have access to the cyclical quests in Arathi Highlands.

The League Will Lose (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53154-the-league-will-lose) - Warfronts Equipment Cache (https://www.wowdb.com/items/164577-warfronts-equipment-cache) (item level 340 warfront gear piece)
Executing Exorcisms (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53190-executing-exorcisms) - 300 Azerite Power
Boulderfist Beatdown (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53148-boulderfist-beatdown), Sins of the Syndicate (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53173-sins-of-the-syndicate), Twice-Exiled (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53193-twice-exiled), and Wiping Out the Witherbark (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53150-wiping-out-the-witherbark) - 200 War Resources Each.

Horde should have their Arathi Highlands rare mobs' loot lockouts reset. They don't seem to have reset for Alliance, however.
Alliance can now start contributing resources in the Boralus Harbor to fill the bar to activate the Battle for Stromgarde Scenario.



https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/july/thumb/warfrontbanner.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7856-Warfronts-Guide-Battle-for-Azeroth)



The Lion's Roar - Horde Arathi Highlands World Boss
The Lion's Roar (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7855-Battle-for-Azeroth-World-Boss-Guide-Loot-and-Locations#lionsroar) is now available for Horde players to kill for a chance at ilvl 370 loot!
