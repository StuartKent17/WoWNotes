The main phase of the Zombie Invasion has now started !
The infection now lasts 2 minutes instead of 5
Necropolis from the pre-Naxxramas events are back throughout the world ! The undeads are everywhere ! The pre-naxxramas mini-boss in Stratholme (Balzaphon) is also back and still drops his level 60 gear
The quest to kill Tenris Mirkblood (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?topic=22901.0) is now available at the Light's Hope Chapel and in most of the major cities !
You cannot kite huge packs of undead anymore, they now have a debuff to reduce your speed by 50%.

ABOUT PRINCE TENRIS:

He's not too hard. Get a tank, he hits for nothing regularly.

Go into karazhan, clear attunemens trash. Kill him if you want, don't if you dont. Run up the stairs where the repair guy is and clear that upper ring. There is a blood soaked door on the right up the stairs. Opening it will get you in combat with the boss, so be ready. Just have a tank tank him, and drag him around the circle whenever his Spirits build up to much, as they explode for 6k damage. Every so often he does a "Blood Mirror" thing and shoots a red beam out, which transfers all damage that he takes to that person, SO DONT HIT HIM WHEN HES DOING IT. Hunters can FD, You can vanish, bubble, etc to get it off.

He's not terribly difficult, and if you don't kill your own people or explode he's probably 5 mannable, I did it with 7 and it was cake once we stopped killing eachother and exploding.

If you have questions i'll try to answer them.
