Demon Invasion Event Test June 9
On Thursday, June 9, starting at 10:00 a.m. PDT, we will activate a new realm in the Legion Beta. This Beta realm will be named “Demon Invasion", and it will be dedicated to testing the pre-expansion demon invasion event with as many testers as possible for approximately 24 hours. We expect to remove the realm after 24 hours.

We ask that all Beta testers please join us in focusing testing on the “Demon Invasion” realm when it is available. While you may see demon invasions on other Beta realms, testing this content requires that your character remain at level 100, so we ask that you test on the “Demon Invasion” realm only.

Please direct your pre-Legion event feedback to the Beta General Discussion forum, and please direct any feedback you have about other Legion subjects to the appropriate dedicated subforum.

Thank you very much! We’ll see you at the defense of Azeroth!


Legion - Demon Invasion
The Demon Invasion event is going to have focused testing today, here is what we already know:


Several zones are going to be invaded: Tanaris, Westfall, Northern Barrens, Hillsbrad Foothills, Mulgore, and Azshara.
The Wetlands and Dun Morogh also have invasions in the data, but aren't referenced by the achievement.
Each invasion is a scenario entered by any Level 100-101 character that moves in to the invasion area.
Each scenario has 4 steps:

Defend against a general invasion at a point, such as Orgrimmar Rear Gate or Tarren Mill.
Defeat two lieutenants and a commander at that point.
Defeat the various invading forces marked on the map. Currently this seems to be a zone wide objective, will a fraction of a percent progress per NPC.
Defeat a boss NPC at the original invasion point that requires a group.

Completing the scenario rewards Stand Against the Legion (http://beta.wowdb.com/achievements/11200-stand-against-the-legion).
Completing all of the invasion scenarios rewards Defender of Azeroth: Legion Invasions (http://beta.wowdb.com/achievements/11201-defender-of-azeroth-legion-invasions)
You may also obtain Large Legion Chest (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/139049-large-legion-chest) and Small Legion Chest (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/139048-small-legion-chest), which contain Nethershard (http://beta.wowdb.com/currencies/1226-nethershard) and item level 685 armor (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/armor?filter-expansion=7&filter-search=fel&filter-ilvl-min=685&filter-ilvl-max=685)
Nethershard (http://beta.wowdb.com/currencies/1226-nethershard) will probably come from other sources as well, as there is a vendor that sells item level 700 neck, finger, trinket, and back items, as well as armor set transmogs.

Warcraft Movie - China Box Office Update
The Warcraft Movie continues to do well internationally, with Deadline posting (http://deadline.com/2016/06/warcraft-china-record-opening-box-office-imax-results-1201769124/) numbers for China:


The movie had a $46 million opening day in China.
This is the largest non-weekend movie opening of all time.
The previous record holder was Age Of Ultron with $28.3 million.
The midnight screenings accounted for $7.6 million, close to the all time record of Furious 7 ($7.9 million).
IMAX pre-sales also set a new record at $8.2 million.
The movie set another record by playing on 67.5% of all screens.
Projections were $122 million - $152 million for the opening week in China, but the movie may do even better than that.
The international total so far is around $121 million.
