For the really oblivious people who misinterpret this as a straight up Ulduar nerf, I'll spell it out for you:

The damage reduction to the aforementioned bosses is parallel to the damage reduction you would have recieved if you were stacking Curse of Weakness & Demoralising Shout / Roar. They've effectively "baked" this into each boss so the "stacking" requirement isn't so heavy. (ie. Every ideal composition would regard these two debuffs/classes as mandatory)

So what does this mean?

If you weren't stacking Curse of Weakness & Demoralising Shout before today, each encounter will now play out as though you were.

If you were stacking Curse of Weakness & Demoralising Shout before today, you won't notice any difference whatsoever.
