Submit Questions for the Developers
We have our first interview in a long time coming up at Blizzcon and as always, we want to let the community ask questions! This time our interview will be covering the design of Legion.

If you have any questions you think we should ask, be sure to add them to the comments of the linked post (http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/1885061-Submit-Questions-for-the-Developers) for consideration. We will pick the best questions and add them to our list!


Don't post any questions in the comments of this news post. Use the linked topic (http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/1885061-Submit-Questions-for-the-Developers) instead.
These should be questions about Legion, not Warlords of Draenor.
No class specific questions please. This includes things like balance between the different healing/tank/DPS classes and specs.
Keep questions reasonably short.

Day of the Dead
Day of the Dead (http://www.wowdb.com/world-events/409-day-of-the-dead) was updated last year. There are several wrestling inspired costumes you can purchase for <span class="money-gold" alt="g">100</span> from Chapman (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/34382-chapman). Once in the costume, you can do battle with other costumed players. You can use the costumes even after the holiday is over (https://twitter.com/Muffinus/status/526831207347023872)!

There seems to be a bug with the achievements this year, as you can't kill other players with the costume on, only bring them to 1 health. You need to be dueling for them to die.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/october/thumb/dotdCostumes.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/october/dotdCostumes.jpg)

If you haven't already done the other part of the holiday, be sure to check out the guide on Icy Veins (http://www.icy-veins.com/wow/day-of-the-dead).

Anyone assuming "It's Lordaeron" or "It's Dalaran" is failing at story concept. This is during the First War. That city, bar none, is the pre-sacked Stormwind. Not the one we have now, but the one a generation or two older, that was destroyed in the First War, before Lordaeron, Dalaran, or any other northern kingdoms were even involved in the ongoings of the Orcish Horde.

Small history lesson: The orcs did not move past Blackrock Mountain, northwards, until they dealt with Stormwind and the human forces from it. The First War was Stormwind vs Blackrock Mountain, and Stormwind lost (yes, the original game had 2 outcomes, the Orcs losing was due to a lack of foresight to a sequel, and was retconned out as not the actual storyline come Warcraft II). It was not until they pushed north, forming alliances with the Amani Trolls, and the refugees of Stormwind arrived in Lordaeron, that the northern kingdoms became involved in the Second War. That is when Kul Tiras (naval units in WC2), Lordaeron, Dalaran, Arathor, and Quel'Danas all stepped up to deal with them.
