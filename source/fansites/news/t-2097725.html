Patch 7.1 Live This Week
Patch 7.1 goes live this week! It brings the Karazhan dungeon, Trial of Valor raid, a continuation of the Suramar story, new pets, mounts, achievements and much more! Keep in mind that Trial of Valor will not be open for another two weeks.

PvP Changes
Patch 7.1 makes several changes to PvP:


Honor talents are no longer lost when you Prestige (More (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/765977-honor-talents-will-not-reset-71/#post19))
The amount of Artifact Power you will earn from a PvP victory is displayed in the UI
You will earn extra Honor and Artifact Power for your first win of the day in all unrated and rated PvP activities
Rated and Unrated Battleground wins will reward a piece of gear. Losses have a chance to reward gear based on the score.
Skirmish and Arena victories will have an increased chance to drop gear.
The item level of PvP gear now more closely mirrors the type of item level progression you see from World Quests
Each rated PvP bracket (2v2, 3v3, Rated Battlegrounds) has a weekly quest that rewards items based on your best rating from the previous week.
The quests for Arena brackets will require 10 wins, and the quests for the Rated Battleground bracket require three wins.
Below 2000, the quests reward Gladiator appearance gear and above 2000 they reward Elite appearance gear.

Return to Alcaz Isle
Alcaz Isle (http://wow.gamepedia.com/Alcaz_Island) has been invaded by Dr. Weavil and the Drudge Gnomes!


The island is now full of strong sentries and NPCs, as well as Dr. Weavil himself!
You won't be able to fly there, as the island has anti-air guns that will dismount you.
Dr. Weavil drops the Big Red Raygun (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/142265-big-red-raygun) toy
He also has Machine Fluid (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/142263-machine-fluid) and Drudge Fluid (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/142264-drudge-fluid) for your pets
Three new mechanical spiders to tame: Copper Arachnodrone (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/114992-copper-arachnodrone), Silver Arachnodrone (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/114993-silver-arachnodrone), and Golden Arachnodrone (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/114991-golden-arachnodrone)
