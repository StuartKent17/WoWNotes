Darkmoon Faire Has Begun
The brand new Darkmoon Faire started today and the new games await! You can get there via the locations where the old fair used to be, right outside of Thunder Bluff and Stormwind.

The daily quests now give just 1 x Darkmoon Prize Ticket (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/71970/darkmoon-prize-ticket/) each instead of 5.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2011/november/darkmoonfaire_th.jpg (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/2543-Patch-4.3-The-New-Darkmoon-Faire-is-on-Test-Realms!/)

Epic Gems
Epic quality gems were released in Patch 4.3 however they are not obtainable through prospecting ore this time. You can obtain them from:



Bosses within the Dragon Soul raid.
Mote of Darkness, which are obtained from the first 7 boss' in Dragon Soul, are used to purchase a Crystalline Geodes (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/78890/crystalline-geode/) from Dasnurimi on the ground floor of Wyrmrest Temple within the Dragon Soul raid that reward either an uncommon, rare, or epic gem.
Essence of Corrupted Deathwing drops from Madness of Deathwing in Dragon Soul and are used to purchase Elementium-Coated Geode (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/78891/elementium-coated-geode/) from Dasnurimi on the ground floor of Wyrmrest Temple within the Dragon Soul raid which obtain either a Shadow Spinal (Purple), Lava Coral (Orange) or Queens Garnet (Red).
Patterns are purchaseable from vendors in Orgrimmar & Stormwind. Purchasing a Tome of Burning Jewels (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/71949/tome-of-burning-jewels/) costs 4 x Illustrious Jewelcrafter's Token (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/52446/illustrious-jewelcrafters-token/) and rewards a random epic quality JC pattern, or you can purchase individual specific patterns for 5 x Illustrious Jewelcrafter's Token (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/52446/illustrious-jewelcrafters-token/) per pattern.
Currently Jewelcrafting only gems are not being upgraded.
