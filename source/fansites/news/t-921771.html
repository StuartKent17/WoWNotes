ArsTechnica (http://arstechnica.com/ask-ars/2011/04/how-to-build-your-own-computer-ask-ars-diy-series-part-i.ars/) recently released a nice guide for new builders (Part 2 (http://arstechnica.com/software/guides/2011/04/ask-ars-computer-building-diy-series-part-ii-software.ars) and Part 3 (http://arstechnica.com/gadgets/guides/2011/05/how-to-build-your-own-computer-ask-ars-diy-series-part-iiicases.ars)).
