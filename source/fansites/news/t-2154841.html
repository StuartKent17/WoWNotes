Skeleton is the general shape of the body, posture and movement

Well, sort of. The skeleton is the invisible bones and joints that are used to drive the animations for the model. It's also called an animation rig, or simply a rig.

Rigging and animating is probably the most time- and skill-intensive element of the art process, so reusing rigs allows them to dramatically reduce the workload on their artists, which allows them to produce more models.
