Patch 3.1 - Compiled PvE Loot List
The old Ulduar Loot Summary (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=853) page has been removed from the menu and replaced with the Compiled PvE Loot List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=861). This list include:

All the items discovered in Ulduar during the PTR testing
Emblems of Conquest (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?item=45624) Rewards

Argent Tournament Rewards

About 30 items have been added to the Ulduar loot list since the last update, the Ulduar-only lists will be available again very shortly.
