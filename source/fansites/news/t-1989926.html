Level scaling system as I understand it:

Say You are a level 100 in full greens, you have 500k HP, you go around and hit a mob for 10% of its health (Ignore the amount of health 10% represents for you). Then this mob hits you for 5% of your health (That's 25k HP).

A level 110 in full greens with 1m HP (1 million or twice your health); He then hits the mob for 10%
Then the mob hits him for 5% of his health (50k HP).

Now suddenly a level 110 in FULL MYTHIC gear with 2m HP comes by and hits this same mob, and he takes 40% of the mobs HP.

So a player 100 with level appropriate greens is as bad as a 110 with level appropriate in greens; A player 100 with level appropriate epics* is as good as a 110 with level appropriate epics.

So TL;DR

Everyone hits the mob for some % of its HP; The mob hits for a % of each individual HP. And while level does not improve your performance, (level appropriate) item quality does.

*The level 100 with epics is only at his best while he remains at 100, once he hits 101 (and reaches 50% of XP according to the latest post) he is no longer using the best possible gear for his level.
