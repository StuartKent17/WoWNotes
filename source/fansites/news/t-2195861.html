North America - Mage Tower Construction Complete
The Mage Tower has been completed on North American realms! This grants:


Access to the solo challenge scenarios that unlock new Artifact appearances
Veiled Wyrmtongue Chests, which are more lucrative
Portals to different Broken Shore zones
Knowledgeable (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/237137-knowledgeable) - When you loot an item which grants Artifact Power in a Raid or Dungeon, there's a chance you will receive a bonus Artifact Power token.




http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/april/thumb/mageTower01.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/april/mageTower01.jpg) http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/april/thumb/mageTower02.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/april/mageTower02.jpg)


The Mage Tower will remain active for three days, so use these buffs wisely. The Artifact challenge scenario is very difficult.


Your first attempt at the scenario is free
Subsequent attempts cost 100 x Nethershard (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1226-nethershard) each
The challenge varies based on your spec
