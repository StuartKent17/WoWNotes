- First, go here: http://polarski.cz/graph_paper/

- Use "Large Paper"

- Let each room be a 3x3 square. You won't use the corner boxes of those squares, just the N, S, E, W and center boxes.

- Fill in the corresponding N, S, E, W box with Orange for a path and Black for blockade. If there's a rune or orb, fill in the center box of that 3x3 square with the color of the rune or orb.

Tips:
- Remember orient yourself by putting your back to the south exit/blockade. You'll know which one that is because there's stairs and you'll face the altar and the torches will be in front of it.
- As you map your grid, if you realize you're about to butt an Orange entrance against a Black blockade, then don't go thru that entrance. If you do, it will teleport you to a random part of the maze.
- Sometimes, no matter what, you'll get teleported to a random part of the maze anyway. If so, stop and look at the room and look at your map, try to see if you can match the entrance/exit pattern with your map. If there's more than one possibility, pick an entrance and go thru it, then match up that room and the previous room with your map.
- If you get into a string (3 or more in a row) of straight rooms (N/S or E/W) you're probably (but not always) headed in the wrong direction.
