General

Players are now able to whisper or send party invites to players on any realm.


Classes

Movement speed bonuses from Stag Form combined with Stampeding Roar (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/77764) should now stack additively (previously, it did not and resulted in a loss of movement speed when both were combined).


Balance

Starfall (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/48505) and Sunfall should now correctly only hit enemies afflicted by the casting Druid's Moonfire (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/8921) or Sunfire (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/93402) without an active Glyph of Untamed Stars glyph.


Feral

Initial attacks for Rake (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/1822) and Thrash (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/106830) should no longer be incorrectly benefitting from Bloodtalons twice.


Talents

Fixed an issue where Wild Charge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/102401) in Aquatic Form (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/1066) was not working correctly.

Marksman

Chimaera Shot now deals 20% less damage in PvP combat.

Talents

Rogues with the Subterfuge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/108208) talent no longer incorrectly break out of Stealth (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/1784) from Prayer of Mending (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/33110) (Priest).
Fixed an issue where Subterfuge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/108208) and Vanish (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/1856) interacted incorrectly, resulting in the wrong action bar being displayed for a short time.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/276-Shaman) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/shaman) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#c))

Enhancement

Fire Nova (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/1535) visuals should now be visible to the casting Shaman.


Restoration

Lava Burst (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/51505) no longer deals extra damage in PvP combat for Restoration Shaman.


Garrisons, Followers, and Outposts

General

Music Roll: Totems of the Grizzlemaw (http://www.wowdb.com/items/122236-music-roll-totems-of-the-grizzlemaw) should now play the correct Grizzly Hills track.
Increased the spawn rate for the chest containing Music Roll: Night Song (http://www.wowdb.com/items/122205-music-roll-night-song) in Darnassus.


Garrison Campaign Quests

Scouting Missives (http://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-search=Scouting%20Missive) can now be completed if the player has already completed the daily version of the quests.
Herbs Galore: Completing the quest should now award 25 Primal Spirits (up from 15).
Portable Portals (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/38351-portable-portals) (Horde version): Fixed an issue where Gez'la was not offering the quest to eligible players.


Garrison Buildings

Dwarven Bunker / War Mill: Increased the chance for obtaining Follower armor and weaponry sets from work orders.
Mage Tower / Spirit Lodge, Level 1: It should now support up to 7 work orders (was incorrectly only able to support 1 work order).


Quests

Gorgrond

Mossy Fate: Players should now be able to complete the quest if they receive Kaz the Shrieker as a Follower.


Classes
Creatures and NPCs

Heirloom vendors Estelle Gendry (Horde) and Krom Stoutarm (Alliance) are now ensuring that they have a lot more personal space.


PvP

Ashran

Being in a Premade Group should no longer prevent players from entering Ashran.
Fixed an issue where players in the same raid or party but in different instances of Ashran would still see each other's markers on the map.


Battlegrounds and Arenas

Battlegrounds

[Requires a realm restart] Low-level items should no longer have an excessive amount of Bonus Armor.
War Games now should have a 25-second resurrection timer.
Temple of Kotmogu: Paladins can no longer use Divine Shield or Hand of Protection on players holding an orb in Temple of Kotmogu.
Temple of Kotmogu: Hunters can no longer use Camouflage to drop an orb in Temple of Kotmogu.


Collections

Heirlooms

[Requires a realm restart] Musty Tome of the Lost (http://www.wowdb.com/items/122390-musty-tome-of-the-lost) should now correctly accept Heirloom armor upgrade tokens (was incorrectly using weapon upgrade tokens instead).


Battle Pets

Battle Pet healing abilities should now heal for an amount much closer to the values stated in the tooltip.


Toy Box

S.E.L.F.I.E. Cameras can now be used while under a stealth effect.


Mounts

Chauffeured Chopper can no longer be driven by party members that are visiting another player's Garrison.


Classes
Items

Increased the amount of Stamina granted from Draenor food.

Tier 1 Stamina food now gives a bonus of 112 Stamina (up from 75).
Tier 2 Stamina food received no change and remains at 150 Stamina.
Tier 3 Stamina food now gives a bonus of 187 Stamina (up from 125).


The following glyphs are no longer bind on pickup.

Glyph of the Solstice (Druid)
Glyph of Flying Fists (Monk)
Glyph of Cleanse (Paladin)
Glyph of Purification (Priest)
Glyph of Purify Spirit (Shaman)


Primal Combatant's Medallion of Adaptation (http://www.wowdb.com/items/122709-primal-combatants-medallion-of-adaptation) should now correctly count towards the PvP trinket set bonus, Gladiator's Distinction.


Bug Fixes

General

Fixed an issue where Touch of the Naaru's visuals was not correctly attached to character models.
Primal Combatant's Medallion of Adaptation should now convert into the correct faction version for characters that had undergone a Paid Faction Transfer.

Achievements

Don't Call Me Junior (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/9901-dont-call-me-junior): Exploration Mission Master is no longer a requirement for completing the achievement.
[Requires a realm restart] Securing Draenor (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/9562-securing-draenor): Removed Challenge at the Ring of Blood as one of the requirements for completing the achievement.
What A Strange, Interdimensional Trip It's Been (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/9838-what-a-strange-interdimensional-trip-its-been): Credit should now be correctly awarded for defeating Rukhmar with Pepe atop your head.

We've gone over the feedback regarding visiting profession traders. There's now a hotfix being worked on to remove the random element and have profession traders rotate out daily. The hotfix isn't ready yet, but it will be implemented as soon as possible. In short, no more fur traders visiting for multiple days in a row.

The hotfix has been implemented on the live realms. Flight Path Logic
One specific flight path that I notice from doing the jukebox quest is trying to fly from the piece you get at Pinchwhistle to Everbloom to get the piece there. The flight path connects through a series of points that goes in a western arc through the middle of Talador then up through Gorgrond when it could easily have gone through a straighter path north.
This one surprised me, and you don't have to point all the way to Everbloom. In recreating what you did, I went to Pinchwhistle and hovered over Beastwatch, and saw the issue.

Thank you for the good example. (Blue Tracker (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/363498-flight-paths-still-illogical/) / Official Forums (http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/16410891906?page=3#57))
