Update - Almost forgot to mention that the Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/) is now updated with the latest build from the Patch 3.0.8 PTRs. Glyphs will be updated shortly as well.

PTR 3.0.8 - Build 9464
The test realms have been updated to a new build. It doesn't look like there is a lot of changes here but some of them are really interesting.

New installer and launcher interfaces
The patch installer (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/january/newinstaller.jpg) and launcher (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/january/newlauncher.jpg) have been updated with a new interface.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/january/newlauncher_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/january/newlauncher.jpg) http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/january/newinstaller_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/january/newinstaller.jpg)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/january/blizzdownload_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/january/blizzdownload.jpg)

Undocumented Changes
Undocumented changes are merely comparison of tooltips between the new version and the previous one. Changes made to spells aren't always reflected in tooltips and vice versa. Keep in mind that it's a test server and things might changes before it hits the live servers.
Items
Libram of Reciprocation (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40706) now has a chance to increase critical rating. (Old - Haste rating)

Enchanting
Enchant Weapon - Lifeward (http://www.wowhead.com/?spell=44576) - Permanently enchant a melee weapon to sometimes heal the wielder when striking in melee. (Old - healed for 140 to 160, it might just be a tooltip change and the healing could be the same as before)
Enchant Weapon - Titanguard (http://www.wowhead.com/?spell=62257) Permanently enchant a melee weapon to increase Stamina by 50. (Down from 75)

Engineering
Scrapbot (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40769) now let you repair your equipment.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/deathknight.jpg Death Knight (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=650) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?deathknight))
Blood
Vampiric Blood (http://www.wowhead.com/?spell=55233) now Temporarily grants the Death Knight 15% (down from 20%) of maximum health and increases the amount of health generated through spells and effects by 35% (down from 50%) for 20 sec. After the effect expires, the health is lost.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/druid.jpg Druid (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=700) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?druid))
Balance
Celestial Focus (http://www.wowhead.com/?spell=16924) doesn't affect Starfall anymore.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/hunter.jpg Hunter (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=620) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?hunter))
Survival
Lock and Load (http://www.wowhead.com/?spell=56344) now has a 3/7/10% chance to proc on periodic damage. (Up from 2/4/6%)
Tranquilizing Shot (http://www.wowhead.com/?spell=19801) cooldown has been increased from 6 to 8 seconds. Mana cost decreased from 12% of base mana to 8%.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/paladin.jpg Paladin (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=610) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?paladin))
Glyph
Glyph of Turn Evil (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=41102) now also increases the cooldown of the spell by 8 sec.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/rogue.jpg Rogue (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=630) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?rogue))
Assassination
Ambush (http://www.wowhead.com/?spell=48691) now awards 2 combo points. (Up from 1)
Lethality (http://www.wowhead.com/?spell=14137) now only increase the damage of of all combo moves that do not require stealth.

Just wanted to note that the Titanguard for weapons was pulled from the PTR. This was on the front page of mmo 3 days ago :).
Here is the reasoning for it:
Had this enchant gone live it would have become THE enchant for nearly all tanks. It would not have been an interesting choice. We would have had to keep introducing higher tiers of the same enchant, or else players would just keep using this version for perpetuity. We put Stamina already on many items. We don't think your weapon needs one as well. I received messages from several tanks for very prominent guilds asking us to please not go forward with this enchant. They understood why it was bad for the game.

Death knights have access to superior enchants in part because they do not have a shield or ranged weapon slot and the stats provided by those items.

The PTR is for testing. If you find yourself frustrated that things change between the PTR and live, I would suggest you stop paying attention to the PTR notes. Even if a change like this had already gone live, we probably would have changed it anyway. We did not like what this enchant would have done for the game.

And
There are already stamina enchants on many pieces of gear. Casters can go that route of stacking spellpower on as much gear as possible, but if they ignore int and mana regen, that spellpower will be for naught. With tanks, we have found that given the choice between stamina and anything else (assuming you are comparing things of equivalent level) stamina will always win. One of the few enchants where you actually have a decison would be replaced by yet another stamina enchant. I am pretty confident the same thing would happen with a stamina to cloak enchant.

Here is the player's point of view: Ah, an enchant for my weapon. That will help me survive and help out my healers, therefore making me a slightly better tank. Maybe I'll be slightly better prepared for KT or Sarth + 3 or Ulduar. Awesome.

Except... here is the developer's point of view: Okay, we just buffed every tank in the game by 750 health. That means we now have to make every mob hit slightly harder to compensate. Otherwise we are just buffing tank survivability for no reason. The net effect to you would be pretty negligible in the long run, while any tank who chose not to put stamina on his or her weapon would suddenly be at a disadvantage.
