Patch 8.0.1 Hotfixes - October 5, 2018
Items

Heart of Azeroth

Retaliatory Fury (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/280579-retaliatory-fury) should now activate from abilities cast on friendly targets.



Player versus Player

https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/275-Rogue) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/rogue) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#Q))

Assassination

The Fan of Knives (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/51723-fan-of-knives) radius increase granted by Flying Daggers (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/198128-flying-daggers) (PvP Talent) and Fan of Blades (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/278664-fan-of-blades) (Azerite Trait) no longer stack.
Fan of Blades effectiveness reduced by 75% when engaged in combat with enemy players.
