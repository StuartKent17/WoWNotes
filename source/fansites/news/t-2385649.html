Mythic: The Temple of Sethraliss - Restore the Avatar of Sethraliss in the Temple of Sethraliss on Mythic difficulty.
Ready for War - Complete the war campaign in Zandalar and Kul Tiras.
Ready for War - Complete the war campaign in Kul'Tiras and Zandalar.
Allied Races: Dark Iron Dwarf - Complete the Dark Iron dwarf unlock quest line. Reward: Dark Iron Dwarf Race Unlocked
Allied Races: Dark Iron Dwarf (Hidden Character Achievement) - Complete the quest below.
Allied Races: Mag'har Orc (Hidden Character Achievement) - Complete the quest below.
Allied Races: Mag'har Orc - Complete the Mag'har orc unlock quest line. Reward: Mag'har Orc Race Unlocked
Halls of Containment - Defeat the following bosses in the first section of Uldir.
Crimson Descent - Defeat the following bosses in the depths of Uldir.
Heart of Corruption - Defeat the following bosses and extinguish the corruption in the lowest reaches of Uldir.
Mythic: Taloc - Defeat Taloc in Uldir on Mythic difficulty.
Mythic: MOTHER - Defeat MOTHER in Uldir on Mythic difficulty.

Mythic: Zul - Defeat Zul in Uldir on Mythic difficulty.
Mythic: Mythrax - Defeat Mythrax in Uldir on Mythic difficulty.
Mythic: G'huun - Defeat G'huun in Uldir on Mythic difficulty.
Cutting Edge: G'huun - Defeat G'huun in Uldir on Mythic difficulty, before the release of the next raid tier.
Ahead of the Curve: G'huun - Defeat G'huun in Uldir on Heroic difficulty or higher, before the release of the next raid tier.
