Legion - The Nighthold: Tichondrius
Tichondrius (http://wow.gamepedia.com/Tichondrius) is a boss in the Nighthold.


<iframe width="900" height="506" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/HKTRQwy3g08?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>


Overview - Tichondrius uses foul carrion magic to corrupt players. First afflicting them with <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/206480-carrion-plague">Carrion Plague</a> and then targeting them with <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/206474-seeker-swarm">Mass Carrion Swarm</a>. Players must use Fel Spires to avoid the dark energy that Tichondrius periodically releases with <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/213531-echoes-of-the-void">Echoes of the Void</a>. As the fight progresses, Tichondrius will use <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/206311-illusionary-night">Illusionary Night</a> to trap all players in an illusion for 30 sec. Inside of the illusion he will assault players with <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/215988-carrion-nightmare">Carrion Nightmare</a>.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Damage Dealers - Carefully position to avoid spreading <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/206480-carrion-plague">Carrion Plague</a>.
Quickly kill Phantasmal Bloodfangs and obtain <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/206466-essence-of-night">Essence of Night</a> while trapped in the <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/206365-illusionary-night">Illusionary Night</a>.
Coordinate <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/212795-brand-of-argus">Brand of Argus</a> detonations to avoid lethal raid wide damage.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/healer.png" title="Healer" /> Healers - Players with <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/206480-carrion-plague">Carrion Plague</a> will take sustained damage until the debuff can fade during <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/206365-illusionary-night">Illusionary Night</a>.
Position for and quickly dispel <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/216040-burning-soul">Burning Soul</a>.
Tanks will take significant damage while the Nightborne are active.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Tanks - Position adds outside of <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/206893-vampiric-aura">Vampiric Aura</a>.

Raid Testing Schedule - April 15-18
On Friday, April 15, and Monday, April 18, we will continue our Legion Alpha raid testing.

Remember that Heroic difficulty scales for any group size between 10 and 30 players, so feel free to assemble and bring a group of any size within that range. Mythic, of course, requires 20 and does not scale.

There are a couple of bugs active in the current build - the obvious phasing issue, and an issue that causes many player abilities (and some boss abilities) to not properly consume resources. We have a hotfix in flight for the second issue as I write this; the phasing issue is under investigation, but shouldn't impact raid tests. Of course, should anything change on that front, we'll update our testing plans accordingly.

Discipline Priest Feedback -- Build 21466 -- 13-Apr
You guys have pretty much figured it out, but, right--the passive damage increase on the artifact should not increase Atonement healing. The traits on the artifact itself should ultimately increase Disc's total healing ability (including both Atonement and non-Atonement) by the same amount as the other healers' artifacts. We do want to figure out how to make this clearer.

Disc should be able get use out of DPS trinkets, but we have to make sure they're not overpowered. For example, they have to add less healing than healing trinkets. Corrupted Starlight as the twin problems of being AoE (which will always risk being overpowered for Atonement purposes) and being an RPPM proc (which results in it adding the same amount of DPS to Disc's low damage as it does to a caster's high damage). We will likely reduce RPPM proc rates for Disc on trinkets like this. (Blue Tracker (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/408216-discipline-priest-feedback-build-21466-13-apr/) / Official Forums (http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/20743224180?page=1#18))
