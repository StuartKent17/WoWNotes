Update - Added a few more unconfirmed loot to the Loot Table.

Tonight on the EU-Vek'nilash server, SK-Gaming (http://www.sk-gaming.com/wow/) managed to get the world first kill of M'uru, the fifth boss of Sunwell Plateau. Only Kil'jaeden remains but sadly, we'll have to wait for the opening of the last door of the instance in a few weeks.

Loot

Loot table
3 "Naaru Sliver" trinkets were in the Pre-PTR 2.4 leak. The Steely Naaru Sliver (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/january/steelynaarusliver.jpg) has been confirmed on live servers and we can assume that the Blackened and Glimmering had their stats unchanged. Some of these items didn't drop from M'uru yet but it's very likely we'll see them very soon.
