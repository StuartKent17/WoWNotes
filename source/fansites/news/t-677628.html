There's a few things missing from your ability list that I've seen.

Dominate Mind is 25-man only
Animate Dead is the ability creating Reanimated Adherents/Cultists
Summon Vengeful Spirit in phase 2. (who cast Vengeful Blast when they catch a player, 25,000 Shadowfrost damage to targets within 20 yards)

Fanatics have Fanatic's Determination (99% immunity to physical damage)

She stacks Touch of Insignificance on her tank, 10% threat generation reduction per stack.
