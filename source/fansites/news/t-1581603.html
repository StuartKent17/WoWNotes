Warlords of Draenor Garrisons - Inn and Tavern
Today we are taking a look at the Inn and Tavern building (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/36-lunarfall-inn#related:buildings) in the garrison. Currently only the first two tiers are accessible on beta until you earn the Stay Awhile and Listen (http://beta.wowdb.com/achievements/9703-stay-awhile-and-listen) achievement and unlock the Level 3 Blueprint.

The Inn/Tavern (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/36-lunarfall-inn#related:buildings) take up a Medium plot which can also be used for a Barn (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/133-barn), Gladiator's Sanctum (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/161-gladiators-sanctum), Lumber Mill (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/138-lumber-mill) or Trading Post (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/145-trading-post). Once upgraded to Tier 3, your garrison will have two Medium plots available.

Abilities
This building is good for earning more followers, novelty items from the daily quests, and gold from the Treasure Hunt missions.


Level 1 - Each day, a visitor offering a dungeon quest with generous rewards will spend the day at your inn.
Level 2 - Recruits random followers each week. You can specify traits and abilities you're especially interested in.
Level 3 - Unlocks the particularly lucrative Treasure Hunt missions.


Follower Recruitment
Once a week you can select a follower with a trait or ability that you are interested in to recruit. You are then offered a choice of three followers.
