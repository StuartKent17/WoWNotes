Class changes
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman
The global cooldown on casting totems has been reduced. It is now 1 second instead of 1.5
Flametongue Weapon now reduces the effectiveness of healing spells on your target by 50%. Lasts 5 seconds and refresh every hits. (Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/february/flametonguedebuff.jpg))
Toughness now Increases your armor value from items by 2/4/6/8/10% and reduces the duration of movement impairing effects on you by -10/20/30/40/50%.
Shamanistic rage now let you regenerate mana equal to 30% of your attack power instead of 15. Duration reduced to 15 seconds. (Was 30 seconds)



http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock
Lifetap (Rank 1) now converts 5% maximum life into 5% maximum mana. (Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/february/lifetapranks.jpg))
Lifetap (Rank 2) now converts 12% maximum life into 12% maximum mana. (Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/february/lifetapranks.jpg))
Lifetap (Rank 3) now converts 20% maximum life into 20% maximum mana. (Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/february/lifetapranks.jpg))
