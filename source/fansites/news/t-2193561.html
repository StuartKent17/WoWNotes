Blue Posts
Lag Tolerance Change
We've renamed the custom lag tolerance cvar to "SpellQueueWindow".

You can change it by entering the following in the chat window:
/console SpellQueueWindow X

Replace "X" with the lag tolerance you'd like to test (in milliseconds). (Blue Tracker (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/1054348-spell-queue-bug/) / Official Forums (http://eu.battle.net/forums/en/wow/topic/17615331877?page=1#2))

Legion Companion App Update
The Legion Companion App has been updated to support World of Warcraft Patch 7.2, including:

View the status of Legionfall Buildings and contribute to their construction.
Research, collect, and use Artifact Knowledge items.
View Legion Assault status and related quests.
Bug fixes.
(Official Notes (https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.blizzard.wowcompanion&hl=en))
