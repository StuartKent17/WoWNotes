Call to Arms: Stormsong Valley
This week's Call to Arms is Call to Arms: Stormsong Valley (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52782-call-to-arms-stormsong-valley) or Call to Arms: Stormsong Valley (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52957-call-to-arms-stormsong-valley). Head to Stormsong Valley with warmode enabled to kill 10 players of the opposing faction and loot an air supply. This will reward you with 250 honor and 75 reputation for either 7th Legion (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2159-7th-legion) or The Honorbound (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2157-the-honorbound).


No Artifact Knowledge Yet
Much like in Legion, the amount of artifact power needed for your Heart of Azeroth (https://www.wowdb.com/items/158075-heart-of-azeroth) will begin to decrease week by week. This has not started yet, however, and can be expected soon in the future.
