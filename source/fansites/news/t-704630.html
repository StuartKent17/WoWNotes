Patch 3.3.3a is now Live
A minor patch has been deployed to fix tons of bugs, we don't have the "real" patch notes for the moment but it most likely fixes some of the issues reported in the list posted earlier this week.
3.3.3 Known Issues List [Updated 3/25/2010] (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/13/23767614599-333-known-issues-list-updated-3232010.html)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- World of Warcraft Client Patch 3.3.3a
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We are updating World of Warcraft with a minor bug fix patch. For updated information regarding the changes made in this patch, please visit the ficial Bug Report forum at: http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/board.html?sid=1&forumId=10023

Gemming Old Items Issue
There is currently an issue that we are aware of where gems and enchantments are missing from items that had them before we released patch 3.3.3 this week. This appears to be affecting some equipment that was acquired before the Wrath of the Lich King expansion. We are planning to resolve this during the next maintenance on Tuesday, March 30th, and recommend that you do not try to gem or re-gem any of this gear until after that time. We will provide further updates if this situation changes.

* The issue where PowerPC G4/G5 users were getting disconnected after login has been fixed.
