Patch 7.2 - New Quests
Several new quests were added in Patch 7.2, including new profession quests! These quests have you collect materials before you can craft the new legendary items.

Patch 7.2 - Crafted Legendary Items
The new crafted legendary items currently don't have a proc. All of them require 65 x Blood of Sargeras (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/124124-blood-of-sargeras), profession specific materials, legendary materials from the above quest line, and a Felessence Legion-Flask (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/146659-felessence-legion-flask).

The Flask has creation spells in the database, being upgraded from an Empty Felessence Vessel (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/146655-empty-felessence-vessel) to the Felessence Legion-Flask (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/146659-felessence-legion-flask) through Uncommon (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/146656-felessence-vessel), Rare (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/146657-felessence-phial), Epic (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/146658-felessence-phylactery), and finally a legendary version (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/146659-felessence-legion-flask).
