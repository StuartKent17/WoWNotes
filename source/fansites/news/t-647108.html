Iron Council Hard Mode EU First Kill
Silent (http://silent-guilde.fr/) (EU-Illidan) defeated the Iron Council in hard mode last night, the Hall of Fame (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=864) has been updated.

Patch 3.1.2 - Battle-Bots
Patch 3.1.2 will include a new mini-pet, the Battle-Bot. We don't have many details on these bots for the moment but it looks like they will have a couple of abilities and will use multiple types of fuel.
Battle-Bot - Right Click to summon and dismiss your Battle-Bot
Blue Battle Fuel - Fill up your Battle-Bot with blue fuel.
Red Battle Fuel - Fill up your Battle-Bot with red fuel.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/april/battlebots_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/april/battlebots.jpg)
