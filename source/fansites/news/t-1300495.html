Patch 5.3 Live This Week
Patch 5.3 is going live this week, less than three months after Patch 5.2 went live (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/3152-Patch-5-2-Live-This-Week)!


Heroic Scenarios give 120-150 Valor Points for your first every day and a chance to get a item level 516 item (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items?filter-patch=16758&filter-ilvl-min=516&filter-ilvl-max=516&filter-reqlvl-min=90). Challenge modes also give you a chance at item level 516 gear.
The legendary quest line continues, with a reward of item level 600 cloaks (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32861-cloak-of-virtue).
The Kor'kron have taken over the Troll area in Orgrimmar, as well as Razor Hill, and are advancing on Sen'jin Village. You will team up with Thrall, Vol'jin, and Chen Stormstout to push them back and retake Razor Hill.
The weekly Barrens quest (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32862-battlefield-barrens) will give you a chance to buy an item level 489 item (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items?filter-patch=16758&filter-random-stats=1&filter-ilvl-min=489&filter-ilvl-max=489).
There are several new mounts in this patch, with the most interesting being the http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/97989.jpg Enchanted Fey Dragon (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/97989-enchanted-fey-dragon).
You can now use items in your bank and Void Storage for transmogrification.
A quest (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32816-path-of-the-last-emperor) was added that will reveal the final Sha and reward you with item level 502 boots.
Four new scenarios, one battleground, and a new arena were added to the game.
There are lots of PvP changes, with a 65% base Resilience, less PvP Power and Resilience on gems, no more Resilience on most gear, the Conquest catch up cap implemented, and more!
The Brawler's Guild has some new bosses and other updates, along with some new shirts (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/armor/shirts?filter-patch=16758).
Heirloom shields (http://www.wowdb.com/items/armor/shields?filter-quality=128) are finally on the vendors.
You can now level mining and herbalism in Pandaria, no matter what skill level you are at.
There are many more changes, so be sure to read the full post!

Heroic Scenarios
Patch 5.3 adds Heroic Scenarios, which are made more difficulty by NPCs that do more damage and have increased health.


Heroic Scenarios require a pre-made group and an item level of 480 to queue for.
The first Heroic Scenario of the day will reward 120 Valor Points, with an additional 30 if you are able to complete the time based bonus objectives.
You will also earn a http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/98134.jpg Heroic Cache of Treasures (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/98134-heroic-cache-of-treasures), which has a chance to drop epic item level 516 gear (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items?filter-patch=16758&filter-ilvl-min=516&filter-ilvl-max=516&filter-reqlvl-min=90).
Doing another scenario after the first reverts to the old http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/92813.jpg Greater Cache of Treasures (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/92813-greater-cache-of-treasures) and 70 Valor Points plus 30 Valor Points for the bonus objectives.
You can only complete a Heroic Scenario or Normal Scenario for the daily bonus Valor Points reward, not both.
Heroic Deeds (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32900-heroic-deeds) is a one time only quest, found in your faction's Vale city, which will grant you the http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/98546.jpg Bulging Heroic Cache of Treasures (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/98546-bulging-heroic-cache-of-treasures). This has a guarenteed item level 516 item inside.

Legendary Questline
<span id="legendaryquest32"></span>Patch 5.3 continues Wrathion's questline with a few more quests. You first visit Wrathion (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32598-a-reckoning) so that he can send you to collect blessings from the four Pandaria Celestials (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32805-celestial-blessings). There are four challenges, one for tanks, healers, and two different ones for DPS. After completing the challenge most suited to your class and spec, you will return to Wrathion to receive (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32861-cloak-of-virtue) one of the item level 600 cloaks below. The line ends with you waiting for Patch 5.4 (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32870-preparing-to-strike) and the Siege of Orgrimmar.

If you aren't caught up just yet, keep in mind that the A Test of Valor (http://ptr.wowdb.com/achievements/8031-a-test-of-valor) quest now only requires you to earn 3000 Valor Points, down from 6000.

Be sure to listen to the audio if you are interested in the Burning Legion and what is coming!

The Darkspear Rebellion
Patch 5.3 starts setting up the story for Patch 5.4 and brings the fight back to Azeroth. One of the first things Horde players will notice is the Troll area of the Valley of Spirits in Orgrimmar has been taken over by the Kor'kron Guard (http://www.wowpedia.org/Kor%27kron_Guard), with Kor'kron Overseers taking aim at the residents. The trainers, bank, auction house, and every other NPC has been shut down, only talking about how dangerous it is now.

Going west to the Barrens, it looks different if you are level 90, similar to the Krasarang Wild changes. You can help to escort the Barrens Caravans through safely, as they are attacked when they pass through the Barrens. Doing so will give you a box of surplus supplies, required for a new weekly quest (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32862-battlefield-barrens-weekly).

Four new Kor'kron camps have been added around the Barrens, harvesting Stone (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/97543-korkron-stone), Lumber (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/97530-korkron-lumber), Oil (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/97544-korkron-oil), and Meat (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/97545-korkron-meat) for the war effort. It appears they have also hired some Goblin Mercenaries to handle some of the work.

There are several quests that will also take place here, such meeting up with Sully and Amber in Durotar (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32808-a-little-field-work), who go through the new Dranosh'ar Blockade area to gather intelligence. You then head down (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32812-the-darkspear-rebellion) to find Vol'jin and Chen Stormstout in Sen'jin Village. After this you begin to collect supplies from the Barrens (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32819-battlefield-barrens), defend Sen'jin Village (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32813-battle-of-senjin-village) from the Kor'kron assault, and finally capture Razor Hill (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32814-battle-of-razor-hill) from the Kor'kron.

The Alliance are taking part in this quest line with separate Alliance caravans in the Barrens, and are able to open a dialogue after rescuing Zen'tabra (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32810-the-darkspear-rebellion) from the Kor'kron.

Battle of Razor Hill
The Horde begin by traveling to Sen'jin village after completing two of the new scenarios, and speaking with Vol'jin (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32867-voljin-of-the-darkspear). He sends you in to the Barrens (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32819-battlefield-barrens) to collect 15 Lumber, Stone, Oil, and Meat, which requires killing one mob per item, looting crates with the items, or escorting the caravans that travel through the Barrens.

Deepwind Gorge Battleground
Deepwind Gorge is set in the western part of the Valley of the Four Winds, in a gorge that is only present in the battleground, not in the regular world. This is a 15 player battleground for level 90 players.

The battleground has two different objectives, the first being the three mines. There is a northern Pandaren mine, central mine, and a southern Goblin mine. The mines are captured like the bases in other battlegrounds, just clicking the flag and capturing it. While you hold the mine NPCs will mine gold for your faction, bringing you closer to the 1600 resources required to win.

The second objective is the cart of gold placed at each faction's base. You can click the cart to attach it to yourself, and then walk back to your base to capture it. You do not need your own team's cart to be present to capture, unlike the normal battleground flags. Holding the cart limits your movement speed to normal running speed. If you kill the enemy cart holder, you must click on the cart to return it to your base before someone on the other team clicks to pick it up. Capturing the cart rewards you with 20 bonus honor and 200 resources and actually steals 200 from the enemy team.

Brawler's Guild Changes
Patch 5.3 adds several new bosses to the Brawler's Guild, as well as some other things:


Card Trader Ami will sell you cards (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items?filter-search=Challenge+Card) that allow you to fight bosses from ranks you have already defeated.
Defeating some of the new bosses will give you access to some of the new Brawler's Guild shirts (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/armor/shirts?filter-patch=16758). These shirts all have an equip effect, which looks similar to the effect of Precious' Ribbon (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/52019-precious-ribbon), rewarding you with an extra buff on you at all times.
Defeating all of the new bosses will give you the http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/98543.jpg Wraps of the Blood-Soaked Brawler (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/98543-wraps-of-the-blood-soaked-brawler).
A new VIP lounge has been added on the zeppelin circling the arena for Rank 8 players. The Alliance also have a VIP lounge in the balcony.
Reaching Rank 9 will give you another invite to the brawler's guild.
Reaching Rank 10 rewards access to purchase the http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/98405.jpg Brawler's Burly Mushan Beast (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/98405-brawlers-burly-mushan-beast).
Spectator totems and consoles have been added to give you a better view of the fight.
The Alliance fight pit floor has been raised to make it easier to view the fight.

Heirloom Shields and Patch 5.2 Heirloom Price Reductions
Patch 5.2 added two new Heirloom shields to the game, but there were some issues with them and they never were added to vendors.

It appears the issues have been resolved, and we will see http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/93903.jpg Weathered Observer's Shield (http://www.wowdb.com/items/93903-weathered-observers-shield) and http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/93902.jpg Flamescarred Draconian Deflector (http://www.wowdb.com/items/93902-flamescarred-draconian-deflector) in Patch 5.3 at the cost of 2175 Justice Points.

Patch 5.3 will also reduce the price for the new heirloom items added in Patch 5.2 (http://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-patch=16408&filter-quality=128) by 60%. Don't forget that two new Heirloom shields (http://www.wowdb.com/items/armor/shields?filter-quality=128) that cost 2175 Justice Points each are also coming in Patch 5.3!

Please note that only the upgrade Heirlooms that were added in Patch 5.2 have had a cost reduction, the old level 80 heirlooms are still the same price.


Patch 5.2 Heirlooms purchased with Justice points had costs reduced from 2175, 2725, and 3500 Justice Points to 870, 1090, and 1400 Justice Points.
Patch 5.2 Heirlooms purchased with Honor points reduced from 2175, 2725, and 3500 Honor points to 870, 1090, and 1400 Honor points.
Patch 5.2 Heirlooms purchased with Darkmoon Faire prize tickets reduced from 110, 130, and 160 prize tickets to 44, 60, and 64 prize tickets.

Profession Changes
There aren't many profession changes in Patch 5.3, but the major one makes leveling mining and herbalism possible in Pandaria with a low skill level.


Miners can now level mining from a low skill level completely in Pandaria. Nodes will yield http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/97512.jpg Ghost Iron Nuggets (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/97512-ghost-iron-nugget) with the amount being determined by your skill level. You can combine ten nuggets to make one ore.
Herbalists can now level herbalism from a low skill level completely in Pandaria. Nodes will yield a partial herb (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/trade-goods/herb?filter-patch=16758) with the amount being determined by your skill level. You can combine 10 of the partial herbs to form a complete herb.
Currently on the PTR, you will sometimes get fragments and the actual herb or ore when gathering near the minimum level for that node type.
The Engineering mount and pet (Skyclaw and Pierre) won't make it (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/3222-Engineering-Items-Delayed-BlizzCon-Tickets-Wed-Pet-Battle-Changes-Blue-Tweets-DLC) in to Patch 5.3.
