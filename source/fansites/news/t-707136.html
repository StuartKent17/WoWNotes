That is precisely the point!

1. With the buff changes in Wrath, it is no longer absolutely necessary to bring class X for buff Y as class Z can also provide buff Y.

2. It is impossible for Blizzard to balance out class DPS exactly without giving everyone the same abilities

3. The new philosophy is to have pures do more dps than hybrids but within a small enough gap that for all but the most hardcore players, "bring the player not the class" applies.

This thread actually reflects that pretty well. Some enh top charts, others lag behind. This is going to be the case except in the top 5% of the guilds, or less. So unless you're in that top 5%, your dps is much more a function of your skill than of your class' abilities, and this buff most likely will not affect you (you're either very good and already topping charts or you have a lot to learn and are sitting around tank level) except in that tiny number of cases where (as was calculated in a previous post) you're 200 or so dps behind a whole bunch of dps and are somehow being sat because you are consistently just that little bit behind.

If you *are* in the top 5%, then straight up damage buffs or nerfs will affect your raid slot, but they're still somewhat random as, as it has been pointed out numerous times, single target stationary dps is where we do pretty well. Movement related abilities might provide a more useful buff, but only in the case where Blizzard has hard evidence that our dps drops off due to movement significantly more than that of other melee given equal skill.

To sum up, I'm not sure exactly why we need a buff, how big the impact of this buff will be, and whether it will be mostly erased by cata and upcoming changes.

Finally, a) as someone pointed out, that EJ chart is based on highly biased data, and should be considered with much suspicion (and note that even the lowest non-zero row on the chart has PLENTY of dps for every fight in icc 10 man normal). b) I'd like to echo another previous post and say that our survivability is mostly dependent on our skill and healer skill.
