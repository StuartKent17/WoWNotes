Some interesting passages summed up:

- New CoT Instance Stratholme.
- Fight alongside Arthas, "cleanse" the city from the scourge and burn the houses.
- It's a 5 man instance for level 80 characters. It's planned that you come back to the instance repeatedly on level 80.
- CoT-Stratholme has nothing to do with unlocking or developing the Deathknight storyline.
- Death-Knight starts probably in the level range 55 to 60.
- You don't need to be 80 to unlock the Death-Knight. 55 to 60 is most likely enough.
- They will explain in detail why "your" Death-Knight manages to defy the cotroll of the lich king.
- Any race can become a death-knight. You unlock the DK with a character and the DK will be of that race even if it is a gnome. :P You'll still keep your old char though.
- Seems like you have to face some challenge or test after the creation of the DK, only if you succeed you get your Death Knight. (all pretty vague, there's no hint however that you can't repeat that challenge or that any other mechanic is planned to avoid anyone and his mom rolling a DK.)
- They developed a system that allows the runes to light up on the weapon when the combo is changed. (combination of runes?)

2007-08-23, 08:53 PM
I don't like the idea of tauren, troll or gnome death knights either... hope they don't release it like that. Keep in mind that the buffed article is german, so maybe some details were lost in translation from what Tigole said to what is written in that interview. *hope*

This is what he's quoted to have said there:


Ja, wir haben eine Entscheidung getroffen. Aber wundert Euch nicht, wenn wir uns doch noch mal anders entscheiden. Wisst Ihr, Spiel-Design ist eine sehr flexible Geschichte. Aktuell wird aber jede Rasse zum Todesritter, als auch Gnome. Wir haben einige Dinge seit der BlizzCon überdacht. So müssen Spielercharaktere wahrscheinlich nicht mehr Level 80 sein, um den Todesritter freizuspielen. Wahrscheinlich geht das schon früher. Wie ein Wahnsinniger auf Level 80 zu spielen, nur um möglichst schnell den Todesritter zu bekommen, macht keinen Spaß. Es bringt die Leute dazu, durch das Spiel zu hetzen. Wir werden daher wahrscheinlich etwas anderes machen. Wir haben viele Ideen dazu. Wir haben uns zwar noch nicht auf eine Sache festgelegt, aber vielleicht wird der Todesritter schon zwischen Level 55 und 60 freigeschaltet. Dann erstellt Ihr Euren Todesritter und dann durchlauft Ihr Eure Bewährungsprobe. Wenn Ihr die bewältigt habt, bekommt Ihr Euren Todesritter.

Which I translate as:


Yes, we made a decision.But don't be surprised if we decide to change our minds. You know, game design is a very flexible matter. Right now, every race can become a Death Knight, even Gnomes. We have thought through some things since BlizzCon. Players probably don't have to be level 80 to unlock the Death Knight, the class will be accessible earlier. Powerleveling to level 80 in order to get to play the Death Knight as soon as possible is no fun and makes people rush through the game. Most likely we'll find a different solution. We do have a lot of ideas regarding this topic. We haven't decided yet, but maybe the DK will be unlockable between level 55 and 60. You can then create your Death Knights, after creation you have to go through a trial. When you pass it you get your Death Knight.

Pretty vague stuff. :/ The trial sounds fun though!
