Allied Races - Lightforged Draenei
These Draenei have fought the Burning Legion across the cosmos for a millenia. Infused with the Light by a Naaru ritual, this faction of Draenei joined the Army of the Light (https://wow.gamepedia.com/Army_of_the_Light) and helped vanquish the Legion on Argus. With their main purpose fulfilled, the Lightforged Draenei have joined their brethren in the Alliance to counter Horde aggression.

Unlocking the Lightforged Draenei
To unlock the ability to make a Lightforged Draenei, the following achievements must be completed on one character on your account. Once done, you can make a Lightforged Draenei on any server that you have a 110 character provided you have purchased Battle for Azeroth.


You Are Now Prepared! (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/12066-you-are-now-prepared) - Complete the Argus campaign
Exalted with The Army of the Light (http://www.wowdb.com/factions/2165-army-of-the-light)

Racials
The Lightforged Draenei racial abilities give you an AoE blast, blacksmithing perks, increased experience when killing demons, a damage and heal upon death, and reduced holy damage taken.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_OrbitalStrike.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_OrbitalStrike.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_ForgeofLight.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_ForgeofLight.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_Demonbane.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_Demonbane.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_HolyResistance.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_HolyResistance.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_FinalVerdict.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_FinalVerdict.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Achievement_AlliedRace_LightforgedDraenei.jpg


Light's Judgement (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255647-lights-judgment) - Call down a strike of Holy energy dealing Holy damage to enemies within 5 yards after 3 sec. 40 yd range, 2.5 min cooldown.
Forge of Light (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255650-forge-of-light) - Summon a forge of light, enabling Blacksmithing. Blacksmithing skill increased by 10.
Demonbane (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255653-demonbane) - Experience gains from killing demons increased by 20%.
Holy Resistance (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255651-holy-resistance) - Reduce Holy damage taken by 1%.
Light's Reckoning (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255652-lights-reckoning) - When you die the Light avenges you, dealing Holy damage to enemies within 8 yards and healing allies.


Mounts
The Lightforged Draenei racial mount is the Lightforged Felcrusher (http://www.wowdb.com/items/155656-lightforged-felcrusher). It is usable by all Alliance races upon completion of Allied Races: Lightforged Draenei (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/12243-allied-races-lightforged-draenei).


A long-time companion of the Draenei, the elekk are stalwart and sturdy steeds capable of standing up to the impending darkness without fear.
