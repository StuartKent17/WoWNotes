Patch 6.2 - Garrison Campaign Rewards
Patch 6.2 expands upon the garrison campaign with a new quest line. Everyone with a Tier 3 garrison can and will want to complete (at least part) of this new line, as it unlocks and rewards several useful things. You will see Yrel, Gul'dan, Vol'jin, Varian and other notable characters.


Unlocks the Shipyard (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/185915-garrison-shipyard) and Naval missions
Unlocks your faction's hub in Tanann Jungle
Rewards some Garrison Resources (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/824-garrison-resources) and Oil (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/1101-oil)
Rewards some reputation with Hand of the Prophet (http://ptr.wowdb.com/factions/1847-hand-of-the-prophet) / Vol'jin's Headhunters (http://ptr.wowdb.com/factions/1848-voljins-headhunters)
Rewards several Baleful items: Baleful Treads (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/124552-baleful-treads), Baleful Tunic (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/124551-baleful-tunic), Baleful Gauntlets (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/124553-baleful-gauntlets), Baleful Girdle (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/124557-baleful-girdle)
Rewards an Empowered Apexis Fragment (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/128225-empowered-apexis-fragment), allowing you to create a 695 Baleful item
Rewards an epic follower: Oronok Torn-heart (http://ptr.wowdb.com/garrison/followers/468-oronok-torn-heart) (Demonic Knowledge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/garrison/abilities/303-demonic-knowledge))
Rewards the Shard of Cyrukh (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/128309-shard-of-cyrukh). Be sure to loot it from the Forgotten Shard of the Cipher (http://ptr.wowdb.com/objects/241671-forgotten-shard-of-the-cipher) after finishing The Cipher of Damnation (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/39394-the-cipher-of-damnation).
