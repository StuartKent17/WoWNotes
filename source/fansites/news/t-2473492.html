Linear was what Saphyron calculated, where +1 ilvl always adds the same amount of stat, so the total ilvl increases at the same pace and so 210 ilvl is 5 % more than 200 ilvl.

Exponential means that the higher you total ilvl is, the bigger the amount of stat +1 ilvl adds. So if 200 to 210 means +10 % budget, and 400 to 410 also means +10 % budget, despite the first being an increase of 5 % in ilvl and the second being 2,5 %, it means that each +1 ilvl between 400 and 410 added much more stat than each +1 ilvl between 200 and 210. So, exponential.

So yes, what you describe is exponential. AND it's exactly what I calculated in my list, which is why you get +110% stat between 530 and 640, while a linear increase would lead to only gaining less than 20%.
