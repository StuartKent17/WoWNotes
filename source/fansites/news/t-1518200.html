Achievement Changes
Pandaria
Exploration

Spell Changes
Mounts

Grinning Reaver (New) (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/163025/#18224-18273) Summons and dismisses a rideable Grinning Reaver mount. The capabilities of this mount depend on your Riding skill and location. Account wide. 1.5 sec cast.
Warforged Nightmare (New) (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/163024/#18224-18273) Summons and dismisses a rideable Warforged Nightmare mount. The capabilities of this mount depend on your Riding skill and location. Account wide. 1.5 sec cast.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/273-Paladin), Talent Calculator (http://ptr.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#I))
Retribution

Sword of Light (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/53503/#18224-18273) Increases the damage you deal with two-handed melee weapons by 30%. Your spell power is now equal to 50% of your attack power, and you no longer benefit from other sources of spell power. Grants 6% of your maximum mana every 2 sec. Increases the healing done by Word of Glory by 30% 60% and Flash of Light by 100%. Paladin - Retribution Spec.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/275-Rogue), Talent Calculator (http://ptr.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#Q))
Talents

Burst of Speed (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/108212/#18224-18273) Increases movement speed by 70% for 4 1% for 0.1 sec and removes any movement-slowing effects. Does not break stealth. Rogue - LvL 60 Talent. 15 Energy. Instant.




Patch 5.4.7 Hotfixes - May 13
Classes

Resolved an issue where players were able to incorrectly stack multiple movement speed buffs.
Paladin

Retribution

Sword of Light now increases healing done by Word of Glory by 60% (up from 30%).


Warlock

Armor Set

Resolved an issue where Warlocks were able to incorrectly retain their Tier-14 4-piece set bonus when the armor is no longer equipped.

Claims of "lazy" are completely unfounded and unfair. This new ravager mount is not a literal skeletal copy-paste with a new model. Even if it was, that would have been a big job.
