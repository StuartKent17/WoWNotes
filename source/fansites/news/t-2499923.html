Patch 8.2 Hotfixes - July 29th, 2019
Characters

Fixed a bug where a character would lose its Trashmaster title after changing factions.


Island Expeditions

Resolved an issue that could prevent players from mounting in Skittering Hollow.


Items

Fixed a bug preventing the Reins of the Azure Drake (https://www.wowdb.com/items/43952-reins-of-the-azure-drake) and Reins of the Blue Drake (https://www.wowdb.com/items/43953-reins-of-the-blue-drake) from dropping on the 25 Player Legacy Raid Size version of Eye of Eternity.
Heart of Azeroth

Increased the secondary stat amount granted from Arcane Heart (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/303006-arcane-heart) (Azerite Trait) by 15%.
Fixed an issue that caused the healing from Arcane Heart (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/303006-arcane-heart) (Azerite Trait) to be lost as overhealing for players at or near full health.



Quests

Azsh’ari Oracles now drop Tomes of Binding for “Forbidden Rituals (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/55846-forbidden-rituals)”.
Collecting Flopping Fish now gives credit toward “GGive Me the Biggest Bag You've Got (https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/13692-give-me-the-biggest-bag-youve-got)”.


World Quests

Nazjatar

Significantly increased the health of Battlemaidens and Champions in the “Azshara’s Elite Commanders” event.





Dark Legacy Comics #689
DLC #689 (http://www.darklegacycomics.com/689) has been released!
