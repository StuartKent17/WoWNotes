Live Developer Q&A with Ion Hazzikostas August 3
Join us live on Twitch (http://twitch.tv/Warcraft) Thursday, August 3, as we sit down with Game Director Ion Hazzikostas for our next live developer Q&A. He’ll be answering many of your questions about the ongoing development of World of Warcraft!

Feel free to submit questions by posting in this forum thread (https://eu.battle.net/forums/en/wow/topic/17616681493#1) or via Twitter using the hashtag #LegionQA.

We’re really looking forward to getting the chance to dive in and answer as much as we can, so we hope to see you Thursday at 11:00 a.m. PDT!


World Boss - The Soultakers

Weekly Event - Wrath of the Lich King Timewalking
Wrath of the Lich King Timewalking (http://www.wowdb.com/world-events/562-timewalking-dungeon-event) is back this week.


The weekly quest (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/44166-a-frozen-path-through-time) from Archmage Timear (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/111246-archmage-timear) rewards a Accolade of Victory (http://www.wowdb.com/items/140176-accolade-of-victory), Seal of Broken Fate (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1273-seal-of-broken-fate), and Cache of Fel Treasures (http://www.wowdb.com/items/147519-cache-of-fel-treasures).
You can find Auzin (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/98690-auzin) in old Dalaran.
Don't forget to keep working towards Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (http://www.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk), Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (http://www.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger), and Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade)!
Reins of the Infinite Timereaver (http://www.wowdb.com/items/133543-reins-of-the-infinite-timereaver) can also drop from any Timewalking dungeon boss.
