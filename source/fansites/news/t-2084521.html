So anyone in the know - what addons are these UI changes going to break?

First off, EnemyGrid (and anything related) will be gutted.

Second, unit frames that showed the direction of out of range players (mostly for healers) will no longer be able to do that. In addition any boss mod that relied on knowing where other players were in relation to you will be broken, as will any boss mod that relied on knowing how close you were to other players will be seriously limited.

For a concrete example, any addon that helped with wrought chaos or shackled torment on Mythic Archimonde will be broken, as will basically any addon that popped up a "GPS nav style" arrow in combat for any reason, or which displayed a "radar" type display to help players spread out (eg, to avoid shadowfel burst).

It's a pretty massive change, honestly, and I'm a little skeptical about it.
