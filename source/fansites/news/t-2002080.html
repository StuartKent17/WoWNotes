Warcraft Movie Box Office Numbers
Warcraft has been released in many markets outside of the US and is doing well!


Deadline (http://deadline.com/2016/05/warcraft-opening-weekend-international-box-office-1201763736/) is reporting that in 19 of the 20 markets it opened in, it was the #1 movie.

In Germany, the movie had the biggest opening of the year so far, with 45% market share.
In Russia, the movie had the second biggest opening, with 65% market share.

Variety (http://variety.com/2016/film/news/box-office-warcraft-opens-international-1201784457/) is reporting that the Russian opening was only behind Deadpool this year, with the seventh biggest opening day of all time in Russia.
In China (https://twitter.com/ChinaBoxOffice/status/735880884621049857), ticket pre-sales for the release night are already the sixth highest ranking movie of all time.
Unfortunately the news isn't as great in the US, with an opening projection of only $25 million (http://variety.com/2016/film/news/box-office-warcraft-conjuring-now-you-see-me-1201783331/).

I would also like to point out that a Demon Hunter can't select a new talent while leveling without going back to town. Its like old school class trainers!!!
This is a bug. You should always be able to select a talent if you have none currently chosen for a given row.

I've seen the movie. It was okay, well, more then okay, but I wouldn't rate it 8.2 / 10 like IMDB does at the moment. If it matters I've played Warcraft since the original game up to WoW and read alot of novels. But I will say that I usually have a hard time enjoying action movies overall, they rarely turn out great. Matrix, Inception and Dark Knight being notable exceptions. But I will say this, the trailers don't do the movie justice. After seeing them and knowing Legendary Pictures were behind it I thought it would turn out to be another Suckerpunch / 300 with an abundance of CGI, action and no substance / bad acting and dialogue, but I was wrong.

It isn't as good as the action/fantasy movies in IMDB top 100, but it is probably one of, if not the, best video game adaption I've seen and for a Warcraft-fan it's a must see. I've seen people who never touched the series before enjoy it too. I am quite excited for potential sequels, there is soooo much good stuff in the universe that would make great movies, but therein lies the problem. Too much!
