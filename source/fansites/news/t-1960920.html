Legion - Tier 3 BMAH Gear Transmog Restrictions
The developers answered one of the questions about transmogging BMAH gear again (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/5500-Eye-of-Azshara-Dalaran-Updates-Wardrobe-System-Class-Updates-Prot-Paladin-Preview#bmah) today.
Tier 3 pieces obtained via the BMAH will have their appearances unlocked account-wide within current transmogrification restrictions. The Frostfire Regalia (www.wowdb.com/item-sets/526-frostfire-regalia) set can be transmogged on a Warlock, for example. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/706976459215142912))


Blue Posts
Secondary Stat Scaling in Legion
The thing I realized then is, that a single-stat item has as much stats as an evenly distributed item on the same ilvl, and even more than an unevenly distributed item.
This was originally done to encourage players to diversify secondary stats -- the closer an item was to a single-stat secondary, the more it suffered from this penalty. We reevaluated the method we were doing this by in 6.2, finding that most people were just confused by it and it didn't affect balance in any significant way, so we changed it. Your secondary stat ratings should add up to a consistent value no matter what the combination is from here on out. (Blue Tracker (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/403410-secondary-stat-scaling-in-legion/) / Official Forums (http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/20742704730?page=4#62))

Ability Damage in PvP
We've discovered a bug where weapon damage is being calculated incorrectly in our PvP stat templates. A lot of damage is overtuned as a result. We're working on fix it as soon as possible. (Blue Tracker (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/403911-overtuned-abilities/) / Official Forums (http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/20742714777?page=2#21))
