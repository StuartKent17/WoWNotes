Ashran Bug
Letting everyone know that we're aware of a potential issue where players already in an active Ashran battle may receive a second pop-up prompt asking if you'd want to join the Ashran battle. This second prompt is a bug and is being looked into. (Blue Tracker (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/387834-is-ashran-broken-today-9-22/) / Official Forums (http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/19072677232?page=4#75))

Renewing Subscriptions in Greece
We wanted to let you know that we’re aware of the payment issues our players in Greece are currently facing. Certain banks are not processing international digital transactions for certain types of products, leaving many of you unable to pay for Blizzard games and services with credit cards or PayPal.

We’re carefully monitoring this situation, and we’d like to remind you of other payment options that are available to you. For World of Warcraft game time:
Pre-paid game cards should be available at local retailers like Plaisio, Public, Media Markt, Seven Spots, e-shop.gr, etc. Those with in-game gold to spare can purchase 30 days of game time on the Auction House with the WoW Token

We’re also working with external partners and local retailers to bring Battle.net Gift Cards to Greece soon. These cards will provide you with an additional payment option by allowing you to charge up your Battle.net Balance in order to purchase items, including game time, in the Battle.net Shop. You can find more information here (https://eu.battle.net/support/en/article/battle-net-balance).(Blue Tracker (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/387759-problem-with-renewing-subscriptions-in-greece/) / Official Forums (http://eu.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/15162509340?page=7#134))
