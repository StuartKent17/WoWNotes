That 4k honor for the daily would be a good thing for several reasons.

First, it makes arena more competitive by allowing a reasonably achievable base level of gear that everyone can acquire. The time requirement will still be comparable to that required playing arena to get this gear back in season 1 when it was some of the best gear in the game.

Second, it makes some decent DPS gear available to a lot of players. This will allow a lot more guilds to progress beyond Kara without needing to farm it endlessly and allows for new members of existing guilds to gear themselves up a lot without needing the entire guild to keep farming Kara forever.

Third, it gives casual players a chance to get some decent gear in an hour or two play a day.

Fourth, it will get everyone doing a couple of BGs a day which can only be a good thing.

Fifth, if you just do the daily quests you will have a bottleneck of getting the marks of honor now for your gear, at an average of about 30 marks per item you will still have to do about 15 bgs for each item, so probably looking at more like 3 bgs a day if you want your marks to keep up with your honor.

And for anyone complaining remember that this is the entry level PvP gear now, and even if you just do the daily quest it will still take you about 50 days to get fully geared up.
