Patch 6.2 Hotfixes - June 30

Classes
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid

Fixed a bug that was preventing Balance and Restoration Druids from gaining attack power from Intellect in Bear Form or Cat Form.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter

Survival 4-piece set (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/188199-item-hunter-t18-survival-4p-bonus) should trigger Shadow Nova on all targets affected by Black Arrow, not just the primary target.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue

Killing Spree (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/51690-killing-spree) no longer provides immunity to Crowd Control abilities, regardless of Battle Fatigue.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior

[In testing] Arms Tier 18 2-piece (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/185800-item-warrior-t18-arms-2p-bonus) set bonus chance reduced to 50% (was 60%). Now triggers when Rend ticks are fully absorbed.



Dungeons and Raids

Completing a Timewalking dungeon now awards you 36g 57s 50c.
Darkmoon Faire Adventure Guide items again drop at the expected rate.


Skyreach

The Cache of Arakkoan Treasures on Mythic difficulty now awards consolation loot if no item is won.


Hellfire Citadel
Socrethar the Eternal

Fel Dominators now spawn more frequently in all non-Mythic difficulties.
Soul of Socrethar's health has been reduced 15% in all non-Mythic difficulties.
Fel Dominators should now instantly shield Socrethar when spawning.

Archimonde

Nether Storm cannot crit or multi-strike.
Archimonde’s Nether Banish no longer counts dead players as valid targets.



Garrison

Shipyard maximum number of ships has been increased by 1 for Tiers 1 and 2, allowing for 6/8/10 active ships at Tiers 1/2/3.



Items

Mindscythe of the Legion (http://www.wowdb.com/items/124369-mindscythe-of-the-legion) and Element-Infused Knuckles (http://www.wowdb.com/items/127329-element-infused-knuckles) now have weapon DPS that is appropriate for caster weapons.
[In testing] Reaper's Harvest (http://www.wowdb.com/items/124513-reapers-harvest) chance to trigger reduced by 60% for Unholy Death Knights.
[In testing] Discordant Chorus (http://www.wowdb.com/items/124237-discordant-chorus) damage done by Fel Cleave reduced by 50%.



Creatures and NPCs

Players should no longer receive zone-wide yells from enemies who are very far away or aren’t available.
Players who are hated by the Steamwheedle Cartel factions are no longer attacked by Goblin Sailors in the Shipyard.
It is no longer possible for players to bring Helix Gearbreaker outside of Deadmines.
Deathtalon (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/95053-deathtalon) will no longer evade when pulled on his pillar.



Bug Fixes

Alliance players who created a Shipyard first are now able to complete the quests to unlock their Fishing Shack.
Interacting with the Hero's Call Board or the Warchief's Command Board once again brings up the command board interface rather than the Adventure Guide.




Warlords PvP Season 2 Begins
With boots and boards and helms and swords, the time has come to strike once more! Warlords of Draenor Arena and Battleground Season 2 has begun, and Horde and Alliance leaders are calling all of their champions to seek victory on the glorious field of battle.

Before you set foot in an Arena or on a Battleground, keep in mind the following adjustments that have been made for this season:

The PvP iLevel of Honor and Conquest gear has increased.

All gear now scales up to iLvl 690.
Wild Aspirant gear is iLvl 700.
Wild Combatant gear is iLvl 715.
Wild Gladiator gear is iLvl 730.

Any PvE gear over iLvl 715 will be scaled down to iLvl 715.
Winning Rated Battlegrounds and completing the weekly Ashran Dominance quest now awards you a random piece of PvP iLvl 730 Conquest gear.


Good luck, and may your enemies fall before you this season!
