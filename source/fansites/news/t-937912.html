What is Real ID Party and how do I invite my friends to groups?

Real ID Party is a brand new addition with Patch 4.2. With Real ID Party, you can invite any Real ID Friends to a party, and queue for a group using the Dungeon Finder feature. This means you can group with others regardless of their realm. Please note, they must be within your region (US, EU, Etc.) in order to be invited. If they can create a character on your realm, they are eligible for your group!

Please note: Real ID Party is only usable with Real ID friends. More information regarding Real ID can be found on the Battle.net Website: Battle dot net/realid/.

In order to invite someone from another realm using Real ID Party, simply open your friends list. At the top of the list are your Real ID friends. Next to their name will be a simple "Plus" sign that you can click, and this will invite them. If they are eligible to queue with you, you can then sign up for a Dungeon Finder group.

Any further information on Real ID Party may be found on the World of Warcraft Community site: battle dot net/wow
