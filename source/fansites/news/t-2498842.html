Weekly Bonus Event - Arena Skirmishes
Sharpen your swords and reinforce your boards, because you’re called to the PvP Arena for the hectic mayhem of Skirmishes.

This Week: Arena Skirmishes
All week, from anywhere in Azeroth, open the Group Finder (default hotkey: i), select the Player vs. Player tab on the bottom of the window, and then under Casual, choose Arena Skirmish and JOIN BATTLE. You’ll be matched up with teammates and provided with opponents to kill in the Arena. You can also queue for a Premade group or create your own group within the Premade tab.

Look for the following all week long:


Chronicler Shoopa (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/144384-chronicler-shoopa) in Zuldazar and in Boralus has a quest for you–The Arena Calls. Or, if you forget to pick it up there, you can start it from within the Adventure Journal.

Quest requirement: Win 10 Arena PvP Skirmishes
Reward: A PvP loot box

Passive buff: +50% honor gains from Skirmishes


If you’ve been looking to make some Honor gains, this is the week to do it!


Brawl - Comp Stomp
In addition to the visual updates, a new brawl is coming that pits players against the smart AI from Island Expeditions in Arathi Basin!
Head into Arathi Basin for a head-to-head battle against AI opponents. This new Brawl will be available March 20. Use the Group Finder tool (hotkey “I”) in the Player vs. Player tab to take part.


Island Expeditions for Week of July 23rd, 2019
The islands expeditions for the week are


Molten Cay with Jungle Trolls
Skittering Hollow with Troggs
Snowblossom Village with Mantid



Call to Arms: Stormsong Valley and Mechagon
This week's Call to Arms is Call to Arms: Stormsong Valley (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52782-call-to-arms-stormsong-valley) and Call to Arms: Mechagon (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/56649-call-to-arms-mechagon). Head to Stormsong Valley and Mechagon with warmode enabled to kill 10 players (25 in Mechagon) of the opposing faction and loot an air supply. This will reward you with conquest, honor and reputation for either 7th Legion (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2159-7th-legion) or The Honorbound (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2157-the-honorbound).
