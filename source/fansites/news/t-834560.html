OK, so I think there's a big problem here with how people are reading these changes. Numeric changes are based off the LAST PATCH NOTES FROM THE PTR. Not from Live. So if say, first PTR patch notes buffed Mind Sear to +100% damage, then a little while later there was another PTR patch that buffed Mind Sear another +100%. The total buff to Mind Sear at that point is actually 200%. Then today there's a patch note saying "Mind Sear nerfed by 33%"; that nerf is based off of the 200% existing improvement. In this hypothetical situation the Mind Sear Spell would still be doing 100% more damage than it does on Live. So lots of the buffs and nerfs you see right now are second or third adjustments. Unless you've been paying attention to the whole chain of Buffs/Nerfs you probably don't know how the current changes relate to numbers on Live. A spell getting nerfed right now might still be a lot better than it is on live, likewise a spell being buffed right now might be less powerful than it is on live even after the buff.

TL;DR: Save your rage for the final Live patch notes. Those will show actual buffs and nerfs relative to current Live content.
