2.3.2 Known issues
There are tons of known issues with 2.3.2, I'm posting them to make sure that everyone notice them and realize the amount of bugs introduced with the patch, even if most of them are minor we can probably expect a 2.3.3 patch to fix everything before the 2.4.

Alliance flight path between Booty Bay and Stormwind goes through a tree.

There is a leanto in Agama'gor, The Barrens that has no tooltip appearing but will be highlighted when moused over it.

Sigh. They still haven't noted on any patch notes or bug lists that the Druid Vengeful Gladiator Dragonhide Gloves (feral season 3) are bugged. It's driving me crazy. They give us this new gloves bonus - finally - but it doesn't work.

If you're curious:
The bonus silences a casting enemy for 2 seconds if we Maim him/her.

The bug:
Maim disorients a player. Since Maim hits first, any spell casting is already interrupted. So the silence bonus checks, sees the target isn't casting a spell, and doesn't proc. The only time the gloves bonus works is if the target resists or is immune to Maim's disorient effect.
