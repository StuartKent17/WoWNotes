Patch 6.2.3 Hotfixes - March 1
Bug Fixes
Rogue

Talents

Fixed an issue where Death from Above (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/152150-death-from-above)'s immunity to crowd control effect may persist incorrectly after dealing damage with Eviscerate or Envenom.

UI

Fixed an issue where changes made to the action bar may not be saved correctly when entering an instance such as a Dungeon, Raid, Battleground, or Arena.
