A few additional changes from the 3.0.8 PTRs

Reputation
You can now get full reputation gains from grey monsters, for exemple you will get 20 Reputation points for killing normal monsters while grinding your Timbermaw Hold (http://www.wowwiki.com/Timbermaw_Hold_reputation_guide) reputation.

Item changes
I think I newsed a few of them in previous news, but most of them should be new.
All the items sold by the Argent Quartermaster (http://www.wowhead.com/?npc=16786) during the latest Scourge Invasion aren't disenchantable anymore.
Reins of the Traveler's Tundra Mammoth (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=44235) can no longer be sold back to the NPCs for 5000 gold.
Titansteel Helms now all have a blue and a meta gem socket. Some of them also got their stats changed/reduced. (Spiked Titansteel Helm (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/spikedtitansteelhelm.jpg), Tempered Titansteel Helm (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/temperedtitansteelhelm.jpg), Brilliant Titansteel Helm (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/brillianttitansteelhelm.jpg)). As a reminder the spell power on Titansteel Guardian (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=41384) has been reduced from 490 to 457.
Darkmoon Card: Death (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=42990) now gives critical strike rating instead of haste.
Savage (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/newdkpvpbonus.jpg) / Hateful (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/newdkpvpbonus.jpg) / Deadly Gladiator's Dreadplate Gauntlets (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/newdkpvpbonus.jpg) had their bonus to Strangulate cooldown removed. They now gives "Whenever you cast Chains of Ice, the Frost Rune used becomes a Death Rune when it activates".
Je'Tze's Bell (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=37835) is now Bind When Equipped.
All the Off-hand items crafted through Inscription (http://www.wowhead.com/?items&filter=sl=23;cr=86;crs=15;crv=0#0-2+1) had their Inscription level requirement removed. (Screenshot: Faces of Doom (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/facesofdoom.jpg), Iron-bound Tome (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/ironboundtome.jpg))
Amanitar Skullbow (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=43284) had its stats changed/fixed. (Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/amanitarskullbow.jpg))
Handwraps of Preserved History (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=44397) had its stats considerably reduced. (Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/handwrapsofpreservedhistory.jpg))
Battlemap Hide Helm (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=37180) had its stats considerably reduced. (Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/battlemaphidehelm.jpg))
MOLL-E (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40768) is now a rare item, lasts 10 minutes (up from 5), with a 8 hour cooldown (Down from 1 day). (Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/moll-e.jpg))
Dragon's Eye (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=42225) and epic gems like Bold Dragon's Eye (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=42142) now only requires 350 Jewelcrafting. Down from 375. (Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/dragonseyes.jpg))
Wintergrasp Mark of Honor (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=43589) is now stackable up to 1000.
Ebonweave Robe (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=42101), Moonshroud Robe (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=42100) and Spellweave Robe (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=42102) can now be sold to NPCs and/or disenchanted.
Fire Seed (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=39969) and Fire Leaf (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=39970) both had their bonus removed. Fire Seed is now a gray item.

Tanking as frost
We adjusted Frost Presence to make up for the nerfs to Icebound Fortitude, Bone Shield and the change to bonus armor from jewelry etc. (Most raiding DK tanks were Unholy at the time, though they have begun to branch out a little.)

Both of those abilities are intended to be more like Shield Block or Last Stand, not something that is up all the time like Frost Presence itself. In most of the estimates I have seen that figure this is a huge nerf use pretty unrealistic estimates of how much Bone Shield is up (thinking their avoidance can make up for the charges). But we can check out numbers again. (Source (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/26/13832573744-gcs-take-on-bone-shield.html))

Comcast Players: Please Read
This issue is resolved according to Blizzard, but a lot of players are still experiencing problems. If you're one of them you might want to check the original post.
Update: We have received confirmation that the issues affecting Comcast player's connectivity to World of Warcraft are now resolved. If you are still having trouble playing World of Warcraft and are Comcast user, please visit our technical support forums and provide the information instructed in the appropriate Comcast sticky thread so that we can work to resolve any issues that may be outstanding.

We have been in communication with Comcast and several other internet backbone and service providers in efforts to isolate and bring to resolution the connectivity issues that have impacted the ability for some Comcast players to play World of Warcraft. As of this morning a 3rd party network who resides between Blizzard's network and Comcast's network informed us that they were experiencing an issue which may be the cause of problems that Comcast players have been experiencing.

We remain in contact with this provider and at this time do not have an ETA but this issue is being looked into by all parties involved. We will continue provide updates as they become available.

Your interpretation is off. The problem with pets is they scale too well -- raid-buffed, pets do amazing damage (while without buffs they can't hold aggro over their hunter). An example of how broken pet scaling is: Blessing of Might on a pet provides a bigger raid DPS increase than BoM on a Rogue.

The theory put forth by players is that if pets scaled more appropriately (in line with their hunter, for instance), they could be both useful solo/5manning and not overpowered in a full-raid-buff situation.
