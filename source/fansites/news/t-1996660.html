Warcraft Movie Promo Details
We finally have the official announcement of the movie promotion! Please see the full post (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/412161-the-warcraft-movie-arrives-in-theaters-soon/) for dates and other details.


Beginning on May 25 through August 1, players who log in to World of Warcraft will receive a set of movie-inspired transmogrification items.

Fight for the Alliance (http://beta.wowdb.com/achievements/11210-fight-for-the-alliance) - Replica Lion's Fang (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/140999-replica-lions-fang) + Replica Lion's Heart (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/141000-replica-lions-heart)
Fight for the Horde (http://beta.wowdb.com/achievements/11211-fight-for-the-horde) - Replica Blood Guard's Cleaver (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/141001-replica-blood-guards-cleaver) + Replica Staff of Gul'dan (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/141002-replica-staff-of-guldan)

Anyone who purchases a ticket at select theaters will receive a free digital copy of World of Warcraft while supplies last.
