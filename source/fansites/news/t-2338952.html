WoW's 13th Anniversary Event
WoW's 13th Anniversary (http://www.wowdb.com/world-events/693-wows-13th-anniversary) is live in the EU!


It appears the Dragons of Nightmare (https://wow.gamepedia.com/Dragons_of_Nightmare_(original)), Azuregos (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/121820-azuregos), and Lord Kazzak (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/121818-lord-kazzak) have returned with item level 900 loot that can Titanforge!
You can bonus roll for another attempt at loot.
The Originals (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/47253-the-originals) is a daily quest that requires level 60, so you may be able to try for this loot every day. Currently the bosses appear to respawn every 5 minutes.
A Time to Reflect (H) (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/43461-a-time-to-reflect) / A Time to Reflect (A) (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/43323-a-time-to-reflect) are up again this year!
Historian Ju'pa (H) (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/110035-historian-jupa) and Historian Llore (A) (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/110034-historian-llore) have some things for sale, including the new Bronze-Tinted Sunglasses (http://www.wowdb.com/items/147885-bronze-tinted-sunglasses)
