Patch 7.3 Hotfixes - September 19
Classes

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/273-Paladin) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/paladin) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#I) / Artifact Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/artifact-calculator#JAAAAAAAA) / PvP Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/pvp-talent-calculator#I))

The Netherlight Crucible trait Infusion of Light will no longer proc from Greater Blessing of Wisdom healing.



Non-Player Characters

Fiona's Caravan should no longer be mysteriously missing on some servers.
Grand Lector Enaara should now properly appear during the world quest "Recurring Madness" on Mac'Aree for all players.


Player versus Player

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/270-Druid) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/druid) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#s) / Artifact Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/artifact-calculator#tAAAAAAAA) / PvP Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/pvp-talent-calculator#s))

Feral

Enraged Maim (Honor Talent) will now be properly removed from targets when they take damage from abilities and spells.




Professions

Herbalism

Fel Lasher drop rate increased. It can now also drop from Astral Glory on Argus.



Quests

The Tomes of Torment are no longer difficult to see during the quest "Lessons of Fire and Pain".
Abandoning "Talgath's Forces" or "Flanking Maneuvers" after completing one quest should no longer prevent you from re-acquiring the other.

Mists of Pandaria Timewalking
Mists of Pandaria timewalking is back! It offers rewards such as Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (http://www.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade)


Visit Mistweaver Xia (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/118828-mistweaver-xia) to purchase timewalking rewards while the event is live
Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (http://www.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade) costs 5000 x Timewarped Badge (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)
Paradox Spirit (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/118063-paradox-spirit) and Infinite Hatchling (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/118060-infinite-hatchling) cost 2200 x Timewarped Badge (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)
Reputation tokens (http://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-patch=23038&filter-search=commendation) (50 x Timewarped Badge (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)) for the Mists of Pandaria factions are also for sale, including friendship items for the Valley of the Four Winds.
Portable Yak Wash (http://www.wowdb.com/items/144393-portable-yak-wash) costs 500 x Timewarped Badge (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)
Adopted Puppy Crate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/144072-adopted-puppy-crate) (1000 x Timewarped Badge (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)) and Chilled Satchel of Vegetables (http://www.wowdb.com/items/143948-chilled-satchel-of-vegetables) (25 x Timewarped Badge (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)) are also for sale
