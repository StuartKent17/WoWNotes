Patch 8.0.1 Hotfixes - July 25
Classes

https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warlock) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#k))

Summoned Demons should no longer fail to be summoned when the Warlock is on steep terrain or near obstacles such as rocks or trees.



Creatures and NPCs

Illusionary Mystics in the Proving Grounds again heal for a correct amount of health.
Enemies who use Ticking Bomb no longer inflict higher damage than intended.
Kosumoth the Hungering should no longer be as hungry.


Darkshore

The NPCs in the Grove of the Ancients will no longer offer their low-level quests while players are progressing through "The Warchief's Agenda”.
Oil Pumps are now interactable by multiple players at once.
Frenzied Wisps will now correctly grant group credit.


Dungeons and Raids

Antorus, the Burning Throne

Creatures throughout Antorus now deal 10% less damage.
Felhounds of Sargeras

Smouldering damage reduced by 65%.

Imonar the Soul Hunter

Several of Imonar’s abilities now deal 25% less damage.




Items

Auto-Self-Cauterizer now correctly removes Bleed effects.


Player versus Player

Toggling War Mode in Orgrimmar or Stormwind should now immediately flag the player for PvP and enable PvP Talents.
Unrated Battlegrounds now award item level 185 gear (was 176).
Fixed an issue that caused the Glorious Pennant (https://www.wowdb.com/items/164310-glorious-pennant) Honor reward to use the same visual appearance as the Esteemed Pennant.




TradeSkillMaster 4 Release Date
The TradeSkillMaster 4 (https://blog.tradeskillmaster.com/patch-8-0-updates-and-tradeskillmaster-4-release-date/) add-on update will release on July 28th 2018!

Why remove cutting edge if the raid is not nerfed!

because the raid got nerfed 275 times already before prepatch. if you didnt get CE by then, they cant help you more than that.

CE got taken away like mage tower challenge because they were made and balanced for classes with sets of abilities that are no longer here, with rotations that dont exist anymore. you can go to Antorus, but most Mythic fights behave differently in terms of source of damage.

Also the fact that the damage are balanced enough not to os everyone, and that mob health is balanced enough you can kill boss doesnt mean the fight is the same.
exemples : mines explosion (not dot) can actually oneshot people on High Command, you cant go 4-5 simultaneous adds on Aggramar anymore, Rent souls on Argus are a joke, allowing tanks to fast clear them now without being monks.

the point of the dmg reductions in antorus over the past few days is not to "nerf" the raid or "allow more player to clear 11/11M", it's to make those encounters possible without very specific comp. Varimathras was hitting tanks for 260 k shadow damage. the dot from dogs could hit for up to 65k every 2seconds, the scythe from argus was oneshotting 260 geared tanks on 3 stacks. without very specific gear, and very specific externals, some encoutners were just not doable anymore. hence why it's called "Hotfix", and not "Nerf".
