Patch 5.1 PTR Notes Update - November 2
Only changed and new notes are below, see the full notes on the Blue Tracker (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/239910-patch-51-public-test-realm-now-live/).
General

Brawler's Guild

Entry into the brawler’s guild is by invitation only. Invitations can be found on the black market auction house or by invitation from somebody within the guild.


Lesser Charm of Good Fortune (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/738-lesser-charm-of-good-fortune) no longer takes up bag space, and is now recorded in the currency tab of the Character Sheet.


Classes

The durations of all Disarm (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/676) effects have been reduced to 8 seconds.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ideathknights.gif Death Knight (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/269-Death-Knight) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/death-knight) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#Y))

Damage received from players (or player-controlled sources) no longer counts towards increasing the healing done by Death Strike (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/49998).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/270-Druid) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/druid) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#s))

A new passive ability has been added: Tooth and Claw. This ability causes autoattacks to have a chance to empower the Druid's next Maul (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/6807). Empowered Maul (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/6807) reduce the damage dealt by the target's next autoattack. Guardian Druids obtain this ability at level 32.
Omen of Clarity (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/113043) now has a different visual effect for Feral Druids.
Glyphs

A new Minor Glyph has been added: Glyph of Focus. This glyph reduces the radius of Starfall (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/50288) by 50%.



http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/271-Hunter) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/hunter) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#M))

Careful Aim (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/34483) now increases the critical strike chance of Steady Shot (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/56641) and Aimed Shot (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/19434) by 75% on targets who are above 80% health, down from 90%.
The duration of Steady Focus (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/53224) has been increased to 20 seconds, up from 10 seconds.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/images.gif Mage (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/272-Mage) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/mage) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#g))

The damage buff from Arcane Charge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/114664) has been increased from 22% to 24%.
Rune of Power (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/116011) duration now shows in the UI, and no longer has a cooldown (was 6 seconds).
Ring of Frost (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/113724) now has a 45 second cooldown, up from 30 seconds, and can no longer affect more than 10 targets.
Pyroblast (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/11366) now has a 3 second cooldown. The cooldown on Pyroblast (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/11366) is affected by haste.
The mana cost of Spellsteal (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/30449) has been increased to 21% of base mana for Fire and Frost mages, up from 7%.
Glyphs

A new Minor Glyph has been added: Discreet Magic. This glyph prevents Nether Tempest (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/114954), Living Bomb (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/44457), Frost Bomb (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/113092), Arcane Barrage (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/44425), and Inferno Blast (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/108853) from affecting targets more than 5 yards away from the primary target.



http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/imonks.gif Monk (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/341-Monk) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/monk) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#o))

All healing spells which cost mana have had their mana cost increased by 10%.
The energy costs of Legacy of the Emperor (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/115921), and Legacy of the White Tiger (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/116781) have been reduced to 20, down from 50.
The energy cost of Healing Sphere (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/115464) has been reduced to 40, down from 60.
The Tiger Power provided by Tiger Palm (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/100787) now reduces target armor by 30% with a single application and no longer stacks.
Stance of the Fierce Tiger (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/103985) now increases the Monk's movement speed by 10% in addition to its other effects. This movement speed increase stacks with other effects.
The healing provided by the Healing Sphere (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/115464) base spell has been increased by 20%.
The chance to generate 1 Chi while channeling Soothing Mist (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/115175) and Crackling Jade Lightning (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/117952) has increased to 35%, up from 25%.
The Power Strikes (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/121817) talent now grants the Power Strikes (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/121817) effect every 22 20 seconds, which causes the Monk's next attack to generate 1 additional Chi.
Mistweaver

Enveloping Mist (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/124682) is now available at level 16, down from level 34.
The healing provided by Chi Burst (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/130651) and Chi Wave (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/132467) has been reduced by 25%.
You now have a chance equal to your crit chance to generate double the amount of Mana Tea (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/115294) Charge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/100).
Renewing Mist (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/119611) now travels to 2 additional targets (was 3 targets).
The mana cost of Soothing Mist (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/115175) has been reduced by 33%.
Haste for Mistweavers is now 50% more effective through Stance of the Wise Serpent (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/115070).
The healing provided when detonating Zen Sphere (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/124081) has been reduced by 25%.



http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/273-Paladin) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/paladin) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#I))

Holy Paladin: The 4 piece bonus now reduces Holy Shock (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/25914) cooldown by 2 seconds, up from 1 second.
Retribution Paladin: The 4 piece bonus now reduces Avenging Wrath (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/31884)’s cooldown by 65 seconds, down from 85 seconds.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/274-Priest) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/priest) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#U))

Atonement (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/81749) healing range has been increased to 40 yards, up from 15.
Chakras now persist through death and zoning.
Discipline Priests can once again learn Focused Will. This ability is obtained at level 30.
Chakra: Chastise (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/81209) now increases the damage dealt by Shadow and Holy spells by 50% (was 15%), and reduces mana costs by 90% (was 75%) while the ability is in effect.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/275-Rogue) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/rogue) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#Q))

The cooldown of Sprint (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/2983) has been reduced to 45 seconds, down from 1 minute.
The cooldown of Blind (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/2094) has been reduced to 90 seconds, down from 3 minutes.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warlock) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#k))

Kil'jaeden's Cunning (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/119049) snare has been increased from 10% per stack to 15% per stack.
Removed the ranged Melee attack from Demonology Warlocks in Metamorphosis (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/103958) form, and increased the damage of Doom by 25% to compensate.
Destruction Warlocks have gained a new ability: Cataclysm. Cataclysm costs one Burning Ember. After a 3 second channeling period, Cataclysm will stun and knockback all burning targets within 10 yards of the caster. This ability has a 1 minute cooldown.
Demonic Gateway (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/111771) duration has been increased to 20 15 minutes, and it will no longer despawn if the Warlock moves too far away. In addition, this ability can no longer be used in any capital city.
Glyphs

A New Major Glyph has been added: Glyph of Supernova. This glyph will cause the Warlock to deal 10% of his maximum health to all targets within 5 yards for each Burning Ember he had when he was slain.



http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/278-Warrior) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warrior) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#E))

Avatar (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/107574) no longer grants immunity to movement impairing effects, and instead now instantly breaks movement impairing effects. This ability also no longer generates addiitonal Rage while active. Its duration has increased to 24 seconds (was 20 seconds).
The cooldown of Intimidating Shout (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/5246) has increased to 90 seconds, up from 60 seconds.


Pet Battles

Pet Battle music is now more appropriate for the type of match being fought.
Several new Pet Battle PvP areas have been added to the world.


Raids, Dungeons and Scenarios

The Tidal Shield cast by High Warlord Naj'entus now has a reduced duration.
Razorgore the Untamed no longer takes extra damage from Black Talon enemies, has more health, and can cast Destroy Egg more frequently.
Viscidus now requires significantly fewer hits to freeze and Shatter.


Bug Fixes

All spells that cause Forebearance can no longer be applied simultaneously.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/imonks.gif Monk (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/341-Monk) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/monk) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#o))

Renewing Mist (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/119611) should now be more responsive.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/274-Priest) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/priest) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#U))

Halo (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/120696) Should now be more responsive.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/275-Rogue) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/rogue) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#Q))

Poisons should now be more responsive.
Revealing Strike (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/84617) should now be more responsive.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warlock) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#k))

Pandemic (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/131973) no longer smooths the damage when extending dots with different amounts of spellpower.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/278-Warrior) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warrior) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#E))

Execute (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/5308) damage with high amounts of Vengeance (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/93098) is now capped at the Warrior's maximum health to prevent it from reaching excessive numbers.
