Terrible, and nothing to aspire towards for the BE models.

That said, having tested PTR my observations are thus:

Females:

-Horseface is by and large not an issue, except on some faces, but with the geometry sets unique to each face not released, doesn't matter too much just yet.
-They have quite the funny idle emote now, with a smile and then a full on sneer. This is pleasing on the more neutral faces, but on the ones with fuller features it can look a bit OTT.
-Physique is much better, fuller and looks healthier and athletic. They look almost as big as human females, but lither, taller and more graceful.
-Casting animations much as before, just more fluid. Unlike the NE female, this has not ruined them for me.
-Their run and walk are both good. Little divergence from before in this respect.
-Their emotes are mostly understated. There's a wee bit of exaggeration in some, but nothing like some of the other, "bouncier" female models.
-The 2H weapons look large when they're being wielded but not when on her back.

So far, 7/10.

Males:

-The face Blizzard has used is just awkward. It works with some options really well, where it gives them a sort of cocky he-man look. But on others it just looks awful. I suppose, again, the unique geometry sets will rectify this.
-Idle pose is significantly less awkward. It could still be better but it looks better, particularly from one side.
-Targeted casting animation is, IMO, significantly better. It has the same vibe that the Eredar one does, almost as though the caster is sculpting the spell, and it does a better job of portraying that air of confidence BE are meant to possess.
-Run and walk are not hugely different to before, but they were fine anyway.
-Musculature is far more visible and a bit more pronounced, so they look sturdy now. The chest is a bit flatter but overall the body has a healthy, strong vibe to it.
-Some of the hairstyles look much better (as in, insanely better) , e.g. the Sephiroth one. Others still need work.
-Some emotes have that irritating big mouth thing most other male models are plagued with. However, overall, they don't have too many of these goofy sort of features. They have a cocky, chauvinist vibe to them, sort of a macho superhero type.

So far, 8/10. My favourite new male model, for sure.

For both:

-They lack the cool DK customisation options draenei got, in their present iteration.
-Skin tones are more pronounced, so they have a significant variety of skin tones now. E.g. the tans look much more noticeable.
-Hair is fantastic on both.
-They look animated and lively, like most of the new models.
-They have a slightly similar vibe to the Cassians of Wildstar now.

What I'd like to see going forward:
-More rectification of the BE male posture and a better channel animation, e.g. when summoning.
-Facial customisation options such as tattoos for both genders, and stubble for males; it suits their roguish charm.
-Full body tattoo options.
-The full gamut of new faces, with their new geometry sets deployed.
-Further improvements and refinements, e.g. new hairstyles or improvement of hairstyles that still look unappealing.
-More pronounced thigh muscularity and buttocks for the males, to balance out the overall body.
-This goes for most models - less clipping of hair!
-Cheer emote just looks... strange on male BE, perhaps less of the gaping maw?
-Stop the bizarre clipping of the waist when using a bow and strafing left on the male BE.
