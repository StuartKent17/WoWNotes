However, killing the Lich King in heroic mode might be harder than it looks. According to the russian guild Экзорсус (http://exorsus.ru/) (EU-Ревущий фьорд) the Lich King heroic mode has been buffed in 10-man instances and his health was buffed from 22.5M to 29.5M, making the fight look a lot harder now. It wouldn't be surprising if the buff also affected the 25-man version of the encounter.
