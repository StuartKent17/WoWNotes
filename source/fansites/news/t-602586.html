I'm trying to keep this post on top a bit longer to get a definitive version (more changes on this soon), however, news are still going so here is a condensed version of the last 3 days news. I also included the 2 hunter changes from Monday.

Live servers
If I'm not wrong, the Hallow's end should be available on all servers tomorrow at 04:00 AM server time, prepare yourself to run to the Scarlet Monastery's Graveyard with a lvl 70 5 man group and kill the Headless Horseman for tons of shiny loots (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/siteImages/images/news/2007/september/headlessloot.jpg)

2.3 Testing
There are no current plans to give free respecs with the 2.3 patch (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2366701493&postId=23664330327&sid=1#66))

Items
The current droprate for <a href="http://db.mmo-champion.com/item/29434/">(Item #29434)</a> in Karazhan and Zul'aman is 1-2 per boss. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2366731869&postId=23664098476&sid=1#1))
The new <a href="http://db.mmo-champion.com/item/34113/">(Item #34113)</a> sell poison, spell reagents, arrows, bullets, and a limited supply (1-2) of super mana and healing potions. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2366571832&postId=23664099696&sid=1#5))
<a href="http://db.mmo-champion.com/item/34227/">(Item #34227)</a> proc rate "scales" with level, equipping it on a level 70 characters is pretty much useless. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2366692968&postId=23663923413&sid=1#26))
There are no plans to increase the droprate of <a href="http://db.mmo-champion.com/item/30183/">(Item #30183)</a> (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2366661349&postId=23663521022&sid=1#41))

Hunters
The min range on ranged attacks will be reduced to eliminate the "dead zone". It doesn't mean that you'll be able to shot at 0 yards, but you will always be able to hit the enemy even if he's standing between 5 and 8 yards. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2366566665&sid=1&pageNo=15#281))
More hunter changes might be added, a mortal strike effect on aimed shot is still a possibility. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2366566665&postId=23663637329&sid=1#8))

Paladins
Fanaticism will now reduce threat caused by all actions by 6/12/18/24/30%, in addition to its current effect. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2366553374&postId=23663518766&sid=1#0))
Precision now increases spell hit chance as well. (Not in patch notes yet) (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2366553309&postId=23663817947&sid=1#29))

Priests
More changes to the discipline tree are scheduled for WotLK. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2366661877&postId=23664103321&sid=1#24))

Warriors
Weapon Mastery now provides a -4% dodge chance reduction. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2366569206&postId=23664371744&sid=1#31))

Others
Flying mounts in Azeroth might happen, but not anytime soon. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2366573475&postId=23663919956&sid=1#65))

Zul'aman preview
Boss strategies on Elitistjerks.com forums (http://elitistjerks.com/f15/t17039-ej_forum_community_zulaman_testing/)

When you enter the instance, you can start an event by talking to a NPC called "Harisson John". A 20 minutes countdown will start, the first boss will yell ... guess what ? You've got 20 minutes to kill him.

Kill order (If you're not doing the event, you don't have to kill them in this order)
1. Nalorakk (Bear god), 2. Akil'zon (Eagle god), 3. Jan'alai (Dragonhawk god), 4. Halazzi (Lynx god), 5. Malacrass (Big warlock troll), 6. Zul'jin (Bad guy)

Best case
If you kill the boss in time, you will free a prisonner and get an epic loot as a reward. (The prisoner will open a chest or some kind of other container, a bit like Majordomo).
Once the boss is killed, you get 20 more minutes to kill the next one, and 20 more minutes after the 2nd kill, etc ...

Worst case
If you wipe on a boss, you can't try it again before the end of the timer, it won't respawn.
Also, if you fail the event at some point, you won't get the 20 minutes countdown back after the kill. If you couldn't kill the first boss in time, you won't get any of the "bonus" loot for the following bosses.
