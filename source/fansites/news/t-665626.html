I'm going to make an attempt at clearing up some confusion about the rogue change.

Current Master Poisoner gives 15/30/45% higher chance of applying Deadly Poison (DP) after Envenom

The new Master Poisoner gives Envenom 33/66/100% chance NOT to consume DP stacks on target.

Essentially, the 45% higher application chance after Envenom was practically a useless effect as you could only really use it to boost your application rate when switching targets directly after an envenom which is situational at best. And so it was removed. It really won't make a difference unless you are constantly switching between 2 boss mobs within the 4-5 seconds of the Envenom buff.
