Patch 5.4 PTR Notes Update
Classes

All fear and horrify effects can no longer cause targets under the effects of a movement speed buff to move faster than normal run speed.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ideathknights.gif Death Knight (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/269-Death-Knight) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/death-knight) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#Y))

General

Death Gate (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/50977) now returns the Death Knight to a location near their original departure point when the spell is cast while in Ebon Hold.

Blood

Riposte is a new passive ability learned by Blood Death Knights at level 76. When the Death Knight dodges or parries any attack, they gain 75% of their Parry and Dodge as additional bonus to Critical Strike for 20 seconds.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/270-Druid) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/druid) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#s))

General

Teleport: Moonglade (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/18960) now returns the Druid to a location near their original departure point when the spell is cast while in the Moonglade area.

Glyphs

Glyph of Fae Silence (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/67484) will now cause Faerie Fire (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/770) to interrupt when used on targets that are immune to Silence (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/15487) effects.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/271-Hunter) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/hunter) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#M))

General

Deterrence (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/19263) now has a 3-minute cooldown (up from 2 minutes) but has 2 charges.
Disengage (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/781) now has a cooldown of 20 seconds (down from 25 seconds).
Readiness (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/23989) has been removed. now finishes the cooldowns on Rapid Fire (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/3045), Feign Death (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/5384), Deterrence (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/19263), Disengage (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/781), and Camouflage (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/51753) when activated (was all Hunter abilities with a base cooldown of less than 5 minutes).
Stampede (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/121818) damage dealt by pets when the ability is used outside of an Arena or Battleground is no longer reduced by 75%


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/imonks.gif Monk (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/341-Monk) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/monk) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#o))

Talents

Invoke Xuen, the White Tiger (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/123904) now has a pet control bar for Xuen, and the talent is no longer on global cooldown for all Monk specializations. summoned version of Xuen will no longer Taunt (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/355) off players that are tank-specialized.

Windwalker

Tigereye Brew (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/116740) received a number of adjustments.

Increases damage by 6% per stack, (up from 1% per stack) but is no longer increased by Mastery.
The Monk gains 1 charge of Tigereye Brew (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/116740) for every 4 Chi spent (down from 1 charge per 3 Chi spent).




http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/273-Paladin) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/paladin) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#I))

Conductivity (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/108282) has been redesigned. Casting Healing Wave (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/331), Greater Healing Wave (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/77472), Healing Surge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/8004), or Chain Heal (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/1064), increases the duration of Healing Rain (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/73920) by 3 seconds. http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warlock) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#k))

Talents

Archimonde's Vengeance (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/108505) has been redesigned and renamed into Archimonde's Darkness. Archimonde's Darkness gives the Warlock's Dark Soul (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/77801) spell two charges. For the passive portion of the talent, each time the Warlock or their pet takes damage, the Warlock now gains 1% of the unmitigated damage back as spell power for 20 seconds.
Soul Link (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/108415) now transfers 20% of damage taken by the Warlock to their pet, and 3% of all damage dealt by the Warlock will be returned as healing for the Warlock and their pet. This ability is always active while the Warlock's pet is active.

Glyphs

Glyph of Dark Soul (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/42464) will be redesigned.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/278-Warrior) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warrior) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#E))

Talents

Bladestorm (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/46924) now deals an increased 180% of weapon damage for Arms Warriors (up from 120%).
Bloodbath (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/12292) snare and bleed effects are now two separate debuffs. Clearing one of the effects will no longer remove the other.

Protection

Revenge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/6572) now generates 20 Rage (up from 15 Rage).
Riposte is a new passive ability learned by Protection Warriors at level 76. When the Warrior dodges or parries any attack, they gain 75% of their Parry and Dodge as an additional bonus to Critical Strike for 20 seconds.
Ultimatum (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/122509) now activates from critical hits with Shield Slam (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/23922) instead of a flat 20% chance, and the ability will make the next Heroic Strike (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/78) or Cleave (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/845) be a critical hit in addition to costing no Rage.


Pet Battles

Pet Battle General

Battle Pet Training cost and the price of pets sold by Battle Pet Trainers have been greatly reduced.

Battle Pets

Tiny Twister: The Sandstorm ability has been replaced with Call Lightning.

Battle Pet Abilities

Battle Pet abilities with a component that increases in damage every time it hits will no longer reset when an opponent's pet gets swapped. (Arcane Blast, Frog Kiss, etc.)
Death Grip cooldown increased to 4 rounds (up from 3 rounds), damage has been reduced by 50%, and hit chance has been reduced to match similar abilities.
Nether Gate damage has been reduced by 33%.
Sweep damage has been reduced by 33%.
