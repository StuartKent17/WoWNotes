Patch 5.3 - Battle of Razor Hill
Today we are taking a look at the Battle of Razor Hill and quests leading up to it from the Horde perspective. You begin by traveling to Sen'jin village after completing two of the new scenarios, and speaking with Vol'jin (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32867-voljin-of-the-darkspear). He sends you in to the Barrens (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32819-battlefield-barrens) to collect 15 Lumber, Stone, Oil, and Meat, which requires killing one mob per item, looting crates with the items, or escorting the caravans that travel through the Barrens. While doing so, you will also earn some Lesser Charms from NPC drops.

After gathering those supplies, you return to Vol'jin in time for the Battle of Sen'jin Village (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32813-battle-of-senjin-village), in which you join Thrall and Chen Stormstout in defending the village (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32813-battle-of-senjin-village) from the Kor'kron attackers.

After a solid victory in Sen'jin Village, you march onward to Razor Hill with Vol'jin and Chen Stormstout. Upon arriving at Razor Hill, the battle is already underway in the air and on the ground. You defeat some guards, named NPCs, and release captive Razor Hill NPCs from cages before heading to see Vol'jin once again. Once at the small new Sanctuary area outside of Razor Hill, Baine Bloodhoof shows up to speak with Vol'jin about helping the revolution.

Be sure to watch the video to get the full story!

Patch 5.3 PTR Notes Update - April 11
Another large PTR notes update tonight, with the interesting non class specific changes below!


Shado-Pan and The August Celestials daily quests no longer have a faction prerequisite to be Revered with the Golden Lotus.
A Test of Valor (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/32476-a-test-of-valor): Reduced the amount of Valor needed for this achievement to 3000 Valor Points, down from 6000.
Picking up the Alliance or Horde flag while in a Rated Battleground now increases damage taken by 50% for characters in a tanking specialization, and increases damage taken by 20% for characters in a non-tanking specialization.
Tyrannical weapons no longer have a conquest-earned prerequisite to purchase them.


Classes

Players with full health no longer count towards the cap for area-of-effect healing abilities and spells.

Shield Slam (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/23922) now receives a 50% bonus to attack damage from attack power (down from 100%) for Warriors below level 85.
