World Boss - Flotsam
Flotsam (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/99929-flotsam) is up this week, giving players a chance to win item level 860 loot and a Life Relic (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/141516-liberated-ungoro-relic). Shaman also have a chance to get The Warmace of Shirvallah (http://www.wowdb.com/items/139573-the-warmace-of-shirvallah).

Weekly Bonus Event - Arena Skirmishes
Sharpen your swords and reinforce your boards, because you’re called to the PvP Arena for the hectic mayhem of Skirmishes.

This Week: Arena Skirmishes
All week, from anywhere in Azeroth, open the Group Finder (default hotkey: i), select the Player vs. Player tab on the bottom of the window, and then under Arena Battles, choose either 2v2 or 3v3 and JOIN BATTLE. You’ll be matched up with teammates and provided with opponents to kill in the Arena.

Look for the following all week long:


Archmage Timear (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/111246-archmage-timear) near Violet Hold in Dalaran has a quest (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/44172-the-arena-calls) for you.

Quest requirement: Win 10 Arena PvP Skirmishes

Passive buff: +50% honor gains from Skirmishes


If you’ve been looking to make some Honor gains, this is the week to do it!


Darkmoon Faire
The Darkmoon Faire is back in town! There are a few things it offers that you can take advantage of:


New in Patch 7.1 - Galissa Sundew (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/55103-galissa-sundew) sells the Darkwater Skate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/142398-darkwater-skate) for 500 x Darkmoon Daggermaw (http://www.wowdb.com/items/124669-darkmoon-daggermaw)
Take advantage of the profession quests (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/world-events/darkmoon-faire?filter-skill-points=-1) that grant you +5 skill in a profession.
Ride the carousel for the WHEE! (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/46668-whee) buff grants a 10% increase to reputation and experience gains.
Grab the Darkmoon Top Hat (http://www.wowdb.com/items/93730-darkmoon-top-hat) buff that grants a 10% increase to reputation and experience gains.
Visit Professor Thaddeus Paleo (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/14847-professor-thaddeus-paleo) to pick up Rank 2 and Rank 3 of the Darkmoon Card crafting recipes.
You can upgrade the Darkmoon trinkets with Obliterum (http://www.wowdb.com/items/124125-obliterum), just like other crafted items.
