Hyjal summit and Zul'aman changes in 2.4
I already posted about the additions of The Fists of Fury (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/february/thefistsoffury.jpg) to the Hyjal Summit trash loot table yesterday, but we now have a lot more details about all the changes made to Hyjal Summit in 2.4
You now get 12 reputation points for each mob killed.
Frostwyrms give 60 reputation points.
Bosses give 375 reputation points.
You should get about 6~7k reputation points for a single run.
The area around Archimonde in Battle for Mount Hyjal has been decreased in size and players are no longer able to go into the hills around his area. (Source (http://www.wowwiki.com/Patch_2.4.0/Undocumented_changes))



Also, I didn't really know where to add this Zul'aman change in today's news, but it looks like [item=33421] now gives 40 defense rating instead of 40 block rating.


Sunwell Heroic rewards ETA - About a month

Patch 2.4 Bugs
There are a few more bugs identified on patch 2.4, according to Hortus a full list of bugs currently affecting the live servers will be released very soon.
Raid reset timer
There's an issue that's causing the "raid reset time" message to display incorrect lengths. There haven't been any changes, it's simply a display issue. I'm not sure at this time if it's an ongoing issue or if it was a temporary error. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=5575607675&postId=55751959087&sid=1#16))

Void Reaver
Void Reaver intentionally no longer targets or faces players that he casts Arcane Orb on. This was done to fix a bug. There is a bug in that there is currently no combat log message for him casting Arcane Orb (just the impact message), causing boss mods to not work. This will be fixed in a future patch. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=5575687557&postId=55751958917&sid=1#12))

Mages
A bug prevent players from training Polymorph: Pig. Please contact a GM if you have completed the quest and they will make every effort to help you. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=5540036694&postId=55751157778&sid=1#4))

Shaman
Mental Quickess doesn't affect Ghost Wolf (with Improved Ghost Wolf 2/2), this look like a bug and Blizzard will check it out. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=5539875308&postId=55392573230&sid=1#4))
The "clearcasting sound" is bugged and no longer plays. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=5539875302&postId=55751158275&sid=1#3))



Fatigue bar on gryphons
Sometimes, players will get a fatigue bar while using a gryphons and will eventually die, this is a known bug and it will be fixed in an upcoming patch. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=5540056054&postId=55392576496&sid=1#10))

Alterac Valley resurrection glitch
Sometimes in Alterac Valley, players won't be teleported to the next graveyard if the one they're currently waiting at is taken by the enemy. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=5539825734&postId=55751158257&sid=1#16))

Quests
Veneratus the Many isn't spawning anymore for the "The Cipher of Damnation - Ar'tor's Charge" quest. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=5539956623&postId=55751963986&sid=1#5))
