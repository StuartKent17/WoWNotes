Draenor Perks in Legion
The Draenor Perks will be retiring in Legion. However, many of their effects will be "baked in" to the baseline abilities. (Blue Tracker (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/394917-perks-in-legion/) / Official Forums (http://eu.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/16884598987?page=1#4))


Blue Tweets
Classes
ShS used to be our 41 and now you're giving it to assassination only? Uh....
Our intention is for Subtlety to be *better* at Shadowstep than the other specs, due to having a better version of it. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/671407153022935040))
If Shadowstrike isn't meeting that intention, we'll iterate as needed until we reach that goal. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/671407412348362753))

PvP
Can you confirm that the legendary effects will, or will not, work in rated/non-rated PvP?
Legendary effects will not work in instanced PvP (like Battlegrounds or Arenas). They *will* work in World PvP. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/671408664423620608))
