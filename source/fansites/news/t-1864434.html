Armory Stats - Flying Achievements
With flying finally here, we are checking back in to see how players are doing on unlocking flight. You can see the numbers from last month here (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/5089-Armory-Stats-Flying-Achievements-August-14-Hotfixes-Blue-Tweets-HS-TGT-Cards).

The sample used today is 1.8 million players (not characters) that have logged in since August 15th. Only level 100 characters are included in the data. Characters are grouped by player, so no matter how many characters a player has, they only count once.

We included the 200 Treasures achievement just for comparison purposes, only 100 is required for Master Treasure Hunter (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/10348-master-treasure-hunter), which is required for Draenor Pathfinder (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/10018-draenor-pathfinder).
