Celebrate 14 Years of World of Warcraft!
WoW's 14th Anniversary (https://www.wowdb.com/world-events/807-wows-14th-anniversary) begins today! New this year are the Overtuned Corgi Goggles (https://www.wowdb.com/items/158149-overtuned-corgi-goggles), sold for 200 x Timewarped Badge (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)


The Dragons of Nightmare (https://wow.gamepedia.com/Dragons_of_Nightmare_(original)), Azuregos (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/121820-azuregos), and Lord Kazzak (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/121818-lord-kazzak) have returned with item level 350 loot!
You could bonus roll for another attempt at loot in previous years. Let us know in the comments if you win something from a bonus roll!
You can try and loot the world bosses daily.
The Originals (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/47253-the-originals) is a daily quest that requires level 60, so you may be able to try for this loot every day. Currently the bosses appear to respawn every 5 minutes.
A Time to Reflect (H) (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/43461-a-time-to-reflect) / A Time to Reflect (A) (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/43323-a-time-to-reflect) are up again this year! You can use QuizHelper (https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/quizhelper) to answer the questions.
Historian Ju'pa (H) (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/110035-historian-jupa) and Historian Llore (A) (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/110034-historian-llore) have some things for sale, including the new Overtuned Corgi Goggles (https://www.wowdb.com/items/158149-overtuned-corgi-goggles)

Feral Bloodswarmers' Sonic Screech damage was halved.


Temple of Sethraliss

Faithless Tenders' Embryonic Vigor cooldown reduction effect decreased from 50% to 10%.


Shrine of the Storms

Deepsea Ritualists' Unending Darkness cast time was increased from 2 seconds to 3 seconds.
Tidesage Enforcer's Deep Smash damage was decreased by 25%.
Tidesage Spiritualists' Anchor of Binding cast had its damage halved.


King's Rest

King A'akul's Hidden Blade damage was halved.
King Timalji's Bladestorm had its self-slowing effect increased from 25% to 60%, giving players more chance to run.
Queen Wasi's Seduction debuff now has a maximum duration of 8 seconds, down from 30 seconds.
Shadow-Borne Champions' Ancestral Fury self-enrage buff was decreased from 100% additional damage to 50% additional damage.




Explosive Affix Nerfed
In Mythic+, Explosives now have 50% less health, and will no longer spawn from crowd controlled enemies. We're also working on a number of nerfs to specific dungeon creatures and mechanics. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/1063263066735824898))


Mythrax Encounter Nerfed
We've recently applied a couple of hotfixes to the Mythrax encounter: Oblivion Sphere now prioritizes ranged targets when possible, and the Existential Crisis achievement now functions as described. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/1063262725646647297))
