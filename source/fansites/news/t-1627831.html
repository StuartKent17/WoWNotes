What value do you give this may I ask?

The eyebrows on Thrall are a unique (or near-unique, if you count the Cinematic Orc) trait. Stuff like that is interesting and usually done for a reason, the given answer being to humanize Thrall a bit and reflect that he was raised by humans. There's also the opposite that's being asked: why do orcs shave (or remove, if they don't shave them) their eyebrows?

This next expansion, we're going to learn a lot about orcs. We're going to learn what drives them to do what they do and what choices they make. We'll learn about their culture, about their thoughts and feelings, traditions, motivations, history. We're going to learn why some were designed visually the way they were. I doubt we'll learn about eyebrows (or maybe Blizzard got enough of a chuckle that they might add something about it, idk) but I'm almost positive this question was never brought to the devs prior to this past weekend. The value, in my mind, is that the developers got to think about something maybe they didn't think about before, if only for a moment. In return, we got a bit of information about a unique trait of an important character.

Was it important information (comparative to other questions that were asked or the desires of the internet)? Absolutely not. I don't think it'll affect the story or character of Thrall in anyway. But its a neat little tidbit and some people get kicks out of that stuff.
