To some of the posters above about a quad-boxed ele shaman setup. I am pretty sure it doesn't get any cheesier than 4 elemental shaman hitting 1 target with a lightning at THE EXACT SAME TIME. This is not a question of whether this is allowed by EULA or that it's still 1 person controlling them - it's a question of reaction speed.

Take 4 elemental shamans, put them all on vent and have them focus fire a target. Keep in mind approx. 100-200ms latency to the WoW server for each person, in addition to Ventrilo latency and finally add the time it takes for them to hear the target being called, target it and then start casting a lightning.

With a quad-box, you basically get rid of all those extra factors meaning, again, they will ALL hit the SAME target at the SAME time. On my warrior with 399 resil and 12.5K HP an elemental shaman with bloodlust / heroism is one of the most dreaded enemies. 4 elemental shaman would kill me without a chance for a spell reflect seeing as all 4 chain lightnings is plenty to take me from 100 to 0. Even if those 4 don't kill me, the consequent shocks / lightning bolts will kill me. For a quad-box of shamans it would take about 5 seconds to kill a warrior or pretty much any target for that reason (unless it's a mage or a paladin preemptively blocking / bubbling).

Basic point I am trying to make is that multi-boxing is akin to cheating by cutting out human factor.

PvE is another story - I could care less for what/when/how someone kills a boss.
