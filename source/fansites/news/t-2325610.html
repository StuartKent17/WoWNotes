Infused with fire and shadow, F'harg and Shatug are the prized pets of Sargeras. Bred for carnage, these hounds delight in eviscerating their master's enemies. With the Army of the Light and their allies advancing upon Antorus, the Legion prepares to unleash these vicious creatures onto the battlefield and put an end to the mortals' invasion.


<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Overview - The Felhounds of Sargeras combat players while performing attacks at specific power thresholds, but each has their own power generation rate. Focusing Power (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/251356-focusing-power) temporarily suspends this power generation. While within 40 yards of each other, Sargeras' Blessing (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/246057-sargeras-blessing) imbues them with 100% increased damage.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Damage Dealers - Be aware of targets afflicted with Desolate Gaze (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/244768-desolate-gaze), as it inflicts damage from F'harg to the targets' direction.
Assist players afflicted with Siphoned (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/248819-siphoned) during Siphon Corruption (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/244056-siphon-corruption), and avoid players afflicted with Enflamed (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/248815-enflamed) during Enflame Corruption (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/244057-enflame-corruption).
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/healer.png" title="Healer" /> Healers - Be aware of targets afflicted with Desolate Gaze (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/244768-desolate-gaze), as it inflicts damage from F'harg to the targets' direction.
Pay attention when a Felhound reaches 100 power, as Erupting Flames (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/257236-erupting-flames) and Erupting Shadows (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/257238-erupting-shadows) inflict sustained raid-wide damage.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Tanks - Be aware of targets afflicted with Desolate Gaze (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/244768-desolate-gaze), as it inflicts damage from F'harg to the targets' direction.
Watch for the Burning Maw (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/251448-burning-maw) or Corrupting Maw (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/251447-corrupting-maw) auras, as they indicate that the targeted boss can cast the aforementioned ability.

Patch 7.3.2 - Undocumented Mount Changes
Nightswifty shared (http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2325459-Poseidus-and-Poundfist-spawn-times-changed!-Living-Infernal-Core-now-drops-from-LFR!) some changes that were made to mounts in Patch 7.3.2:


Living Infernal Core (http://www.wowdb.com/items/137574-living-infernal-core) now drops from Raid Finder difficulty Gul'dan
Reins of Poseidus (http://www.wowdb.com/items/67151-reins-of-poseidus), which drops from Poseidus (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/50005-poseidus) is now easier to get, as his respawn time is now shorter
Sunhide Gronnling (http://www.wowdb.com/items/116792-sunhide-gronnling), which drops form Poundfist (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/50985-poundfist) may be easier to get, as his respawn time might be shorter
