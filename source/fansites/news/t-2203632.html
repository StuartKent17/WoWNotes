World Boss - Apocron
Apocron (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/47061-apocron) is up now, so you can head to the Broken Shore for a chance at item level 900 loot, a Iron Relic, or Life Relic.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/april/apocron.jpg (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/595-Legion-World-Bosses#apocron)


Nether Disruptor and Command Center
The Nether Disruptor and Command Center are active on US realms!

Nether Disruptor

Zone-wide buff: Epic Hunter (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/239647-epic-hunter)

Unlocks new world bosses in the Broken Isles. These bosses drop item level 900 loot.
If your profession is maxed before you complete the building, you get Armorcrafter's Commendation (http://www.wowdb.com/items/147451-armorcrafters-commendation) to start on the crafted legendary item questlines (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/6491-Patch-7-2-Live-This-Week!#craftedLegendary).
Purchase a Nether Portal Disruptor (http://www.wowdb.com/items/147775-nether-portal-disruptor) to disrupt Unstable Nether portals around the Broken Shore. This summons a small boss which drops War Supplies, Beacons, and other loot.

This week's buff: Netherstorm (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/239969-netherstorm) - Chance to earn bonus Nethershards (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1226-nethershard)
Potential buffs: Fate Smiles Upon You (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/239968-fate-smiles-upon-you), Netherstorm (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/239969-netherstorm), Reinforced Reins (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/240985-reinforced-reins), Seal Your Fate (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/239967-seal-your-fate)


Command Center

Zone-wide buff: Forces of the Order (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/239648-forces-of-the-order)

Unlocks Cathedral World Quests, which reward any Armies of Legionfall reputation.
Unlocks a "Challenging Mission" for the Broken Shore that rewards lots of AP.

This week's buff: Worthy Champions (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/240986-worthy-champions) - Free legendary follower equipment. Make sure to head into the Command Center and complete the quest!
Potential buffs: Heavily Augmented (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/240989-heavily-augmented), War Effort (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/239966-war-effort), Worthy Champions (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/240986-worthy-champions), Well Prepared (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/240987-well-prepared)

Build of the Month - April 2017
Each month or so we will post a couple of hardware setups for those of you who are thinking of upgrading their computer!

All of the parts on this page are Amazon affiliate links. When you purchase your parts through them it helps support the site!

This month
The current SSD prices (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/april/ssdgraph.gif) are fairly flat right now, with most SSDs under $0.50 per gigabyte when on sale.

Depending on how much you want to overclock, you may need to spend a bit more for a better CPU cooler like the NH-D15 (http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00L7UZMAK?ie=UTF8&tag=mmochamp-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B00L7UZMAK).

Unless you are doing a lot of video rendering or streaming, you do not need the 7700K and would get more performance from spending that money elsewhere.

Make sure to keep your AMD (http://sites.amd.com/us/game/downloads/Pages/downloads.aspx) and Nvidia (http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us) drivers up to date.

Don't forget to visit the Computer Forum (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/290-Computer) for any extra questions!
