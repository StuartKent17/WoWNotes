Armory Stats - Battle Pet Collection
Today we are taking a look at the battle pet ownership achievement completion rate of players. Now that achievements are account wide, we can group characters by account and get an idea of what percentage of players (not characters) have completed an achievement.

These numbers are based off of the players that have earned the achievements (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/1017-can-i-keep-him) in the pet collection series and Raiding with Leashes (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/7934-raiding-with-leashes), Raiding with Leashes II: Attunement Edition (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/8293-raiding-with-leashes-ii-attunement-edition), Crazy for Cats (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/8397-crazy-for-cats), and Celestial Family (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/8519-celestial-family).

The data used today is a sample of 8.1 million characters from 3 million accounts. The sample is slightly biased, as players who are not in a guild are much less likely to appear in our sample. You can leave suggestions for future stat posts in the comments!

One week for the Celestial Tournament Achievement.
3 weeks for the Master of the Masters Achievement.

Where people are getting trapped - and I admit this where Blizzard sorta drops the ball - is the misleading 15 level 25 pets requirement.
No way in hell one can complete this scenario with only 15 pets.
There's a total of 9 opponents in the scenario which rotate weekly, with each week giving you 3 of them. The sets of 3 are always the same, but still..
The 4 Celestials after those 3 can be beaten with a combo of 4 pets total, unless the RNG gods hate you, and you don't have a backup.

The combo for those is: Pandaren Water Spirit, Chrominius, Darkmoon Tonk. Have Magical Crawdad as healer at hand.
Kill order:
Xuen (he can once in a blue moon brutally crit and your strat fails)
Niuzao (he can once in a while absorb too much dmg, and your strat can fail here too)
Chi-Chi (absolutely no chance against the strat)
Yu-Lon (same, no chance but has to be last, since you use him to heal in between)

Strat, the same on all 4 Celestials:
Water Spirit - Cast Geyser, Cast Whirlpool, SWAP OUT
Chrominius - Cast Howl, Cast Surge of Power.

This is normally enough to kill all 4.. However..
For Niuzao your Chrominius rotation should be: HOWL - BITE - SURGE OF POWER

In between the fights, swap your Tonk for a Crawdad..
Open battle with Yu-Lon with Crawdad, Cast Wish. Swap to injured Pet, wait for heal to go off, abandon battle. Repeat for second hurt pet.
BAM, your team is good to go again. Don't forget to put the Tonk back in before you move to the next.


On Yu-Lon you might have to bring the Tonk in after Chrominius sometimes. His Rotation is: Shock and Awe - Ion Cannon
