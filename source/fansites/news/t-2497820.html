No, that really won't do anything.

Racials were imbalanced (or at least perceived to be) in the past, which caused a shift in hardcore raiders towards the Horde. Those raiders are now almost exclusively playing Horde. Disabling racials leaves them playing Horde, as they currently do, with no incentive to switch to Alliance. Alliance remains irrelevant in the world first race.

The only way to fill that board with Alliance guilds is IF racials are imbalanced in the favor of the Alliance races, but that just flips the problem and doesn't solve it. If WoW, a 14 year-old game, somehow gets a massive infusion of new players, then disabled racials may have a slight effect. Even in that scenario, new players that want to raid at that level will still likely gravitate towards the existing horde-biased raiding guilds.
