Darkmoon Faire
The Darkmoon Faire is back in town! There are a few things it offers that you can take advantage of:


New in Patch 7.1 - Galissa Sundew (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/55103-galissa-sundew) sells the Darkwater Skate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/142398-darkwater-skate) for 500 x Darkmoon Daggermaw (http://www.wowdb.com/items/124669-darkmoon-daggermaw)
Take advantage of the profession quests (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/world-events/darkmoon-faire?filter-skill-points=-1) than grant you +5 skill in a profession.
Ride the carousel for the WHEE! (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/46668-whee) buff grants a 10% increase to reputation and experience gains.
Grab the Darkmoon Top Hat (http://www.wowdb.com/items/93730-darkmoon-top-hat) buff that grants a 10% increase to reputation and experience gains.
Visit Professor Thaddeus Paleo (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/14847-professor-thaddeus-paleo) to pick up Rank 2 and Rank 3 of the Darkmoon Card crafting recipes.
You can upgrade the Darkmoon trinkets with Obliterum (http://www.wowdb.com/items/124125-obliterum), just like other crafted items.

Weekly Event - Timewalking
Wrath of the Lich King timewalking (http://www.wowdb.com/world-events/616-timewalking-dungeon-event) is up this week, running through November 15!


The weekly quest from Archmage Timear (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/111246-archmage-timear) rewards a Accolade of Victory (http://www.wowdb.com/items/140176-accolade-of-victory), Seal of Broken Fate (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1273-seal-of-broken-fate), and Cache of Nightmarish Treasures (http://www.wowdb.com/items/139486-cache-of-nightmarish-treasures)
Timewalking dungeons drop item level 820 gear.
You can find Auzin (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/98690-auzin) in Dalaran. The rewards he sells have been updated to item level 805.
Don't forget to keep working towards Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (http://www.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk) and Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (http://www.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger)!
Reins of the Infinite Timereaver (http://www.wowdb.com/items/133543-reins-of-the-infinite-timereaver) can also drop from any Timewalking dungeon boss.
