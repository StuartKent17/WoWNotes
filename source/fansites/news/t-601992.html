Model changes in 2.2
You can't really miss it, it's everywhere, the scale of some models is changed in 2.2. The most obvious one is the orc shoulders switched back to a "normal" size, but a few other races were changed. Basically, Orcs get bigger shields and shoulders, Draeneis slightly smaller shoulders, but our great winners are ... Night Elves ! They now have slightly (barely noticeable) smaller shoulders, and their weapons and shields are also smaller. You can compare with the screenshots below.

Evocation changes in 2.2
Hortus made a few precisions about power regeneration through skills like Evocation and Innervate in 2.2. Even if it's pretty clear in the patch notes (http://forums.wow-europe.com/thread.html?topicId=301970360&pageNo=1&sid=1#0) I guess it might be useful to some people.

The change in evocation should not increase the amount of mana you gain, it only prevents an occasional timing issue where players were getting less mana then they should.

Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=1371414101&pageNo=1&sid=1#1)
