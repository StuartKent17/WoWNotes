Patch 4.2.2 on Test Realms soon?
A wild Patch 4.2.2 appeared on the test realms download servers a couple of hours ago, and it looks like we'll go through a bugfix patch before we get to see Patch 4.3.


Important - Most of these changes are nothing more than tooltip fixes to reflect Patch 4.2 Hotfixes (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/183042/patch-42-hotfixes) already active on live servers. I'm mostly documenting them for the sake of it.

Achievement Changes
PvP - Arena

Challenger (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/2090/challenger/#14333/14492) now also includes Vicious Gladiators.
Gladiator (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/2091/gladiator/#14333/14492) now also includes Vicious Gladiators.
Duelist (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/2092/duelist/#14333/14492) now also includes Vicious Gladiators.
Rival (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/2093/rival/#14333/14492) now also includes Vicious Gladiators.


PvP - Tol Barad

Occu'thar (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/6045/occuthar/#14333/14492) finally has his own achievement.


Classes
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ideathknights.gif Death Knight (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/269-Death-Knight) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..deathknight) / Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1116-Death-Knight-Cataclysm))
Blood

Improved Death Strike (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/81138/improved-death-strike/#14480/14492) increases damage done by Death Strike by 40/80/120%, up from 30/60/90%. Increases critical strike chance by 10/20/30%, up from 3/6/9%.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/276-Shaman) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..shaman) / Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1124-Shaman-Cataclysm))
Elemental

Shamanism (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/62099/shamanism/#14480/14492) gives an additional 32% benefit from spell power, up from 20%.


Items
World Events

Loot-Filled Pumpkin (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/54516/loot-filled-pumpkin/#14480/14492) now requires Hallow's End
Keg-Shaped Treasure Chest (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/54535/keg-shaped-treasure-chest/#14480/14492) now requires Brewfest
Satchel of Chilled Goods (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/54536/satchel-of-chilled-goods/#14480/14492) now requires Midsummer Fire Festival
Heart-Shaped Box (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/54537/heart-shaped-box/#14480/14492) now requires Love is in the Air


Weapons

Deflecting Star (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/71218/deflecting-star/#14480/14492) is now bound when picked up.
Lava Bolt Crossbow (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/71366/lava-bolt-crossbow/#14480/14492) now increases haste by 81 instead of mastery by 75. Increases critical strike rating by 88, down from 91.
Lava Bolt Crossbow (H) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/71558/lava-bolt-crossbow/#14480/14492) now increases haste by 92 instead of mastery by 85. Increases critical strike rating by 99, down from 103.


Trinkets

Unheeded Warning (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/59520/unheeded-warning/#14480/14492) now increases attack power by 1926 instead of increasing weapon damage by 680. (Yes, the tooltip says 1926 weapon damage, but it's bugged)
Apparatus of Khaz'goroth (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/68972/apparatus-of-khazgoroth/#14480/14492) now increases critical strike, haste, or mastery rating by 508, up from 306.
Apparatus of Khaz'goroth (H) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/69113/apparatus-of-khazgoroth/#14480/14492) now increases critical strike, haste, or mastery rating by 575, up from 345.
Necromantic Focus (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/68982/necromantic-focus/#14480/14492) now grants 39 mastery rating, down from 42.
Necromantic Focus (H) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/69139/necromantic-focus/#14480/14492) now grants 44 mastery rating, down from 48.
Matrix Restabilizer (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/68994/matrix-restabilizer/#14480/14492) now grants 1624 critical strike, haste, or mastery rating, up from 1532.
Matrix Restabilizer (H) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/69150/matrix-restabilizer/#14480/14492) now grants 1834 critical strike, haste, or mastery rating, up from 1730.
Vessel of Acceleration (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/68995/vessel-of-acceleration/#14480/14492) now grants 82 critical strike rating, up from 77.
Vessel of Acceleration (H) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/69167/vessel-of-acceleration/#14480/14492) now grants 92 critical strike rating, up from 87.


Plate

Pauldrons of Roaring Flame (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/70921/pauldrons-of-roaring-flame/#14480/14492) now increases mastery rating instead of hit rating.
Pauldrons of Roaring Flame (H) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/71612/pauldrons-of-roaring-flame/#14480/14492) now increases mastery rating instead of hit rating.


PvP Gear

Most Vicious Gladiator wrists/belts/boots received slight increases to stamina (Ruthless Gladiator's Boots of Alacrity (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/70507/ruthless-gladiators-boots-of-alacrity/#14480/14492), Vicious Gladiator's Waistband of Cruelty (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/70664/vicious-gladiators-waistband-of-cruelty/#14480/14492), Vicious Gladiator's Footguards of Alacrity (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/60607/vicious-gladiators-footguards-of-alacrity/#14480/14492), Vicious Gladiator's Armwraps of Alacrity (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/70516/vicious-gladiators-armwraps-of-alacrity/#14480/14492), etc ...)


Professions
Blacksmithing

Blacksmithing: Masterwork Elementium Deathblade (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/99658/masterwork-elementium-deathblade/#14480/14492) now has a 3.5 Speed and 1615 - 2999 Damage. Down from 3.8 Speed and 1753 - 3256 Damage.


Leatherworking

Leatherworking: Bloodthirsty Fur Cloak (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/99536/bloodthirsty-fur-cloak/#14480/14492), Leatherworking: Bloodthirsty Hide Cloak (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/99535/bloodthirsty-hide-cloak/#14480/14492) are no longer unique.


Tailoring

Tailoring: Bloodthirsty Embersilk Cape (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/99537/bloodthirsty-embersilk-cape/#14480/14492) is no longer unique.
