Weekly Event - Wrath of the Lich King Timewalking
Wrath of the Lich King Timewalking (https://www.wowdb.com/world-events/562-timewalking-dungeon-event) is back this week. Don't forget to check out the Ulduar (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/4273-ulduar) timewalking raid!


The weekly quest (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/44166-a-frozen-path-through-time) from your adventure journal rewards azerite power and Cache of Uldir Treasures (https://www.wowdb.com/items/164258-cache-of-uldir-treasures).
You can find Auzin (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/98690-auzin) in old Dalaran.
Don't forget to keep working towards Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk), Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger), and Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade)!
Reins of the Infinite Timereaver (https://www.wowdb.com/items/133543-reins-of-the-infinite-timereaver) can also drop from any Timewalking dungeon boss.

Call to Arms: Zuldazar
This week's Call to Arms is Call to Arms: Zuldazar (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52951-call-to-arms-zuldazar) or Call to Arms: Zuldazar (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52952-call-to-arms-zuldazar). Head to Zuldazar with warmode enabled to kill 10 players of the opposing faction. This will reward you with conquest and 75 reputation for either 7th Legion (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2159-7th-legion) or The Honorbound (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2157-the-honorbound).
