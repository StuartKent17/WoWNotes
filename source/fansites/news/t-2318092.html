2015 - Illidan, Dwarf, Orc and Wolf, Grommash, Azeroth Choppers
2016 - Illidan, Orc and Wolf, Grommash, Azeroth Choppers
2017 - Illidan, Sylvanas, Grommash, Azeroth Choppers
