Warlords of Draenor Item Stats
Depends on if you are a class that benefits from all stats, some don't... and wish to reforge the bad stat.
Truly bad stats (and they do exist) are on us to fix. When one stat is 5% better and still considered mandatory = tough to fix. (Source (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/407621664751378432))
Miss a break point by few hundred haste? Stuck with wasted stats until perfectly itemized piece drops. Back to BIS lists.
"Miss a break point by a few hundred haste" and "wasted stats" implies a level of min-maxing not necessary for almost all players. (Source (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/407927962176999424))
Barely missing a break point? That's a rounding error for most of us overcome by better use of abilities and movement in a fight. (Source (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/407928377341788160))
haste breakpoints for DoTs and HoTs are important. missing by 1 point means 1 less tick, and they are noticable.
There won't be break points on hots and dots any longer. (Source (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/408258516894240769))
does this mean haste will increase the duration? instead of reducing time between ticks
Haste will increase the number of ticks, possibly resulting in a final fractional tick. (Source (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/408355005985353728))

Please keep Reforging in. Now that hit and exp are gone, I don't see the reason for it to be removed.

Reforging being gone won't stop min-max-ers to min-max their characters, at all. It will stop players defining their style.

Maybe they meant: "want secondary stats to affect play style, not dps". Would be cool (hard as hell) to do that

My dream: lots of abilities for dps, you pick and choose what your rotation uses due to what stat you favor. works in theory?

a statment like that caterning for the people that dont give a shit about playing the game is a ..

Tertiary Stats
New loot system will make loot distribution a PITA. I'm not looking forward to dealing with more loot related drama.
Overall we find sharing fewer items to be better for morale even if the pieces that do drop are highly coveted. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/405460586936274944))
I meant "Overall we find SHARDING fewer...." That changes the whole context. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/405460796815065089))

Think tertiary stats dramas organized raids? Chest drops, give to tank. +lifesteal chest drops, give to tank again. 1st wasted
That will happen on rare occasions. But what if a chest drops with Lifesteal but a secondary the tank doesn't like, say crit? (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/402649275869306880))
My point is that overall the landscape will have more answers than just "Is it BiS? If not, pass." (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/402649412523929600))

Ridiculously powerful items being very rare and random like D3, w/o the ability to grind for these as much as you want. Why?
We want the long tail to be exciting. Otherwise you earn BiS quickly and shard everything else for weeks. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/405459699119570945))

Why does WoD loot seem like Diablo loot system with a 1 week lockout and huge party requirement?
It's not nearly as random as Diablo. We just want to keep that 14th Immerseus kill somewhat exciting. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/405462038899470336))
Lots of people will not see that as something cool or amazing dropping. To them it will feel like the other drops are shit.
Then they're going to go weeks and weeks without a new item if they pass everything that isn't BiS. MC loot style wasn't that bad. (Source (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/408663364684890112))

If Tertiary stats aren't 'common enough to build a set around', then where's the gameplay? Who cares about +0.74% run speed.
Since we don't have to balance around the case of a run speed set, we should be able to make the effects much larger than 0.74%. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/405458496230940672))
Run speed might be a bit too BiS for casters as a tert. stat dont you think?
Run speed will still be pretty rare and on some fights it's totally useless. Seems like a fine position for it overall. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/405464304134017025))


Computers - Setup of the Month (by chaud (http://www.mmo-champion.com/members/523085-chaud))
Each month or every 2 months, depending on the hardware evolutions, the lovely chaud (http://www.mmo-champion.com/members/523085-chaud) will work on a couple of hardware setups for those of you who are thinking of upgrading their computer!

This month
New GPUs from AMD are here, at least on paper. Finding any AMD GPUs is hard now, as is finding some of the Nvidia GPUs. Prices for many GPUs went up due to demand and the older GPUs being discontinued.

The current SSD prices (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2013/december/ssdgraph.gif) are stable, with most SSDs under $1 per gigabyte when on sale.

The Haswell CPUs (http://www.anandtech.com/show/7003/the-haswell-review-intel-core-i74770k-i54560k-tested/1) run a little bit on the warmer side. Depending on how much you want to overclock, you may need to spend a bit more for a better CPU cooler like the NH-D14 (http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002VKVZ1A?ie=UTF8&tag=mmochamp-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B002VKVZ1A) or H90 (http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00B4OCWDE?ie=UTF8&tag=mmochamp-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B00B4OCWDE).

Unless you are doing a lot of video rendering or something that is similarly multithreaded, you do not need the 4770K and should spend that money elsewhere.

Make sure to keep your AMD (http://sites.amd.com/us/game/downloads/Pages/downloads.aspx) and Nvidia (http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us) drivers up to date.

Don't hesitate to post any feedback in the comments of that news post, and don't forget to visit the Computer Forum (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/290-Computer) for any extra questions! If you are interested in Folding@home, take a look in our team's thread (http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/948557-MMO-C-folding-team).
