Hotfixes
The effects of Greater Rune of Warding and Lesser Rune of Warding will now fade upon entering or leaving an instance, or moving between continents to temporarily resolve the issue of the effects becoming permanent.
Pets are no longer affected by shatter from Gruul.
Players with divine shield and similar immunities will now correctly receive credit for the quest "The Ashtongue Ruse."
The on-use abilities of the Commander's Badge, Captain's Badge, and Ancient Cornerstone Grimoire can no longer be used while in an Arena.
The effectiveness of Lady Jaina Proudmoore and Thrall in Caverns of Time: The Battle for Mount Hyjal has been greatly reduced.
