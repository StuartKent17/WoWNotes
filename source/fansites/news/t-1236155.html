Patch 5.1 BMAH Additions
Patch 5.1 added three new items to the BMAH. The first is a weapon with a fairly unique model, the Sun-Lute of the Phoenix King (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44924-sun-lute-of-the-phoenix-king)! The on use effect turns you in to Sig Nicious (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/23626-sig-nicious#related:screenshots).

It has a reported starting bid of 70k, the same as the Arcanite Ripper (http://www.wowdb.com/items/39769-arcanite-ripper). You can find a full list of the items on the BMAH here (http://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-source=128).


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/december/thumb/sunLute2.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/december/sunLute.jpg)

The other two items are old Proto-Drakes. The first is the Reins of the Plagued Proto-Drake (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44175-reins-of-the-plagued-proto-drake), which was a reward of Glory of the Raider (10 player) (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2137-glory-of-the-raider-10-player). The other is the Reins of the Rusted Proto-Drake (http://www.wowdb.com/items/45802-reins-of-the-rusted-proto-drake), a reward of Glory of the Ulduar Raider (10 player) (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2957-glory-of-the-ulduar-raider-10-player).

Computers - Setup of the Month (by chaud (http://www.mmo-champion.com/members/523085-chaud))
Each month or every 2 months, depending on the hardware evolutions, the lovely chaud (http://www.mmo-champion.com/members/523085-chaud) will work on a couple of hardware setups for those of you who are thinking of upgrading their computer!

This month
The current SSD prices (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/december/ssdgraph.gif) seem to have bottomed out for now, with almost all SSDs under $1 per gigabyte. A few sales have even gone under 50 cents per gigabyte, so look for something on sale! No new CPUs, GPUs, or SSDs worth waiting for are expected before late in the first or the second quarter next year.

Unless you are doing a lot of video rendering or something that is similarly multithreaded, you do not need the 3770K and should spend that money elsewhere.

Make sure to keep your AMD (http://sites.amd.com/us/game/downloads/Pages/downloads.aspx) and Nvidia (http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us) drivers up to date.
