No, all that happens is that when you log into a character any existing heirlooms, with upgrades, will be "unlocked" in the new tab. Unlike toys nothing will be consumed.

For instance: You have a Int Heirloom cloak(1-85) enchanted in your alt, in the new tab it will be unavaible to create more of it unless you log into that alt, which will identify your heirloom and unlock it with its upgrade in the tab, but the current enchanted item will remain in your inventory/bank for whenever you want to use or mail to another alt.
