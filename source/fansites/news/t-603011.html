Blue posts
Hunters
Hunter shot rotation issues are fixed in the next 2.3 PTR update, spells in /cast and /castsequence will no longer toggle. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=879058320&postId=27201197169&sid=1#266))



Interface
In 2.3 chat channels are rate limited on the client in the same way whispers are now. Each chat channel is limited separately, at a rate of 10 messages in 10 seconds. This means that you can send one message every second without ever seeing delays, or a burst of 10 messages every 10 seconds without seeing delays. Messages that exceed this rate are not dropped, but simply delayed in order until they can be sent. This does not affect SendAddonMessage() or party/raid/guild chat. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=879058320&postId=27199790927&sid=1#262))



Items
There is a possibility of adding more space to characters in the future, but there isn't any finalized decisions on this. (Source] (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2720553229&postId=27200291779&sid=1#19))
