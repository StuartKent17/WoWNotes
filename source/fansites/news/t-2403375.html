Blizzard has given us some beta keys to help you prepare for Battle for Azeroth! This week you will need to finish up exploring Broken Isles (https://beta.wowdb.com/achievements/11188-broken-isles-explorer) if you haven't already.


This giveaway is for 200 US/EU Battle for Azeroth Beta keys!
This giveaway runs from June 11 to June 17
To enter complete the Broken Isles Explorer (https://beta.wowdb.com/achievements/11188-broken-isles-explorer) achievement and then log in to the contest site.
You will need to log in with your Battle.Net account (https://bna.mmo-champion.com/login) (It's safe! (https://dev.battle.net/docs/read/log_in_with_blizzard)) so that we can confirm you have earned the achievement.
You can be sure that you are entered by checking the contest status page (https://bna.mmo-champion.com/contest/7).
Winners will be selected by June 19
You will need to check the contest status page (https://bna.mmo-champion.com/contest/7) to see if you have won and claim your key.
Winners will have 5 days to view their key. After that another winner will be selected.
