Pilgrim's Bounty
The Pilgrim’s Bounty (http://www.wowdb.com/world-events/404-pilgrims-bounty) is coming up from November 20 to November 27 and with it comes a time to reflect upon one’s good fortune and share feasts with everyone around you!


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/thumb/pilgrimbounty2.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/pilgrimbounty.jpg)


New in 2017
With the introduction of the Appearance tab in 7.0, the non-transmoggable Pilgrim set can be saved and transmogged during the duration of the holiday.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/thumb/pilgrim.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/pilgrim.jpg)


The Spirit of Sharing
The main area where the festivities take place is right outside the capital cities for the Horde and Alliance and in a couple of smaller towns.


Players can interact with the many foods options set on these tables and will gain a buff by eating or passing each of the servings.
There are 5 buffs in total that you can gain that when combined together will reward the The Spirit of Sharing (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/61849-the-spirit-of-sharing) buff that grants 10% increase in reputation gains for one hour. This is great if you are working on Legion reputations and completing World Quests or working on other reputations for a little boost.



http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/thumb/pilgrimhub.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/pilgrimhub.jpg)


Cooking
Pilgrim’s Bounty is centered around cooking and there are 5 recipes to add to your cooking collection. All you need to do is buy the Bountiful Cookbook (http://www.wowdb.com/items/46809-bountiful-cookbook) from Bountiful Barrel (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/35337-bountiful-barrel) located at each capital city table to learn them. The recipes are used for several quests, dailies, and the Now We’re Cookin’ (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/3577-now-were-cookin) achievement related to the holiday.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/thumb/pilgrimtable.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/pilgrimtable.jpg)

Cosmetic Items
5 daily quests can be found around the bountiful tables at capital cities that award a piece of a non-transmoggable Pilgrim’s set or a Pilgrim's Bounty (http://www.wowdb.com/items/116404-pilgrims-bounty) cache.


The cache can contain the Fine Pilgrim's Hat (http://www.wowdb.com/items/116401-fine-pilgrims-hat), the Frightened Bush Chicken (http://www.wowdb.com/items/116403-frightened-bush-chicken) battle pet and the Silver-Plated Turkey Shooter (http://www.wowdb.com/items/116400-silver-plated-turkey-shooter) toy, along with several consumable foods and turkey shooters.
The quests are centered around cooking so having at least a 280 skill level will help but if you do not have the skill level, you can buy the items needed from Bountiful Barrel (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/35337-bountiful-barrel) at each capital city table.

Achievements
There are 9 achievements for the Pilgrim’s Bounty holiday, 7 required for the meta achievement Pilgrim (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/3478-pilgrim) that will award you with the title the Pilgrim (http://www.wowdb.com/titles/168-the-pilgrim) along with the Plump Turkey (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/32818-plump-turkey) battle pet and 2 that are separate.

Do note that Pilgrim (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/3478-pilgrim) is not required to complete the What a Long, Strange Trip It’s Been (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2144-what-a-long-strange-trip-its-been) meta achievement for the Violet Proto-Drake (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44177-reins-of-the-violet-proto-drake).

Build of the Month - November 2017
Each month or so we will post a couple of hardware setups for those of you who are thinking of upgrading their computer!

All of the parts on this page are Amazon affiliate links. When you purchase your parts through them it helps support the site!


This month
New CPUs from Intel have been released but are proving challenging to actually find in stock. They will require a new motherboard as well. We will update our build list with the 8600K (http://amzn.to/2hGQIbj) and other Coffee Lake CPUs when stock improves. If you can find an 8600K (http://amzn.to/2hGQIbj) in stock, be sure to use a Z370 motherboard (http://amzn.to/2hDm6HR), as older motherboards are not compatible even though they have the same pin count.

The current SSD prices (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/september/ssdgraph.gif) are fairly flat right now, with most SSDs under $0.50 per gigabyte when on sale.

Depending on how much you want to overclock, you may need to spend a bit more for a better CPU cooler like the NH-D15 (http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00L7UZMAK?ie=UTF8&tag=mmochamp-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B00L7UZMAK).

Unless you are doing a lot of video rendering or streaming, you do not need the 7700K and would get more performance from spending that money elsewhere.

Make sure to keep your AMD (http://sites.amd.com/us/game/downloads/Pages/downloads.aspx) and Nvidia (http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us) drivers up to date.

Don't forget to visit the Computer Forum (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/290-Computer) for any extra questions!
