Patch 7.2 - Wyrmtongue's Cache
After completing the quests that send you to find treasure all over the Broken Shore, The Motherlode (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/46666-the-motherlode) will unlock. This leads you to a cave, with a vendor, keys, and chests, similar to the one on Timeless Isle.

A world quest involving the vendor (Treasure Master Iks'reeged (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/45379-treasure-master-iksreeged)) is sometimes available, allowing you to turn in 100 x Nethershard (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1226-nethershard) for a Wyrmtongue's Cache Key (http://www.wowdb.com/items/143559-wyrmtongues-cache-key). You can also purchase keys for 2000 x Nethershard (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1226-nethershard).

This key opens various chests around the cave. These chests can contain:


1 - 100000 Nethershard (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1226-nethershard)
300+ Order Resources (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1220-order-resources)
Artifact Power items
Scraps (http://www.wowdb.com/items/146953-scraps)
Pilfered Sweeper (http://www.wowdb.com/items/147867-pilfered-sweeper)
Fel Meteorite (http://www.wowdb.com/items/147869-fel-meteorite)
Strange Dimensional Shard (http://www.wowdb.com/items/147870-strange-dimensional-shard)

Patch 7.2 Hotfixes - April 12
Artifacts

The requirement "Complete 100 dungeons while using your Hidden Artifact appearance" to unlock Hidden Artifact appearance color variant 2 has been reduced from 100 to 30 dungeon completions.

Developers’ notes: The time and effort required for this Hidden Artifact unlock wasn’t consistent with the other two color unlocks. Additionally, now that Challenge Artifact appearances are in the game, requiring you to continue using your Hidden Artifact appearance (which can't be transmogged into the more prestigious Challenge appearance) for 100 dungeons was too much.



Bonus Event

Corrected an issue preventing some players from accepting The Arena Calls skirmish event quest.
