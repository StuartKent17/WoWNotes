Weekly Event - Wrath of the Lich King Timewalking
Wrath of the Lich King Timewalking (https://www.wowdb.com/world-events/562-timewalking-dungeon-event) is back this week. Don't forget to check out the Ulduar (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/4273-ulduar) timewalking raid!


The weekly quest (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/44166-a-frozen-path-through-time) from your adventure journal rewards azerite power and Cache of Palace Treasures (https://www.wowdb.com/items/170145-cache-of-palace-treasures), rewarding a piece of loot from normal Eternal Palace.
You can find Auzin (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/98690-auzin) in old Dalaran.
Don't forget to keep working towards Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk), Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger), Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade), Beastlord's Irontusk (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/167894-beastlords-irontusk), and Beastlord's Warwolf (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/167895-beastlords-warwolf)!
Reins of the Infinite Timereaver (https://www.wowdb.com/items/133543-reins-of-the-infinite-timereaver) can also drop from any Timewalking dungeon boss.
