Here is the deal with the boost -

Back in MoP, the level 90 boost was introduced to help players joining for the new expansion (WoD) would be able to jump right in to play the expac they just bought. Now that WoD is part of the basic game, this is replaced by the level 100 boost, so you can jump in right to playing Legion. There's no need for a level 90 boost any more, like there's no need for a level 80 one, it doesn't bring you to current (levelling) content any more.

If you buy the "brown box", you don't get a boost.
Accounts upgraded to WoD for free don't get a boost.
However if you purchased a WoD key recently, that item explicitly comes with a level 90 boost so they will honor that and give a boost to someone with a WoD key currently. That's for WoD keys, not the new brown box base game.
Also, if you do have a WoD key right now, you can't use it! Because WoD is free, no longer for sale, the keys no longer work. So you would need to talk to a GM to use it - which you can do here https://battle.net/support/en/case/
