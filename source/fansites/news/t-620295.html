Wrath of the Lich King - World Event
As I write this news, it looks like the pre-Wrath of the Lich King world event has been started on one of the US live servers, we're still unsure if it's just an error or if Blizzard is testing the event on a few servers. Below is a small preview of what to expect during this event .... You can probably expect more updates in the next hours if the event is extended to all realms.

Zombies will randomly spawn in the world (and near cities) and infect anyone they damage.
People infected by zombies can either get cured by other players or by Argent Healers deployed in most of the zones.
If a player or NPC isn't cured within 10 minutes ... He will turn into a zombie !
Players changed into zombies have access to a new set of skills (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/october/wotlkworldevent_7.jpg) to let them attack and infect other players. They become friendly with other zombies and hostile to all player factions.

Also ...
Don't waste your time finding and creating a level 1 on this server, being hostile with all player factions also means that guards will kill you on sight and pretty much instantly in the case of a level 1 character.
This is probably just the first part of the world event, it doesn't look fully deployed at all

You might want to get ready for some serious zombie invasion in the next days ....

The Keys to a successful life as a Ghoul.

1. Numbers numbers numbers #'s 1v1 against non-ghouls you are toast. Stay clear of Ret Pallies. They will rock you and your 3 ghoul friends world. 10+ is a very good number of ghoul buddies to have.
2. Wear as much spell power as possible. Attack power gives you negative attack power as a ghoul. Spell power gives you more attack power as a ghoul. (Unsure if this is a bug with phy attack power. :-[ )
3. Find some humanoid npcs near were you and your ghoul buddies can get reinfected in the event that you meet your ghoulish end.
4. You don't need to kill a player to turn them to a ghoul quickly. The more ghouls attacking the player the faster they can be converted. If you have 10 people that are ghouls all attacking the same person, that person will turn in just a few seconds.
5. Stay close to each other. Lurch affects all ghouls in a small area near the person that used lurch. With enough people you can affectively chain lurches to keep from being slowed or snared.
6. Causing paladins to bubble or mages to ice block is a good thing. As soon as their immunity wears off they instantly turn into a ghoul.
