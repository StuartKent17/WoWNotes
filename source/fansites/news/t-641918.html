Patch 3.1.0 - PTR Build 9757
A new build has been deployed on test realms, stay tuned for more changes.

Talent Calculator
The talent calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com) has been updated to the latest build.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/march/talentcalc9757.jpg (http://talent.mmo-champion.com)


Achievements Changes
The Ulduar immortal achievements had their description slightly changed ...
Champion of Ulduar (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2903) - Defeat each boss in Ulduar on Normal Difficulty without allowing any raid member to die to that boss at any point during that raid lockout period.
Conqueror of Ulduar (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2904) - Defeat each boss in Ulduar on Heroic Difficulty without allowing any raid member to die to that boss at any point during that raid lockout period.

A few more Ulduar achievements were changed as well.
Iron Dwarf, Medium Rare (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2923) - 2 Minutes time limit removed.
Heroic: Iron Dwarf, Medium Rare (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2924) - 2 Minutes time limit removed.
Minimal Casualties (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2920) has been removed.
Heroic: Minimal Casualties (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2922) has been removed.
Getting Back to Nature (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2982) - Defeat Freya while she is affected by 25 stacks of Attuned to Nature on Normal Difficulty.
He Feeds on Your Tears (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=3004) - Defeat Algalon the Observer on Normal Difficulty without any raid member dying to Algalon at any point during that raid lockout period.
Observed (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=3037) - Defeat Algalon the Observer on Heroic Difficulty without any raid member dying to Algalon at any point during that raid lockout period.
Supermassive (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=3003) - Now requires closing 3 Black Holes instead of 4.


Professions Changes
Alchemy
Flask of Stoneblood (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=53758) now increases the player's maximum health by 1300. (Up from 750)
Flask of Endless Rage (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?item=46377) - Now requires 3xGoldclover (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?item=36901) instead of 3xFire Leaf (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?item=39970)

Engineering
Gnomish Lightning Generator (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=55039) now deals 1530 to 1870 damage. (Down from 2610 to 3190)


Ulduar Boss Changes
Algalon - Boss Page (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=850)
Cosmic Smash (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=62311) now deals 35613 to 41387 damage. (Up from 32375 to 37625)
Black Hole Explosion (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=65108) now deals 15675 to 17325 Shadow damage. (Up from 14250 to 15750 damage)
Quantum Strike (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64395) - Strikes at an enemy, inflicting 15675 to 17325 Physical damage. (Up from 14250 to 15750 damage)
Phase Punch (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64412) - Massive blow that inflicts 8788 to 10212 damage and slowly fades the target to another plane of existence. (Up from 6938 to 8062 damage)
Big Bang (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64584) - Inflicts 107250 to 112750 Physical damage to nearby enemies. (Up from 97500 to 102500 damage)

General Vezax - Boss Page (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=849)
Shadow Crash (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=62659) now reduces mana costs by 70%. (Down from 75%)

Hodir - Boss Page (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=848)
Singed (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=65280) - Now inflicts 3000 Fire damage (up from 1500) and increases damage taken from magical spells and effects by 2%. (Up from 1%)

Iron Council - Boss Page (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=842)
Overwhelming Power (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=61888) range has been increased from 5 yards to 60 yards. It now causes a meltdown after 25 sec. (Down from 30 sec.)

Kologarn - Boss Page (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=843)
Overhead Smash (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63356) now has a 4 seconds cooldown.
Arm Sweep (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63766) is now instant. (Down from 0.75 sec. cast)


Glyph Changes
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid
Glyph of Survival Instincts (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=65244) - Your Survival Instincts ability grants an additional 15% of your maximum health.

Defeat each boss in Ulduar on Heroic Difficulty without allowing any raid member to die to that boss at any point during that raid lockout period.
