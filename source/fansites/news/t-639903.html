Mimiron
Health - 1,742M (25-Man) / 1,742M (10-Man)

Phase 1 - Leviathan MKII
As soon as you engage Mimiron, he jumps into the Leviathan MKII vehicle. (A personal version of The Flame Leviathan)
Health - 6.97M (25-Man) / ? (10-Man)
Proximity Mines - Drops about 10 mines next to the Leviathan, walking over them will make them explode for 20,000 fire damage. Avoiding them is part of the Heroic: Mind the Mines (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2990) achievement.
Napalm shell (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63666) - Deals 9,425 to 10,575 damage in a 5 yards radius, also deals 6000 Fire damage per second for 8 seconds to anyone staying in this zone. (2 Seconds casting time)
Plasma Blast (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64529) - Deals 30,000 Fire damage per second for 6 seconds. (3 Seconds casting time)
Shock Blast (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63631) - Deals 100,000 nature damage to targets within 15 yards. (5 Seconds casting time)
Rocket Strike (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63041) - Deals 1,000,000 damage in a 3 yards radius. You have a few seconds to avoid it. (3 Seconds casting time)


Phase 2 - VX-001 <Anti-personnel Assault Cannon>
After the destruction of Leviathan MKII, Mimiron jumps in his VX-001.
Health - 6.97M (25-Man) / ? (10-Man)
Rapid Burst (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64531) - Deals 3299 to 3701 damage to all the targets in a front of VX-001.
P3Wx2 Laser Barrage (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63293) - The VX-001 starts spinning and deals 20,000 to all targets in a cone in front of him. (Same mechanics as C'thun Eye Beam or Lurker Below Spout)
Rocket Strike (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63041) - Launch one of the rocket attached on the back of VX-001 on a target and deals 1,000,000 damage. You have a few seconds to avoid it.
Heat Wave (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63677) - Deals 9425-10575 fire damage and 3000 damage per second for 5 seconds to all the targets within 80 yards.


Phase 3 - Aerial Command Unit
Mimiron boards his Aerial Command Unit, he then flies over the raid and blasts player from here. Mimiron is untankable during this phase and will spawn adds regularly.
Health - 4.6M (25-Man) / 1.1M (10-Man)
Plasma Ball (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64535) - Ranged attack aimed at the target with the highest threat. Deals 14138-15862 Fire damage. (2 Seconds casting time)
Spawn Add - Assault Bot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/march/assaultbot.jpg) - Health: 1M (25-Man) / 500k (10-Man) - Uses Magnetic Field (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64668) that increase damage taken by 30% and immobilizes the target. These bots will drop a Magnetic Core (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/march/magneticcore.jpg) when they die. Deploying this magnet under the Aerial Command Unit will temporarily bring it to the ground and disable it. While it remains disabled, it will take an additional 50% damage from all sources.
Spawn Add - Bomb Bot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/march/bombbot.jpg) - These bots will walk through the raid for a moment and eventually explode for 9425 to 10575 fire damage in a 5 yards radius. Just avoid them, this is part of the Heroic: A-bomb-inable (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2992) achievement.
Spawn Add - Junk Bot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/march/junkbot.jpg) - Health: 100k (25-Man) / 60k (10-Man) - Very weak adds, just DPS them down.


Phase 4 - V0-L7R-0N
The Leviathan MKII, VX-001, and Aerial Command Unit previously defeated merge to create V0-L7R-0N, a huge robot made of all these vehicles. You have to DPS down each section of the robot individually to kill them at the same time.
Health - 50% of the total health for each section.
Leviathan MKII
Shock Blast (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63631) - Deals 100,000 nature damage to targets within 15 yards. (5 Seconds casting time)
Proximity Mines - Drops about 10 mines next to the Leviathan, walking over them will make them explode for 20,000 fire damage. Avoiding them is part of the Heroic: Mind the Mines (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2990) achievement.

VX-001
Hand Pulse (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64536) - Deals 7069 to 7931 damage in a 50000 yards radius.
P3Wx2 Laser Barrage (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63293) - The VX-001 starts spinning and deals 20,000 to all targets in a cone in front of him. (Same mechanics as C'thun Eye Beam or Lurker Below Spout)
Rocket Strike (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63041) - Launch one of the rocket attached on the back of VX-001 on a target and deals 1,000,000 damage. You have a few seconds to avoid it.

Aerial Command Unit
Plasma Ball (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64535) - Ranged attack aimed at the target with the highest threat. Deals 14138-15862 Fire damage. (2 Seconds casting time)
