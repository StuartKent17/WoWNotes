Allied Races - Void Elves
During the final push against the Legion on Argus, Alleria Windrunner (https://wow.gamepedia.com/Alleria_Windrunner) learned to harness the void without falling to madness by her mentor The Locus-Walker (https://wow.gamepedia.com/Locus-Walker). The Void has a reputation for corrupting those who use it, but Alleria has since managed to train a group of outcasted Blood Elves to resist it as well. She and her followers have joined her husband to fight for the Alliance.

Unlocking the Void Elves
To unlock the Void Elves Allied Race, the following achievements must be completed on one character on your account. Once done, you can begin a short questline to actually unlock the race. You will then be able to make a Void Elf on any server that you have a 110 character provided you have purchased Battle for Azeroth.


You Are Now Prepared! (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/12066-you-are-now-prepared) - Complete the Argus campaign
Exalted with The Argussian Reach (http://www.wowdb.com/factions/2170-argussian-reach)

Racials
The Void Elf racial abilities give you reduced shadow damage taken, ability empowerment, reduced void storage/transmog prices, no spell casting delays from damage and a teleport.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_ChillofNight.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_ChillofNight.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_EntropicEmbrace.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_EntropicEmbrace.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_EtherealConnection.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_EtherealConnection.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_PreturnaturalCalm.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_PreturnaturalCalm.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_SpatialRift.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_SpatialRift.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Achievement_AlliedRace_VoidElf.jpg


Chill of the Night (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255668-chill-of-night) - Reduces shadow damage taken by 1%.
Entropic Embrace (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255669-entropic-embrace) - Your abilities have a chance to empower you with the Essence of the Void (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/256374-entropic-embrace).
Ethereal Connection (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255667-ethereal-connection) - Reduces the cost of Void Storage and Transmogrification by 50%.
Preternatural Calm (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255670-preternatural-calm) - Your spell casts are not delayed by taking damage.
Spatial Rift (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/256948-spatial-rift) - Tear a rift in space. Reactivate this ability to teleport through the rift.



Mounts
The Void Elf racial mount is the Starcursed Voidstrider (http://www.wowdb.com/items/156486-starcursed-voidstrider). It is usable by all Alliance races upon completion of Allied Races: Void Elf (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/12242-allied-races-void-elf).


Those that follow the void tend to prefer the companionship of a steed without form or substance.
