Warlords of Draenor - Blackrock Foundry: The Blast Furnace

The Blackrock clan's unique ability to smelt the black ore, a substance so dense that ordinary flame does not even soften it, has always been their most closely-guarded secret. Deep within the Foundry, their furnace contains a primal force as old as Draenor itself, raging within the confines of its prison as it emanates a preternatural heat.


Overview - The objective of this encounter is to destroy the Foundry's massive furnace. Players must first disable the Blast Furnace's Heat Regulators, which will release the Heart of the Mountain, a blazing fire Fury that powers the furnace. Primal Elementalists then join the fight and channel beams to control the raging Fury. Once the Primal Elementalists have fallen, the Heart of the Mountain is unleashed and must be dealt with directly.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Damage Dealers - Furnace Engineers attach bombs to random targets. Detonate these bombs near a Heat Regulator to damage them.
Defeat Slag Elementals near Primal Elementalists to temporarily disrupt their protective shield.
Interrupt Firecallers' <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/155186-cauterize-wounds">Cauterize Wounds</a>.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/healer.png" title="Healer" /> Healers - Players in the vicinity of Foreman Feldspar suffer Fire damage as long as they are close to him.
Foreman Feldspar targets random players with his <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/156937-pyroclasm">Pyroclasm</a> ability.
Every time the Heart of the Mountain reaches 100 Energy, he will cast <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/155209-blast">Blast</a>.
Slag Elementals pick targets at random to fixate on and attack.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Tanks - Position Security Guards so they cannot protect nearby allies with their <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/160382-defense">Defense</a> ability.
Slag Elementals fixate on random players, and cannot be taunted.
Firecallers will heal their allies for a significant amount.
When facing the Heart of the Mountain, take turns tanking in order to manage your stacks of <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/155240-tempered">Tempered</a> and <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/155242-heat">Heat</a>.

Phase 1 - Sabotage - Players must disable the Heat Regulators located on each side of the Blast Furnace to disable it.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/deadly.png" title="Deadly" /> The Blast Furnace -

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/healer.png" title="Healer" /> Blast (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155209) - The Blast Furnace periodically unleashes a burst of heat upon the whole room. All players suffer 112,676 Fire damage.

Furnace Engineer -

Electrocution (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155201) - The caster fires a bolt of electricity at a random enemy that inflicts 33,802 Nature damage. The electricity will jump to up to 2 other targets within 5 yards of the initial target.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/interruptable.png" title="Interruptable" /> Repair (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155179) - The caster attempts to repair a nearby Heat Regulator for 3% of its maximum health.
Bomb (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155192) - The caster attaches bombs to random enemies. After 15 sec the bombs will detonate.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Drop Lit Bombs (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/174726) - The caster drops their sack of bombs. The force of dropping to the ground causes them all to arm; they will explode shortly after being picked up. If all the bombs are taken out of the sack, the sack will dissappear. Otherwise the sack will explode after 15 sec.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Bellows Operator -

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Loading (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155181) - Periodically increases the heat level of the Blast Furnace by 2.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Get in the Fight! - In Mythic Difficulty, the Bellows Operators will stop operating the bellows of the furnace and engage players in melee combat after reaching 50% health remaining.

Security Guard -

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Defense (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/160382) - The caster provides protection for their nearby allies, preventing nearly all damage.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Slay Elemental (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/176143) - The caster strikes the target, disabling any protection their elemental nature would normally provide and causing the target to take an additional 50% more damage for 20 sec.<br/>Only works on Slag Elementals.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/deadly.png" title="Deadly" /> Foreman Feldspar -

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/interruptable.png" title="Interruptable" /> Pyroclasm (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/156937) - The caster hurls a massive glob of pyroclastic sludge at a random target for 70,422 Fire damage.
Rupture (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/156934) - The ground beneath the target's feet rips open, revealing pyroclastic flows that inflict 56,338 Fire damage to all players in the vicinity every second.
Hot Blooded (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/158247) - Foreman Feldspar's very presence burns his enemies with Fire damage.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Melt Armor (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/175104) - The caster lashes forward with their molten gauntlets, inflicting 43,000 Fire damage and melting the victim's armor.<br/>Further instances of this attack received within 30 sec inflict 30% more damage.


Phase 2 - The Heart Exposed - The Heart of the Mountain escapes from the Blast Furnace! Primal Elementalists join the fight in an effort to keep the Heart of the Mountain from destroying everything.

The Blast Furnace -

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/healer.png" title="Healer" /> Blast (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155209) - The Blast Furnace periodically unleashes a burst of heat upon the whole room. All players suffer 112,676 Fire damage.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Slag Pool (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/163532) - The Slag Pool continues to heat up the Heart of the Mountain as long as it remains in the area.
Cooling Down (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/156880) - When the Heart of the Mountain exits the Blast Furnace it loses heat.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Primal Elementalist (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/117013) -

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Damage Shield (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155176) - The caster raises a shield of elemental forces, making them immune to all damage.

Shields Down (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/158345) - Primal Elementalists are vulnerable to damage when their Damage Shield has been removed.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/magic.png" title="Magic" /> Reactive Earth Shield (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155173) - The Reactive Earth Shield will heal the target after 8 sec.

Firecaller -

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/interruptable.png" title="Interruptable" /> Cauterize Wounds (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155186) - The caster seals the target's wounds with flames, cauterizing them and healing the target for 20% of their maximum health.<br/><br/>Players healed in this way are healed for 1/10th this value.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/interruptable.png" title="Interruptable" /> Lava Burst (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/159408) - The caster hurls a massive glob of lava at a random target for 77,466 Fire damage.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Volatile Fire (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/176121) - Afflicts random enemies with a seed of Volatile Fire that will explode after 8 sec. When the seed explodes, it inflicts 71,450 Fire damage to all enemies within 8 yards of the explosion.
Erupt (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/159115) - The caster causes lava underneath the target Slag Elemental to rise up out of the ground, heating them up. The Slag Elemental is energized to full.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Slag Elemental - Slag Elementals will go Dormant and remove the Damage Shield of any nearby Primal Elementalist when they are reduced to 1 hitpoint. While Dormant, the Fury's Blasts will restore 25 Energy. When the Slag Elemental reaches 100 Energy they will revive.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/interruptable.png" title="Interruptable" /> Burn (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155200) - The caster inflicts 42,254 Fire damage to their current target.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Fixate (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/12021) - Slag Elementals fixate on a random target, focusing their spells on the victim.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Slag Bomb (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/176133) - Upon reaching zero health remaining, the Slag Elemental explodes for 100,000 Fire damage to all enemies within 8 yards and goes Dormant.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Hardened Slag (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/176141) - Hardened Slag is impervious to nearly all damage.

Security Guard -

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Defense (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/160382) - The caster provides protection for their nearby allies, preventing nearly all damage.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Slay Elemental (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/176143) - The caster strikes the target, disabling any protection their elemental nature would normally provide and causing the target to take an additional 50% more damage for 20 sec.<br/>Only works on Slag Elementals.


Phase 3 - Freedom! - With all of the Primal Elementalists defeated, the Heart of the Mountain attacks everything in site.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/deadly.png" title="Deadly" /> Heart of the Mountain -

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/healer.png" title="Healer" /> Blast (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155209) - The Blast Furnace periodically unleashes a burst of heat upon the whole room. All players suffer 112,676 Fire damage.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Tempered (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155240) - Exposure to Heat from the Heart of the Mountain's attacks gradually increases damage taken from Heat. Tempered will only increase its efficacy when exposed to greater Heat levels than your current Tempered level and any time Heat is initially applied to you.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Heat (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155242) - Heats up the victim's arms and armor. This heat inflicts 28,169 Fire damage every second for each application and is increased by the victim's Tempered level.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Melt (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/155225) - The caster heats the floor under a random enemy. Players entering the affected area suffer 70,422 Fire damage every second.

CASC Patching
Ah, file bloat. In live, major patches tend to take the time to resort and scan the files to get rid of old and useless ones, which the beta doesn't.
There's some truth to this. For example, the user who simply patches from 6.0 to 6.1 (on 6.1 patch day) would have a different amount of data than the user who participated in the 6.1 PTR and received a dozen or more builds.

CASC is still a relatively new system, and the battle.net team will continue to work on it past WoD launch. I know file cleanup is something on their plate, but I don't have an ETA on that kind of tech.
