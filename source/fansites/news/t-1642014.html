Patch 6.0.3 Hotfixes: November 25
Update 1, Class Tuning Changes: 12:00pm PST
Please note that some of the class tuning changes listed below are not yet live and are still in the process of being implemented.

Racials

Orc

Hardiness now reduces stun duration by 15% (up from 10%).

Undead

Will of the Forsaken has a 2-minute cooldown once more (down from 3 minutes).


Classes

General

Blood Plague (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/59879), when used in PvP combat now has a duration of 24 seconds and may no longer have its periodic duration extended past 30 seconds.
Frost Fever (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/55095), when used in PvP combat now has a duration of 24 seconds and may no longer have its periodic duration extended past 30 seconds.


Talents

Necrotic Plague, when used in PvP combat now has a duration of 24 seconds and may no longer have its periodic duration extended past 30 seconds.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/270-Druid) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/druid) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#s))

Balance

Moonfire (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/8921), when used in PvP combat now has a duration of 24 seconds and may no longer have its periodic duration extended past 30 seconds.
Sunfire (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/93402), when used in PvP combat may no longer have its periodic duration extended past 30 seconds.


Feral

Predatory Swiftness (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/16974) no longer increases the healing done by Healing Touch (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/5185).


Talents

Heart of the Wild (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/108288) bonuses are now halved when used while in PvP combat.
Pulverize (Guardian) now extends up to 130% of its normal duration when refreshed, similar to periodic effects.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/images.gif Mage (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/272-Mage) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/mage) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#g))

Arcane

Arcane Blast (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/30451) damage has been increased by 10%.


Fire

Pyroblast (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/11366) damage has been increased by 35%.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/imonks.gif Monk (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/341-Monk) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/monk) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#o))

General

Rising Sun Kick (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/107428) (Windwalker, Mistweaver) now causes enemies to take 15% increased damage (down from 20%).
Tiger Palm (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/100787) (Brewmaster, Windwalker) now extends up to 130% of its normal duration when refreshed, similar to periodic effects.


Brewmaster

Guard (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/115295) damage absorption shield now absorbs 100% more damage.
Stance of the Sturdy Ox (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/115069) now defers 30% of damage taken to be divided over 10 seconds (up from 20%).


Glyphs

Glyph of Keg Smash (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/121253) should now correctly increase the range of Keg Smash (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/121253).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/273-Paladin) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/paladin) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#I))

Protection

Mastery: Divine Bulwark (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/76671) now increases Shield of the Righteous (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/53600)' damage reduction by 0.5% per point, down from 0.75% per point.
Grand Crusader (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/85043) now also has a 30% chance to trigger its effect from Crusader Strike (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/35395) and Hammer of the Righteous (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/115801).
Shield of the Righteous (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/53600) now reduces physical damage taken by 25% (up from 20%).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/276-Shaman) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/shaman) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#c))

General

Chain Lightning (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/421) damage has been increased by 20%.
Lava Burst (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/51505) (Elemental, Restoration) damage has been increased by 35%.


Enhancement

Affliction

Agony (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/131737) damage has been increased by 20%.

Demonology

Shadow Bolt (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/686) damage has been increased by 25%.
Soul Fire (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/6353) damage has been increased by 25%.
Touch of Chaos (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/112089)' damage has been increased by 25%.


Destruction

Chaos Bolt (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/116858) damage has been increased by 8%.
Conflagrate (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/17962) damage has been increased by 8%.
Immolate (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/348) damage has been increased by 8%.

Talents
