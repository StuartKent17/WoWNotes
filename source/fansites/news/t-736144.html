Engineering in Cataclysm - Enchants, Scopes, and Gems
The Engineering page released on NDA lift day lacked content because spells aren't flagged as "Engineering" yet, however it looks like a lot of stuff is already here and there is enough content to give you a small preview of Engineering in Cataclysm.

The items with the biggest omg-epic factor are obviously the helms that I already posted 2 weeks ago.

Reinforced Bio-Optic Killshades (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/81714/reinforced-bio-optic-killshades/) - Plate - Melee.DPS
Specialized Bio-Optic Killshades (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/81715/specialized-bio-optic-killshades/) - Plate - Spell
Deadly Bio-Optic Killshades (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/81716/deadly-bio-optic-killshades/) - Mail - Phys.DPS
Energized Bio-Optic Killshades (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/81720/energized-bio-optic-killshades/) - Mail - Spell
Agile Bio-Optic Killshades (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/81722/agile-bio-optic-killshades/) - Leather - Phys.DPS
Camoflauge Bio-Optic Killshades (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/81724/camoflauge-bio-optic-killshades/) - Leather - Spell
Lightweight Bio-Optic Killshades (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/81725/lightweight-bio-optic-killshades/) - Cloth - Spell


New engineering-only enchants are available:

Gnome-Matic Phase Fingers (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/82175/gnome-matic-phase-fingers/) - Permanently attaches gnome-matic phase finger technology to a pair of gloves, allowing a skilled engineer to increase their haste rating by 340 for 12 sec. The gloves can only be activated every minute.
Quickflip Deflection Plates (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/82177/quickflip-deflection-plates/) - Permanently attaches quickflip deflection plates onto gloves, allowing you to increase your armor by 1500 for 12 sec. Gloves can only be activated once every minute. Attaching the armor webbing causes the gloves to become soulbound. An Engineering skill of 500 is required to keep the webbing active.
Tazik Shocker (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/82180/tazik-shocker/) - Permanently attaches a tazik shocker to your gloves, allowing a skilled engineer to deal 1654 to 2020 Nature damage to an enemy at long range. The shocker can only be used once every 45 sec. Can only be used on the engineer's gloves, and doing so will cause them to become soulbound.
Spinal Healing Injector (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/82200/spinal-healing-injector/) - Permanently attaches a specialized injector kit to your belt, allowing you to inject a Mythical Healing Potion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/57191/mythical-healing-potion/) directly into your bloodstream for increased effect. Only a skilled engineer can activate the modified belt. Can only be attached to your own belt, and doing so causes the belt to become soulbound.
Z50 Mana Gulper (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/82201/z50-mana-gulper/) - Permanently attaches a specialized injector kit to your belt, allowing you to power-drink a Mythical Mana Potion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/57192/mythical-mana-potion/) for increased effect. Only a skilled engineer can activate the modified belt. Can only be attached to your own belt, and doing so causes the belt to become soulbound.


Scopes are also here:

Gnomish X-Ray Scope (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/81932/gnomish-x-ray-scope/) - Attaches a permanent scope to a bow or gun that increases its ranged critical strike rating by 88. Attaching this scope to a ranged weapon causes it to become soulbound.
R19 Threatfinder (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/81933/r19-threatfinder/) - Attaches a permanent scope to a bow or gun that increases its ranged hit rating by 88. Attaching this scope to a ranged weapon causes it to become soulbound.
Safety Catch Removal (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/81934/safety-catch-removal-kit/) - Removes the safety mechanism from a bow or gun, increasing its ranged haste rating by 88. Using this kit causes the bow or gun to become soulbound.


Last but not least, a couple of very interesting items were datamined on Cataclysm Beta servers. It looks like engineers will get a new type of items, very similar to gems.

2010-07-12, 01:55 PM
Pretty much everything here is either a placeholder or a renamed spell that already exists, so people really need to chill out until we get more accurate information.

The most interesting thing is, of course, the "gems". The speculation our guild has concluded upon is that these "gems" will simply go into special engineering-only sockets which we will either be able to add to off-suit items (trinkets, rings), or will be found on engineering-only equipment like the goggles.

By doing this, Blizzard no longer has to create multiple versions of each piece of engineering gear, nor do they have to create a brand new tier of these items as each new raiding tier is released.
