Outlaw Rogue Hidden Artifact Appearance
ixzo (http://www.mmo-champion.com/members/749667-ixzo) was able to unlock (http://imgur.com/a/hITPZ) the Outlaw Rogue hidden artifact appearance! There are still some unclear details, but the process appears to be:


Reach Artifact Knowledge level 5.
Obtain the left binding from Dargrul (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/91007-dargrul) in any difficulty of Neltharion's Lair.
Obtain the right binding from Ash'Golm (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/95886-ashgolm) in any difficulty of Vault of the Wardens.
After obtaining either binding, you can start Bindings of the Windlord (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/43558-bindings-of-the-windlord) at Thrymjaris (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/97061-thrymjaris).
Obtain 10 x Infernal Brimstone (http://www.wowdb.com/items/124444-infernal-brimstone) and complete the quest.
The bindings are not a guaranteed drop, so farming may be required.
More information can be found in the forum thread (http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2070940-Thunderfury-Hunt-READ-THE-OP-WE-FOUND-IT!/page57).
