Patch 7.3 - New Mounts
There are a lot of new Mounts coming in Patch 7.3!

Reputation
There are 2 new factions coming in Patch 7.3, Argussian Reach (http://ptr.wowdb.com/factions/2170-argussian-reach) and Army of the Light (http://ptr.wowdb.com/factions/2165-army-of-the-light).

Argussian Reach (http://ptr.wowdb.com/factions/2170-argussian-reach) - New Faction in Argus that is unlocked by following the main story line. You can earn reputation with them by completing normal quests, world quests, emissary quests, and invasion quests. They have 6 mounts available at Exalted that are sold by Toraan the Revered (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/127151-toraan-the-revered) for 10,000g each.


Amethyst Ruinstrider (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/253004-amethyst-ruinstrider)

Cerulean Ruinstrider (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/253007-cerulean-ruinstrider)
Russet Ruinstrider (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/253006-russet-ruinstrider)
Sable Ruinstrider (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/242305-sable-ruinstrider)

Raids
There are 4 mounts that are associated with the new raid Antorus, The Burning Throne (http://ptr.wowdb.com/zones/8638-antorus-the-burning-throne). These are the Antoran Charhound (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/253088-antoran-charhound), Antoran Gloomhound (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/253087-antoran-gloomhound), Violet Spellwing (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/253639-violet-spellwing) and the Shackled Ur’zul (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/243651-shackled-urzul).


Antoran Charhound (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/253088-antoran-charhound) - Rare drop from Hounds of Sargeras Boss Fight.
Antoran Gloomhound (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/253087-antoran-gloomhound) - Complete the achievement Glory of the Argus Raider (http://ptr.wowdb.com/achievements/11987-glory-of-the-argus-raider).
Violet Spellwing (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/253639-violet-spellwing) - Complete the quest Blood of the Titan (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/48954-blood-of-a-titan) by looting Blood of the Unmaker (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/152900-blood-of-the-unmaker) from Argus the Unmaker (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/124828-argus-the-unmaker) on Heroic difficulty or higher.
Shackled Ur’zul (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/243651-shackled-urzul) - Drops from Argus the Unmaker (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/124828-argus-the-unmaker) on Mythic difficulty.

PvP
The Vicious War Fox (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/242897-vicious-war-fox) is being added to the Vicious Saddle (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/103533-vicious-saddle) mount vendor for both Horde and Alliance. You can find the vendors in Orgrimmar (http://ptr.wowdb.com/zones/1637-orgrimmar) from Deathguard Netharian (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/73151-deathguard-netharian) for Horde players and Stormwind City (http://ptr.wowdb.com/zones/1519-stormwind-city) from Necrolord Sipe (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/73190-necrolord-sipe) for Alliance players.

The Fierce Gladiator’s Storm Dragon (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/227994-fierce-gladiators-storm-dragon) will be the Gladiator mount for Legion Season 5.

Miscellaneous
How to acquire these mounts is currently unknown, but we do know they're coming in Patch 7.3.

Fel-Spotted Egg (Mana Rays)
Similar to the Mysterious Egg (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/39878-mysterious-egg) and Primal Egg (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/94295-primal-egg), the Fel-Spotted Egg (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/153190-fel-spotted-egg) can hatch into one of the 6 new Mana Ray mounts in 5 days upon acquiring the egg. The egg can drop from Varga (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/126208-varga), Sabuul (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/126898-sabuul), Kaara the Pale (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/126860-kaara-the-pale), and Naroua (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/126419-naroua).
