Aeus gave more informations about EU Battlegroups qualifiers on the official forums.

We’ve seen some uncertainties about how the top 38 teams from Europe will be determined, and wanted to clarify that each Battlegroup will have a number of teams qualifying based on the size of the Battlegroup.

There will be a minimum of one team qualifying from each Battlegroup, so even the smaller Battlegroups will be represented in the second qualifying round.

More details on the qualifying rules will be posted soon.
