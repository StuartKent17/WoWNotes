Addon Spotlight - QuestMyMap
I had to level a few more alts lately and I was more than happy to find QuestMyMap (http://www.wowinterface.com/downloads/info16518-QuestMyMap.html). Also, Addon Spotlights are great because I can just copy/paste the description from the addon author!

Download
QuestMyMap on WoWInterface (http://www.wowinterface.com/downloads/info16518-QuestMyMap.html)

QuestMyMap is a small addon without configuration that aims to provide a nicer quest map and some fixes.

Features:
Resizes the quest map to full scale
Drag border of the quest list to resize it (or shift-click / rightclick to toggle)
Quest levels on quest titles
Hides quest log and quest rewards on world map
Moves quest selection frame to a transparent overlay
Paste quest links from the world map with Shift-Click
Rightclicking a selected quest opens the quest log
Difficulty coloring for tooltips (make sure you activate the Interface option "Quest Difficulty Color")

I've registered on here, because I feel the need to point something out.

The ten man loot is close to useless, because the majority of items can be attained through Emblems of Frost, and at which rate the EoF items are better than the drops on Halion.

Drape of the Violet Tower (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/50468/drape-of-the-violet-tower/) effectively is better than Abduction's Cover (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/53115/abductions-cover/), the new cape from the ten man instance. However, if you prefer haste, Abduction might be a very small upgrade, but not enough to get excited about.
Belt of Omission (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/50996/belt-of-omission/) COMPLETELY outdoes Misbegotten belt (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/53118/misbegotten-belt/) - Upgrade in every possible stat except for one crit point, and attainable from Frost Emblems too.
Gloves of the Great Horned Owl (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/50981/gloves-of-the-great-horned-owl/) more or less outdo Changeling Gloves (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/53117/changeling-gloves/), despite the gloves having one more socket slot.
Shadow Seeker's Tunic (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/50972/shadow-seekers-tunic/) outdoes Gloaming Sark (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/53114/gloaming-sark/) by far.

What can we conclude by this?

Out of six loot items, two are not replaced by emblems, all other items can effectively be replaced just by gathering frost emblems.

Not only that, but the only two loot items that do matter, are both for Melee DPS, meaning that there is no incentive for healers, ranged DPS, and agility classes(tank offspecs can use these as last resort) to even head down to the Ruby Sanctum on 10m mode.
