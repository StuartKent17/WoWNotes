Issilien dismisses Mograine's theory and tries to destroy the artifact with a blast of holy energy, but it instead absorbs the attack and becomes purified by the Light.

From reading the flavortext of the quest, it sounds like the Silver Hand had wrested another such artifact from the Scourge deep in the heart of Naxxramas and purified it for the sake of recreating the blade (Inquisitor Fairbanks confirms the original cannot be cleansed as its corruption is too deep). It also sounds like the Vykrul have retaken the artifact for the Lich King and is perhaps on a timer because they intend on re-tainting the artifact.

The original Ashbringer was imbued with a part of Highlord Mograine's own soul. When he was betrayed and murdered by his own son, his soul became twisted with anger and vengeance, thus wholly corrupting the blade itself. This is also why the wielder of a Corrupted Ashbringer will also occasionally hear a whispering voice emanating from it while it's equipped.
