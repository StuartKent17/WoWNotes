Whilst I do agree that completeing all achievements does take time and effort, I don't really think you have to spend an insane amount of hours per day. The guild he's in also has the goal to do all dungeon&raid achievements. Their guild leader is Zoltan, the first person ever to reach 10k acheivement points.
