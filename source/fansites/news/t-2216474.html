Armory Updated
An updated version of the Armory went live today! It includes most of the features on the old character profile pages and features an updated design.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/may/simca.jpg (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-us/character/malfurion/simca)


Potential New Book - Troll Wars
A listing appeared on the French version of Amazon (https://www.amazon.fr/World-Warcraft-Trollkriege-Paul-Kemp/dp/3833235373/) for "World of Warcraft: Die Trollkriege (https://www.amazon.fr/World-Warcraft-Trollkriege-Paul-Kemp/dp/3833235373/)" by Paul Kemp. Keep in mind that this is not an official announcement.

Following the legendary War of the Ancients, a group of Night Elven magi were banished from their homeland. They looked for new land to settle on the coast of Lordaeron. At this time, the area was already settled by several loosely-allied weak tribes, while the Troll race had an advanced civilization. The arrival of the foreign Magi and their alliance with the Human race lay the foundation to one of the most serious conflicts Azeroth had ever witnessed, a conflict that would be known throughout history as the Troll Wars (http://wow.gamepedia.com/Troll_Wars).

Weekly Bonus Event - Legion Dungeons
The Legion Dungeon Event is live this week!


You will get one extra piece of loot from the final dungeon boss on difficulties below Mythic+.
Pick up Emissary of War (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/44171-emissary-of-war) from Archmage Timear (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/111246-archmage-timear) in Dalaran to earn Cache of Nightborne Treasures (http://www.wowdb.com/items/140152-cache-of-nightborne-treasures)
This quest requires you to clear 4 Mythic(+) dungeons and rewards Heroic Nighthold loot.


All week, open Group Finder (default hotkey: i) and then select Dungeon Finder to queue for any Legion dungeon on Normal or Heroic difficulty. Alternately, get a group of veteran dungeoneers together and travel to one of the Legion dungeons on Mythic difficulty:


The Arcway
Assault on Violet Hold
Black Rook Hold
Court of Stars
Darkheart Thicket
Eye of Azshara
Karazhan
Halls of Valor
Maw of Souls
Neltharion’s Lair
Vault of the Wardens


Look for the following all week long:


Archmage Timear (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/111246-archmage-timear) near Violet Hold in Dalaran has a quest (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/44171-emissary-of-war) for you. Or, if you forget to pick it up there, you can start it from within the Adventure Journal.

Quest requirement: Complete 4 Legion dungeons on Mythic difficulty.
Rewards: One loot box containing a piece of gear from Heroic difficulty Nighthold.

Passive bonus: The final boss in each dungeon drop additional loot on all difficulties (except Mythic Keystone difficulties).
