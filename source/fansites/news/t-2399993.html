This Week in WoW History
We're taking a look back at old MMO-Champion posts and World of Warcraft news that occurred during this time in previous years.

Keyring Slot
The Keyring bag slot is a relic of the past, that most people probably don’t even remember. In a time when 18 slot bags were the biggest out there, and many unused items taking up bag space, dedicated keyring bag slots were a nice quality of life feature. In patch 4.2 a new feature was added which removed key requirement for most gates in the game, thus eliminating the need for a dedicated key space.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/may/thumb/stratholme.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/2313-Patch-4-2-We-Bid-Thy-Keyring-Adieu)

Grand Expedition Yak
Wrath of the Lich King had the Traveler’s Tundra Mammoth, and then Mists of Pandaria got the Grand Expedition Yak. Some may not remember, the initial version of this mount cost 60,000 gold before any discount, and it held two vendors. One of these was the usual Traveling Trader, and the other was an Arcane Reforger. After the Reforge system was removed from the game, that vendor was replaced with a Transmogrifier leading to this mount becoming the famous Transmog Yak.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/may/thumb/grandyak.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/2788-Grand-Expedition-Yak-Mount-Previews-Blue-Posts-Arena-Tournament-Finals-Fan-Art)

Cosmetic Class Accessories
Class Accessories such as quivers for Hunters, vials of poison for Rogues, and tomes for Paladins were something that the community has been asking for. In the days of the Cataclysm, and previous expansions, some classes had a Relic slot next to their weapon, but these were never a visual addition to any spec. In 2014 an idea was presented, which would add further customization to characters, and allow cosmetic changes to these Relics without actually affecting the power level of the player.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/may/thumb/artaccess.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4008-Artcraft-Cosmetic-Class-Accessories)

Overwatch Short: Hero
This short takes place on Dorado, where Alejandra is sent to the store to pick up some flour. On the way, she runs into members of the Los Muertos, who are beating up an Omnic. After this gang steals her money, a mysterious soldier equipped with a Heavy Pulse Rifle and a Tactical Visor takes them on in a fight.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/may/thumb/hero.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/5713-Armory-Stats-Proving-Grounds-Robert-Kazinsky-Interview-Tweets-Warcraft-Movie)

Tomb of Sargeras Preview
The third raid of Legion was featured in the usual Raid Preview blue post in 2017. After fighting through the Broken Shore, players reach the Tomb of Sargeras, the final point of invasion of the Legion. Deep in this temple the Avatar of Sargeras was locked away by Aegwynn’s ward, however after Gul’dan successfully broke it, the Avatar awakens. After defeating the Avatar, players got a chance to fight the demon lord himself, Kil’jaeden.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/may/thumb/tombboss.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/6646-Glowcap-Festival-Tomb-of-Sargeras-Preview-Tweets-Overwatch-Offense-Hero-Secrets)
