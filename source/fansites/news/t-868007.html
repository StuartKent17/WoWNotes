Patch 4.1 Updated TBC Maps
Patch 4.1 will add the last wave of missing dungeon maps to the game by updating all the TBC content! Most TBC 5-man dungeons and raid instances got a shiny new world map.

The latest PTR build also included the maps of Zul'Aman and Zul'Gurub and the updated map for Baradin Hold with a brand new wing/boss! Keep in mind that the next arena season is scheduled for Patch 4.2, you won't see that part of Baradin Hold before a few months.

I need to do some research. I don't know what the Blood Shield + Unholy Presence trick is about. When I solo old content (pre-Wrath raids or Wrath dungeons) I do it in Blood presence. Apparently I am doing it wrong.

Easyly explained.

When you use Blood Presence, your Death Strikes heals for 15% of incoming damage for last 5s or 7% of max health. With a little more armor and life from Blood presence.
In Unholy presence, you get faster Rune Regen, faster GCD, run speed, and, using the Glyph of Dark Succor, DS that heal for 15% max health or more, wich is a MAJOR boost to survivability AND dps all around.

Specially on content where you won't have often 15% of the incoming damage for 5s > 15% max health because it would mean taking your whole life pool in damage over 5s to get the same amount of heal, and since the Blood Shield amount depends on the amount healed, that glyph makes it insanely big. :D

It's the same reason that makes Blood DK quite painful in pvp when you don't have peeps to cc/burn them fast, they can heal a lot in a small timeframe.
(btw the nerf is mostly there for pvp and bg in particular, not old content, but it will affect it too).

At the moment, someone running non-trivial old content in Blood Spec/Blood Presence and without the Dark Succor glyph is doing it dead wrong honestly, it's too good to pass (hell, nearly twice as much hps from DS, and the faster rune and gcd regen on top of it, meaning more DS and damage/shield :p).
