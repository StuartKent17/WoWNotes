Weekly Bonus Event - Arena Skirmishes
Sharpen your swords and reinforce your boards, because you’re called to the PvP Arena for the hectic mayhem of Skirmishes.

This Week: Arena Skirmishes
All week, from anywhere in Azeroth, open the Group Finder (default hotkey: i), select the Player vs. Player tab on the bottom of the window, and then under Arena Battles, choose either 2v2 or 3v3 and JOIN BATTLE. You’ll be matched up with teammates and provided with opponents to kill in the Arena.

Look for the following all week long:


Archmage Timear (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/111246-archmage-timear) near Violet Hold in Dalaran has a quest (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/44172-the-arena-calls) for you.

Quest requirement: Win 10 Arena PvP Skirmishes
Reward: A PvP loot box, AP, Honor

Passive buff: +50% honor gains from Skirmishes


If you’ve been looking to make some Honor gains, this is the week to do it!


Brawl - Tarren Mill vs. Southshore
June 13–20 - Originally introduced as a part of the World of Warcraft 10-Year Anniversary event, this Brawl harkens back to the early days of WoW PvP, with an epic battle between the two towns of Tarren Mill and Southshore. In this game of war, you’ll need to work as a team and deplete your foes’ resources to claim victory.

We've identified an issue with the brawl's matchmaking that is resulting in most of the comments I see here. We currently have a hotfix in testing to resolve this issue, so hang tight!

Let's see what the Broken Shore campaign has to offer:

Week 1: Do the Broken shore scenario; unlock all fly points
Week 2: Do a Legion Assault
Week 3: Recruit a 6th champion
Week 4: Turn in the shards you already had in your inventory
Week 5: Do some Sentinax farming
Week 6: Do the world quests you already did to get flying 3 weeks ago
Week 7: Wait your turn on the Felsoul Hold cannons so you can shoot 100 bats from the sky since everyone told you it was the fastest way
Week 8: Compete against every player to find 10 chests before they depop by logging in early morning when few people are playing
Week 9: Wait until you have 3 elites spawned on the shore to kill
Week 10: Click in your class hall until you complete the mission (or use Classhall Commander to autobuild your team)

I've highlighted the "interesting" things in the campaign. Interesting in that they involve doing things you potentially didn't do on your own at some time. The 3 elites is slightly more interesting than the rest, but you've probably killed at least once by happenstance.

So the big problem is that most of the interesting stuff was frontloaded. You did the interesting bits early in the campaign, and the easy/not very engaging come later.
