Armory Stats - Challenge Modes
Today we are taking a look at the Challenge Mode achievement completion rate of players. The data used today is a sample of 6.1 million characters from 3.1 million accounts. We also did this for the end of Mists of Pandaria, which you can see here (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4584-Armory-Stats-MoP-Challenge-Modes-Blue-Tweets-Setup-of-the-Month-DLC-469).

The first chart looks at the overall percentage of players that have each challenge mode achievement. The second chart defines a participating player as one with the completion achievement for that challenge mode.

You can click the titles in the legend to turn off and on different data types.

Warlords of Draenor introduced challenge mode weapons for transmog, rather than the armor sets offered during Mists of Pandaria (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/932-Challenge-Mode-Armor-Sets). The challenge modes also offer gear (www.wowdb.com/items/119032-challengers-strongbox#related:contains-item;_!) (item level 660 (http://www.wowdb.com/items/119010-doomchain-collar?bonusIDs=237,591) as of Patch 6.1.) from the daily quests (www.wowdb.com/items/119032-challengers-strongbox#related:reward-of-quest;_!), unlike the Mists of Pandaria quests.


Challenge Warlord (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/8895-challenge-warlord) - 1.23% of players
Challenge Warlord: Bronze (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/8897-challenge-warlord-bronze) - 1.02% of players / 83.15% of participating players
Challenge Warlord: Silver (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/8898-challenge-warlord-silver) - 0.86% of players / 70.16% of participating players
Challenge Warlord: Gold (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/8899-challenge-warlord-gold) - 0.63% of players / 51.32% of participating players
