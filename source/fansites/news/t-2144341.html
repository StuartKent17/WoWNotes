Enchanted Fey Dragon Mount Giveaway
We are starting out the new year by giving away some mounts!


The giveaway is for 10 x Enchanted Fey Dragon (http://www.wowdb.com/items/97989-enchanted-fey-dragon)
To enter the giveaway, just reply to this post! Make sure to mention if you play on US or EU servers in your reply.
The giveaway will be closed on Sunday, January 8th at 11:59 PM EST and winners will be chosen randomly within five business days.
Winners will be contacted by PM on the site. There is no email alert by default, you will need to log in and check your PMs (http://www.mmo-champion.com/private.php?folderid=0).
A note will be added to the top of the current news post when winners have been PMed.
Winners have 72 hours to respond before another winner will be selected to replace them.
This giveaway is open to residents of the US, Canada (except Quebec), Australia, Austria, Belgium, China, France, Germany, Hong Kong, Japan, the Netherlands, Singapore, Spain, and the United Kingdom.
See the official rules (http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2144348-Giveaway-Rules?p=44003093&viewfull=1#post44003093) for more details.
