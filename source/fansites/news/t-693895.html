Strategy

The fight might be confusing the first time but isn't that hard (to give you an idea, I could heal it without too much problem with a rare-geared shaman alt that I rarely play).

The Standard Apothecary Serving Kit (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49631/standard-apothecary-serving-kit/) contains two potions :

Cologne Neutralizer (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49352/cologne-neutralizer/) makes you immune to Apothecary Baxter's attacks / green clouds.
Perfume Neutralizer (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49351/perfume-neutralizer/) makes you immune to Apothecary Hummel's attacks / purple clouds.

The radius of the
Alluring Perfume (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/68641/alluring-perfume/) and
Irresistible Cologne (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/68946/irresistible-cologne/) is fairly small, the player tanking each Baxter uses the Cologne Neutralizer (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49352/cologne-neutralizer/) and the one tanking Hummel uses the Perfume Neutralizer (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49351/perfume-neutralizer/), making them pretty much immune to most of the damage they deal.

Apothecary Frye should be tanked by your main tank, he doesn't have any auras but will throw vials of perfume and cologne on the group. The easy way to handle it for DPS / Healers is just to pick an immunity at the beginning of the fight and just stay in the clouds they're immune to, if you feel like keybinding your potions you can just change your buff everytime the cloud color changes.

Make sure the apothecaries are tanked back to the group, their
Alluring Perfume Spray (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/68607/alluring-perfume-spray/) and
Irresistible Cologne Spray (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/68948/irresistible-cologne-spray/) will hurt your group pretty badly if they are facing the raid.

Crazy Apothecaries will also spawn throughout the encounter, they do not hurt a lot but will eventually suicide with
Unstable Reaction (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/68957/unstable-reaction/) and deal some damage, they are a minor annoyance and most of the time you will probably just ignore them.

As long as you understood the tanking basics and if your group can avoid clouds or switch their immunities on time, the fight is pretty straightforward and it won't take long to kill all 3 bosses.
