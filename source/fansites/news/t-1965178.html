Forum Update - Quote Notifications and Mentions
We recently added a feature that has been requested for a long time, notifications when you are quoted on the forum! You can always use the Suggestions and Feedback forum (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/253-Suggestions-amp-Feedback) to provide feedback or suggest features.


You can mention another user by including "@username" in your post.
When you are quoted or mentioned, a notification will appear at the top right.
Clicking those notifications will take you to your profile page, where there is a list of notifications and mentions.
You can restrict who you get alerts from in the settings, as well as who can view your list of notifications and mentions.
Using the mentions feature to bother other users or summon moderators is against the forum rules (http://www.mmo-champion.com/faq.php). Report a post if it needs moderator attention.
