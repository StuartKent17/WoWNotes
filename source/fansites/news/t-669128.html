Faction Change Service Now Available in Europe
The new World of Warcraft Faction Change service is now live in Europe. As we mentioned previously, http://forums.wow-europe.com/thread.html?topicId=9895129741&sid=1 (http://forums.wow-europe.com/thread.html?topicId=9895129741&sid=1) we’ve been in the process of developing a new service for World of Warcraft that will allow players to change their faction from Alliance to Horde or Horde to Alliance for a fee. After purchasing a Faction Change for a character, a player can choose any race of the opposite faction that is compatible with the character’s class. Please note that all achievements and quest lines relating to the Argent Tournament are reset when a character uses the Faction Change service.

For more information on the Faction Change service, please read the FAQ, http://eu.blizzard.com/support/article.xml?articleId=39810 (http://eu.blizzard.com/support/article.xml?articleId=39810) or visit the Faction Change page http://www.wow-europe.com/account/ (http://www.wow-europe.com/account/) to get started.

We would also like to inform everyone that we will be offering a Race Change Service in the future. This decision was based largely on community feedback, and often in regards to the Faction Change service. Players who choose to switch races will be able to change their race within their same faction and class. Additional information and updates about Race Changes will be published at a future date. (Source (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/1/10920732119-1709-faction-change-service-now-available.html))

A couple of things to note after changing faction.

Shadow Vault and the Ebon Blade; The entire Shadow Vault quest line has to be redone in order to open it up again (Yes, your rep remains. I was exalted when transferring).

Any quest chains you did, that have a horde/alliance equivalent, but have a different starting point, looked to be reset.

Your dailies get reset.

Argent Tournament being reset has already been mentioned.

You will be missing certain flight points in Northrend. For some reason I didn't have Westfall Brigand in Grizzly Hills and Nessingwary's Camp in SB. I didn't notice any missing from Outland or Azeroth, but will check again later.

Overall, after completing the Northrend Loremaster achievement as horde and then changing faction, it looks as though I have maybe 100 or so quests that I can now do.
All I can say is Ka-ching!$$ I made 600g last night, just from repeating the Shadow Vault chain and there's probably another couple of thousand gold there for me again.

So a couple of tips before you change factions.
Do the Northrend Loremaster achievement first.
On the day you change, do your 25 dailies first.
Load up on faction pets and recipes.

You will still have the Northrend Loremaster achievement.

Some quest will become available to you, since they are different to the opposite faction. Basically, any quest that has a different ID to the one you have already done, will be available for you.

Yes, you can now do the starting area quests for your new factions, because, as I said, these quests will all have different ID's from the ones you have previously done.

If you have the 10k gold from quests achievement, then you will retain that achievement. You do NOT lose any COMPLETED achievements. However, some incomplete achievements will probably be altered. For example, people have posted that their Kalimdor Loremaster achievement progress went from 500/700 down to 100/700.
If that's something your not prepared to let happen, then complete the Loremaster achievement first, before switching factions.
