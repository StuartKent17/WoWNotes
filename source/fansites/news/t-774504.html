Cataclysm Beta - Build 13189 (Codename Kitten)
Apparently, Cataclysm Beta Build 13183 (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/2011-Cataclysm-Beta-Build-13183) will never reach the beta servers and has been replaced with 13189. this build has a few extra changes. And yeah, I'm in the mood for another stupid codename.

Achievements Changes
Feats of Strength

Veteran of the Shifting Sands (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/5533/) - Completed the gates of Ahn'Qiraj quest chain up to and including Treasure of the Timeless One (even if someone else ended up with the mount).




Spell Changes

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/270-Druid) / 3.3.5 Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?druid#,,12213) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..druid) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1117-Druid-Cataclysm))
Restoration

Healing Touch (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/5185/) now costs 30% of base mana, down from 38% of base mana.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/273-Paladin) / 3.3.5 Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?paladin#,,12213) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..paladin) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1670-Paladin-Cataclysm))
Holy

Speed of Light (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/85499/) has been revamped to only affect Holy Radiance - Speed of Light (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/85499/) - Grants 1/2/3% spell haste and reduces the cooldown of Holy Radiance by 30 sec. Casting Holy Radiance increases your movement speed by 20/40/60% for 4 sec. /
Clarity of Purpose (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/85463/) now reduces cast time by 0.3 sec at Rank 2, down from 0.35 sec.


Retribution

Zealotry (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/85696/) now requires 3 Holy Power to use, but does not consume Holy Power.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/274-Priest) / 3.3.5 Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?priest#,,12213) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..priest) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1123-Priest-Cataclysm))
Holy

Chakra (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/14751/) now has a 30 sec cooldown, down from 1 min.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/276-Shaman) / 3.3.5 Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?shaman#,,12213) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..shaman) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1124-Shaman-Cataclysm))
Elemental

Hex (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/51514/) now has a 30 yards range, up from 20 yards.


Glyphs

Windfury Weapon (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/55562/) - Increases the chance per swing for Windfury Weapon to trigger by 2%.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/278-Warrior) / 3.3.5 Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?warrior#,,12213) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..warrior) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1676-Warrior-Cataclysm))
Fury

Intimidating Shout (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/5246/) now causes the the targeted enemy to cower in fear and up to 5 additional enemies within 8 yards to flee. (Old - causing up to 5 enemies within 8 yards to cower in fear.)
