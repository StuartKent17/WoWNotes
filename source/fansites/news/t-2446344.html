Blizzcon 2018 - Day 1 Recap

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/july/wowmini.png" style="margin-bottom:-2px; margin-right:0px; vertical-align:bottom" /> World of Warcraft


WoW Classic is slated for release in the Summer of 2019.
New charity pet: - Whomper! (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pjtPCZ8HO5o) Proceeds go to Code.org.
New WoW Cinematic: "Lost Honor" (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LKZ7ygLTR0g) featuring Saurfang & Anduin.
Kul Tiran Humans & Zandalari Trolls Allied Races (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/8124-Kul-Tiran-Human-and-Zandalari-Troll-Classes) were updated to show available classes and racials.
Patch 8.1 is slated for release on December 11th, 2018. New raid will not open until early 2019.
New in-game cinematic "Terror of Darkshore" (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HLKS0Jq9U_E).
Patch 8.1.5 will bring updates to the Darkmoon Faire, Brawler's Guild, Timewalking, Microholidays and more.
Patch 8.2.5 will update Goblin and Worgen models.
A future patch will be called Rise of Azshara and include lots of new content, release date to be determined.
See the full WoW Panel recap here. (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/8125-World-of-Warcraft-What-s-Next)



<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2011/november/d3.png" style="margin-bottom:-2px; margin-right:0px; vertical-align:bottom" /> Diablo


Diablo Immortal (https://diabloimmortal.com/en-us/) is a new mobile game that takes place between the stories of Diablo 2 and Diablo 3.
Diablo Immortal cinematic reveal (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5iajnrdSj1o).
Gameplay trailer for Diablo Immortal (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZkDAk-p9LFQ).
The game will feature 8 zones, 4 instanced dungeons and 6 classes to start. Fully integrated to Battle.net.

Barbarian
Crusader
Demon Hunter
Monk
Necromancer
Wizard

Additional details can be found in our complete recap. (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/8122-Diablo-What-s-Next-Panel)
Diablofans also put together a list of details (http://www.diablofans.com/news/49070-mobile-game-diablo-immortal-announced).



<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/november/overwatch.png" style="margin-bottom:-2px; margin-right:0px; vertical-align:bottom" /> Overwatch


Ashe (https://playoverwatch.com/en-us/heroes/ashe/) - A damage dealer with a rifle and dynamite explosives.
Ashe's origin story (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yvT9wKCrEyo&) showcases her villianous backstory.
Ashe's introduction video (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G8LbOecWGug) showing her unique skills.
New animated cinematic short "Reunion" (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PKYVvPNhRR0) featuring Ashe and McCree.
See the full Overwatch Panel recap by clicking here. (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/8123-Overwatch-What-s-Next-Panel)



<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2013/march/hearthstoneIcon.png" style="margin-bottom:-2px; margin-right:0px; vertical-align:bottom" /> Hearthstone


Rastakhan's Rumble (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YG35phnCKRc) is the next expansion.
Troll themed expansion will feature 135 new cards.
New keyword card type called Overkill.
Expansion will release on December 4th, 2018.
For more information including card previews, click here for a full recap. (https://www.hearthpwn.com/news/6044-the-rastakhans-rumble-information-panel-from)



<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2013/october/hotsIcon.png" style="margin-bottom:-2px; margin-right:0px; vertical-align:bottom" /> Heroes of the Storm


Orphea (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WVoeUUeYehY) is the next hero, a character born in the Nexus.
Heir of Raven Court (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9jguWUOA8yw) cinematic featuring Orphea.
Tons of additional updates are available in the full recap. (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/8126-BlizzCon-2018-Heroes-of-the-Storm-What-s-Next)



<img src="https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/november/WC3Reforged.png" style="margin-bottom:-2px; margin-right:0px; vertical-align:bottom" /> Warcraft 3: Reforged

Warcraft 3: Reforged was announced! Submit your questions now (https://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2446287-Warcraft-3-Reforged-Submit-Questions-for-the-Developers?p=50417439#post50417439) for the developer Q & A!


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/november/wc3submitquestions.jpg (https://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2446287-Warcraft-3-Reforged-Submit-Questions-for-the-Developers?p=50417439#post50417439)


Warcraft 3:Reforged will fully integrate with modern Battle.net and feature updated models, textures and animations.
Release is set for sometime in 2019. Can be pre-purchased today. (https://us.shop.battle.net/en-us/product/warcraft-iii-reforged)
There are exclusive items available for other Blizzard titles if you pre-purchase the Spoilers of War edition.
A cinematic reveal trailer (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=72UbFQO5-m0) was released.
Gameplay trailer (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Q2zfx5hQ3CE) showing the before and after with new models and textures.
Blizzard also released a trailer for The Culling Campaign (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ICMpPrg77g0).
The official forums for the game are live (https://us.forums.blizzard.com/en/warcraft3/) and the developers are looking for feedback.
