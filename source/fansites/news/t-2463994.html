Glory of the Dazar'alor Raider World First
Glory of the Dazar'alor Raider (https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/13315-glory-of-the-dazaralor-raider) has been completed by the Achievements Discord (https://discord.gg/achievements)! The achievement rewards the Dazar'alor Windreaver (https://www.wowdb.com/items/166539-dazaralor-windreaver) mount, one of the three pterrordax mounts available in Battle of Azeroth.

If you're looking for a group to complete the achievement and get the Dazar'alor Windreaver (https://www.wowdb.com/items/166539-dazaralor-windreaver) mount, then you can join the Achievements Discord (https://discord.gg/achievements)! You can also opt into roles to receive notifications of achievement runs, which makes forming groups much easier.

Congrats to everyone involved in getting this World First feat: Ashbone (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/tarren-mill/Ashbone), Nightswifty (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/draenor/Nightswifty), Illidjari (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/laughing-skull/Illidjari), Hõlycow (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/kazzak/hõlycow), Myzvale (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/tarren-mill/myzvale), Zeeldax (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/twisting-nether/Zeeldax), Mcback (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/defias-brotherhood/Mcback), Askii (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/malganis/Askii), Noxia (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/kazzak/Nóxia), Tillie (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/draenor/Yunu), Uguluugulu (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/frostwolf/Uguluugulu), Torionp (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/tarren-mill/Torionp), Dewmê (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/tarren-mill/Dewmê), Xirev (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/vashj/Xirev), Hailul (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/khadgar/Hailul), Koge (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/kazzak/Kogeshaman), Samouri (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/tarren-mill/Samouri), Karnatron (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/draenor/Karnatron), Baran (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/draenor/Rbaran), Alfakyn (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/argent-dawn/Alfakyn), Drenaila (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/kazzak/Drenaila), Vingo (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/archimonde/Vingo), Tizzle (https://worldofwarcraft.com/en-gb/character/tarren-mill/Tizzle).
