Before, jewelcrafting gave 2 advantages, that no other profession could match:
a) Enabling a socket bonus without using a sup-par hybrid gem.
b) Enabling a meta gem without using sub-par gem colors.

Both advantages were of different gravity for classes/specs, more pronounced for dps-specs (blue sockets habe virtually no use as dps and the best meta requires two blue gems) and less pronounced for healers (every socket color gives a bonus: yellow=int/crit/haste, red=spell, blue=spi/mp5). Yes, even healers prefer to stack one certain stat, but other colors are not totally lost to them.
So because Dragoneyes were prismatic, a jewelcrafter had a bonus worth of about an axtra socket, or in other words: the bonus from jc was 50% better than other professions.

With this change the professions are equal, if you compare the bonus stats that you can get from nearly all professions: Spellpower, Attack Power, Stamina. Tailoring might still be a bit ahead, but the margin is small.

But Jewelcrafting still has an advantage (together with Blacksmithing): Free choice of bonus.
Most professions only give you the option of three or four stats, while JC and BS gives you the full choice of all available gems, including a lot of stats that no other profession can offer. This again benefits some classes/specs more than others.

While before the dps-specs mostly profited from not having to use sup-par socket colors, the actual stat that gives them the most is available from nearly all professions: raw spellpower or attack power (please don't start about strength > ap for some classes). With the socket color bonus gone, JC now is equal to other professions in relation to dps-specs.

This leaves one type of characters, who before benefited from the customizability, and will continue to benefit: Healers. And that is because of the one stat, that has become their primary source of mana regeneration - Intellect.


TLDR:
- For DPS, JC will now be equal to other professions. (they lose the "cheating" around bad socket colors)
- For Healers and some other specs, JC and BS were and will be superior. (because of the free choice of the bonus stat)
