Patch 8.2 PTR - Build 30613
A new build is on the way to the PTR!

Ashran Epic Battleground Testing – Play With The Blues
We’re coming together to test the Ashran epic battleground experience in the Rise of Azshara PTR, and would love for you to join us!

On Friday, May 31, beginning at 10:00 a.m. PDT (1:00 p.m. Eastern), we’ll be queueing up for Ashran. Get your PTR character(s) ready at level 120 on the Broxigar server, and then at 10:00 a.m.:


Open Group Finder (default hotkey: i)
Select the Player vs. Player tab
Select Quick Match
In the dropdown, choose “Specific Battlegrounds”
Select “Ashran” and then hit the Join Battle button


Thank you very much, and we look forward to fighting in Draenor with you!

yes i know its spelt poison but english usually likes to do -sion, so my fingers are just used to typing that.

Well anyways, it's supposed to be venom because the difference between venom/poison is the method of delivery of the toxin.

If something bites you and injects a toxin in the wound, it's venomous (hence's snapdragon's bite)

Now if the toxin is ingested, comes into contact with skin, or inhaled, it's poisonous.

A Rogue's Poison can be derived from a Venom, but when referring to animals it refers to how the animal delivers the toxin (both venom and poison are derivatives of the word 'toxin')

Think of a puffer fish and a blue-ringed octopus. Both have tetrodotoxin, but a puffer fish is poisonous because the delivery of said toxin only happens when you eat it, whereas a blue-ringed octopus delivers tetrodotoxin via a bite (i.e. it eats you or is defending itself by biting you).
