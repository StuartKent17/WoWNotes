PvP Tuning - January 11, 2019
We’re currently working on some targeted adjustments to three specs in PvP. We don’t have a precise time these will go live, but we’re estimating late today or perhaps tomorrow afternoon:

https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/imonks.gif Monk (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/341-Monk) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/monk) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#o))

Mistweaver
Surging Mist (PvP Talent) mana cost increased to 3.8% of base mana (was 2% of base mana), and healing reduced by 20%.
Way of the Crane (PvP Talent) increases physical damage by 25% (was 35%), and now heals for 150% of damage dealt (was 200%).


https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/276-Shaman) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/shaman) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#c))

Elemental
Earth Shock damage reduced by 10% when engaged in combat with enemy players.
Stormbringer increases Lightning Bolt damage by 115% when engaged in combat with enemy players (was 125%).
Surge of Power can now cause up to an additional 2 Lightning Bolt overloads against enemy players (was 3 additional overloads).


https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/270-Druid) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/druid) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#s))

Balance
Celestial Alignment increases damage of your spells by 10% when engaged in combat with enemy players (was 15%).
Incarnation: Chosen of Elune increases damage of your spells by 18% when engaged in combat with enemy players (was 25%).
Shooting Stars now generates 2 Astral Power when engaged in combat with enemy players (was 4 Astral Power).


Our goal is to get these adjustments made before the weekend, and when they go live, we’ll confirm here in this thread, on Warcraftdevs, and in the next updated to our Hotfixes blog post.

I would really love some insight into what guides arena balancing. I mean we have Disc Priest, Assassination Rogue and Shadow Priest as the most highly represented specs in high level 3s yet none of them seem to get touched, yet specs that are far less represented are getting nerfed. Priests account for 15% of the top rated characters in 3s and 13% in 2s while making up about 8% of the level 120 player population. There are more level 120 DKs than there are Rogues yet Rogues are 8 times more highly represented at the top end of arena. It gets even weirder if you start looking at racials, Orcs being about 7% of the population of level 120 players make up over 30% of the high rated arena toons.

Looking at these changes Mistweaver is the strongest spec in 2s. Elemental doesn't seem to be doing particularly well in any bracket. Balance seems to be strong in RBGs but in arenas almost non existant. While you could maybe justify the Mistweaver spec based on their 2s strength the others just seem strange. They are at best mid-range specs with a representation that doesn't really stand out so why are they getting nerfed when the obvious culprits that are doing well in every bracket, Assassination, Disc and Shadow Priest go untouched? It is just hard to see what the reasoning could be and I wish Blizzard would share some of the data on which they make these decisions because they often seem to be hitting the wrong targets.

My best guess is that they are only balancing around the opinions of a small number of high end players but that doesn't seem to be making for a game that is balanced for the general population of players and it seems quite likely that whatever system they are using a few favoured specs seem to be creeping through without any real adjustment despite the fact that the data seems to show they badly need it. Even if you want to argue that the representation is due to players who want to do arena being far more likely to roll Rogue or Priest isn't that fact itself indicitave of a problem?
