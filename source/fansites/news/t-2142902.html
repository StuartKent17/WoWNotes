Addon - Reagent Banker
Reagent Banker (https://mods.curse.com/addons/wow/255228-reagent-banker) is a simple addon that auto deposits reagents to the reagent bank (http://wow.gamepedia.com/Reagent_Bank) when you visit the bank. There are some additional options to modify behavior of the addon and print what is deposited to chat.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2016/december/thumb/reagentBanker.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2016/december/reagentBanker.jpg)


Patch 7.1 - Ravika and Lenny "Fingers" McCoy
One small change in Patch 7.1 is the expanded availability of the Patch 5.3 toys. With the launch of Legion, these toys were added to vendors you had to visit before doing the Broken Shore intro (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/5896). Now you can also find these vendors out in the world, on Echo Isles (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/113615-ravika#mapper:6453:0) and in Stormwind (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/2795-lenny-fingers-mccoy).

Bom'bay's Color-Seein' Sauce (http://www.wowdb.com/items/97921-bombays-color-seein-sauce) is also back on the vendor. It was not available on the version of Lenny that appears before the Broken Shore, but is now on both versions.
