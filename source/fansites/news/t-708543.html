The Pally hate exists because of mostly two reasons.

1) Their attack/tanking mechanics were dramatically simplified for WotLK and caused a massive glut of paladins to appear everywhere; which was intentional on Blizzard's part when they mentioned in Blizzcon 2008 that they wanted Paladins to be a 'simple, appealing class' for new players to learn how to play in an MMO, assuming the lore around Paladins would appeal to them. They are, hence, popular and numerous and more often than not played by new players, so hated.

2) People will always dislike defensive immunity. Offensive immunity is more welcome, in contrast (Cyclone is rarely angsted about). Things that interrupt the flow of offense and forcing you to switch targets against your favor, or use an otherwise-useless utility skill (Mass Dispel/Shattering Throw) will always cause people to angst.

But you are absolutely right on the proportion of complaints that will arise when the Paladin preview will come out. As compared to what is seen on the official forums when previews were put out for all the other classes compared to what kind of traffic the official Paladin forums gets every day, the amount of gloating and jeering from other non-paladins vastly outdoes the actual QQ from paladins. The anti-paladin QQers will never notice this, or likely even care -- it'll just simply remain as one of the great ironies of the universe.
