Patch 5.3 Dev Interview
Today we are taking a look at an interview over on Game Diplomat (http://gamediplomat.com/wow-pet-battle-interview-patch-5-3-with-cory-stockton/) with Cory Stockton.

Pet Battles

There aren't any developers that work only on pet battles, so the entire team contributes.
The Lurker Below was going to be a Patch 5.3 battle pet, but it didn't look good when scaled down.
Patch 5.3 enhanced pet battle PvP because players were interested in the PvP side of pet battles, doing things like forming tournaments.
A very large percentage of level 90 players are doing pet battles, or at least trying them.
Patch 5.4 will let you see the health of pets when other players are doing pet battles in the world, as the spectating (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/3239-Patch-5-3-Pet-Battle-Spectating-Blue-Tweets-Riot-Toxic-Player-Behavior-Fan-Art) added in 5.3 does not let you target any of the pets.
If players are enthusiastic about pet battle PvP, there will have to be more focus on pet balance than there is now.
Miss and Dodge had been recognized as a problem since Patch 5.1 and were going to be addressed in Patch 5.2, but the devs ran out of time. The changes were finally put into place in Patch 5.3.
Pets dropping from old raid bosses worked well, as most people enjoyed collecting them.
Wrath of the Lich King raid bosses would be next for pet drops after the second set of Burning Crusade raid bosses get pets. This isn't guaranteed though, as there aren't solid plans in place yet for when the second set of Burning Crusade raid bosses will get pets.
Catching a Unborn Val'kyr (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/71163-unborn-valkyr) will affect the spawn time and location of the next one.



Patch 5.3 Hotfixes - May 29
Classes

General

Pets will now correctly auto-cast Bite, Claw, and Smack again.

Talents

Blink Strike (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/130392)' teleportation will no longer work for pets that have been rooted.

Beast Mastery

Beast Cleave (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/115939) damage against nearby targets will no longer be affected by damage taken modifiers on the primary target.



http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/imonks.gif Monk (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/341-Monk) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/monk) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#o))

Windwalker

Fists of Fury (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/113656) is now able to be cast while affected by a Silence (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/15487) effect and Silence (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/15487) effects will not interrupt the ability while it's being channeled.




Raids, Dungeons, and Scenarios

Wailing Caverns

Fixed an issue where Muyoh could be killed, preventing players from completing the dungeon.



PvP

Conquest Quartermasters (Armsmaster Holinka (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/70101-armsmaster-holinka) and Roo Desvin (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/70108-roo-desvin)) should be available once again.


Items

Meta and yellow gem sockets have been added to helmets (http://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-patch=16758&filter-random-stats=1&filter-ilvl-min=489&filter-ilvl-max=489&filter-slot=2) created by combining Radical Mojo with a Latent Kor'kron Helm.
http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/92813.jpg Greater Cache of Treasures (http://www.wowdb.com/items/92813-greater-cache-of-treasures), awarded for completing Normal difficulty Scenario should now properly have a chance to contain Motes of Harmony.
http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/91091.jpg Tyrannical Gladiator's Greatsword (http://www.wowdb.com/items/91091-tyrannical-gladiators-greatsword) and http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/94323.jpg Tyrannical Gladiator's Decapitator (http://www.wowdb.com/items/94323-tyrannical-gladiators-decapitator) now give 6,195 PvP Power. This is in-line with other Season 13 ilevel 498 two-handed weapons.
Ra'sha's Sacrificial Dagger (http://media-azeroth.cursecdn.com/icons/items/16/95566.jpg Ra'sha's Sacrificial Dagger) can no longer be used while in Bizmo's Brawlpub or Brawl'gar Arena.
(CN, TW, KR) Upgrades for items from Terrace of Endless Spring on 25-player normal difficulty now should correctly cost 250 Valor Points.


Battlefield: Barrens

Markers on the World Map for Barrens Commanders are now properly removed when defeated.
Jessina the Blazing's (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/71256-jessina-the-blazing) Unstable Magma Totem now has an arming time after spawning and can no longer be hit by area-of-effect damage.
Kor'kron Immolators (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/71007-korkron-immolator) and Kor'kron Earthshakers (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/71188-korkron-earthshaker) no longer use Corrupted Fire Nova or Corrupted Stoneskin totems.
Kor'kron Motivators (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/71052-korkron-motivator), Kor'kron Grunts (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/71318-korkron-grunt), and Kor'kron Spotters (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/71055-korkron-spotter) now use Mortal Strike instead of Wild Cleave.


Brawler's Guild

Grandpa Grumplefloot's Bad Luckydos (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/70735-bad-luckydo) will no longer hop towards players that are not in the arena.




Patch 5.4 Battle Pet Abilities Adjustments
Thank you to everyone who has shared their feedback with us so far, we appreciate it. Based on some of the comments we’ve received, here is an updated list of potential changes we’re hoping to include in Patch 5.4:


The Magic passive now caps damage taken at 35% of maximum health, buffed from 40%. The Dragonkin passive effect now activates when the enemy is taken below 50% health, up from 25%. The Aquatic passive now reduces DOT damage by 50%, up from 25%. The Elemental passive effect now only affect negative, direct effects of weather (e.g. the accuracy reduction of Sandstorm won’t be applied, but the damage reduction will). The Critter passive now makes critters completely immune to roots, stuns, and sleeps.


Keep that feedback rollin’ in!
