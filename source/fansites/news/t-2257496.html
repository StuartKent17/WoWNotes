Patch 7.2.5 Hotfixes - July 17
Dungeons and Raids

Upper Return to Karazhan

Shade of Medivh

The impact damage of Inferno Bolt has been reduced by 25% in Mythic difficulty.

Mana Devourer

Energy Discharge now deals 20% less damage in Heroic and Mythic difficulties.
Energy Void now deals 50% less damage in Heroic and Mythic difficulties.

Viz’aduum

Chess: King’s health has been lowered by 30%.
Disintegrate now deals 25% less damage in Heroic and Mythic difficulties.
Disintegrate now has a cast time of 2.5 seconds in the first phase.
Explosive Shadows now deals 25% less damage in Heroic and Mythic difficulties.
Shadow Phlegm now deals 20% less damage in Heroic and Mythic difficulties.




Items

The Arsenal: The Warglaives of Azzinoth (http://www.wowdb.com/items/150372-arsenal-the-warglaives-of-azzinoth) awarded by the I'll Hold These For You Until You Get Out (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/11869-ill-hold-these-for-you-until-you-get-out) should now sheathe in the same formation as the original Warglaives.



World of Warcraft Chronicle Volume 3
Another volume of the World of Warcraft Chronicle series is coming out next year! You can preorder World of Warcraft Chronicle Volume 3 for the March 27, 2018 release now! According to IGN (http://www.ign.com/articles/2017/07/17/world-of-warcraft-chronicle-vol-3-coming-from-blizzard-and-dark-horse), this one focuses on the events that shaped the modern history of Azeroth and the backstories of Arthas, Illidan, Jaina, and other iconic characters.

You can purchase Volume 1 (http://amzn.to/2us2WwM) and Volume 2 (http://amzn.to/2vaEpts) on Amazon as well.
