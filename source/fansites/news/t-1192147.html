Mists of Pandaria Beta - Build 16048
A new beta build will be deployed on beta realms soon. We will be updating these notes frequently, so keep checking back!

Spell Changes
Companions

Terky (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/28487/#16030-16048) is now a Battle Pet.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ideathknights.gif Death Knight (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/269-Death-Knight))

Death Strike (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/49998/#16030-16048) now does 280% weapon damage, up from 230%. Damage increased by 43%.


Blood

Heart Strike (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/55050/#16030-16048) now does 115% weapon damage, down from 170%. Damage reduced by 54%.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/271-Hunter))
Survival

Black Arrow (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/3674/#16030-16048) damage reduced by 24%. Now has 11.5% AP scaling, down from 15%.
Explosive Shot (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/53301/#16030-16048) damage and RAP scaling increased by 20%.
Improved Serpent Sting (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/82834/#16030-16048) now increases the damage of your Serpent Sting by 50%, down from 100%.


Pets

Burrow Attack (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/93433/#16030-16048) no longer costs 50 focus, instead costs 10 Focus, plus 5 per sec.
Fire Breath (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/34889/#16030-16048) now increases magic damage taken by 5% for 45 sec, down from 8%.
Froststorm Breath (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/92380/#16030-16048) no longer costs 50 focus, instead costs 10 Focus, plus 5 per sec.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/imonk.gif Monk (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/341-Monk))
Brewmaster

Gift of the Ox (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/124502/#16030-16048) healing reduced by 50%. AP scaling remains unchanged.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/276-Shaman))

Searing Totem (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/3599/#16030-16048) now has a 25 yd range, up from 20.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock))
Talents

Grimoire of Sacrifice (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/108503/#16030-16048) now has a 60 min duration, up from 20.


Demonology

Hellfire (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/1949/#16030-16048) mana cost is now 2% of Base Mana, plus 2% per sec, down from 4% of Base Mana, plus 4% per sec.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/278-Warrior))

Defensive Stance (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/71/#16030-16048) now decreases damage taken by 25%, up from 15%.


Protection

Revenge (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/6572/#16030-16048) now generates 15 Rage in Defensive Stance, up from 10.
Shield Slam (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/23922/#16030-16048) now generates 20 Rage in Defensive Stance, up from 15.
Unwavering Sentinel (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/29144/#16030-16048) now increases your armor value from items by 25%, up from 10%.


Racials
Worgen

Running Wild (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/87840/#16030-16048) now can't be cast in Tree of Life Form, Moonkin Form.


Raid & Dungeon Abilities

Crush (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/122774/#16030-16048) Garalon crushes his foes, inflicting 800,000 Physical damage to enemies within 12 yards and 100,000 75,000 Physical damage to all enemies.
Overwhelming Assault (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/123474/#16030-16048) Inflicts 150% weapon damage to his current target and increases damage from further Overwhelming Assaults by 100% for 45 sec. 50% for 45 sec.
Spiritual Grasp (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/115982/#16030-16048) Inflicts 40,500 Inflicts 54,000 Shadow damage to the target.
