Hallow's End 2014
Hallow's End (http://www.wowdb.com/world-events/324-hallows-end) begins tomorrow morning and this year there are finally some updates! Our friends over at Icy Veins have the full guide (http://www.icy-veins.com/wow/hallows-end-2014).


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/october/hallowsend2014.jpg (http://www.icy-veins.com/wow/hallows-end-2014)

This year includes Headless Horseman (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/23682-headless-horseman) loot that has been upgraded to item level 540, new pets that cost 150 x Tricky Treats (http://www.wowdb.com/items/33226-tricky-treat#related:currency-for), costumes for you and your pug, new wands, and more! The Lich King costume will cost Tricky Treats (http://www.wowdb.com/items/33226-tricky-treat#related:currency-for) from a vendor.

Players are now able to undelete their characters.
