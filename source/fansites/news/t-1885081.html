Submit Questions for the Developers
We have our first interview in a long time coming up at Blizzcon and as always, we want to let the community ask questions! This time our interview will be covering the design of Legion.

If you have any questions you think we should ask, be sure to add them to the comments of the linked post (http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/1885061-Submit-Questions-for-the-Developers) for consideration. We will pick the best questions and add them to our list!


Don't post any questions in the comments of this news post. Use the linked topic (http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/1885061-Submit-Questions-for-the-Developers) instead.
These should be questions about Legion, not Warlords of Draenor.
No class specific questions please. This includes things like balance between the different healing/tank/DPS classes and specs.
