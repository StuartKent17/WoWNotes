http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=23425527108&postId=237647604369&sid=1#17

IE To replace the sound "UR_XT002_Special01.wav" you would first create in your Data folder a folder called "Sound" in that would be a folder "Creature" then in that a folder "XT002Deconstructor". in the folder "XT002Deconstructor" you would put a "UR_XT002_Special01.wav" file. Make it a short 4k no noise .wav and you silence the "UR_XT002_Special01.wav" sound.
