Priest streaming here again. Just as an FYI to those watching, one of his abilities was working completely incorrectly. When he exhales all the gas he inhaled 3 times over, it's supposed to do a base 50k shadow damage to the entire raid. The Gas Spore people he marks occasionally throughout the fight explode, but give a buff to everyone they hit that reduces the damage of that 50k explosion significantly per stack. Basically you want to balance people needing more mitigation, but not exploding the entire raid every time.

It's supposed to be a dichotomy of spreading out to avoid the Vile Gas and not having 5 people explode everyone every time, and getting all 25 people the necessary stacks of mitigation to survive every "50k" Pungent Blight.

The way the fight was on this test, it was bugged and everyone had lots of mitigation all the time. With shields on the entire raid we never even took damage from Pungent Blight, which is a huge mechanic to the encounter.

So yes, of course it seemed like a pushover. I can't wait to see how punishing it will be on Hardmode. The Sunwell comment was more directed to this entire expansion of casual raiding, and was largely a joke. This is the internet, lighten up about it.

2009-10-22, 02:18 AM
The spores are released throughout the fight.

You need to spread out to avoid the Vile Gas casts, which can be quite bad if you're very clumped up.

However, when people are targeted with the spores, you must quickly run to them and take the damage from the spores, as this will build up a stacking "Inoculation" debuff, which reduces shadow damage by 25% per stack.

Festergut will inhale the gas in the room three times (reducing it's damage per inhale) until it's completely inhaled, then he will exhale (Pungent Blight) for massive shadow damage. If you have enough Inoculation stacks you will survive this damage. If you do not, you will die.

But supposedly the Pungent Blight is bugged and not doing enough damage.
