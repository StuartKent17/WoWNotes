Toy Box Enhanced Addon
Toy Box Enhanced (http://www.curse.com/addons/wow/toy-box-enhanced) is an addon that slightly extends the default toybox UI, adding additional toy filters, allowing you to hide toys, and saving your filter options.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/december/thumb/toyboxEnhanced.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/december/toyboxEnhanced.jpg)


Redprints Addon
Redprints (http://www.curse.com/addons/wow/redprints) is a simple addon that colors blueprints red after you have already learned them, allowing you to see which ones you already know at a glance.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/december/thumb/redPrints.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/december/redPrints.jpg)
