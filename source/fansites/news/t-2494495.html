Patch 8.2 Hotfixes - June 28
Creatures and NPCs

Elites required for combat ally WANTED quests are now a more appropriate size.
Mirecrawlers and slugs that have attached themselves to walls and ceilings no longer evade when engaged in combat.
Chief Curglrrmgur can now be tapped by all players regardless of faction.
Fabious no longer cares about your threats, just lookin' good.
Players can again successfully rescue Murloco.


Items

Resources for Junkyard Tinkering in Mechagon can now be stored in Engineering bags and the reagent bank.

Heart of Azeroth

Fixed an issue that prevented the Blood of the Enemy (Essence) from being purchasable after earning the Battlefield Brawler achievement.




Player versus Player

Resolved an issue that could prevent Concentrated Flame (Essence) from being cast on enemy players in Arenas, Rated Battlegrounds, and free-for-all PvP areas.

Havoc

Burning Soul’s absorption shield reduced to 25% when engaged in combat with enemy players (was 50%).



https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/274-Priest) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/priest) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#U))

Twist Magic’s healing reduced to 25% when engaged in combat with enemy players (was 50%).


https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/276-Shaman) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/shaman) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#c))

Pack Spirit’s healing reduced to 25% when engaged in combat with enemy players (was 50%).

Developers' note: These can have a much higher uptime in PvP combat compared to the rest of the game resulting in a larger than intended amount of healing. As a result, we’re further reducing their effect in PvP. These changes were intended to be included in the Rise of Azshara content update notes and have been live since its release.

https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warlock) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#k))

Crashing Chaos (Azerite Trait) updated. It now additionally lowers the cooldown of Summon Infernal by 15 seconds, and its damage increase has been reduced by 33%.



Quests

Chitterspine Meat will no longer drop for players who have already completed "Hungry Hungry Hydras".
The Rolly Gupler now can be slain during “A Gulper Ate the Rolly”.
Players now receive credit for entering the spider tank during “Gnomercy!”
Players can again interact with the Prototype Arcane Engine during “Shadowed Halls and Dusty Cogs.”
Fixed an issue that prevented Sign of the Emissary from granting increased reputation gains with Rustbolt Resistance.


World Quests

"Summons from the Deep" bosses can now be tapped by all players who fight them.
"Runelocked Chest" objective difficulty has been adjusted.
