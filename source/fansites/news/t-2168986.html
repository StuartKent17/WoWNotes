I really think Stormwind would be great with flocks of people around. It's not as if you can't just run through them, but if it actually seemed POPULATED I think it'd be awesome. I wonder why they don't do that. Think of the main city (I forget the name) in FFXV; it's not swarming with an absurd number of NPCs, but it absolutely feels more "alive" than Stormwind does.

Planet Coaster came out in November - I watched a couple guys play with the beta on Youtube, it looks like a great game, if you're into building games. Some of the stuff people have built already is pretty amazing. I'm avoiding it, because I know it will suck me in and never let go. :)
