Patch 5.4.7 PTR - Build 17859
Build 17859 will be deployed to the PTR realms soon.

Spell Changes
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/278-Warrior), Talent Calculator (http://ptr.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#E))

Charge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/100/#17841-17859) Charge to an enemy, rooting stunning it for 1.5 sec. Generates 20 Rage. 8-25 yd range. Instant. 20 sec cooldown.


Talents

Disrupting Shout (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/102060/#17841-17859) Interrupts all spellcasting within 10 yards and prevents any spell in that school from being cast for 4 sec. This ability shares a 15 sec cooldown with Pummel. Warrior - LvL 45 Talent. Instant. 40 sec cooldown.
Warbringer (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/103828/#17841-17859) Your Charge no longer stuns, instead pinning also knocks a target to the ground and rooting stunning it for 4 sec and reducing movement speed by 50% for 15 sec. Warrior - LvL 15 Talent.

Patch 5.4.2 Hotfixes: February 5
Creatures

Huolon's spawn point has been moved to the ground.
[Requires a realm restart.] Ordos can no longer get stuck channeling Eternal Agony indefinitely.


Timeless Isle

Players under the effects of the Censer of Eternal Agony no longer gain Blood Coins for defeating players in their party or raid.


Raids, Dungeons, and Scenarios

Siege of Orgrimmar

Galakras

Resolved an issue where players that perform a soft-reset on the raid instance after defeating Galakras would incorrectly zone into the raid at Norushen.

Kor'kron Dark Shaman

Resolved an issue where Earthbreaker Haromm and Wavebinder Kardris could potentially path underneath the world.


Icecrown Citadel: Fixed an issue where defeating The Lich King too quickly could prevent players from receiving loot.
