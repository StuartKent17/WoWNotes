Item changes in 2.2
As you probably all know, a lot of item changes were made during the last PTR patches. I thought it might be a good idea to list all the 2.2 item changes for all the people who wants to know if their equipment will get changed. (If some of them are still missing please post in comments)

All weapons from Serpentshrine Cavern bosses were slightly upgraded. (Live -> PTR Comparison (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/august/sscchanges2.jpg))
All weapons from Serpentshrine Cavern trash mobs were slightly upgraded. (Wildfury Staff (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/august/wildfurychanges.jpg) and Serpentshrine Shuriken (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/august/serpentshrineshuriken2.jpg))

Set Bonuses
Hunter Tier 5
(2) Set: Causes your pet to be healed for 15% of the damage you deal.
Old bonus : Your pet's attacks have a chance to heal your pet for 172 to 198.

Shaman Tier 6
(2) Set: Your Lesser Healing Wave ability costs 10% less mana.
Old bonus : Your Chain Heal ability costs 10% less mana.
(4) Set: Increases the amount healed by your Chain Heal ability by 5%.
Old bonus : Increases the amount healed by your Lesser Healing Wave ability by 5%.

Warlock Arena Season 2
(4) Set : Reduces the casting time of your Fear spell by 0.2 sec.
Old bonus : Reduces the casting time of your Immolate spell by 0.2 sec.

Warlock Tier 5
(2) Set: Causes your pet to be healed for 15% of the damage you deal.
Old bonus : Healing spells heal your pet for 30% of the amount that they healed you.
(4) Set: Your Shadowbolt spell hits increase the damage of Corruption by 10% and your Incinerate spell hits increase the damage of Immolate by 10%.
Old bonus : Your shadowbolt spell hits increase the damage of Corruption by 3% and your Incinerate spell hits increase the damage of Immolate by 3%.
