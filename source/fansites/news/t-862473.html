Companion Pets

Blood

Blood Shield (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/77513/blood-shield/#13698/13707) now only works when you're in Blood presence


Frost

Frost Strike (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/49143/frost-strike/#13698/13707) now deals 130% weapon damage plus 360, up from 110% weapon damage plus 305.
Howling Blast (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/49184/howling-blast/#13698/13707) now scales from 48% of Attack Power on the primary target, up from 40% of Attack Power. Base damage on additional targets reduced by 17%, from 865 to 721.


Item Set

Item - Death Knight T11 DPS 4P Bonus (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/90459/item-death-knight-t11-dps-4p-bonus/#13698/13707) now also procs from Killing Machine.

Feral

Stampeding Roar (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/77764/stampeding-roar/#13698/13707) no longer breaks prowling.
Swipe (Bear) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/779/swipe/#13698/13707) now has a 3 sec cooldown, down from 6 sec
Feral Swiftness (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/24866/feral-swiftness/#13698/13707) has a new effect - In addition, your Dash and Stampeding Roar have a 50/100% chance to remove all movement impairing effects from affected targets when used.


Restoration

Lifebloom (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/33763/lifebloom/#13698/13707) now costs 7% of base mana, down from 11%.
Efflorescence (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/34151/efflorescence/#13698/13707) now restores health equal to 4/8/12% of the amount healed by your Swiftmend to the three most injured targets within 8 yards, every 1 sec for 7 sec.
Malfurion's Gift (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/92364/malfurions-gift/#13698/13707) has a new effect - In addition, the cooldown of your Tranquility is reduced by 150/300 minutes. (Ok we'll just assume they mean seconds)


Glyphs

Glyph of Rake (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/54821/glyph-of-rake/#13698/13707) has been replaced with Glyph of Pounce (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/54821/glyph-of-pounce/#13698/13707) Increases the range of your Pounce by 3 yards.

Power Word: Barrier (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/62618/power-word-barrier/#13698/13707) now has a 3 min cooldown, up from 2 min. Now reduces damage by 25%, down from 30%.


Item Set

Item - Priest T11 Healer 4P Bonus (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/89911/item-priest-t11-healer-4p-bonus/#13698/13707) no longer requires the target to be affected by Weakened Soul.


Glyphs

Glyph of Divine Accuracy (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/63246/glyph-of-divine-accuracy/#13698/13707) now also affects Holy Fire.

Pet - Felhunter

Shadow Bite (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/54049/shadow-bite/#13698/13707) now deals an additional 30% damage for each of your DoT on the target, up from 15%.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/278-Warrior) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..warrior) / Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1676-Warrior-Cataclysm))
Arms

Lambs to the Slaughter (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/84588/lambs-to-the-slaughter/#13698/13707) now refreshes the duration of Rend on the target.


Fury

Precision (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/29592/precision/#13698/13707) now also increases auto attacks damage by 40%.


Protection

Shield Mastery (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/84608/shield-mastery/#13698/13707) now reduces magic damage taken by 7/14/20% for 6 sec, instead of 8/16/25%.
