Glyphs Hotfix
Due to an error, there were a few glyphs that were unable to be crafted via Inscription, which made them unobtainable. These glyphs are:

Glyph of the Solstice (http://www.wowdb.com/items/118061-glyph-of-the-solstice) (Druid)
Glyph of Purification (http://www.wowdb.com/items/118064-glyph-of-purification) (Priest)
Glyph of Purify Spirit (http://www.wowdb.com/items/118062-glyph-of-purify-spirit) (Shaman)
Glyph of Flying Fists (http://www.wowdb.com/items/118060-glyph-of-flying-fists) (Monk)

To make sure players have access to them, we've added them to the Inscription vendors in Stormshield and Warspear. Alliance players can purchase them from Joao Calhandro (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/87063-joao-calhandro), and Horde players can purchase them from Maru'sa (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/87551-marusa).

Update: We're also working on adding Glyph of Cleanse (http://www.wowdb.com/items/118063-glyph-of-cleanse) (Paladin) and Glyph of Frostbrand Weapon (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/161654-glyph-of-frostbrand-weapon) (Shaman). Glyph of Cleanse will be added to the Inscription vendors like the other glyphs. Glyph of Frostbrand Weapon's item is missing from the game files, so we can't add it to the vendors via hotfix. Instead, it will be automatically taught to all Shaman level 75 or higher.
