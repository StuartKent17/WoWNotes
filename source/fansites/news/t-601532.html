Yesterday's compilation was a bit messy, this one should be complete and we shouldn't see too many changes. I'd like to thanks all the people who sent me screenshots, or posted them on the forum. I probably forgot to answer a few people and I apologize for that.

Patch Notes
2.2 Patch notes are hidden somewhere here



Major mechanics change
DoTs damages are now lowered by resilience.
Most crowd control spells got their duration reduced to 10 sec in PvP (Seduce, Freezing Trap, etc ...)



New Mounts
Spectral Tiger Video (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_8gb3luiMqk&eurl=http%3A%2F%2Fwww%2Emmo%2Dchampion%2Ecom%2Findex%2Ephp). It will be a reward from the upcoming trading card game expansion.
Broom Flying Mount Video (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_bDyp8H3_oo&eurl=http%3A%2F%2Fwww%2Emmo%2Dchampion%2Ecom%2Findex%2Ephp). No official informations yet, but it's probably some kind of reindeer-like transformation for an haloween event.



New items
New Arena shields. Price and skin are the same as the current warrior shield. (Healing Shield (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/july/shield1.jpg), Spell Damage Shield (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/july/shield2.jpg))
Old AQ enchants available from reputation vendor. (Few examples here (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/july/newenchant.jpg), materials requirements here (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/july/enchantmats.jpg))
You get reputation and trash loots from trash mobs in Hyjal Summit. (Reputation from bosses lowered a bit)
New epic gems (list here (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/july/newgems.jpg))
New Violet Eye rewards. (List here (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/july/newvioleteye.jpg)



Zul'aman
Zul'aman monsters available in the game files, but not in game. (Preview here (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/july/zulaman_monsters_small.jpg))
Zul'aman preview #1, Tigole post about it.



Voice Chat
It's not activated on the PTR yet, but it's still rumored to be released in 2.2, most of the interface is already available in the current PTR client and Rob Pardo said that the voice chat will be available in the "next software update" during an interview with OCRegister (http://gadgetress.freedomblogging.com/2007/07/11/blizzard-adds-more-goodies-to-world-of-warcraft/)



Item Changes
[item=32590] got a new skin, available here (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/july/nethervoid.jpg).
[item=28826,en,eu] was boosted a bit, you can check the new stats here (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/july/shurikenofnegation.jpg)
[item=22861] and [item=22866] were changed (name and stats). (Stats (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/july/newflask.jpg), Blinding Light (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/july/blindinglight.jpg) and Pure Death (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/july/puredeath.jpg) mats lists.)
Most of the AQ enchantments mats were changed from Nexus to Prismatic shards.
You can now transmute Nexus Shards into Small Prismatic Shards. You can also split 1 Large Prismatic into 3 Small.
Mage Arena Season 2 set stats got changed. (Stats here (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/july/mageseason2.jpg))
Warlock Arena Season 2 set 4-parts bonus was changed from "Reduces the casting time of your Immolate spell by 0.2 sec." to "Reduces the casting time of your Fear spell by 0.2 sec."
Warlock Tier 5 armor set bonuses were changed.
(2) Set: Causes your pet to be healed for 15% of the damage you deal.
Old bonus : Healing spells heal your pet for 30% of the amount that they healed you.
(4) Set: Your Shadowbolt spell hits increase the damage of Corruption by 10% and your Incinerate spell hits increase the damage of Immolate by 10%.
Old bonus : Your shadowbolt spell hits increase the damage of Corruption by 3% and your Incinerate spell hits increase the damage of Immolate by 3%.
