Patch 7.3 - Profession Changes
Patch 7.3 adds new crafted items, materials, upgrade items, and more!


There are several new crafted items that use new Argus materials, with multiple recipe ranks.

Rank 1 of the recipes is usually unlocked by completing a quest.
Rank 2 of the recipes are purchased from the Army of the Light vendor for 2,000 gold.
Rank 3 of the recipes drop from a boss in Antorus, the Burning Throne.

Primal Obliterum (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/152296-primal-obliterum) is the new upgrade item

It is crafted from Obliterum (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/124125-obliterum) and Primal Sargerite (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151568-primal-sargerite). It can be used to upgrade only the new crafted items.
Primal Sargerite (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151568-primal-sargerite) is the new Blood of Sargeras (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/124124-blood-of-sargeras), acquired in the same ways as Blood of Sargeras (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/124124-blood-of-sargeras) was.
There is a new shoulder enchant to obtain Light's Fortune (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/153248-lights-fortune): Boon of the Lightbearer (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/153247-boon-of-the-lightbearer)

Herbalism got a new herb (Astral Glory (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151565-astral-glory)) and pet (Fel Lasher (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/128137-fel-lasher)).
Mining got a new ore (Empyrium (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151564-empyrium)) and pick (Krokul Mining Pick (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/153290-krokul-mining-pick))
Skinning got a new skin: Fiendish Leather (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151566-fiendish-leather)
Cooking got a new feast: Feast of the Fishes (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/251258-feast-of-the-fishes)
There are several turn in quests (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/professions?filter-patch=24473&filter-search=Work+Order&sort=category) for profession materials that grant skill and reputation.



Alchemy
Alchemists got a new trinket, a potion, and elixirs.


Astral Alchemist Stone (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151607-astral-alchemist-stone) - A new craftable trinket:

Rank 1 is rewarded from Ascending Alchemy (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/48016-ascending-alchemy). This quest requires Astral Glory (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151565-astral-glory) x 75, Primal Sargerite (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151568-primal-sargerite) x 5, and a Infernal Alchemist Stone (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/127842-infernal-alchemist-stone).
When you heal or deal damage you have a chance to increase your Strength, Agility, or Intellect by 6,599 for 15 sec. Your highest stat is always chosen.
Increases the effect that healing and mana potions have on the wearer by 40%. This effect does not stack.
Can be used as a Philosopher's Stone.

Lightblood Elixir (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/247619-lightblood-elixir) is rewarded from Limited Supplies (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/48002-limited-supplies)
Tears of the Naaru (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/247688-tears-of-the-naaru) is rewarded from Tracking the Trackers (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/48013-tracking-the-trackers)
Astral Healing Potion (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/251646-astral-healing-potion) and Astral Mana Potion (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/251660-astral-mana-potion) can be purchased from Alchemist Funen (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/125346-alchemist-funen)
