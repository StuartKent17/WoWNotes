Spell Comparison
Not a lot of new changes here if you already went through the patch notes update.

Items
Item - Shaman T10 Elemental 2P Bonus (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/70811/) - Your Lightning Bolt and Chain Lightning spells reduce the remaining cooldown on your Elemental Mastery talent by 2 sec. (Old - Your Lightning Bolt spell reduces the remaining cooldown on your Elemental Mastery talent by 1 sec.)
Item - Shaman T10 Elemental Relic (Shocks) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/71198/) - The periodic damage from your Flame Shock spell grants 44 haste rating for 30 sec. Stacks up to 5 times. (Old - Your Earth Shock, Flame Shock, and Frost Shock spells grant 73 haste rating for 30 sec. Stacks up to 3 times.)
Item - Death Knight T10 DPS Relic (Obliterate, Scourge Strike, Death Strike) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/71226/) - Your Obliterate, Scourge Strike, and Death Strike abilities grants 73 Strength for 15 sec. Stacks up to 3 times. (Old - Stacked up to 5 Times)
Item - Icecrown 25 Heroic Caster Trinket 1 Base (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/71645/) - Your harmful spells have a chance to increase your spell power by 121 and an additional 121 every 2 sec for 20 sec.

Racial
Will of the Forsaken (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/7744/) now shares a 45 sec cooldown with other similar effects.
