Patch 7.1.5 PTR Hotfixes
Lots of hotfixes went out to the PTR last night.

Item Changes

Distilled Titan Essence (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/143613/#23194-23244): Now upgrades Legendary items up to Itemlevel 940, up from Itemlevel 925.
Ullr's Feather Snowshoes (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/137033/#23194-23244): The remaining cooldown on Trueshot is now reduced by 0.8 second per damaging shot, down from 1 second.
Archimonde's Hatred Reborn (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/144249/#23194-23244): Absorb shield is now 30% of maximum health, down from 50%.
Maraad's Dying Breath (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/144273/#23194-23244): Each target hit by Light of Dawn now increases the next Light of the Martyr by 10%, down from 20%.




Spell Changes
Item Set Bonuses

Item - Druid T19 Feral 4P Bonus (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/211142/#23194-23244): Shred and Swipe now deal 10% increased damage per Bleed effect on the target, up from 8% increased damage.
Item - Druid T19 Restoration 4P Bonus (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/211170/#23194-23244): Rejuvenation now has a 1.5% chance jump to a new target at full duration, down from 2%.
Item - Hunter T19 Marksmanship 2P Bonus (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/211331/#23194-23244): Every 35 Focus you spend reduces the cooldown of Trueshot by 1 second, up from every 20 Focus spent.
Item - Priest T17 Discipline 2P Bonus (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/165614/#23194-23244): Penance channels for an additional 0.9 seconds, down from an additional 1 second.
Item - Priest T17 Holy 2P Bonus (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/165621/#23194-23244): Prayer of Mending jumps to 1 additional target, down from 4 additional targets.
