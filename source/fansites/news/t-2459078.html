Bonus Event - Cataclysm Timewalking
Cataclysm Timewalking (https://www.wowdb.com/world-events/587-timewalking-dungeon-event) is back this week.


The weekly quest (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53034-a-shattered-path-through-time) from your dungeon guide rewards Azerite Power and Cache of Uldir Treasures (https://www.wowdb.com/items/164258-cache-of-uldir-treasures).
You can find Kiatke (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/101759-kiatke) in Stormwind and Orgrimmar. Her unique items include Tosselwrench's Mega-Accurate Simulation Viewfinder (https://www.wowdb.com/items/133542-tosselwrenchs-mega-accurate-simulation-viewfinder) and Gurboggle's Gleaming Bauble (https://www.wowdb.com/items/133511-gurboggles-gleaming-bauble).
Don't forget to keep working towards Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk), Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger), and Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade)!
Reins of the Infinite Timereaver (https://www.wowdb.com/items/133543-reins-of-the-infinite-timereaver) can also drop from any Timewalking dungeon boss.

Call to Arms: Nazmir
This week's Call to Arms is Call to Arms: Nazmir (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52954-call-to-arms-nazmir) or Call to Arms: Nazmir (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52949-call-to-arms-nazmir). Head to Nazmir with warmode enabled to kill 10 players of the opposing faction. This will reward you with conquest and 75 reputation for either 7th Legion (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2159-7th-legion) or The Honorbound (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2157-the-honorbound).
