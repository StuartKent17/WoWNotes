MMO-Champion is recruiting !
For the first time since the beginning of the site, MMO-Champion is officialy recruiting people to help on the content for the upcoming Wrath of the Lich King expansion. We're mostly looking for people interested in writing and updating articles but any offer to improve the site is welcome.

Requirements
Decent English writing skills.
Excellent autonomy, we really need people who will think of ways to improve content by themselves without waiting for orders.
Good knowledge of Wrath of the Lich King. (Which shouldn't be a problem if you've been reading this site for at least 6 months)
Basic understanding of HTML (But if you're only interested in doing content, it's just a matter of understanding where to fill your information and anyone can do that)
Very high resistance to sarcasm. Seriously.



How to apply
Send me a personal message (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?action=pm;sa=send;u=1) with some information about you.
Please include a sample of your work in the message, sending a PM saying "hi, I want to work on the site !" just won't work.



Important stuff
This is NOT a paid position, in the end you could probably get paid if you did an awesome job but don't expect anything before months.
This is NOT a way to get a beta key.

Activision Blizzard Announcement
We’re excited to announce that our planned team-up with Activision, first made public back in December 2007, is now a reality. All regulatory and shareholder approvals have been granted, meaning Blizzard Entertainment -- along with the other companies that made up Vivendi Games -- is now part of a new organization called Activision Blizzard. While this transition doesn’t impact our games, websites, personnel, day-to-day operations, or development philosophies, the combined resources of Activision Blizzard will make it easier than ever for us to deliver the best games possible to our players around the world.
