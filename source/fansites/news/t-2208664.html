Breaching the Tomb - Mark of the Sentinax
The next step of Breaching the Tomb (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/11546-breaching-the-tomb) is available now. Head to the Broken Shore to pick up Mark of the Sentinax (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/47139-mark-of-the-sentinax) from Illidan. This will also grant another 1500 Armies of Legionfall reputation.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/april/thumb/dadgar.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/april/dadgar.jpg)


World Boss - Shar'thos
The Sleeping Corruption (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/42779-the-sleeping-corruption) is up this week, so you can head to Val'sharah to kill Shar'thos (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/108678-sharthos) for a chance at item level 860 loot, a Shadow Relic (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/141518-decaying-dragonfang), and a piece of the Fury Warrior hidden artifact appearance (http://www.wowdb.com/items/140659-skull-of-sharthos).


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2016/november/wbshart.jpg (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/595-Legion-World-Bosses#sharthos)


Weekly Event - Wrath of the Lich King Timewalking
Wrath of the Light King Timewalking (http://www.wowdb.com/world-events/562-timewalking-dungeon-event) is back this week.


The weekly quest (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/44166-a-frozen-path-through-time) from Archmage Timear (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/111246-archmage-timear) rewards a Accolade of Victory (http://www.wowdb.com/items/140176-accolade-of-victory), Seal of Broken Fate (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1273-seal-of-broken-fate), and Cache of Nightborne Treasures (http://www.wowdb.com/items/140150-cache-of-nightborne-treasures). The cache can contain tier set pieces.
You can find Auzin (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/98690-auzin) in old Dalaran.
Don't forget to keep working towards Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (http://www.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk), Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (http://www.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger), and Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade)!
Reins of the Infinite Timereaver (http://www.wowdb.com/items/133543-reins-of-the-infinite-timereaver) can also drop from any Timewalking dungeon boss.
