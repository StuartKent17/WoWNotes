Patch 3.2 Mounts
After the companion's pet preview yesterday, it's time to post a small preview of the mounts added in Patch 3.2.

Argent Charger / Argent Warhorse
The Argent Charger (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/47179/argent-charger/) (Paladin Only) and the Argent Warhorse (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/47180/argent-warhorse/) (All classes) are sold by the new Quartermaster only available to players who achieved Exalted Argent Champion of the Alliance (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/2817/exalted-argent-champion-of-the-alliance/) / Horde (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/2816/exalted-argent-champion-of-the-horde/) and got the Crusader title, you can buy them for 100 x Champion's Seal (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/44990/champions-seal/) each.

The Ochre Skeletal Warhorse (epic) and Striped Dawnsabre (rare) have been added so that each faction now has 18 rare and 15 epic racial ground mounts.

Post 3.2 the racial mounts will be as follows:
Humans, Night Elves, Gnomes, Orcs, Forsaken and Blood elves each have 4 rare and 3 epic mounts.

Draenei, Dwarfs, Troll and Tauren have 3 rare and 3 epic ground mounts.

To make it even across the board, The Dwarfs, Draenei, Trolls and Tauren will have to be given a new rare ground mount each.

That will probably be implemented before faction changes. It will make the race change a teeny bit less complicated.
