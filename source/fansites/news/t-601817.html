Update : All screenshots are now updated with 2.2 PTR / 2.1 Live comparison.

The amount of reagents needed for several enchantments (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/august/enchantchanges3.jpg) has been lowered.
[item=21756] cooldown has been changed from 30 minutes to 20 minutes.

[item=23576] required level has been changed from 55 to 60
[item=30067], [item=32540], and [item=32541] now requires 300 in enchantment skill to be disenchanted.




Tigole interview on Mondespersistants.com
Mondespersistants.com (http://wow.mondespersistants.com/?article=403&page_art=2), a french fansite, has interviewed Tigole and Chris Robinson during the Games Convention, the video and the transcript (in both english and french) is now available here (http://wow.mondespersistants.com/?article=403&page_art=2).

As usual here is a short summary :
Arthas will "use" both alliance and horde players to defeat things that cannot be defeated by his brainless minions, the same way the Lich King used Arthas in the first place.
Death Knight will probably be able to use and inscribe any weapon with runes. (Even if they might add a few Death Knight specific weapons)
Few things about instance we already know, Azjol'Nerub will be a mega-huge underground zone full of spiders, the Nexus will have 3 instances and you will fight Malygos in the last one (lvl 80, 25 players).
They might change old battlegrounds like AV, but they're still not sure if they will be able to do it because of the heavy changes it implies.
Mal'ganis (http://www.wowwiki.com/Mal'Ganis) will be the final boss of CoT : Stratholme.
Sunwell Plateau will have 6 bosses in the 25 man raid dungeon, and a 5 people normal and heroic instance.
A "famous character" is trying to re-ignite the sunwell and summons Kil'jaeden back.
