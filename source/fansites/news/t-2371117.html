Patch 7.3.5 Hotfixes - January 24
Collections

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idemonhunters.gif Demon Hunters can now loot companion pets from Throne of Thunder (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/6622-throne-of-thunder) and Siege of Orgrimmar (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/6738-siege-of-orgrimmar).


Dungeons and Raids

Siege of Orgrimmar (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/6738-siege-of-orgrimmar)

Sha of Pride (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/71734-sha-of-pride) will no longer use Banishment (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/146823-banishment) against a solo player in Mythic difficulty.




Quests

The Hyjal (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/616-mount-hyjal) quest "Good News... and Bad News (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/29066-good-news-and-bad-news)" can now be accepted from Matoclaw (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/39928-matoclaw) if abandoned.
The Teldrassil (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/141-teldrassil) quest "The Sprouted Fronds (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/2399-the-sprouted-fronds)" will no longer appear on the map to players who cannot accept it.
Players now receive Augmented Arcane Prison (http://www.wowdb.com/items/35671-augmented-arcane-prison) from Raelorasz (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/26117-raelorasz) if they’re standing more than 3 yards away from him, for “Mustering the Reds (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/11967-mustering-the-reds)”.
Reduced the difficulty of the Drakuru (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/26423-drakuru) encounter during the Zul'Drak (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/66-zuldrak) quest "Betraya (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/12713-betrayal)l".
Several Silithus (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/1377-silithus) creatures now level scale all the way down to 40 for "Vyral the Vile (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/8321-vyral-the-vile)".
When it’s “Your First Day as a Pirate (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/26631-your-first-day-as-a-pirate)”, Captain Keelhaul (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/2548-captain-keelhaul) should no longer fall out of his pirate ship.


World

The portals to Deepholm (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/5042-deepholm) can now be used by level 82 players, as intended.
