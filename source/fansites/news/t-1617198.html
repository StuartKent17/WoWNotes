Warlords of Draenor - Creeping Carpet and Dustmane Direwolf
Today we are taking a look at two of the mounts you can craft in Warlords of Draenor, the Creeping Carpet (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/169952-creeping-carpet) and Dustmane Direwolf (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/171844-dustmane-direwolf).

The Creeping Carpet (http://www.wowdb.com/items/115363-creeping-carpet) can be crafted by tailors. It requires 100 x Hexweave Cloth (http://www.wowdb.com/items/111556-hexweave-cloth), which is a daily cooldown that makes 10 per day at max Tailoring skill. The Tailoring Emporium (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/128-tailoring-emporium) can help to produce additional cloth. It also requires 20 x Sorcerous Air (http://www.wowdb.com/items/113264-sorcerous-air).

The Riding Harness (http://www.wowdb.com/items/108883-riding-harness) works in a similar way, requiring 100 x Burnished Leather (http://www.wowdb.com/items/110611-burnished-leather) and 20 x Sorcerous Air (http://www.wowdb.com/items/113264-sorcerous-air).

World of Warcraft Arena Championship Opening Weekend
One week from today, on October 31, the top eight 3v3 Arena teams in the world will converge in ESL’s studios in Burbank, CA to commence the BlizzCon 2014 World of Warcraft Arena Championship, and you’re invited to watch the action free in HD.

Brackets and Matchups
In this first round, two groups of four teams will compete to advance to Anaheim. The two randomly-drawn groups are:

Group A

EU – Skill-Capped EU
NA – Penny for Your Thought
CN – Popped CD No Skill
NA – 3 Amigos


Group B

EU – Playing with Fire
KR – Push Push
EU – Bleached Bones
NA – Death Innovators


Six matches will be played on October 31:

Skill-Capped EU vs. 3 Amigos
Penny for Your Thought vs. Popped CD No Skill
Playing with Fire vs. Death Innovators
Push Push vs. Bleached Bones
Group A: Winner vs. Winner
Group B: Winner vs. Winner

All eight teams will still be in the game heading to Anaheim the following week, with the group winners sitting pretty in possession of two of the final four spots in the semi-finals.

Schedule
The action kicks off at 12:00 noon PDT on October 31, with each match set to start at the top of the hour following the previous match.
