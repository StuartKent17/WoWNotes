http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/november/level80worldfirst_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/november/level80worldfirst.jpg)

MMO-Champion: Hello Nymh, and congratulations for your level 80 ! Could you tell us a bit more about yourself ?
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Nymh: I play a warlock named Nymh (http://eu.wowarmory.com/character-sheet.xml?r=Drek%27thar&n=Nymh) on the french server Drek'thar. I used to be the guild leader of Harmonie, a french top PvE guild who was among the first 10 guilds to kill Kil'jaeden in France, but we eventually decided to merge with the 2nd guild of our server. The result is Genesis, which was originally an pre-BC high level guild. In real life I'm 21 and currently employed, I took a few days of vacation for the release of the expansion.

MMO-Champion: You reached the max level 27 hours after the european servers went online, that's pretty fast even if you spend all your time leveling ... Could you tell us how you achieved that ?
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Nymh: The idea first came up when I saw this post (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?topic=16919.0) from Jynxx (http://www.wowarmory.com/character-sheet.xml?r=Turalyon&n=Jynxx). The idea looked nice and I decided to run a few tests on beta servers. It took about 3 hours to level from 78 to 79 and considering that it was one of the longest level, it seemed good enough to use this strategy to powerlevel at the release.

The biggest problem was to find a spot with enough monsters to grind without too much downtime ... I spent some time on beta servers and eventually found the crypt in Drakil'ji Ruins (Grizzly Hills), I think that's one of the only place in Wrath of the Lich King where monsters repop in about 30 seconds, mobs here are also neutral and it makes grinding a little safer.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/wotlk/zones/small/grizzlyhills_64.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/wotlk/zones/grizzlyhills_64.jpg) http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/wotlk/zones/small/grizzlyhills_65.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/wotlk/zones/grizzlyhills_65.jpg) http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/wotlk/zones/small/grizzlyhills_66.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/wotlk/zones/grizzlyhills_66.jpg) http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/wotlk/zones/small/grizzlyhills_67.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/wotlk/zones/grizzlyhills_67.jpg)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/november/drakiljinmap_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/november/drakiljinmap.jpg)

I also had to optimize my downtimes and decided to level with a healer out of party, Pavelomm (http://eu.wowarmory.com/character-sheet.xml?r=Drek'Thar&n=Pavelomm) was nice enough to spend the last 27 hours healing me without taking any XP and he obviously played a huge role in the process.

When I reached level 76 I realized that my leveling speed was slightly lower than what I experienced in beta, I decided to change the template from the original post (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?topic=16919.0) and switched to something with 2 points in Shadow Mastery to make my DoTs stronger and the leveling a bit easier. (It usually takes 2 DoTs to kill a monster)

MMO-Champion: The first level 80 was supposed to be Darus, a paladin who did some powerleveling in instances with Athene and a few other people and eventually got suspended by Blizzard at level 79. (and reseted to level 70 a few hours later) Did you hear about him ?
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Nymh: Yeah I had a few people monitoring other servers to give me updates about the situation here, I don't really know how Darus did that but I assume it will be hotfixed by Blizzard very soon.
