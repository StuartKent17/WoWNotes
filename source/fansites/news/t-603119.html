PvP

2.3 Patch
Warrior shouts and some feral druid spells can't be casted for free after a resurrection in a BG, this is working as intended. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2856197212&postId=28557112007&sid=1#4))

If you're doing well in Karazhan, you should be able to go right into Zul'aman. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2856007253&postId=28557410073&sid=1#2))



Wrath of the Lich King
There are no new races to let the dev team focus on Death Knights, Northrend, and all of the other features. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2856216361&postId=28557408184&sid=1#27))
