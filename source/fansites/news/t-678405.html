Patch 3.3 - PTR Build 10805
A new build has been deployed on PTRs, stay tuned for more changes!

The Halls of Reflection (Last wing of the 5-man dungeon) are now available for testing

Mounts
2 Frostbrood Mounts ? Smells like Glory of the Icecrown Raider ... But at this time the rewards on the achievements are still placeholders, wait & see.

Bloodbathed Frostbrood Vanquisher (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/72808/) - Summons and dismisses a rideable Bloodbathed Frostbrood Vanquisher. This is an extremely fast mount. This mount can only be summoned in Outland or Northrend.
Icebound Frostbrood Vanquisher (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/72807/) - Summons and dismisses a rideable Icebound Frostbrood Vanquisher. This is an extremely fast mount. This mount can only be summoned in Outland or Northrend.


Achievements
Feats of Strength have been added to the game, Invincible (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/october/flyinghorse_updated.jpg) is now a confirmed drop from Arthas!

Invincible's Reins (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4625/) - Obtain Invincible from Arthas in Icecrown Citadel.
Big Love Rocket (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4627/) - Obtain the Big Love Rocket from Apothecary Hummel in Shadowfang Keep during Love is in the Air.
And I'll Form the Head! (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4626/) - Obtain Mimiron's Head from 25-player Yogg-Saron with no Keepers assisting you.

Icecrown achievements have been updated and do not look like placeholders anymore.

Storming the Citadel (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4604/) - Defeat the first four bosses in Icecrown Citadel in 25-player mode.
The Crimson Hall (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4606/) - Defeat the bosses of The Crimson Hall in Icecrown Citadel in 25-player mode.
The Frostwing Halls (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4607/) - Defeat the bosses of The Frostwing Halls in Icecrown Citadel in 25-player mode.
Boned (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4610/) - Defeat Lord Marrowgar without anyone taking damage from Bone Cyclone in 25-player mode.
Full House (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4611/) - Defeat Lady Deathwhisper with at least five different types of Cultists active at the time of her demise in 25-player mode.
I'm on a Boat (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4612/) - Claim victory in the Gunship Battle without any raid member visiting the enemy gunship more than once in 25-player mode.
I've Gone and Made a Mess (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4613/) - Defeat the Deathbringer before Mark of the Fallen Champion is cast three times in 25-player mode.
Dances with Oozes (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4614/) - Defeat Rotface without a Big Ooze casting Unstable Ooze Explosion in 25-player mode.
Flu Shot Shortage (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4615/) - Defeat Festergut while none of the players in your raid group ever had 3 stacks of Inoculated in 25-player mode.
Nausea, Heartburn, Indigestion... (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4616/) - Defeat Professor Putricide without using Regurgitated Ooze on the Abomination in 25-player mode.
The Orb Whisperer (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4617/) - Defeat the Blood Council without anyone in the raid taking more than 22,000 spell damage in a single hit.
Once Bitten, Twice Shy (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4618/) - Defeat the Blood Queen once as a vampire and again without becoming a vampire in 25-player mode.
Portal Jockey (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4619/) - Enter every portal spawned by Valithria Dreamwalker before healing her to full health in 25-player mode.
All You Can Eat (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4620/) - Defeat Sindragosa in 25-player mode without any member of your raid receiving more than 4 stacks of Mystic Buffet.
