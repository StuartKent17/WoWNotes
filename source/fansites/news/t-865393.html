Patch 4.2 - Firelands Raid Preview
Yesterday you had a sneak peek at the Firelands Daily Area (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/2202-Firelands-Daily-Area-Preview-Blizzard-Anniversary-Video-MMO-Report-Comics), it's time to take a look at the only thing you all want to see: the Firelands Raid instance!

Just like the daily area, it's a little hard to gather a lot of information about it because the instance isn't even supposed to be in this PTR patch but hopefully the couple of screenshots/maps below will be enough while you wait a few more months (you could probably count in weeks, Patch 4.2 won't be far behind Patch 4.1) before you see it on live realms.


Firelands Raid - Encounters
According to the Blizzcon 2010 Raids & Dungeons Panel (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/2034-Blizzcon-2010-Raids-Dungeons-Panel-Press-Trailer), the Firelands raid will have 7 bosses. We already know 3 of them from achievement icones:



Fandral Staghelm (http://www.wowpedia.org/Fandral_Staghelm), former Archdruid of Darnassus.
Alysra (http://www.wowpedia.org/Alysra), the green drake in charge of transporting Fandral to a safe location at the end of the Hyjal questlines in Cataclysm. (Note: Just because there's an icon related to Alysra doesn't mean it's really a boss, in the case of Valithria Dreamwalker (http://www.wowpedia.org/Valithria_Dreamwalker) the goal of the encounter was to save her)
Ragnaros the Firelord (http://www.wowpedia.org/Ragnaros), that one isn't really a surprise, right? And he's so pretty now! (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2011/february/ragnaros2.jpg)
