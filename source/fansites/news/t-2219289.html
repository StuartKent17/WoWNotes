FEEDBACK: Timewalking Raid: Black Temple
Black Temple Timewalking is now open for testing. We expect to keep the zone open for the entire weekend. A minimum of 8 players is required to queue for this event. Players need to queue via Vormu found in the Terrace of Light, Shattrath.

Note: Using the Dungeon Finder option to queue for Black Temple will not function properly.

Please use this thread for feedback and bug reports regarding the raid, or related issues. Noting the nature of your group (Mythic guild premade, PUG, etc.) is helpful, as are any logs.

Artifact Knowledge Catch Up
There is still the catch-up timer for Artifact Knowledge beyond Rank 25.

Also for alts, I'm sure many of you are aware already, but for those who aren't - Your main can buy Artifact Research Compendiums after every 5th rank of Artifact Knowledge (AK). For example, at AK35 your main will be able to buy a compendium with Order Resources that can be sent to alts to immediately get that alt to AK30. This continues right the way up to AK50 where you main will be able to send AK45 compendiums to alts. (Blue Tracker (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/1061233-no-research-catch-up-in-72-after-ak-25/) / Official Forums (http://eu.battle.net/forums/en/wow/topic/17615463181?page=3#50))

Enhancement Shaman 4pc Set Bonus
The intention of the set bonus was to only increase the damage of the initial hit of Crash Lightning, not all of the cleave damage from using Stormstrike/Lava Lash after pressing Crash Lightning. I made a change to it that will show up in a future build to work this way.
