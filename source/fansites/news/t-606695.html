Arena class representation
Vhairi (http://vhairi.blogspot.com/2008/04/us-stats-april-2008.html) released a bunch of interesting stats on his blog (http://vhairi.blogspot.com/2008/04/us-stats-april-2008.html), analyzing the amount of players playing each class in Arena Tournaments for all 3 brackets.
2v2 Arena is clearly dominated by Druids and Warriors, followed by Rogues and Warlocks. At high level the population of Mages, Paladins, Hunters, and Shamans is almost non-existant compared to the first 4 classes.
3v3 isn't really different, Warriors, Druids, and Rogues are still on top, with the additions of Priests. At high level the amount of warlocks match the other "unpopular" classes.
5v5 is where all the non-druid healers hide, with Priests, Shamans, and Paladins on top. The most popular non-healing classes are Warriors and Warlocks.
