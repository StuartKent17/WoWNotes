Patch 3.3.3 - PTR Build 11643
A new build has been deployed on test realms. So far it doesn't look like there are a lot of changes out there but stay tuned, test realms can be full of surprises!

Pets / Mounts
Lil' XT (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/75906/lil-xt/) finally got its own model, the name is VERY similar to Lil' K.T. (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/69677/lil-k-t/) and it wouldn't be surprising to see this pet appear on the Blizzard Store at some point.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2010/march/lilxt_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2010/march/lilxt.jpg)

On a sidenote, both Celestial Steed (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/76153/celestial-steed/) and X-53 Touring Rocket (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/76154/x-53-touring-rocket/) got their own spell and are confirmed to be 310% flying mount.
Celestial Steed Video (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8KvDDFi0Fuo)
X-53 Touring Rocket Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2010/march/rocketmount3.jpg)

Spell Comparison
These changes are generated by comparing the data available in the client, keep in mind that they could be temporary (or just wrong) if they are not in the official notes.
Items
Item - Coliseum 25 Normal Healer Trinket (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/67698/) - Now affects all spells instead of just helpful spells.
Item - Coliseum 25 Heroic Healer Trinket (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/67752/) - Now affects all spells instead of just helpful spells.

Icecrown Citadel
Strength of Wrynn (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/73762/) and Hellscream's Warsong (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/73816/) now also boost the effect of damage absorption spells.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ideathknights.gif Death Knight (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/class-death-knight/) / Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?deathknight))
Glyphs
Glyph of Icebound Fortitude (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/58717/) now always grants at least 40% damage reduction.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/class-priest/) / Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?priest))
Glyphs
Glyph of Scourge Imprisonment (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/56179/) now reduces the cast time of your Shackle Undead by 1 sec. (Up from 0.5 sec)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/class-rogue/) / Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?rogue))
Glyphs
Glyph of Feint (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/57149/) now reduces the energy cost of Feint by 20. (Up from 10)
Glyph of Expose Armor (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/57148/) now increases the duration of Expose Armor by 12 sec. (Up from 10 sec)
Glyph of Deadly Throw (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/57145/) now increases the slowing effect on Deadly Throw by 20%. (Up from 10%)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock
Dark Pact (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/59092/) now has a 100 yards range (up from 30 yards) and ignores the line of sight.


Official Notes Update
The Patch 3.3.3 PTR Notes (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=23329393385&sid=1) have been updated to reflect the changes made in this build.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/class-warlock/) / Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?warlock))
Dark Pact (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/59092/): This ability no longer requires line-of-sight with the summoned demon, and the range has been increased from 30 yards to 100 yards.

Technical
Frame Rate: A maximum capacity of 200 frames per second has been added. To disable the frame rate limit, the following line should be added to the Config.wtf file: SET maxFPS "0".
