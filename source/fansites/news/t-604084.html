The idea was interesting, but quickly dismissed by Bornakk : You won't be able to "sacrifice" a character to get an improved Death Knight character by carrying over reputation, titles, equipment, or just getting a slightly higher level than newly created Death Knight characters.
If you want to "sacrifice" a character, you are welcome to role-play it out that way, but we don't plan to force this on players just to try out a new class. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=3709656595&postId=36863851921&sid=1#4))

Hero Classes
It's always nice to have an official statement about this with all the rumors (Blademaster, Necromancer, etc ...) going around since the announcement of WotLK. Nothing is decided yet, period.
This just about covers it. We have announced and are developing the first (and currently only) Hero Class, the Deathknight. Because of this a lot of discussions have come up regarding future Hero Classes and the Demon Hunter is near the top of the "most often brought up" list due to its popularity and strong lore background.

Would a Demon Hunter be fun to play? Yes, but so would a number of other possibilities.

To be straight to the point: We have not announced any other Hero Classes at this point in time and we will have to wait and see what the future holds after the Deathknight makes its impact on Azeroth.
