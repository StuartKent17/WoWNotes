World of Warcraft Chronicle Volume 3 Art
FANTOM Print (https://www.facebook.com/FANTOMprint/posts/10155907013317497) released some art from the upcoming WoW Chronicle Volume 3. You can preorder World of Warcraft Chronicle Volume 3 for the March 27, 2018 release now! According to IGN (http://www.ign.com/articles/2017/07/17/world-of-warcraft-chronicle-vol-3-coming-from-blizzard-and-dark-horse), this one focuses on the events that shaped the modern history of Azeroth and the backstories of Arthas, Illidan, Jaina, and other iconic characters.

You can purchase Volume 1 (http://amzn.to/2us2WwM) and Volume 2 (http://amzn.to/2vaEpts) on Amazon as well.
