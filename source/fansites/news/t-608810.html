Bornakk on Zul'aman
Zul'Aman does have a fairly steep curve in difficulty, but having only two 10-person dungeons makes it so having a challenge like this in clearing it makes sense. This can also be seen as an example of why having separate progression paths for 10 and 25 person dungeons in Wrath of the Lich King will likely work out better.

This dungeon can also be a pretty clear example of how Badge of Justice gear that you gather from running Karazhan and Zul'Aman can help get a group prepared to take things on.

When Zul'Aman was released the "bear runs" were designed for players in high-end gear and at the time even they had issues completing it consistently if at all. Keep in mind that the game evolves over time and the players along with it and as they learn the strategies, what to kill, what the best order for things is, it all becomes easier. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=7116597023&sid=1&pageNo=12#228))

WWI Dance and Costume Contest Sign Ups Open Now!
So you want to show off your skills to the world by participating in one of the 2008 Blizzard Entertainment Worldwide Invitational contests? From Monday June 16th until June 20th, you can register to participate in the Dance and Costume contests that will take place at the Worldwide Invitational on June 28th. Just head over to the Contest Registration (http://eu.blizzard.com/wwi08/contest.xml?locale=en_GB) webform and let us know you'll be joining in! Also, if you would like to get advice or opinions on your costume, of if you simply want to show off your work in progress, don't hesitate to share your creation with the community in this thread (http://forums.wow-europe.com/thread.html?topicId=4357315962&sid=1).

Also, we are extending the deadline for submissions for the Fan Art and Machinima contests! Artists and movie-makers have until the end of Wednesday, June 18th to get their submissions to us! Check out the contest information page (http://eu.blizzard.com/wwi08-contest/contest-form.html?locale=en_GB) to learn more!

A note to our movie-makers: any music included in your submissions must either be your own original work or from a Blizzard product. Use of copyrighted music is grounds for disqualification.

World of Warcraft: Ashbringer # 1

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/june/wow-ashbringer_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/june/wow-ashbringer.jpg)"Yes ! It is finally revealed ! Wildstorm and Blizzard Entertainment bring fans of the World of Warcraft MMORPG a spinoff Limited Series spanning four-issues telling one of the most craved in-game storylines: The Ashbringer. The name of Mograine is finally revealed. This story will strongly tie-in with the new quests in the upcoming World of Warcraft: Wrath of the Lich King."

As the Lich King's plague of undeath ravages the human kingdom of Lordaeron, a mighty blade is forged from a dark orb! In the hands of Highlord Alexandros Mograine, the blade will become legend. In time, both blade and man will become . . . Ashbringer. In anticipation of the eagerly awaited game expansion, WORLD OF WARCRAFT: WRATH OF THE LICH KING, WildStorm and Blizzard have created this 4-issue miniseries written by Blizzard Creative Director Micky Neilson and featuring art by Ludo Lullabi and Tony Washington.

This issue will feature two covers. For every 10 copies of the Standard Edition (with a cover by Chris Robinson), retailers may order one copy of the Variant Edition (with a cover by Lullabi and Washington).

The comic will be on sale on September 10th. Make sure you read the full article on Blizzplanet.com for more details (http://www.blizzplanet.com/) !
