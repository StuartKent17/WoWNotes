Brewfest 2015
Brewfest (http://www.wowdb.com/world-events/372-brewfest) is back again, with item level 670 trinkets, a few new rewards, and heirloom weapon upgrades! This year the event runs from September 20 to October 6. You will need 500 x Brewfest Prize Token (http://www.wowdb.com/items/37829-brewfest-prize-token) to purchase the new rewards.

If you are still leveling alts, don't forget to grab the Brewfest Enthusiast (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/42138) buff at 6:15 AM and 6:15 PM server time outside of Ironforge and Orgrimmar at the Tapping of the Keg ceremony.
