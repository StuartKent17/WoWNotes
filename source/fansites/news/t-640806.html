Patch 3.1.0 - PTR Build 9733
A new build has been deployed on test realms, stay tuned for all the changes!

Talent Calculator - Build 9733
The Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com) has been updated to the latest build.

Glyph Changes
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid
Glyph of Survival Instincts - Your Survival Instincts ability grants an additional 10% of your maximum health.
Glyph of Savage Roar - Your Savage Roar ability grants an additional 3% bonus damage done. (Down from 6%)
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/images.gif Mage
Glyph of Molten Armor - Your Molten Armor grants an additional 20% (Up from 15%) of your spirit as critical strike rating.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest
Glyph of Hymn of Hope - Your Hymn of Hope lasts an additional 2 sec.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock
Glyph of Succubus - Your Succubus's Seduction ability also removes all damage over time effects from the target. (Old - Increases the duration of your Succubus's Seduction by 3 sec.)


Class Spell Changes
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/deathknight.jpg Death Knight (3.1 Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=650) / 3.1 Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?deathknight))
Frost
Howling Blast (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=51411) cooldown has been reduced from 10 sec to 8 sec.
Rime (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=59057) now has a 15% chance to reset the cooldown on Howling Blast and cause your next Howling Blast to consume no runes.
Unholy
Death Strike (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=49924) damage has been lowered, it now deals 60% weapon damage (down from 75%) plus 178.2 (down from 222.75) for Rank 5.
Night of the Dead (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=55623) has been moved from Tier 8 to Tier 4.
Ghoul Frenzy (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63560) has been moved from Tier 6 to Tier 7.
Master of Ghouls (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=52143) has been moved from Tier 4 to Tier 6.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/druid.jpg Druid (3.1 Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=700) / 3.1 Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?druid))
Feral
Primal Gore (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63503) doesn't affect Rake anymore.
Restoration
Revitalize (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=48545) now gives your Rejuvenation and Wild Growth spells have a 5/10/15% chance to restore 8 Energy, 4 Rage, 1% Mana or 16 Runic Power per tick. (Old - No info on the % in the tooltip)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/mage.jpg Mage (3.1 Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=670) / 3.1 Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?mage))
Fire
Living Bomb (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=55362) AOE damage has been greatly increased. Rank 3 now deals 690 Fire damage (up from 306) to all enemies within 10 yards.

Improved Scorch (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=12873) now Increases your chance to critically hit with Scorch, Fireball and Frostfire Bolt by an additional 1/2/3%. (Old - Scorch only, 3/6/9%)
Frost
Ice Barrier (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=43039) now prevents spellcasting from being delayed by damage. (Old - prevented spells from interruption)
Winter's Chill (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=28593) now also Increases your chance to critically hit with Frostbolt by an additional 1/2/3%.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/paladin.jpg Paladin (3.1 Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=610) / 3.1 Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?paladin))
Retribution
Seal of the Martyr (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=53720) now makes all your melee attacks deal [ 48% of weapon damage ] (up from 22%) additional Holy damage. Unleashing this Seal's energy instantly causes [ 11% of AP + 18% of Spell Power + 26% of min weapon damage ] to [ 11% of AP + 18% of Spell Power + 26% of max weapon damage ] (Down from 16/25/36%) Holy damage at the cost of health equal to 33% of the damage caused.
Seal of Blood (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=31892) now makes all your melee attacks deal [ 48% of weapon damage ] (up from 22%) additional Holy damage. Unleashing this Seal's energy instantly causes [ 11% of AP + 18% of Spell Power + 26% of min weapon damage ] to [ 11% of AP + 18% of Spell Power + 26% of max weapon damage ] (Down from 16/25/36%) Holy damage at the cost of health equal to 33% of the damage caused.
Crusader's Strike (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=35395) has been reverted back to normal - An instant strike that causes 110% weapon damage.
Protection
Divine Guardian (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=53530) *New Talent* - Tier 4 - Improve the effectiveness of your Divine Sacrifice spell by an additional 5/10% and increases the duration of your Sacred Shield by 50/100% and the amount absorbed by 10/20%.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/rogue.jpg Rogue (3.1 Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=630) / 3.1 Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?rogue))
Assassination
Lethality (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=14137) now affects all combo point-generating abilities. (Old - all combo moves)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/shaman.jpg Shaman (3.1 Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=660) / 3.1 Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?shaman))
Elemental
Totem of Wrath (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=57722) range has been changed from 30 to 40 yards.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/warrior.jpg Warrior (3.1 Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=600) / 3.1 Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?warrior))
Arms
Shattering Throw (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64382) will now reduce the armor on the target by 20% for 10 sec or remove any invulnerabilities. (Old - reduced armor AND removed any invulnerabilities)
Bladestorm (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=46924) will now instantly Whirlwind up to 4 nearby targets.
Juggernaut (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64976) now affects both Slam and Mortal Strike
Fury
Improved Berserker Stance (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=29763) now increases strength by 4/8/12/16/20%. (Up from 3/6/9/12/15%)
Protection
