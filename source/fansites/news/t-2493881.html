Fabious Mount Solved
The Secret Finding Discord (https://discord.gg/wowsecrets) has solved Fabious (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/169201-fabious)! You'll need to break out your S.E.L.F.I.E. Camera MkII (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/122674-s-e-l-f-i-e-camera-mkii) and head to 25, 29 in Nazjatar. Once Fabious spawns, you'll need to take a selfie with the NPC and the mount will be yours!

Rank 4 Essence Required Rank 3 Before Unlock
Earning Rank 3 of an Essence is a prerequisite to unlocking its Rank 4.
At the same time, some activities that that are required for Rank 4, such as earning a 2400 rating in PvP, can be accumulated while you’re still at Ranks 1 and 2.


Nazjatar Quest Reward Weapon Item Level
The quest reward weapons for one of the early quest in Nazjatar was 415 on ptr release build, but on life they’re only 370.


Mobile App Update with Rise of Azshara
There is an update to the app that came today with Rise of Azshara/8.2. It may take a while for the update to propagate out but we have confirmed that people are receiving it. Keep an eye out for updates on your device and you will be able to use the app again soon.
