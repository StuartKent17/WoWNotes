Patch 3.2 Companion Pets
This patch will have its share of new companion pets and most of them have been discovered on PTRs now.

Oracle / Wolvar Orphans
Curious Wolvar Pup (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/46544/curious-wolvar-pup/) and Curious Oracle Hatchling (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/46545/curious-oracle-hatchling/) are the new pet rewards for the Children's Week (http://www.wowwiki.com/Children%27s_Week). These orphans have their own questlines in Northrend

Playmates! (http://db.mmo-champion.com/q/13950/playmates/)
The Bronze Dragonshrine (http://db.mmo-champion.com/q/13933/the-bronze-dragonshrine/)
Home Of The Bear-Men (http://db.mmo-champion.com/q/13930/home-of-the-bear-men/)
The Dragon Queen (http://db.mmo-champion.com/q/13954/the-dragon-queen/)
The Mighty Hemet Nesingwary (http://db.mmo-champion.com/q/13957/the-mighty-hemet-nesingwary/)
A Visit To The Wonderworks (http://db.mmo-champion.com/q/13938/a-visit-to-the-wonderworks/)
Back To The Orphanage (http://db.mmo-champion.com/q/13960/back-to-the-orphanage/)

You will receive a mail a moment later with your Wolvar/Oracle pet attached.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/july/oraclehatchling_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/july/oraclehatchling.jpg) http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/july/wolvarhatchling_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/july/wolvarhatchling.jpg)

Shimerring Wyrmling
The Shimmering Wyrmling (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/46820/shimmering-wyrmling/) is sold by the Silver Covenant and Sunreavers Quartermasters, you can buy it for 40 x Champion's Seal (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/44990/champions-seal/). It isn't bound on pick up but it can only be used by people exalted with The sunreavers / The Silver Convenant depending on your faction.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/july/shimmeringwyrmling_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/july/shimmeringwyrmling.jpg)

Raptors
8 New raptor companion pets have been added to the game in this patch. You can find a lot of them on rare elite raptors throughout the world, the droprate is currently 100% for all of them.

Darting Hatchling (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/48112/darting-hatchling/) (SShot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/july/dartinghatchling.jpg)) drops from Dart (http://www.wowhead.com/?npc=14232). (Rare Elite - Barrens)
Deviate Hatchling (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/48114/deviate-hatchling/) (SShot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/july/deviatehatchling.jpg)) is a rare drop from the raptors in the Wailing Caverns instance. (Barrens)
Gundrak Hatchling (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/67415/gundrak-hatchling/) (SShot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/july/gundrakhatchling.jpg)) seems to be a rare drop from the Gundrak Raptors (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?npc=29334) in Zul'Drak.
Leaping Hatchling (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/48118/leaping-hatchling/) (SShot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/july/leapinghatchling.jpg)) drops from Takk the Leaper (http://www.wowhead.com/?npc=5842). (Rare Elite - Barrens)

Ravasaur Hatchling (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/48122/ravasaur-hatchling/) (SShot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/july/ravasaurhatchling.jpg)) drops from Ravasaur Matriarch (http://www.wowhead.com/?npc=6581). (Rare Elite - Un'Goro Crater)
Razormaw Hatchling (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/48124/razormaw-hatchling/) (SShot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/july/razormawhatchling.jpg)) drops from Razormaw Matriarch (http://www.wowhead.com/?npc=1140). (Rare Elite - Wetlands)
Razzashi hatchling (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/67420/razzashi-hatchling/) (SShot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/july/razzashihatchling.jpg)) is a rare drop from Razzashi Raptors (http://www.wowhead.com/?npc=14821) in Zul'Gurub.
