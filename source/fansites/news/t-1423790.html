Warcraft Movie Cast Announcement
Legendary announced (https://twitter.com/Legendary/status/413461860550070272) that Daniel Wu (http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0943079/) and Clancy Brown (http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000317/) will also be joining the Warcraft movie cast. Daniel Wu recently appeared in Europa Report and Clancy Brown regularly appears in and does voices for TV shows. It isn't his first time working on a Warcraft project though, as he was Thrall (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aZ6tg1Id6-c&list=PL780265A4FFFD41E8#t=3) in the cancelled Warcraft Adventures: Lord of the Clans (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Warcraft_Adventures:_Lord_of_the_Clans) game.
