Azerite Knowledge Continues This Week
As a reminder, Blizzard is extending Azerite Knowledge until the week of April 23rd.
With scheduled weekly maintenance in each region, we will resume the Azerite Knowledge effect next week.

Azerite Knowledge will then continue for four more weeks, with its final increment for Season 2 beginning the week of April 23 and ending with weekly maintenance the week of April 30.

We feel like this is the right way to make sure that Azerite powers on current gear are more broadly attainable for as many players as possible.

In this region, weekly maintenance should begin at 7:00 a.m. PDT on Tuesday, April 2.


Bonus Event - Cataclysm Timewalking
Cataclysm Timewalking (https://www.wowdb.com/world-events/587-timewalking-dungeon-event) is back this week.


The weekly quest (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53034-a-shattered-path-through-time) from your dungeon guide rewards Azerite Power and Cache of Dazar'alor Treasures (https://www.wowdb.com/items/165724-cache-of-dazaralor-treasures).
You can find Kiatke (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/101759-kiatke) in Stormwind and Orgrimmar. Her unique items include Tosselwrench's Mega-Accurate Simulation Viewfinder (https://www.wowdb.com/items/133542-tosselwrenchs-mega-accurate-simulation-viewfinder) and Gurboggle's Gleaming Bauble (https://www.wowdb.com/items/133511-gurboggles-gleaming-bauble).
Don't forget to keep working towards Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk), Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger), Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade), Beastlord's Warwolf (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/167895-beastlords-warwolf), and Beastlord's Irontusk (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/167894-beastlords-irontusk)!
Reins of the Infinite Timereaver (https://www.wowdb.com/items/133543-reins-of-the-infinite-timereaver) can also drop from any Timewalking dungeon boss.
