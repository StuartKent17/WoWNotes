Raids & Dungeons
In 2.3, Badges of Justice drop from Zul'aman and Karazhan bosses (1-2 per bosses). You also get 2 badges from the heroic daily quest. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2518909551&postId=25741587220&sid=1#4))
Nether Vortexes won't drop from Zul'aman bosses, and no changes will be made to make them drop in the Black Temple or Hyjal summit. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2366661349&pageNo=27&sid=1#523))

PvP
Alterac Valley changes are being watched very closely on 2.3 PTRs, they will go live, but might be tweaked before. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2512088480&pageNo=1&sid=1#18))

Professions
The Mad Alchemist's Potions "side effect" is actually a random buff from other elixirs. However it won't replace existing elixir buffs if you have one on. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2517896652&pageNo=6&sid=1#114))

Items
Old versions of classic dungeons items will automatically be upgraded with the 2.3. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2518769315&pageNo=1&sid=1#2))

Interface
In 2.3 you will be able to check other people's talents by inspecting them. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=101150523&pageNo=15&sid=1#299))

Others
Blizzard is in the process of implementing alternate solutions to help assist the lower population realms. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2520305189&pageNo=8&sid=1#141))
