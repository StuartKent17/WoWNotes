Weekly News Recap
It has been a busy week, so here is your chance to catch up on any news you may have missed!


June 29

Guild leveling was removed (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/322503-missing-guild-perks/), we looked at the Death Knight and Rogue Tier 17 armor sets (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4115-Warlords-Menu-Death-Knight-and-Rogue-Tier-17-Horde-Garrison-Blue-Posts-News-Recap), as well as the latest version of the Horde garrison (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4115-Warlords-Menu-Death-Knight-and-Rogue-Tier-17-Horde-Garrison-Blue-Posts-News-Recap).

June 30

We looked at Shadowmoon Valley (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/887-Warlords-of-Draenor-Shadowmoon-Valley) and the latest version of the Alliance Garrison (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4116-Shadowmoon-Valley-Preview-Alliance-Garrison-Connected-Realms-Forging-Frostmourne).

July 1

The auction house will be cross-faction (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4119-Cross-Faction-Auction-House-in-Warlords-of-Draenor) in Warlords of Draenor.
We looked at Spires of Arak (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4124-Spires-of-Arak-Blue-Posts-Heroic-Strike-and-Beta-Iteration-OpenRaid-Tier-16-Event) and a blue post about warrior design (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4124-Spires-of-Arak-Blue-Posts-Heroic-Strike-and-Beta-Iteration-OpenRaid-Tier-16-Event) for Warlords of Draenor.

July 2

Warlords of Draenor Beta Build 18505 (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4127-Warlords-of-Draenor-Beta-Build-18505) was released, with many new additions:

A new legendary item: Corrupted Blood of Teron'gor (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/114240-corrupted-blood-of-terongor)
Hunter and Paladin Mythic Tier 17 sets.
Warlock Tier 17 sets.
Paladin and Rogue Elite Season 16 sets.
Monk Season 16 set.
Several new creatures and items.


July 3

Rob Pardo announced that he is leaving Blizzard Entertainment (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4131-Rob-Pardo-Leaving-Blizzard-Entertainment).
Jonathan LeCraft shared a Molten Corgi pet (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4134-Warlords-of-Draenor-Molten-Corgi-Pet) on Twitter.
Blizzard posted an official preview of Frostfire Ridge (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4129-Warlords-of-Draenor-Zone-Preview-Frostfire-Ridge).

July 4

Blizzard shared a little bit more of the Mists of Pandaria cinematic (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4137-Mists-of-Pandaria-Cinematic-Ending-Bottoms-Up!).
Chris Robinson gave us another update on the same face issue (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4138-New-Character-Model-Faces-Valorous-Buff-Live-Warrior-Changes-Blue-Posts-DLC-446).
Blizzard posted about the creatures found in Frostfire Ridge (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4138-New-Character-Model-Faces-Valorous-Buff-Live-Warrior-Changes-Blue-Posts-DLC-446).
Celestalon shared some upcoming warrior changes (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4138-New-Character-Model-Faces-Valorous-Buff-Live-Warrior-Changes-Blue-Posts-DLC-446).
The Heart of the Valorous (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/161795-heart-of-the-valorous) buff returned for the weekend.
