Love is in the Air
This year, Love is in the Air will run from February 2nd through the 16th. This event was based around Valentine’s Day, giving players a chance to give and receive gifts throughout Azeroth.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/february/thumb/liita04.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/february/liita04.jpg)

A quick update on changes to Holiday Loot with the launch of 'Love is in the Air' today:

When we changed Holiday dungeons to scale based on a player’s level, we aimed to increase participation in those dungeons. We also wanted to avoid a situation where a player with an army of low-level characters had a better chance at receiving account-wide rewards than a player with one or two max-level characters. At the same time, we didn't want to impact the amount of chances that players who maintain several max-level characters had in previous years.

As a compromise, we made account-wide rewards a random drop for any character capable of participating in current-expansion content. For the remainder of Legion, players level 98 and above fit that criteria. The minimum level requirement will increase again in Battle for Azeroth, and will continue to increase in future expansions.


New in 2018

Heirloom upgrades added to the event vendor.
Event specific cosmetic items can be transmogged.
Neck slot items are now item level 880.
Players that are lower level than current expansion content receive Heart-Shaped Carton (http://www.wowdb.com/items/147907-heart-shaped-carton), which doesn't contain the Big Love Rocket (http://www.wowdb.com/items/50250-big-love-rocket)



Lovely Charm Collection
To start collecting Lovely Charms (http://www.wowdb.com/items/49655-lovely-charm), players must purchase a Lovely Charm Collector's Kit (http://www.wowdb.com/items/49661-lovely-charm-collectors-kit) from Kwee Q. Peddlefeet (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/38039-kwee-q-peddlefeet). With it in their inventory, players will be rewarded with Lovely Charms (http://www.wowdb.com/items/49655-lovely-charm) whenever they fight enemies which would grant XP or Honor.


The Event Boss: Apothecary Hummel (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/36296-apothecary-hummel)

Gift to Faction Leaders
The Faction Leader daily quests are repeatable quests that require players to turn in a Lovely Charm Bracelet (http://www.wowdb.com/items/49916-lovely-charm-bracelet) to the leader of their choice. The rewards will always be 5 Love Token (http://www.wowdb.com/items/21815-love-token) and a Lovely Card, which increases stats based on the faction leader.
