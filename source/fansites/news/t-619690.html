This is a 67% reduction in the number of procs of sudden death you will see.

So a raid buffed warrior is going to see somewhere between a 33% and a 66% reduction in the number of sudden death procs which is a pretty substantial nerf considering the message from blizzard was that the proc rate was supposed to remain the same but with less of an overall requirement to focus gear on crit. Even that is at a relatively low crit chance for a raiding DPS warrior. If you start pushing towards 50% crit chance the nerf will be much more pronounced.

Secondly, by claiming making it work on hit instead of on crit is a PvP buff you are demonstrating a basic misunderstanding of how "on crit" abilities work in PvP. If your opponent has resillience which reduce your chance to crit them it makes no difference to the rate at which "on crit" abilities proc. So if you have 30% crit chance and your opponents resillience reduces that to an effective 20% crit chance you will still proc "on crit" abilities from 30% of your attacks. This change was made soon after TBC to prevent the effective double nerf effect resillience was having on any "on crit" procs. Also, in PvP burst damage is far greater than sustained damage, even if the overall number of sudden death procs would remain the same it would still be slightly less useful since an execute after a crit will always be better than an execute after a hit in PvP simply by virtue of being a bigger burst. For PvP if they remove the burst nature they actually need to increase the proc rate to compensate.

If you want to argue that the nerf is justified then go ahead but arguing that it isn't a nerf is just plain wrong and claiming it is better for PvP just shows your ignorance of PvP mechanics.

At those low numbers the best thing to do would be to make the proc chance increase the longer you go without a proc. 3/6/9% chance increasing by 1/2/3% for each attack that doesn't proc. So if 5 attacks in a row don't proc you have a 24% chance your next attack will proc. Even that would be a lower proc rate overall but it would remove some of the RNG dependence of arms right now and help reduce those situations where you are on full rage for a long time just praying for a proc to help end the rage wastage.

The fact that sudden death synergises so well with overpower made for some nice combinations.
