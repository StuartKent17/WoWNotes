Wrath of the Lich King
The rested bonus will reset on all characters at the release of expansion to prevent people rushing through the content. Taking the rested bonus into account would just end up in tweaking to make the leveling longer in the first levels, making it too long for people without any rested bonus. European Armory Updated
The european armory (http://eu.wowarmory.com) has been updated with the guild bank features announced a few days ago on the US servers.

1 - Go to http://eu.wowarmory.com (http://eu.wowarmory.com)
2 - Search for your guild name
3 - Go to the "guilds" tab and select your guild
4 - At the top there are tabs for "Bank Contents" and "Bank Log" to browse.
