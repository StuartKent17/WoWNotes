Professions Changes
Alchemy

Alchemy: Free Action Potion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/6624/free-action-potion/) now lasts 30 - (Player level - 77) * 3 if the player is above level 77. That's 30 - 8 * 3 = 6 seconds at level 85.

Changing alchemist trinkets from Unique to Unique-equipped provides no effective change.

I don't think you understand the difference between unique and unique-equipped.

Unique means "You can only have one copy of a unique item in all of your inventory, including what you have in the bank. If you have an opportunity to acquire another of the same item then you will be unable to. For example, if a mob drops the same item that you already possess, you won't be able to loot the item." (from wowpedia)

Unique-equipped means "...you can have only one of those items equipped at any one time. You can have as many as you see fit in your inventory, but only one actually equipped to one of your slots" (also wowpedia)

As I already described, this is a nice change for those of us who can dual-spec into a different role that would use a completely different trinket (say balance to feral).
