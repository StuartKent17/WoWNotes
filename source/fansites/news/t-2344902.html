PvP Hotfixes with Weekly Maintenance
With weekly maintenance in each region, starting tomorrow, we'll implement the following hotfixes to address several bugs and balance issues in PvP.

Player versus Player

Intellect, Strength, Agility, and Versatility granted by Concordance of the Legionfall in PvP situations is reduced by 65% for all players (was 60% reduced).

Frost

Pillar of Frost (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/51271) increases Strength by 12% in PvP situations (was 20%). Frozen Core increases Frost damage dealt by 5% in PvP situations (was 10%).
