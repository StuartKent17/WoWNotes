"insanely hard fight". I wonder what is meant by that? hard for co-ordination, healing?


A phrase I coined a while back that has gained some notoriety in the local hardcore raiding community is that "once I'd have said that Archimonde was unforgiving", meaning that people dying at Archimonde triggering the random orb effects is nothing compared to how quickly and surely you wipe if a single mistake is made on some later bosses.

M'uru is the hardest of these, by far. It's not just one, or two or three people that can instantly wipe the raid with a mistake, it's like 15, and tricky parts pop up several times a minute, and you have to hold out for a good while enduring the onslaught of adds until you can finally tip M'uru over to ph2/Entropius phase. Similarly, it's not just co-ordination, or just healing that is the hard part, it's pretty much everything.

So basically, it's a fantastic fight! A lot of the Naxx fights we're hard to to retarded things like praying for no dc's at Thaddius, or number of geared tanks at 4H, but quite a lot of us have missed a fight we really have to chew at to beat.
