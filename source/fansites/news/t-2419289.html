Patch 8.0.1 Hotfixes - August 7, 2018
Appearances and Animations

Fixed display issues with Void Elf Heritage Armor and Shadowform with Glyph of Shadow.
Isle of Conquest Airship Cannon's ball of fire visual should again match the actual trajectory of the shot.


Classes

https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ideathknights.gif Death Knight (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/269-Death-Knight) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/death-knight) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#Y))

Unholy

Raise Dead's Claw ability will no longer incorrectly gain damage from Mastery: Dreadblade. The Dark Transformation upgrade to Sweeping Claws still benefits from Mastery.





https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/270-Druid) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/druid) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#s))

Sunfire will no longer apply to secondary targets that are immune to its effects, such as Rogues with Cloak of Shadow active.
Restoration

Photosynthesis now increases rate of all HoTs by 20% (was 15%).
Photosynthesis haste effect now affects Efflorescence.
Cultivation (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/200390) healing reduced by 17%.
Rejuvenation (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/774) healing increased by 6%.



https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/images.gif Mage (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/272-Mage) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/mage) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#g))

Arcane

Mana Shield drains 50% of damage as mana (was 20%).

Fire

Phoenix Flames's single-target damage now correctly hits Totems and similar targets.

Frost

When using Concentrated Coolness, the Regalia of the Arcane Tempest 4-piece set bonus will properly reduce the cooldown of Frozen Orb.



https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/imonks.gif Monk (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/341-Monk) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/monk) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#o))

Chi Wave healing increased by 25%.
Mistweaver

Renewing Mist healing correctly generates threat.

Windwalker

Blackout Kick damage reduced by 27%.



https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/273-Paladin) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/paladin) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#I))

Retribution

All damage abilities have been reduced 3%.



https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/274-Priest) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/priest) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#U))

Smite (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/585) damage reduced by 6%.
Divine Star (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/110744) healing increased by 25%.
Discipline

Shadow Word: Pain (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/589) reduced by 9%.
Shadowfiend (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/34433) damage reduced by 16%.
Mindbender (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/200174) damage reduced by 8%.
Fixed a bug where Sins of the Many was not increasing Atonement healing from Shadowfiend/Mindbinder.

Holy

Binding Heal (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/32546) now costs 2.4% of mana (was 2.0%).
Holy Word: Salvation cast time increased to 2.5 seconds (was 2 seconds).
Angel's Mercy cooldown reduction increased.



https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/276-Shaman) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/shaman) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#c))

Restoration

Cloudburst now stores 30% of healing done (was 25%).
Unleash Life instant healing increased by 26%.



https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/275-Rogue) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/rogue) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#Q))

Assassination

All damage abilities reduced 4%.

Outlaw

All damage abilities reduced 3%.

Subtlety

All damage abilities reduced 4%.




https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warlock) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#k))

Affliction

Summon Darkglare will no longer remove all nearby Rogues from stealth.



https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/278-Warrior) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warrior) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#E))

Arms

All damage abilities increased 3%.




Creatures and NPCs

Myra Tyrngaarde was having some trouble understanding how to cross the bridges in Ironforge, but she's been shown the correct path.


Social

Fixed a bug preventing Guild ranks with invite permissions from properly inviting.
Fixed a bug that caused Guild and officer note updates to be delayed if the member was offline.
Fixed multiple bugs that caused some players to be unexpectedly removed from General and Trade chat channels.
