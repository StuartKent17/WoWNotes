Class Tuning - September 11
With maintenance next week, starting on Tuesday, September 11, we'll be making some targeted tuning adjustments to several specs based on how they’re performing in a variety of content. Here’s an outline of those adjustments:


https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/270-Druid) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/druid) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#s))

Feral

All damage increased by 5%.
Restoration

All healing spells increased by 3%.
Rejuvenation mana cost reduced to 10.5% (was 11%).

https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/271-Hunter) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/hunter) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#M))

Beast Mastery

All damage reduced by 5%.
https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/images.gif Mage (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/272-Mage) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/mage) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#g))

Fire

All damage increased by 6%.
Flamestrike (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/2120-flamestrike) damage increased by 15%.
https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/273-Paladin) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/paladin) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#I))

Holy

Holy Light (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/82326-holy-light) and Flash of Light (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/19750-flash-of-light) healing increased by 7%.
https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/274-Priest) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/priest) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#U))

Discipline

Smite (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/585-smite) damage reduced by 9%.
Holy

All healing reduced by 3%.
Shadow

Shadow Word: Pain (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/589-shadow-word-pain) and Vampiric Touch (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/34914-vampiric-touch) damage increased by 20%.
https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/275-Rogue) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/rogue) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#Q))

Outlaw

All damage reduced by 5%.
Deadshot (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/272935-deadshot) (Azerite Trait) reduced by 20%.
Subtlety

All damage reduced by 5%.
https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/276-Shaman) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/shaman) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#c))

Restoration

Healing Rain (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/73920) healing increased by 12%.
Riptide (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/61295) healing increased by 6%.
https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warlock) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#k))

Affliction

Summon Darkglare (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/205180-summon-darkglare) damage reduced by 20%.
Demonology

All damage increased by 5%.
Destruction

All damage increased by 5%.

We will note the final changes along with other hotfixes in the Tuesday hotfixes update added to our hotfixes blog post.
