Mists of Pandaria Timewalking
Mists of Pandaria timewalking is back! It offers rewards such as Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (https://www.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade). Your Adventure Guide offers A Shrouded Path Through Time (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53035-a-shrouded-path-through-time) which rewards a Cache of Uldir Treasures (https://www.wowdb.com/items/164258-cache-of-uldir-treasures).


Visit Mistweaver Xia (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/118828-mistweaver-xia) to purchase timewalking rewards while the event is live
Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (https://www.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade) costs 5000 x Timewarped Badge (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)
Paradox Spirit (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/118063-paradox-spirit) and Infinite Hatchling (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/118060-infinite-hatchling) cost 2200 x Timewarped Badge (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)
Reputation tokens (https://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-patch=23038&filter-search=commendation) (50 x Timewarped Badge (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)) for the Mists of Pandaria factions are also for sale, including friendship items for the Valley of the Four Winds.
Portable Yak Wash (https://www.wowdb.com/items/144393-portable-yak-wash) costs 500 x Timewarped Badge (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)
Adopted Puppy Crate (https://www.wowdb.com/items/144072-adopted-puppy-crate) (1000 x Timewarped Badge (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)) and Chilled Satchel of Vegetables (https://www.wowdb.com/items/143948-chilled-satchel-of-vegetables) (25 x Timewarped Badge (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)) are also for sale

Brawl - Tarren Mill vs. Southshore
Originally introduced as a part of the World of Warcraft 10-Year Anniversary event, this Brawl harkens back to the early days of WoW PvP, with an epic battle between the two towns of Tarren Mill and Southshore. In this game of war, you’ll need to work as a team and deplete your foes’ resources to claim victory.


Call to Arms: Tiragarde Sound
This week's Call to Arms is Call to Arms: Tiragarde Sound (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52948-call-to-arms-tiragarde-sound) or Call to Arms: Tiragarde Sound (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52956-call-to-arms-tiragarde-sound). Head to Tiragarde Sound with warmode enabled to kill 10 players of the opposing faction. This will reward you with conquest and 75 reputation for either 7th Legion (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2159-7th-legion) or The Honorbound (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2157-the-honorbound).

If you capped conquest last week you can now cap it this week for an item level 355 belt.
