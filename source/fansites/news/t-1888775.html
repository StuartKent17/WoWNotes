1.) It's 1.9 active players. Note Chaud did not say CHARACTERS. This means he's accounting for accounts thus 1 account = 1 player. For players that have multiple accounts, even 3 accounts = 1 player. I have 3 accounts but I only count once in Chaud's data.

2.) Each account can have up to a potential 50 toons. There are people who play on multiple servers. I myself play on 3. I also have well over 50 toons across my 3 accounts. I still only count once in Chaud's data.

3.) The info Chaud collects is from the NA and EU armory. He doesn't look at the Asian data. If he did, then he could just make a graph that shows how many players are on the NA, EU, and Asian servers on a regular basis so we can see how many really play in a given region.

Achievements connect characters to an account so the only way for an account to be counted multiple times is if you only have character achievements turned on. Most players don't bother with that feature (or know of it) so most players will only be counted once. The number that would know of it and use it so they would thus be counted multiple times would be fairly insignificant.

So 1.9 million active accounts for the west (excluding Asia) is entirely feasible. That equates to around 950k accounts per NA and EU if the two are 50-50 ... which isn't likely (especially after Apexis pointed out it's likely closer to 1.2m for the US and 500k for the EU).
