Winter Veil 2016
The Feast of Winter Veil (http://ptr.wowdb.com/world-events/141-feast-of-winter-veil) has returned with some updates!


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/december/WinterVeilGuide.jpg (http://www.icy-veins.com/wow/feast-of-winter-veil-2016)


Snowglobes have been added to Orgrimmar, Ironforge, and Dalaran! While inside, you have the holiday goblin transformation and are able to fly
The BB King (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/4437-bb-king) achievements have been hotfixed to remove Varian and Vol'jin from the required leaders
The Endothermic Blaster (http://www.wowdb.com/items/128636-endothermic-blaster) is the gift this year, rewarding Brr... It's Cold in Here (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/10751-brr-its-cold-in-here)
Miniature Winter Veil Tree (http://www.wowdb.com/items/117371-miniature-winter-veil-tree?bonusIDs=3493) is now item level 835.
Finely-Tailored Red Holiday Hat (http://www.wowdb.com/items/139299-finely-tailored-red-holiday-hat) is dropped by Talixae Flamewreath (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/104217-talixae-flamewreath) in Court of Stars
Finely-Tailored Green Holiday Hat (http://www.wowdb.com/items/139300-finely-tailored-green-holiday-hat) is dropped by Illysanna Ravencrest (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/98696-illysanna-ravencrest) (Black Rook Hold) and King Deepbeard (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/91797-king-deepbeard) (Eye of Azshara)
The Abominable Greench (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/54499-the-abominable-greench) now scales with your level, so you can do the quest (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/6983-youre-a-mean-one) on a non max level character
Greatfather Winter (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/104710-greatfather-winter) spawns hourly near 50, 79 at the top of the mountain, rides down, granting 500 Order Resources to anyone along the way.
New Winter's Veil music (http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2069857-Patch-7-1-PTR-Build-22578-Music?p=42335821#post42335821) was added in Patch 7.1
Disposable Winter Veil Suits (http://www.wowdb.com/items/139337-disposable-winter-veil-suits) hasn't been seen yet, but is listed as a Winter's Veil item
Don't forget to do the followup to You're a Mean One... (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/6983-youre-a-mean-one), as it rewards Smokywood Pastures Special Gift (http://www.wowdb.com/items/17726-smokywood-pastures-special-gift), which has a small chance to contain Winter's Grasp (http://www.wowdb.com/items/128649-winters-grasp) or Flames of Ragnaros (http://www.wowdb.com/items/118572-flames-of-ragnaros).
The Garrison related Winter Veil activities (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/5369-Winter-Veil-2015-Legion-Increased-View-Distance-Tweets-Overwatch-Teaser) are still around, so check those out if you missed them last year.

Blizzard Holiday Activity 2016-2017
Hey all!

As you are undoubtedly aware, Winter Veil is soon to come to Azeroth, bringing with it a season of good cheer and high spirits, as well as a time where we gather together with those who we hold closest to us - family, friends, and colleagues.

In the same spirit, winter is upon us, and we wanted to take this chance to share some notices on our activity throughout the remainder of the holiday season and on into the New Year.


There will be less Blizzard employee activity and engagement across most World of Warcraft forums, especially the PTR forums and General Discussion.
There will be reduced activity and engagement on World of Warcraft's social media, particularly notable is WarcraftDevs, which will have reduced activity through to the new year.
Customer Support staff will still be hard at work during the holidays - if you need to contact Customer Support for any reason, you will be able to do so through the Blizzard Support site. Please take note that there will still be Support staff monitoring the forums and assisting with moderation, and our Customer Support forums will remain active through the holiday season!


It’s important to note that we will still be going over threads posted during the holidays when we return, so continue to share your thoughts and feedback while we’re away!

We’d like to thank everyone for their understanding as we step away from our keyboards and take time to travel and visit with our loved ones. We look forward to returning after the holidays in 2017, for what looks to be an amazing year for both Blizzard and World of Warcraft.

From all of us here at Blizzard: Happy holidays, happy New Year, and we will see you in 2017!
