Weekly Event - Burning Crusade Timewalking
Burning Crusade Timewalking is back this week. Don't forget to check out Black Temple Timewalking (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/6690-Black-Temple-Timewalking) during the event!


The weekly quest (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/44164-a-burning-path-through-time) from Archmage Timear (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/111246-archmage-timear) rewards a Accolade of Victory (https://www.wowdb.com/items/140176-accolade-of-victory), Seal of Broken Fate (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1273-seal-of-broken-fate), and Cache of Antoran Treasures (https://www.wowdb.com/items/153504-cache-of-antoran-treasures).
You can find Cupri (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/98685-cupri) in Shattrath.
Don't forget to keep working towards Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk), Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger), and Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade)!
Reins of the Infinite Timereaver (https://www.wowdb.com/items/133543-reins-of-the-infinite-timereaver) can also drop from any Timewalking dungeon boss.

PvP Brawl - Warsong Scramble
Every other week a new PvP Brawl will be available to players looking for something just a little different. Each Brawl will offer a variety of modes, rules, and scenic changes to the usual Battleground gameplay, and you’ll find them in the Group Finder tool (hotkey “I”) in the Player vs. Player tab. Just like Random Battlegrounds, you’ll receive a reward for achieving victory.

Warsong Scramble – Capture the Flag
Do you have a flag? Warsong Gulch has always been the scene of an epic Capture the Flag battle, but what happens when you can capture your enemy’s flag without your own at base? Find out when you step into this PvP Brawl! We’ve also added a few more power-ups to the field of battle to stir things up a bit. To win, your team will need to be the first to capture 5 flags.
