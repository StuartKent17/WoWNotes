Update 10:00 AM CEST - A more complete list of changes is now available.
Interface
You can now compare your achievements with other players. Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/july/achievements1.jpg)
It seems that the action icon now appears when you mouseover the target frame of a NPC. It doesn't look like it's implemented yet but we can assume that in the future, we will be able to left click the frame of the NPC instead of his model to prevent all the issues caused by players blocking NPCs.
The Death Knight starting area now have its own loading screen. Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/july/dkloading.jpg)
The first features of the threat meter have been added to the game. Screenshot #1 (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/july/threatmeter1.jpg) Screenshot #2 (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/july/threatmeter2.jpg)
- Your % of threat (based on the member of the party with the highest threat on the monster) is now displayed in the scrolling combat text.
- Your % of threat is now displayed in the tooltips of monsters.
- According to the first reports, the threat is just sent to the client without any encoding now, it will be very easy to build mods to display the exact threat of each member of the raid in the future.



http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter
Two new stable slots are available for your pets ! The respective price for the 4th and 5th slots is 50 and 150 gold. Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/july/stableslots.jpg)



http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock
Howl of Terror (Demon Form) cooldown has been increased from 40 seconds to 3 minutes.



Professions
Alchemy
Alchemical Blood has been renamed to Mixology.



World
The lighting bug affecting most of the indoor zones is now fixed.
A new "battleground room" is available in Dalaran !

Update 03:15 AM CEST - The very first few features of the threat meter are here !
Your % of threat (based on the member of the party with the highest threat on the monster) is now displayed in the scrolling combat text.
Your % of threat is now displayed in the tooltips of monsters.
According to the first reports, the threat is just sent to the client without any encoding now, it will be very easy to build mods to display the exact threat of each member of the raid in the future.

Please, read ALL patch notes before ringing the nerf-bell.

The Agility totem will be removed from the game. Instead, Strength of Earth totem will both buff Strength and Agility (by the same amounts as the separate totems do now). This means you get buffed, as you can keep up 4 useful totems now, instead of just 2 (give you were in a melee party) - you get to have Agility and Windfury totems up, and Windfury totem is also useful for you as it buffs your melee-haste.

The 4th useful totem to you is Flametongue totem, which will buff your spell-damage done. You probably didn't realize this yet, but Enhancement Shamans will be great hybrids as they will be able to cast anything instantly after 5 crits.
