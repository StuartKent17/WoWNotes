Patch 3.2 - Where can I get Epic Gems?
The patch will probably hit the live servers very soon and a lot of people ask me the same question : Where can I get the Epic Gems? It shouldn't be hard to answer that, currently there are 4 ways to get them :

Jewelcrafting / Prospecting
You can find epic gems by prospecting Titanium Ore (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/36910/titanium-ore/), it's probably not the best way to get it because Titanium is already fairly expensive but if you need as many gems as possible and don't want to wait, go for it. Prospecting Titanium will give you an epic gem 25~30% of the time, the droprate for rare gems seems to be the same, and you will always get an uncommon gem. If you're lucky enough you can even get 2 epics on the same prospect.

Note: You will also get Titanium Powder (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/46849/titanium-powder/) 60% of the time, you can exchange 10 of them for a Dalaran Jewelcrafter's Token (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/41596/dalaran-jewelcrafters-token/) and get your new recipes even faster.

Alchemy
New transmutations are available for these gems. (20 Hours cooldown)

Transmute: Ametrine (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/66658/transmute-ametrine/)
Transmute: Cardinal Ruby (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/66659/transmute-cardinal-ruby/)
Transmute: Dreadstone (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/66662/transmute-dreadstone/)
Transmute: Eye of Zul (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/66664/transmute-eye-of-zul/)
Transmute: King's Amber (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/66660/transmute-kings-amber/)
Transmute: Majestic Zircon (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/66663/transmute-majestic-zircon/)

PvE - Emblems of Heroism
You can buy any of the epic gems for Emblem of Heroism (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/40752/emblem-of-heroism/). These emblems do not drop anymore in 3.2 and are replaced by Emblems of Conquest in heroic instances / raids dungeons but you can still exchange higher tier emblems. (Emblem of Conquest (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/45624/emblem-of-conquest/) -> Emblem of Valor (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/40753/emblem-of-valor/) > Emblem of Heroism (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/40752/emblem-of-heroism/).

[color=#A335EE]Cardinal Ruby (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/36919/cardinal-ruby/), [color=#A335EE]Majestic Zircon (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/36925/majestic-zircon/), [color=#A335EE]King's Amber (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/36922/kings-amber/) can be bought for Emblem of Heroism (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/40752/emblem-of-heroism/) x 20.
[color=#A335EE]Ametrine (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/36931/ametrine/), [color=#A335EE]Dreadstone (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/36928/dreadstone/), [color=#A335EE]Eye of Zul (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/36934/eye-of-zul/) can be bought for Emblem of Heroism (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/40752/emblem-of-heroism/) x 10.

PvP - Honor
Last but not least, you can buy any of the epic gem for 10,000 Honor Points (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/43308/honor-points/).

As with most things in WotLK, we can reference back to BC to see market tendencies.

And of cource, as with all my posts, no quotes or blue copies, you can just believe me or not :P

---

The addition of epic gems will allow top level players that little extra edge on top level gear. They will allow those 'rich' players to buy the best gems possbile. They will allow... well... really anyone who can get their hands on them the ability to do so.

So how are these gems aquired? Well just as in BC there are a few different ways, granted there are quite a few more way with this round of epic gems.

But how will this all affect the market? What about blue gems? And what can we expect to see for prices?

---

For this section I will use the base as follows. Bloodstone = 5g, Cut = 7.5g. Scarlet Ruby = 75g, Cut = 100. I dont care if these arn't the real prices, they are just for putting everythign into proportion.

Remember that it is very unlikly that wee will see values go over the current values of storm jewles (which hold the same stats). Once in the market and more players get the gems I would expect the gems to fall first to the 500g mark, and then again to the 400g mark. by the time the market has become saturated and the average player has the ability to get the gems on a regular basis I would expect the value to fall to and stay at around 250g. In this the value of blue gems should fall, but should not bottom out comppletly as most players would not place an epic gem into newer gear that will soon be replaces. if the current value of rares that are cut is 100g, I would expect to see thesesfall to about 75g.
