Warlords of Draenor - UI Improvements
Today we are taking a look at a few of the UI improvements coming in Warlords of Draenor.


The Dungeon Journal now includes short overviews of the fight for each role, which should help players that don't know the fights.
The Key Binding UI has also been improved to a tabbed list rather than a very long list you have to scroll through.
You are now able to manage addons in game without having to log out. The Simple Addon Control (http://www.curse.com/addons/wow/simple-addon-control) or Addon Control Panel (http://www.curse.com/addons/wow/acp) addons will do the same thing now.

Computers - Setup of the Month
Each month or every 2 months, depending on the hardware evolutions, we will post a couple of hardware setups for those of you who are thinking of upgrading their computer!

This month
The current SSD prices (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/may/ssdgraph.gif) are stable, with all SSDs under $1 per gigabyte when on sale.

The Haswell refresh is here for non-overclockable CPUs, with the overclockable versions likely coming in the next few months. These may offer somewhat better overclocking performance, which may worth waiting for if you are overclocking to the limits of air cooling.

The Haswell CPUs (http://www.anandtech.com/show/7003/the-haswell-review-intel-core-i74770k-i54560k-tested/1) run a little bit on the warmer side. Depending on how much you want to overclock, you may need to spend a bit more for a better CPU cooler like the NH-D14 (http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002VKVZ1A?ie=UTF8&tag=mmochamp-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B002VKVZ1A).

Unless you are doing a lot of video rendering or something that is similarly multithreaded, you do not need the 4770K and should spend that money elsewhere.

Make sure to keep your AMD (http://sites.amd.com/us/game/downloads/Pages/downloads.aspx) and Nvidia (http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us) drivers up to date.

Don't hesitate to post any feedback in the comments of that news post, and don't forget to visit the Computer Forum (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/290-Computer) for any extra questions! If you are interested in Folding@home, take a look in our team's thread (http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/948557-MMO-C-folding-team).
