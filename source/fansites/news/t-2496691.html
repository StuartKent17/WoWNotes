Gaining Experience with Your Nazjatar Faction Bodyguards
By now you should have noticed that the main method of gaining experience with your chosen bodyguard for the day requires doing three daily quests (marked with blue exclamation points) that grant 100 experience each. However, there are a few other item drops found around Nazjatar that should help speed this process up a bit. Those of you wanting to finish Undersea Usurper (https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/13638-undersea-usurper) for Snapback Scuttler (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169194-snapback-scuttler) should pay attention because one of the requirements is Aqua Team Murder Force (https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/13761-aqua-team-murder-force) / Aqua Team Murder Force (https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/13762-aqua-team-murder-force), which requires you to get all 3 of your bodyguards to rank 30!

Uncommon Items

Eel Filet (https://www.wowdb.com/items/170167-eel-filet)

Turn into Vim Brineheart (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/149906-vim-brineheart) (H) or Farseer Ori (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/149805-farseer-ori) (A) at your Nazjatar hub (or to them straight away if they are your active bodyguard) to give them 50 experience.
Small chance to drop from Chasm-Haunter (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152556-chasm-haunter) and lesser chance from any eels in Nazjatar.

Fathom Ray Wing (https://www.wowdb.com/items/170176-fathom-ray-wing)

Turn into Neri Sharpfin (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/149904-neri-sharpfin) (H) or Hunter Akana (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/149804-hunter-akana) (A) at your Nazjatar hub (or to them straight away if they are your active bodyguard) to give them 50 experience.
Small chance to drop from Deepglider (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152291-deepglider), Soundless (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152290-soundless) and lesser chance from any type of fathom rays in Nazjatar.

Giant Crab Leg (https://www.wowdb.com/items/170171-giant-crab-leg)

Turn into Poen Gillbrack (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/149902-poen-gillbrack) (H) or Bladesman Inowari (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/149803-bladesman-inowari) (A) at your Nazjatar hub (or to them straight away if they are your active bodyguard) to give them 50 experience.
Small chance to drop from Sandclaw Stoneshell (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152795-sandclaw-stoneshell) and lesser chance from any crabs in Nazjatar.

Reefwalker Bark (https://www.wowdb.com/items/170183-reefwalker-bark)

Turn into your Nazjatar hub to give all of your bodyguards 20 experience each.
Small chance to drop from Anemonar (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152566-anemonar), Kelpwillow (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152567-kelpwillow), Oronu (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152397-oronu), Urduu (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152568-urduu) and lesser chance from any coral ancients in Nazjatar.


Rare Items

Ancient Reefwalker Bark (https://www.wowdb.com/items/170184-ancient-reefwalker-bark)

Turn into your Nazjatar hub to give all of your bodyguards 100 experience each.
Chance to drop from Anemonar (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152566-anemonar), Kelpwillow (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152567-kelpwillow), Oronu (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152397-oronu), Urduu (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152568-urduu).
This item will only drop once per week for your character.

Naga Deployment Orders (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169945-naga-deployment-orders) / Naga Deployment Orders (https://www.wowdb.com/items/170085-naga-deployment-orders)

Use this item by right clicking it to give all of your bodyguards 150 experience each.
Very small chance to drop from the glimmering chests spawned while using Scrying Stone (https://www.wowdb.com/items/167077-scrying-stone).
The second way to obtain one is to use your Azsh'ari Stormsurger Cape (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169489-azshari-stormsurger-cape) to see if Mrrl (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152084-mrrl) is selling an Ominous Looking Tome (https://www.wowdb.com/items/170153-ominous-looking-tome) that day.

This item does require you to find and purchase a Hungry Herald's Tentacle Taco (https://www.wowdb.com/items/170100-hungry-heralds-tentacle-taco) from Murloco (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152593-murloco) as part of the Murloc trading process.

You can receive these orders multiple times a week if you are extremely lucky.


Epic Items

Unusually Wise Hermit Crab (https://www.wowdb.com/items/168053-unusually-wise-hermit-crab)

Select one of your bodyguards and use the item to give them 75 experience.
Purchased as one the basic options for Mrrl (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/152084-mrrl) to be selling that day (no cape required)






Reaching Rank 20 with a Bodyguard
Upon reaching rank 20 with your Neri Sharpfin (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/149904-neri-sharpfin) (H) or Hunter Akana (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/149804-hunter-akana) (A) bodyguard, you will be given the quest Good Girl (H) or Wild Tame (A) which gives you the Snapdragon Kelpstalker (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169199-snapdragon-kelpstalker) (H) or Deepcoral Snapdragon (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169200-deepcoral-snapdragon) (A) mount! Unlocking one of these mounts will also unlock the equivalent for the opposite faction to use.

Thanks to forum user Ryken (https://www.mmo-champion.com/members/603492-Ryken) for clarifying the other bodyguard rewards!


Reaching Rank 20 with Vim Brineheart (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/149906-vim-brineheart) (H) or Farseer Ori (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/149805-farseer-ori) (A) rewards Book of the Unshackled (https://www.wowdb.com/items/170154-book-of-the-unshackled) (H) or Carved Ankoan Charm (https://www.wowdb.com/items/170155-carved-ankoan-charm) (A).
Reaching Rank 20 with Poen Gillbrack (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/149902-poen-gillbrack) (H) or Bladesman Inowari (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/149803-bladesman-inowari) (A) rewards Lustrous Glimmershell (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169353-lustrous-glimmershell) (H) or Brilliant Glimmershell (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169354-brilliant-glimmershell) (A).

Nazjatar Rares Guide
Here you will find an alphabetical list of all rares in Nazjatar, notable loot, and how to spawn them. Note that their location on the map is indicated by their number on this list. For information on the rares of Mechagon, check our our guide here (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/8608-Darkmoon-Faire-Mechagon-Rares-Guide-DLC-686)!

Spawn Conditions - On a timer

Notable Drops - Blind Eye (https://www.wowdb.com/items/170189-blind-eye) (Turn in to Gloomseeker Yarga in an underwater cave in the Hanging Reef for a bag of gold and sometimes a random Nazjatar item.
