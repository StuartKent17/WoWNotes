Arena changes
It seems that you cannot communicate with other teams anymore during arena matches in 2.3.
Whispers are blocked.
All /say messages are changed to a generic emote. (****** says something unintelligible.")





Hunter Bugfixes
The hunters nerfs are definitely here on purpose and have been added to the patch notes. You should still be able to drink during trash mobs fights.
Fixed a bug in which Hunters were able to use Readiness in conjunction with Freezing Trap on two different targets.
Fixed a bug in which Hunters were able to disengage from combat using Feign Death during boss encounters.



Skald fire elementals bug
According to this blue post (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2968178446&postId=29491835017&sid=1#2), it seems that the fire elementals in Skald (north of Blade's Edge Mountains) are bugged and instantly respawn. I checked it this morning and it's still working so I guess it could be a good time to farm primal fires.


Blue posts
3 or 4 days of blue posts so I had to filter out a few of them but the most important posts/issues should be reported. You can almost consider this as a 2.3.1 / 2.3.2 patch preview, tons of bug fixes to make you happy.

General
All your global cooldowns concerns should go here. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2856225038&postId=29492341731&sid=1#2178))
Spells cast during Arena Preparation do not cost mana but still cost reagents, this is intended. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2968657139&postId=29492021159&sid=1#1))



PvP
Blizzard is going to watch AV after the recent changes closely. They may make additional changes, but they want to see how things play out for a bit and everyone adjusts. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2968108668&postId=29491241686&sid=1#11))
NPCs selling season 1 gear for honor can't just be added to the game, they're part of the "Season 3 event" and it would require to add all the NPCs, including the one selling the season 3 Gear. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2967986860&postId=29491818440&sid=1#40))



Hunter
Blizzard can't find any bug with the loss of Happiness in 2.3, if you have more specific information about this please report them. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2968107895&postId=29493236292&sid=1#94))
Same goes for the pathing issues in instances (most of the time in stairs or on ramps), please report any information about this. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2968107895&postId=29493236292&sid=1#94))
The issue where pets aren't attacking if a player has their back up against a wall or a corner will be fixed in a future patch. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2968107895&postId=29493236292&sid=1#94))
Hunters should be able to feed their pets during combat again in a future patch. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2968107895&postId=29493236292&sid=1#94))



Mages
The issues with spells requiring ground targetting (e.g. Blizzard) is fixed for 2.3.2.



Paladins
The mana cost of Righteous Fury is being fixed in a future patch. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2968228398&postId=29491834872&sid=1#2))



Priests
The bug preventing the refresh of Prayer of Mending will be fixed in a future patch. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2856015050&postId=29492034500&sid=1#45))



Shamans
The bug preventing the refresh of Earth Shield will be fixed in a future patch. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2856015050&postId=29492034500&sid=1#45))



Quests
Quantities will be added to the quest objective of daily cooking quests. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2969688494&postId=29493235048&sid=1#0))
Everyone should be able to do the Battlegrounds daily quest but a bug is preventing this, it will be fixed and the reward will scale accordingly by level. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2855963526&postId=29491503327&sid=1#58))



Items
[item=34220] is being changed in an upcoming patch to require at least 2 blue gems instead of exactly 2 gems.
The size of your first backpack (the one you can't replace) might be increased in the future. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2968656884&postId=29491419495&sid=1#11))
Hippogryphs do not walk, this is why they don't have a walking animation. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2856013672&postId=29491201438&sid=1#1))



Professions
Alchemy
The old mithril -> truesilver transmute is being changed to a 1 day cooldown. They're also going to be looking at the possibility of adding new metallic transmutes, but no promises. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2968227114&postId=29491234744&sid=1#38))

Engineering
Upcoming patch will have Arrow Makers and Shell Machines listed in the appropriate section of the Auction House. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2968228130&postId=29491235083&sid=1#1))

Leatherworking
New Quivers and Ammo Pouchs are changed in an upcoming patch : they won't be unique anymore.



Raids & Dungeons
The issue with the Badges of Justice in chests (Mechanar / Karazhan) is known and Blizzard is working on a solution. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2855964645&postId=29493216118&sid=1#24))



Interface
A bug prevent people from selling stuff by drag and dropping stacked items to the vendor. While you wait for the fix you can always sell them by right clicking the items in your bags. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2969688030&postId=29491434437&sid=1#5))
Anything you can interact with now should have the "loot sparkles". Food crates, herbs, mining nodes, etc. It may be that some herbs are so small that the sparkleys just don't show up as prominently with the way the particle effect generates. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2856024427&postId=29491235285&sid=1#5))
The ability to query all of the auction house results was temporarily disabled. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=879058320&postId=29491234074&sid=1#313))
There isn't anything new about the Itemrack feature promised at the Blizzcon, wait & see. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2968178220&postId=29491433906&sid=1#4))



Others
The official talent calculator will be updated soon. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=2968108579&postId=29493235904&sid=1#1)) (=Use the one from Wowhead (http://www.wowhead.com/))

1) Karazhan - Our group was fighting on the incline between the platforms immediately adjecent to Shade of Aran and Illihoof. We pulled the four ghosts onto the incline and fought them there. While on this platform, when I sent my pet to attack a mob standing on the incline, my pet was sent dashing for the instance entrance down through the library the way we came.

2) Serpentshrine Cavern - The first pull in the instance. While standing on the platform at the top of the 2nd Elevator and fighting the mobs as they were tanked on the platform at the bottom of the decline (near where the pack hangs out), my pet and another hunter's pet pathed forwards through the instance along the bridge (towards Hydross), pulling all packs along the way. I was standing either on the decline or on the platform adjacent to the elevator.

3) Serpentshrine Cavern - Hydross encounter. During the 1st phase, Hydross is standing on his platform, doing his frost aura debuff, all hunter pets are able to attack him normally. But when the first transition takes place, our raid moves Hydross off of his platform and tanks him on the slightly lower level. While on this level, Hunter pets are unable to attack him, and instead path towards the front of the instance along the bridge.
