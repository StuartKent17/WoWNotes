Patch 5.4 - Visions of Time: Courting Destiny
Patch 5.4 adds a quest line on the Timeless Isle that involves working for Kairoz (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/72870-kairoz) of the Keepers of Time (http://wowpedia.org/Keepers_of_Time). He is on the Timeless Isle because the Bronze Dragonflight is not as powerful as it once was, only being able to catch furtive glimpses through time. He has created an hourglass that will allow for a window into the timeways, but you must collect 50 x Epoch Stones (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/105715-epoch-stone) to make it usable.

After collecting the stones, you embark on a series of weekly quests to use the Vision of Time (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/105930-vision-of-time) in different locations to calibrate the Vision of Time. Kairoz eventually will reveal (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/33376-seeking-fate) that there is a traitor among the Bronze Dragonflight.

As the hourglass grows more powerful, he will send you (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/33378-courting-destiny) to the Temple of the White Tiger to see a glimpse of Garrosh and Anduin, which is the scene you see below.

Patch 5.4 Hotfixes: September 11
Classes

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/271-Hunter) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/hunter) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#M))

Talents

Dire Beast (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/120679) should no longer summon snakes that were larger than usual while on the Timeless Isle.



http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/images.gif Mage (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/272-Mage) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/mage) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#g))

General

Fixed an issue where a Mana Gem (http://www.wowdb.com/items/36799-mana-gem) modified by the Glyph of Loose Mana (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/42735) was restoring more mana than intended. The total amount of mana restored should now be the same as an unglyphed Mana Gem (http://www.wowdb.com/items/36799-mana-gem).




Quests

A Change of Command (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/32430-a-change-of-command): The gate leading to High Marshal Twinbraid (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/68851-high-marshal-twinbraid) at Lion's Landing should now be open, allowing Horde characters to complete the quest.
A Vision in Time (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/33337-a-vision-in-time): Fixed an issue where the music wasn't playing correctly after using Vision of Time.
Ace in the Hole (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/27089-ace-in-the-hole): Fixed an issue preventing players from being able to complete the quest.
Bad Feeling, Worse Result (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/33107-bad-feeling-worse-result): Players are now able to use the Lin Family Scroll (http://www.wowdb.com/objects/221413-lin-family-scroll) to resummon the Ghost of Lin Da-Gu (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/72726-ghost-of-lin-da-gu).
When Death is Not Enough (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/26937-when-death-is-not-enough): Fixed an issue preventing players from being able to complete the quest.


Creatures

Burning Berserker (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/72895-burning-berserker)s and Eternal Kilnmaster (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/72896-eternal-kilnmaster)s no longer use Fiery Charge (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/147704-fiery-charge) on targets who are not in combat.
Dread Ship Vazuvius (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/73281-dread-ship-vazuvius) now deals less damage with its Cannon Barrage (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/148159-cannon-barrage), the range of its knockback ability has been reduced, and can apply its Eerie Fog (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/148110-eerie-fog) effect more readily.
Nomi (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/64337-nomi) should now have the correct conversation text for players on the appropriate reputation level.
Zarhym (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/71876-zarhym) should now properly despawn after a player has started the event to search for his body.
Rare creatures in the Vale of Eternal Blossoms should now follow the correct tapping mechanics.


Raids, Dungeons, and Scenarios

Siege of Orgrimmar

Thok the Bloodthirsty

Thok the Bloodthirsty (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/71529-thok-the-bloodthirsty)'s rate of energy regeneration should now increase properly after each use of Deafening Screech (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/143343-deafening-screech) on Flexible, Normal, and Heroic difficulty.

Garrosh Hellscream

Embodied Despair (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/72238-embodied-despair) can no longer be stunned.




Items

Capacitive Primal Diamond (http://www.wowdb.com/items/95346-capacitive-primal-diamond) no longer has a 1-second internal cooldown for the Capacitance effect.
Cliffbreaker Breastplate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/101789-cliffbreaker-breastplate) should now correctly have a socket bonus for Strength instead of Intelligence.
Gar'tok, Strength of the Faithful (http://www.wowdb.com/items/103945-gartok-strength-of-the-faithful) should no longer be incorrectly flagged as a caster weapon.
Spark of Zandalar (http://www.wowdb.com/items/96770-spark-of-zandalar) should now be awarding the appropriate amount of Strength to players based on the item's level instead of the player's level.


Timeless Isle

A Rock-breaking Hammer (http://www.wowdb.com/objects/220904-rock-breaking-hammer) will now always be available to players inside of the Mysterious Den.
Snowdrift Tiger Talons (http://www.wowdb.com/objects/223539-snowdrift-tiger-talons), Riverspeaker's Trident (http://www.wowdb.com/objects/223538-riverspeakers-trident), and Flameheart Shawl (http://www.wowdb.com/objects/223537-flameheart-shawl) will now properly destroy rocks in the Mysterious Den.


Bug Fixes

Players in the Silent Sanctuary area in Vale of Eternal Blossoms should no longer be put into an incorrect phase.
Items that starts a quest with Tina Mudclaw (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/58761-tina-mudclaw) should now be dropping at the appropriate friendship faction levels.
Fixed an issue where the Healer Proving Grounds had a chance to cause the realm to crash.
