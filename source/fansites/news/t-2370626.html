Patch 7.3.5 Hotfixes - January 22
Achievements

The consolidated Algalon achievement Observed (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/12399-observed) should now properly grant both the “Starcaller (http://www.wowdb.com/titles/164-starcaller)” and “Astral Walker (http://www.wowdb.com/titles/165-the-astral-walker)” titles.


Characters

Addressed a graphical error with characters who have markings such as tattoos.
Uuna will now react to her surroundings when taken to Suramar, for both Horde and Alliance characters.


Classes

https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idemonhunters.gif Demon Hunter (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/358-Demon-Hunter) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/demon-hunter) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#w) / Artifact Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/artifact-calculator#xAAAAAAAA) / PvP Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/pvp-talent-calculator#w))

Fixed an issue where Fel Eruption (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/211881-fel-eruption) would not deal damage to stun-immune targets.
Rage of the Illidari (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/201472-rage-of-the-illidari?artifactRank=1) again correctly deals damage.


https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/images.gif Mage (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/272-Mage) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/mage) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#g) / Artifact Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/artifact-calculator#hAAAAAAAAA) / PvP Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/pvp-talent-calculator#g))

Frost Mages can again obtain their Frostfire Remembrance artifact appearance.


https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/276-Shaman) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/shaman) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#c) / Artifact Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/artifact-calculator#dAAAAAAAA) / PvP Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/pvp-talent-calculator#c))

Artifact traits and other effects that increase all of your healing correctly benefit Healing Stream Totem (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/5394-healing-stream-totem) and Healing Tide Totem (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/108280-healing-tide-totem).


https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warlock) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#k) / Artifact Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/artifact-calculator#lAAAAAAAA) / PvP Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/pvp-talent-calculator#k))

Haunt (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/48181-haunt) will no longer incorrectly reset its cooldown when used against certain creatures with scaling health.


https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/278-Warrior) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warrior) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#E) / Artifact Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/artifact-calculator#F) / PvP Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/pvp-talent-calculator#E))

Fixed a bug that prevented Warriors who have completed two Artifact acquisitions from starting “One More Legend (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/44417-one-more-legend)” to obtain their third Artifact.



Dungeons and Raids

Deepholm

Fixed a bug where Aeonaxx would fail to drop loot.

Return to Karazhan

Attumen should once again properly respect tank threat for his Mounted Strike and Stamp attacks while astride Midnight.

Shadowfang Keep

Some enemies will now properly benefit from damage scaling when attacked by high-level players, in Normal difficulty.

Vault of the Wardens

Fel Furies that spawn in the Mythic Tirathon Saltheril encounter will no longer evade.



Mounts and Pets

Fixed a bug that caused custom pet names to sometimes partially disappear.
Mailing the Vicious War Fox (http://www.wowdb.com/items/152870-vicious-war-fox) will no longer cause it to become the other faction's War Fox.


Professions

Skinning

Noxious Whelps can again be skinned for Green Whelp Scales.



Quests

The Dragonblight quest "An End and a Beginning" can now be completed normally.
The Huge Boulder for the Redridge quest "Saving Foreman Oslow" has been returned.


January 19 - Build 28675

Bugfixes

Addressed tuning issues with enemy health in leveling content.
Fixed framerate and client performance issues on mid- and lower-capacity hardware, especially when coupled with the use of certain addons.
Fixed an Archaeology bug preventing players from being able to survey in appropriate locations.
Restored a large number of missing transmogrification items to Collections.
