Spell Changes

Elemental

Shamanism (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/62099/shamanism/#14911/14942) now gives Lightning Bolt, Chain Lightning, and Lava Burst an additional 36% benefit from spell power, up from 32%.


Alchemy

Alchemy: Draught of War (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/93935/draught-of-war/#14911/14942) now restores 22500 to 27500 health and 9250 to 10750 mana, up from 7200 to 8800 health and 7200 to 8800 mana.
Alchemy: Mighty Rejuvenation Potion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/80490/mighty-rejuvenation-potion/#14911/14942) now restores 9000 to 11000 mana and health, up from 7200 to 8800. Now requires 1 x Volatile Life (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/52329/volatile-life/).
Alchemy: Mysterious Potion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/80487/mysterious-potion/#14911/14942) now restores 1 to 50000 health and 1 to 20000 mana, up from 1 to 30000 health and 1 to 15000 mana.
Alchemy: Mythical Healing Potion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/80498/mythical-healing-potion/#14911/14942) now restores 22500 to 27500 health, up from 18900 to 23100. No longer requires Volatile Life (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/52329/volatile-life/), now requires 1 x Twilight Jasmine (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/52987/twilight-jasmine/), down from 2.
Alchemy: Mythical Mana Potion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/80494/mythical-mana-potion/#14911/14942) now requires 1 x Whiptail (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/52988/whiptail/), down from 2.

Here's how it works. Basically, the 1:1 forced ratio means that both sides must have an equal number of people in Tol Barad games. Let's say that you're realm has a 20:1 Horde:Alliance ratio, or a population of 20,000 Horde to 1,000 Alliance. The certain number of Horde queuing will probably MUCH more than the number of Alliance queueing, making the Alliance queuing population the limiting factor. So basically let's say 5 Alliance guys, or 0.5% of the population get in, so only 5 Horde guys, or 0.025% of the population, can get in because of the 1:1 rule.

In other words, if you're on the more-populated faction of an imbalanced server, your chances of even getting into TB games is much less compared to the other faction. The chances worsen the more unbalanced a realm is.
