Patch 7.1.5 PTR January 4 - Legendary and Spell Hotfixes
Shared

Roots of Shaladrassil (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/132466/#23194-23244): Now heals you for 3% of your maximum health every 1 second, up from 4% of your maximum health every 2 seconds.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter

Roar of the Seven Lions (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/137080/#23194-23244): Bestial Wrath now reduces Focus cost of all abilities by 15%, down from 20%.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/images.gif Mage

Marquee Bindings of the Sun King (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/132406/#23194-23244): Now procs 15% of the time, down from 20%. Now increases damage of next Pyroblast by 300%, up from 275%.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest

Mangaza's Madness (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/132864/#23194-23244) - Increases the number of Mind Blast charges by 1, down from 2.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue

Greenskin's Waterlogged Wristcuffs (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/137099/#23194-23244): Damage of next Pistol Shot increased by 200%, down from 300% in last hotfix (was 700% in previous build).
Insignia of Ravenholdt (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/137049/#23194-23244): Now deals 15% additional damage as Shadow, up from 12% in last hotfix (was 20% in previous build).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock

Reap and Sow (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/144364/#23194-23244) - Reap Souls now lasts 1.5 seconds longer per soul consumed, down from 2.5 seconds.



Spell Changes
Item Set Bonuses

Item - Druid T19 Restoration 2P Bonus (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/211165/#23194-23244): Wild Growth now grants you 4,000 Mastery, up from 3,000 Mastery.
Item - Paladin T19 Holy 2P Bonus (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/211436/#23194-23244): Holy Shock critical strike chance now increased by 5%, down from 10%.

If the only legendaries were ones that provided utility (and in those examples utility means damage mitigation or extra sustain) then we would be seeing posts about how it was impossible for a guild to compete unless everyone had sustain legendaries so they could run one less healer on progression raids.

If my job is to do damage then a legendary that makes me run a little faster or take a little less damage or regen some health isn't interesting. It's just a boring boost to a stat I don't care about.

There are problems with the existing legendary system. They could have been better balanced, the way they are acquired is pretty bad and the idea that bad luck with legendary drops could mean a player could go the entire way through the expansion without ever being able to be competitive in their chosen role is certainly less than ideal. To me the one thing they got right with the legendaries is that they feel legendary. If you get one, and are lucky enough to get a good one that's a pretty amazing feeling. That part they should keep. They definitely need to fix all the other issues but just reducing them to a dull stat stick with a bit of extra utility that nobody really cares about is the wrong solution. It's the easy solution for sure but in this case I would say it is the lazy one.

I like the legendary system. It means pretty much anything I do in game has a chance to give a fantastic reward. It means there are items out there that change how the class feels to play. All this stuff is great. There's room for improvement but there is a lot of good stuff in there. You don't get to a great system by throwing out the entire thing because the first iteration isn't perfect.
