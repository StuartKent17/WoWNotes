Illidan oceanic first kill
On 17th of September at approximately 10:30 Sydney time, guild 'Not Steamboat (http://www.notsteamboat.com/)' achieved an Oceanic first of slaying the end boss of content as we know it, Illidan Stormrage. Not Steamboat (NSB) are located on the Oceanic server Aman'Thul and raid five days a week for four hours each day. (And they are recruiting)

Also, Sanctus who contacted me added a few interesting thoughs in his mail :
The main reason for this email is mostly due to the fact of gaining a unique representation amongst the US servers and EU servers. There are only eight oceanic servers in total and in all progression and new content release cases, oceanic servers is grouped with the US servers as a whole.

We believe australia and fellow oceanic countries have a strong and commited player base and deserve servers located in the oceanic region so we can have pings equal to those of our competitors around the world. We constantly raid with 400ms, this would be considered 'horrible lag' by those who live in the US that get pings of less then 100.
