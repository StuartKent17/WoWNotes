Glowcap Festival
Glowcap Festival (http://ptr.wowdb.com/world-events/648-glowcap-festival) allows players to help keep Fshoo (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/118509-fshoo) alive in Sporeggar to earn reputation.


Head to Sporeggar in Zangarmarsh to take part in this holiday.
Mushrooms will spawn around the town, giving you Glowcap (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/24245-glowcap) and other helpful items:

Sack of Healing Spores (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/144276-sack-of-healing-spores) - Channeled heal for Fshoo.
Sporeggium (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/144261-sporeggium) - 2% healing buff per stack. Stacks to 100 times.
Rimecap (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/144265-rimecap) - Stop all damage and growth for 3 seconds, allowing healers to catch up.
Fungal Lifestalk (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/144262-fungal-lifestalk) - Instant 5% heal for Fshoo.

You can purchase all four of the items above from Mycah (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/18382-mycah), the Sporeggar Quartermaster.
You can also loot a Pungent Truffle (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/235264-pungent-truffle), allowing you to transform into a sporeling for 24 hours.
Fshoo spawns periodically, gaining stacks of Fshoo Grow (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/235202-fshoo-grow). Each stack increases his size by 1% and health by 100%.
Fshoo also loses health at a constant rate, making him harder and harder to heal as the grow stacks appear.
Using the items and healing him to keep him alive as long as possible is the objective.
When Fshoo dies he will give everyone 50 Sporeggar reputation for every stack of the buff he had.

Build of the Month - December 2016
Each month or so we will post a couple of hardware setups for those of you who are thinking of upgrading their computer!

All of the parts on this page are Amazon affiliate links. When you purchase your parts through them it helps support the site!

This month
The current SSD prices (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2016/december/ssdgraph.gif) are fairly flat right now, with most SSDs under $0.50 per gigabyte when on sale.

Depending on how much you want to overclock, you may need to spend a bit more for a better CPU cooler like the NH-D15 (http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00L7UZMAK?ie=UTF8&tag=mmochamp-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B00L7UZMAK).

Unless you are doing a lot of video rendering or streaming, you do not need the 6700K and would get more performance from spending that money elsewhere.

Make sure to keep your AMD (http://sites.amd.com/us/game/downloads/Pages/downloads.aspx) and Nvidia (http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us) drivers up to date.

Don't forget to visit the Computer Forum (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/290-Computer) for any extra questions!
