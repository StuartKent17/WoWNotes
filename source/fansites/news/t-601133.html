Remember this morning ? I said it was going to be a busy day.

You can now use Guild Armory Tools (http://armory.mmo-champion.com/) to get sorted informations from the Blizzard armory !
Guild characters list with their respectives attributes, sortable by whichever attribute you want including spell damage, critical %, etc ...
Tradeskill listing, to get a complete list of all the crafters within a guild. Sortable by tradeskill and level.
Reputation listing, sortable by level toward a faction. Basically the best way to see who isn't revered to needed factions yet


This is a script created by Antiarc , you can visit his website here :
http://wow.tachyonsix.com/armory/
Thanks to him again for all the great work he did.
