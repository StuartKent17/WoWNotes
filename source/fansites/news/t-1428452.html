Blue Tweets
Warlords of Draenor Stats
Minor example is ratio of ambush bonus dmg to backstab bonus dmg is constant at all levels, but backstab scales better with gear
You'd find that all of the base damage on spells/abilities stays in constant ratios at all levels, for a given class, BTW. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408129439516471296))
And that's the problem. For a lvl 30 with 150 SP, this increases Wrath by 138% but Healing Touch by just 28%
Heals had higher base healing to make up for players having base health. However, that didn't line up and scale perfectly. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408140211420278784))
In WoD, there will be no more base health, or base damage/healing on spells, so it's all just scaling; can line up. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408140362843054080))
So whats this going to do to dmg and healing at low lvls if you don't have heirlooms you won't have much stats.
There are still base stats. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408159136526790656))
Is there going to be a base amount of the new secondaries? like even with 0 crit rating u still crit
No. You still crit with 0 crit rating because you have 5% base crit, and crit from agi/int. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408388234477973504))
More similar to haste, where there is no base amount of haste. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408388486824087552))

Does no Dodge/Parry on gear mean Strength -> Parry and Agility -> Dodge are being removed as well?
Nope, those remain. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408330328961339392))

Now that we have 3 new secondary stats (Amplify, etc.). How many of these stats will we see on a single piece of gear? Still 2?
Still typically 2 secondary stats on most gear. May be exceptions, as always, but vast majority of the time, 2. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408297359433412608))

Will melee abilities still be affected by Weapon damage. Or are those abilities now only scaled by AP?
Yes, weapon damage will still be a thing. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408297028947427328))

If this means all dmg spells and heals will scale with primary stat, this is a good thing although simplifies gear
Typically Attack Power or Spell Power, which mostly come from your primary stat. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408122581212880896))

would be nice for weapons to means as much to casters a physical.
Somewhat ironically, weapons are actually *more* important for some casters, than some melees. SP bonus is huge. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408296295367847936))

sorry if this is a repeat question but if I gem say spirit then trade to dps or tank does that gem change too eg haste for dps ?
No, only primaries on armor slots switch. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408089701191405568))
sorry im more asking for the gem side the fact that say pally chest has a gem socket do I have to swap it everytime swap spec ?
Sorry, not sure I understand the question. If you put a Spirit gem in a socket, it provides no value if you respec to Feral. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408112465130438656))
ok thank you that means if I have a gem socket armor piece when swap to my off spec I either need new gear or a different gem
To min-max, yes. However, remember that a different item of the same ilvl likely won't have the gem socket; sockets are free. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408118304197189632))

Will Cleave on gear mean CC'd mobs have to be moved to Uzbekistan to avoid sheep-busting?
No. Cleave won't hit CC'd or mobs, or mobs you aren't in combat with. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408076595295817728))

Does it mean won't be useful or won't be optimal? Is this applying to PVP too?
Spirit will have zero value for non-healers, including in PvP. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408075323813539840))

In WoD, does the baseline yellow attack hit cap include attacks against targets that are higher than 3 levels above you?
Yes. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408034390569021440))
Does that also mean a lvl 1 character will have 100% hit chance against a level 100 character or mobs? aoe questing inc
No. Just up to +3 levels, then you start missing again. Similar to how Crushing Blows still exist now. (Source (https://twitter.com/Celestalon/status/408052825839517696))
