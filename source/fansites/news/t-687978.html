World of Warcraft: MLG Orlando Championship
Today marks the first ever MLG World of Warcraft All-Star Challenge. The All-Star challenge is a double elimination, best of seven format bracket where four teams are drafted by four captains. The main rule is that no captain may select a player on their own normal roster. The draft itself will be broadcast beginning at 12:30pm EST / 6:30pm CET on Saturday with the entire bracket being played out that day.

High-Definition
To purchase the HD WoW Weekend pass for the 2009 MLG PC Circuit Championship live from Orlando, go to www.mlgpro.com/live/2009/Orlando/purchaseHD (http://www.mlgpro.com/live/2009/Orlando/purchaseHD). Important - If you purchase an HD Pass with a Hotmail adress you might have to request the code a 2nd time to get the mail. (The easy solution is to use a non-hotmail adress)
