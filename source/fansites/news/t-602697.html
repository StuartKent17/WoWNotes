thanks



Headless Horseman (70 Elite) is summoned in Scarlet Monestary Graveyard, there is no heroic setting on SM. Go into the Graveyard instance of SM, kill the mobs along the path and you'll see a big pumpkin on the left side. You right click it and get the quest, then click the mound behind the pumpkin to summon him. It's not a hard fight, but he hit my 70 Druid, in average gear, for about 1000, none crit. You do need a group and some decent DPS otherwise it gets messy.

Once summoned he goes to the person who summoned him, so have your tank ready to tag him. When he hits 1% health he loses his head and his body becomes immune to physical attacks, however his head floats about randomly, not attacking anyone. His body continues to waddle about doing a spinning attacking and hitting for about 600 on my druid. Target the head and DPS it down. Eventually he gets his head back, and full health, and goes back to the person highest on the aggro list, hopefully your tank.

Second stage, same as the first.

Third stage he summons pumpkin adds which are annoying and will take down a cloth/leather healer pretty fast, so tank needs to be aware. When they spawn you have a few moments before they start moving around so the tank can get a few whacks on them. When he loses his head on the third stage kill the head, nothing else matters, just nuke the head down. Once the head is dead his body dies.

If your DPS is a bit shoddy you may enter a fourth stage, which is the same as the third. Get too many pumpkin adds and it gets quite chaotic.

You can do this quest daily per character. Each character can summon him once a day, so a 5 man group can summon him 5 times by resetting the instance. Remove someone from your group and get someone else and they too can summon him. If you have alts, they can summon him too.

Hope the above helps someone, I'm not the greatest at writing tutorials, tis how I remember it going.
