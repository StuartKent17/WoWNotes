Spell Changes

Cooking

Cooking: Kibler's Bits (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/43772/kiblers-bits/#14199/14241) now makes your pet bigger for 5 minutes.


Engineering

Engineering: Flintlocke's Woodchucker (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/100587/flintlockes-woodchucker/#14199/14241) is now restricted to hunters.


Mechanics - Mounted

Tarecgosa's Visage (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/101641/tarecgosas-visage/#14199/14241) has been added to the "mounted" spells, but not to the actual mounts. Most likely confirms the theory that the "Tarecgosa Mount" datamined is just a transformation during the questline and not an actual mount.

Feral

Thrash (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/77758/thrash/#14199/14241) damage has been reduced. Now deals [932 + 9.82% of AP] to [1151 + 9.82% of AP] damage, and causes all targets within 8 yards to bleed for [1743 + 5.01% of AP] damage over 6 sec. Down from [1310 + 19.2% of AP] to [1617 + 19.2% of AP] damage, and causes all targets within 8 yards to bleed for [2445 + 9.78% of AP] damage over 6 sec.

Marksmanship

Wild Quiver (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/76659/wild-quiver/#14199/14241) now has a 16.8% chance to proc, up from 14.4%. Each point of Mastery increases the chance by an additional 2.1%, up from 1.8%.
Careful Aim (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/34483/careful-aim/#14199/14241) now only works on targets above 90% health, up from 80%.


Survival

Black Arrow (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/3674/black-arrow/#14199/14241) damage has been increased by 40%. Now scales from 13.3% of AP, up from 9.5%.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/images.gif Mage (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/272-Mage) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..mage) / Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1669-Mage-Cataclysm))
Arcane

Arcane Blast (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/30451/arcane-blast/#14199/14241) damage has been reduced by 5%.

Subtelty

Hemorrhage (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/16511/hemorrhage/#14199/14241) now deals 155% weapon damage (225% if a dagger is equipped), up from 110% weapon damage (160% if a dagger is equipped).

Elemental

Unleash Flame (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/73683/unleash-flame/#14199/14241), Unleash Frost (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/73682/unleash-frost/#14199/14241), and Unleash Wind (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/73681/unleash-wind/#14199/14241) no longer have a cooldowns but Unleash Elements (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/73680/unleash-elements/) still has one. I'm going to assume it's just a fix to prevent bugs.

Arms

Two-Handed Weapon Specialization (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/12712/two-handed-weapon-specialization/#14199/14241) now increases damages with 2H weapons by 12%, down from 20%.


Fury

Dual Wield Specialization (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/23588/dual-wield-specialization/#14199/14241) now increases all Physical damage by 5%, down from 10%.
