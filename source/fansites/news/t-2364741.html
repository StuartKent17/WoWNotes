GTX 680 is supported by both Metal 1 and 2. The reason you aren't running Metal is because Blizzard disabled it in anything before 10.12.x because 10.11's drivers were/are spectacularly buggy.

You can update your Mac Pro 4,1 to a 5,1 freely (http://forum.netkas.org/index.php/topic,852.0.html) and then update to Sierra/High Sierra without issue. Your CPUs are fine. Your RAM is fine. It's Apple cockblocking you. You may need to temporarily install a fresh OS and boot from it (internally or externally, your choice) in order to successfully update the firmware, but once done, Apple's installer will let you do so and you'll have Metal and you'll cry rivers of joyful tears.

Make sure to reset your SMC (https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201295) after the update to ensure the machine has taken to the new firmware properly. SMC resets are recommended after applying full version upgrades to the OS.
