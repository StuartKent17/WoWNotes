Necrotic Strike (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/73975/#15544-15589) - Now does 100% weapon damage, up from 50%.

Death Knight - Talents

Blood Tap (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/45529/#15544-15589) - Now generates 2 (was 1) Blood Charges, up to a maximum of 10 (was 6). Blood Tap consumes 5 Blood Charges, up from 3.

Glyph of Mind Freeze (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/58686/#15544-15589) - Now reduces the cooldown of your Mind Freeze ability by 2 sec instead of increasing interrupt duration.
Glyph of Shifting Presences (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/58647/#15544-15589) - You retain 80% of your Runic Power when switching Presences, up from 50%.

Death Knight - Minor Glyphs

Glyph of the Geist (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/58640/#15544-15589) - Your Raise Dead spell summons a geist instead of a ghoul. Replaced Glyph of Blood Tap.
Glyph of Horn of Winter (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/58680/#15544-15589) - When used outside of combat, your Horn of Winter ability causes a brief, localized snow flurry. No longer increases duration.
