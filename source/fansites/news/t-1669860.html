Battle.Net Shop Mounts Giveaway
Happy New Year! We are celebrating by giving away 10 x Battle.Net Shop mounts. AskMrRobot (http://www.askmrrobot.com/) was nice enough to send us some Premium codes to give away again this year, so a big thanks to them! We are continuing with some mounts, one month Curse Premium (http://www.curse.com/premium) codes, and one month AskMrRobot Premium (https://www.askmrrobot.com/account/premium) codes.

Please note the shorter timeline of this giveaway. You have 24 hours to reply after PMs go out.

Rules

Enter once by replying to this news post.
