Weekly Event - Burning Crusade Timewalking
Burning Crusade Timewalking is back this week. Don't forget to check out Black Temple Timewalking (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/6690-Black-Temple-Timewalking) during the event! Black Temple awards base item level 385 gear.


The weekly quest (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/44164-a-burning-path-through-time) from your adventure guide rewards Azerite Power and Cache of Dazar'alor Treasures (https://www.wowdb.com/items/165730-cache-of-dazaralor-treasures).
You can find Cupri (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/98685-cupri) in Shattrath.
Don't forget to keep working towards Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk), Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger), and Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade)!
Reins of the Infinite Timereaver (https://www.wowdb.com/items/133543-reins-of-the-infinite-timereaver) can also drop from any Timewalking dungeon boss.

Brawl - Tarren Mill vs. Southshore
Originally introduced as a part of the World of Warcraft 10-Year Anniversary event, this Brawl harkens back to the early days of WoW PvP, with an epic battle between the two towns of Tarren Mill and Southshore. In this game of war, you’ll need to work as a team and deplete your foes’ resources to claim victory.

Call to Arms: Tiragarde Sound
This week's Call to Arms is Call to Arms: Tiragarde Sound (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52948-call-to-arms-tiragarde-sound) or Call to Arms: Tiragarde Sound (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52956-call-to-arms-tiragarde-sound). Head to Tiragarde Sound with warmode enabled to kill 10 players of the opposing faction. This will reward you with conquest and 75 reputation for either 7th Legion (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2159-7th-legion) or The Honorbound (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2157-the-honorbound).
