Blizzard has given us some beta keys to help you prepare for Battle for Azeroth! This week you will need to clear the end of Antorus or complete a Mythic 2+ if you haven't already.


This giveaway is for 600 US/EU Battle for Azeroth Beta keys!
This giveaway runs from July 10 to July 15
To enter complete the Seat of the Pantheon (https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/11991-seat-of-the-pantheon) or Keystone Initiate (https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/11183-keystone-initiate) achievement and then log in to the contest site.
You will need to log in with your Battle.Net account (https://bna.mmo-champion.com/login) (It's safe! (https://dev.battle.net/docs/read/log_in_with_blizzard)) so that we can confirm you have earned the achievement.
You can be sure that you are entered by checking the contest status page (https://bna.mmo-champion.com/contest/8).
Winners will be selected by July 16
You will need to check the contest status page (https://bna.mmo-champion.com/contest/8) to see if you have won and claim your key.
Winners will have 5 days to view their key. After that another winner will be selected.
Having trouble? Read this information first. (https://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2403375-Battle-for-Azeroth-Beta-Key-Giveaway-Week-2-We-ve-Seen-It-All!?p=49545634&viewfull=1#post49545634)
