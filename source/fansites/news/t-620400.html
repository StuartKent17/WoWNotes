The reason why you can interact with such things is simple.
The sourge was an own faction since the very early beginning of WoW besides the horde, the alliance, the goblin factions and gnome factions, troll faction and what we all know.

So, why can you interact with this stuff?

The only reason is, that the "scourge faction" isn't bugless yet.
Just think as a game designer:
You put mobs in the game, give them the faction "scourge". Okay, sounds logic,
if you're alliance or horde or goblin or stuff you can actually kill it.
Thats what it supposed to do. Just get killed.

The other things like the spike with the head on it near thrall (I think it's this head
which you can turn in, some orc from BWL drop it) was supposed to be used
by the alliance and by the horde, since they didn't know that they will inplement
the ability to be friendly to the scourge (or in other words: to join the scourge), so
these objects ingame are "unscripted" if you interact with them as a member of the scourge.

Might be turn out fun, let's see what kind of bugs / exploits pop up next :)
