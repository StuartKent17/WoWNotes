Patch 8.1.5 PTR - Zandalari Troll Embrace of the Loa Shrines
The Zandalari Troll racial ability Embrace of the Loa (https://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/292752-embrace-of-the-loa) now requires your Zandalari troll to use the ability in front of one of the Zandalari loa shrines around Dazar'alor. This grants you the buff of the loa and will remain until you replace it with another loa shrine. This activates a 5 day cooldown during which you cannot change the buff. You can talk to guards in Dazar'alor to get the specific shrine marked on the map.


Embrace of the Loa (https://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/292492-embrace-of-the-loa)

Embrace of Krag'wa (https://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/292364-embrace-of-kragwa) - (Top left shrine on map) Taking damage has a chance to grant you [ Greater of {300% of Spell Power} or {300% of Attack Power * pctD * (1 + 0)} ] additional Health and [ Greater of {50% of Spell Power} or {50% of Attack Power * pctD * (1 + 0)} ] Armor.
Embrace of Kimbul (https://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/292363-embrace-of-kimbul) - (Top right shrine on map) Your damaging abilities have a chance to cause the target to bleed for [ Greater of {66% of Spell Power} or {66% of Attack Power * pctD * (1 + 0)} * 3 ] over 6 sec. This effect stacks up to 3 times.
Embrace of Bwonsamdi (https://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/292360-embrace-of-bwonsamdi) - (lower left shrine on map) Your damaging abilities have a chance to deal [ Greater of {66% of Spell Power} or {66% of Attack Power * pctD * (1 + 0)} ] Shadow damage and heal you for 100% of the damage done.
Embrace of Paku (https://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/292361-embrace-of-paku) - (Bottom left shrine on map) Your abilities have a chance to grant you 5% critical strike for 0 sec.
Embrace of Gonk (https://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/292362-embrace-of-gonk) - (Bottom right shrine on map) Increase movement speed by 5%.
Embrace of Akunda (https://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/292359-embrace-of-akunda) - (lower right shrine on map) Your healing abilities have a chance to heal their target for [ Greater of {50% of Spell Power} or {50% of Attack Power * pctD * (1 + 0)} ].

Embrace of Krag'wa - Give you armor and hp still. Less effective for healer and dps but fairly decent.

Embrace of Paku - Your abilities have a chance to grant you 5% critical strike for 0 sec. Totally cross specs
Embrace of Gonk - Increase movement speed by 5%. Totally cross specs
Embrace of Akunda - Your healing abilities have a chance to heal their target. Less effective for dps and tank but still proc, and if you pikk this one you probably are a main healer and have an heal as dps and tank spec.

On this race you have the opportunity for free every 5 days to optimize your char for the spec you want to play. Will be a less effective for others specs, but it's pretty like every other races. And in top of that you still have a perfectly cross spec crit proc.

The only down side can be uptime/damage/heal/... for now it's still on PTR.
