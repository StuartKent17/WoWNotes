Legion - Invasion Event
There are some pieces of the pre-expansion invasion event currently in the Alpha, but they are very much incomplete.


The Legion will invade several different zones of Azeroth. Right now they can be found in Tanaris, Hillsbrad, and the Wetlands.
Right now there are small invasions, larger invasions, and named NPCs marked on the map when you are in each of those zones.
There are a few quests that seem to be related, piloting a vehicle to kill Legion invaders, as well as timed kills.
Several of the invasion sites have a chest you can open. Currently the chest only contains a placeholder, Timeless Isle items.
There are also buffs you can pick up at some of the invasion sites, such as Felrune Barrier (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/185783-felrune-barrier).
There are several scenarios that may be related to the event invasion point.
