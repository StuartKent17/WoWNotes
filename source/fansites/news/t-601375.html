Addon Highlight
A lot of people have been asking about it on the forums, I thought it could be a good thing to mention Ratingbuster (http://www-en.curse-gaming.com/downloads/details/4991/).
On most of my items screenshots, you can notice stats summary, and rating conversion into %, that's the mod that does everything. It's very customizeable and I suggest that you read the whole mod description on Curse to tweak it to your desires.

Also, just another bit of information... On my travels around the various WoW fansites, I found a very interesting video on worldofwar that had an intereview with some of the developers.

A summary of what was said was basically:
Onyxia deep breath was 100% random, it wasnt triggered by lack of dots or standing too close. One patch it bugged which led to much speculuation on the forums.

Naxxramas was the dungeon most effort was put into and the plans for it had been set out before WoW even shipped.

The craze of guilds releasing kill movies was something unheard of in previous MMOs because you didnt want to reveal your strategy to anyone. It started because a guild called Conquest were punished by WoW for exploiting in Molten Core (back in the day) and to get their own back on Blizzard they released kill movies and tactics.

WoW developers actually have their own guild on live servers but they are only doing KZ casually.
