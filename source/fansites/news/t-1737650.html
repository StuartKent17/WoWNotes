Blackrock Foundry Item Level Increase Effects
Our friends at AskMrRobot have updated their site with the item level changes and done the math (http://blog.askmrrobot.com/2015/03/brf-item-level-increase/) to figure out how much the item level increase will help you. You can load your character (http://www.askmrrobot.com/wow/player) and see your new item level.

If you uploaded combat logs or want to guess your percentile ranking, you can use the boss level DPS benchmarks (http://www.askmrrobot.com/wow/combatlog/stats/dps/heroic/gruul#per=95,ilvl=675) to see how much DPS on each boss you can expect to gain from your new item level. You can also use the Wipe Rate graphs (http://www.askmrrobot.com/wow/combatlog/stats/kill-rate/heroic/blackhand#) to see how much an item level increase should help your raid.
