Fetid Devourer Preview - Uldir: Battle for Azeroth
Today we are taking a look at Fetid Devourer, one of the bosses in Uldir, a Battle for Azeroth raid.


<iframe width="900" height="506" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/pPsgoqYrrHY?rel=0" frameborder="0" allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen></iframe>

The experiments within Uldir conducted tests upon countless lifeforms, including several loa who inhabited the forests around Zandalar. After the experiments proved lethal, the loa's remains were disposed of improperly, much to the delight of the dark consciousness imprisoned within Uldir.


Overview - Periodically, Waste Disposal Units around the room will activate, dumping waste into the room and leaving behind Corruption Corpuscles. Players must kill these before they release Enticing Essence (https://beta.wowdb.com/spells/262364-enticing-essence), or Fetid Devourer will move to them and Consume Corruption (https://beta.wowdb.com/spells/262370-consume-corruption), healing and gaining energy. At 50% health, Fetid Devourer will enter into a Fetid Frenzy (https://beta.wowdb.com/spells/262378-fetid-frenzy).

Tanks - The primary tank on Fetid Devourer will take more consistent - but overall higher - damage.
The second tank should stand near the primary tank to be hit by Terrible Thrash (https://beta.wowdb.com/spells/262277-terrible-thrash), and be prepared for large spikes of damage when it hits.
Be prepared for the damage increase due to Fetid Frezy (https://beta.wowdb.com/spells/262378-fetid-frenzy)!
Healers - Terrible Thrash (https://beta.wowdb.com/spells/262277-terrible-thrash) deals very high damage.
After players are afflicted with Malodorous Miasma (https://beta.wowdb.com/spells/262313-malodorous-miasma), they will develop Putrid Paroxysm (https://beta.wowdb.com/spells/262314-putrid-paroxysm), which inflicts high damage and requires focused healing.
Be prepared for the damage increase due to Fetid Frezy (https://beta.wowdb.com/spells/262378-fetid-frenzy)!
Damage Dealers - Kill Corruption Corpuscles before they finish releasing Enticing Essence (https://beta.wowdb.com/spells/262364-enticing-essence).
When Chimaera nears 100 energy, prepare to avoid Rotting Regurgitation (https://beta.wowdb.com/spells/262291-rotting-regurgitation)!

Thrashing Terror (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/262255) - Every fourth attack, Fetid Devourer will use Thrash on the closest player to the current tank.

Terrible Thrash (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/262277) - Thrashes the target, inflicting 300% of a standard melee attack to them.

Rotting Regurgitation (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/262291) - When Fetid Devourer reaches 100 Energy, he unleashes a Rotting Regurgitation, inflicting 52 Nature damage, knocking back and applying Maldorous Miasma to targets in a cone in front of him.
<img src="https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Shockwave Stomp (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/262288) - Inflicts 25 Physical damage to all players and knocks them back.
Malodorous Miasma (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/262313) - The foul stench of the area sickens players, inflicting 11 Nature damage to afflicted players every 2 sec. for 18 sec. This effect stacks.<br/>When this effect expires, it mutates into Putrid Paroxysm on the affected player.

Putrid Paroxysm (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/262314) - Inflicts 29 Nature damage every 2 sec. for 6 sec. This effect stacks.<br/>If a player with Putrid Paroxysm would be afflicted with Malodorous Miasma, they instead gain a stack of Putrid Paroxysm.

Fetid Frenzy (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/262378) - At 50% Health, Fetid Devourer frenzies, increasing damage dealt by 25% and damage taken by 50%.
Consume Corruption (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/262370) - Whenever Fetid Devourer senses Enticing Essence, he will move to consume the source, healing Fetid Devourer for 10% and granting 20 Energy.
<img src="https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Waste Disposal Units - Periodically, Waste Disposal Units around the room will activate, dumping waste into the room and leaving behind Corruption Corpuscles. On Mythic Difficulty, one of the Trash Chutes will activate early, creating a Mutated Mass - a larger Corruption Corpuscle that casts Enticing Essence more slowly. Standing inside the stream of waste from a Waste Disposal Unit or being hit by Rotting Regurgitation will also apply Malodorous Miasma.

Corruption Corpuscle -

Enticing Essence (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/262364) - Releases pheromones, drawing Fetid Devourer to consume the Corruption Corpuscle.

<img src="https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Mutated Mass -

Enticing Essence (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/262364) - Releases pheromones, drawing Fetid Devourer to consume the Corruption Corpuscle.
