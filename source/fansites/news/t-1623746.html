Patch 6.0.3 Hotfixes - November 3
Darkmoon Faire

Reduced the levels of Moonfang, Moonfang Packmate, Moonfang Dreadhowl, Moonfang Snarler, and Darkmoon Rabbit back to pre-Warlords expansion levels. The creatures needs to respawn for the changes to take effect.
Heirloom items sold at the Darkmoon Faire should no longer have a gold cost associated with them.
Firebird's Challenge (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/36481-firebirds-challenge): Blazing Rings are no longer shared with other players.
It's Hammer Time (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/29463-its-hammer-time): Characters that have been flagged as PvP enabled should now be able to complete the quest.

Earthquake (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/61882) knock down effect is no longer affected by diminishing returns, but the knock down effect now only has a duration of 1.5 seconds.


Quests

Blood of the Betrayer: Rogues should now be able to remove Arcane Infused Armor from Nalice, and be able to complete the quest.
Dah, Nunt... Dah, Nunt...: Players should no longer remain stunned indefinitely and be able to complete the quest.
Get Kraken!: Increased the health for North Sea Kraken by 1000%.


Dungeons, Raids, and Scenarios

Raids

Throne of Thunder

[Requires a realm restart.] Dark Winds debuff should no longer incorrectly persist on the character, preventing them from being able to use mounts.

Ulduar

XT-002 Deconstructor's Searing Light ability should no longer deal an excessive amount of damage.

The Obsidian Sanctum

Defeating Sartharion too quickly should no longer cause him to not drop a mount.

Black Temple

Illidan Stormrage: Fixed an issue where defeating Flames of Azzinoth too quickly in phase 2 may cause Illidan to not land.

Dungeons

Ahn'kahet: The Old Kingdom: Resolved an issue where players defeating multiple packs of creatures in the room too quickly could cause Jedoga Shadowseeker to remain not attackable.
Hellfire Ramparts: Players should now be able to loot the Reinforced Fel Iron Chest after defeating Vazruden the Herald.
Razorfen Downs: Resolved an issue where Amnennar the Coldbringer may sometimes fail to be summoned when Death Speaker Blackthorn is defeated.


Battlegrounds and Arenas

Battlegrounds

Increased Honor gains for Arathi Basin, Deepwind Gorge, Eye of the Storm, and The Battle for Gilneas. Honor is now awarded for every 150 resources gathered (up from Honor being awarded for every 250 resources gathered).
Returning a flag in Battlegrounds should correctly play an appropriate sound once more.


Items

Made a number of adjustments where lower level food items were providing a larger than intended buff.


UI

Timeless Isle Tokens and legendary weapons looted during the fight with Kael'thas Sunstrider in Tempest Keep are no longer included in the guild news panel.
Bags set to "Ignore this bag" should now be saving the setting correctly when the character logs out or zones into a new area.
/console colorblindshader [0-8] has been re-enabled and replaced with /console colorblindsimulator [0-8].


Bug Fixes

Resolved a frame rate stuttering issue that was affecting some Mac OS clients.
[Requires a realm restart.] Mobile Armory's guild chat should be working once more.



Warlords of Draenor - Garrisons: Gladiator's Sanctum
Today we are taking a look at the final Medium building, the Gladiator's Sanctum. You can find the other Medium building previews in the Warlords of Draenor menu above this post.

The Gladiator's Sanctum (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/161-gladiators-sanctum) takes up a Medium plot which can also be used for a Lumber Mill (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/138-lumber-mill), Barn (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/133-barn), Trading Post (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/111-trading-post) or Inn / Tavern (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/36-trading-post). Once upgraded to Tier 3, your garrison will have two Medium plots available.

Abilities
This building is good for PvP perks, achievements, and titles.


Level 1 - Increases your out-of-combat regeneration in outdoor Draenor zones and allows the collection of broken bones from defeated enemy players (used for Conqueror's Tribute).
Level 2 - Enables safe fall and underwater breathing in outdoor Draenor zones. Also enables the Nemesis quest series for defeating enemy players of specific races.
Level 3 - Show your might over opposing hostile players across Draenor and hone your strength on the blood-soaked arena floor. Slay your enemies and return with proof of their demise to earn powerful tributes!



Level 3 Unlock
The Level 3 version of this building is unlocked by completing the The Bone Collector (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/9495-the-bone-collector) achievement, which requires you to collect 4,000 Broken Bones (http://www.wowdb.com/items/118043-broken-bones) from killing players.


Work Orders
The Gladiator's Sanctum allows you to collect Broken Bones (http://www.wowdb.com/items/118043-broken-bones) from killing players, with ~4 earned per kill. You can then trade 10 of these bones for Gold, Honor Points, and Conquest Points.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/october/thumb/sanctumWorkOrder.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/october/sanctumWorkOrder.jpg)


Training Dummies
This building has multiple DPS training dummies, as well as a Healer training dummy.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/october/thumb/trainingDummy01.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/october/trainingDummy01.jpg) http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/october/thumb/trainingDummy02.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/october/trainingDummy02.jpg)


Highmaul Coliseum
Upon unlocking the Level 3 building, you can queue for Highmaul Coliseum. The Coliseum is a scenario that has a large number of players that participate in free for all PvP to the last man standing. If the fight goes on too long, the environment may finish it for you.

The winner gets The Last of Us (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/9725-the-last-of-us) achievement, along with a title and some items. If you didn't win, you will get some mail with a Highmaul Lockbox (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/119000-highmaul-lockbox), which has a chance for an item level 620 PvP item, Apexis Crystals, some honor, or other things.

You fought well...
For a complete failure. Perhaps this box of trinkets will help you in achieving victory next time.

Try not to lose so badly again. Just remember that second place is first loser.

- Gharg

P.S. It might also just be a box full of junk. Who knows?

Many of these achievements reward a title for killing many players of another race. Once you select an enemy race (http://beta.wowdb.com/quests/36955-nemesis-hunter-hunted), you cannot change to another until you have completed the selected one (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/36925-nemesis-becoming-death).
