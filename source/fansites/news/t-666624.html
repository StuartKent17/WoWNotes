People ... this is 10-man. If you have been running 25-man Ulduar and defeating hard modes left and right, then 10-man TOC IS GOING TO BE EASIER FOR YOU! Period. That is the way it was designed!!

The natural progression paths are SEPARATE!

10-Naxx --> 10-EoE --> 10-Uld --> 10-ToC --> 10-Ice
25-Naxx --> 25-EoE--> 25-Uld --> 25-ToC --> 25-Ice

If you have 10 good players then you can progress all the way to 10-Ice without ever setting foot in a 25-man raid. That is the entire point of having this progression path. So, naturally, completing 25-Uld with Hard Modes is going to make 10 ToC much easier as you already have gear comparable to 10-ToC or even 10-ToC-HM.

I also have to agree with Pickahoad ...


if the content held off the best raiding guilds in the world for long periods of time, how accessible do you think it would be to everyone else?


This is what Blizzard was changing with this expansion. Making end-game raiding accessible to a lot more raiders, not just the upper 1% or 2% of all players (as they did with Naxx in Vanilla).
