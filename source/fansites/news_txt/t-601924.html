2.2 Patch
Friday, Hortus made a post on the PTR forums about downtimes in the next weeks, I don't really know how I missed it.

Because we are currently implementing many fixes for sound issues in 2.2 we will likely be pushing new patches to the PTR late at night several times in the upcoming weeks.

As I'm usually not around that late in the evening I can not always make announcements when it goes down.

So if the server goes down late at night, do not panic, it will most likely come back up in a few hours with a new patch.
