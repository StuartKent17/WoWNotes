Battle for Stromgarde Warfront Available for Hordewarfronts
The Horde have completed the progress bar to launch The Battle for Stromgarde Warfront! Horde characters can now queue for the 20 man PvE large scale scenario to fight for control of Arathi Highlands against the opposite faction AI. Pick up the quest Warfront: The Battle for Stromgarde (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53416-warfront-the-battle-for-stromgarde) and complete the scenario for an item level 370 piece of gear! Warfronts award item level 340 loot baseline as well upon completion. The timer bar says that Horde characters have 7 days to complete the Warfront before the Alliance characters will be able to start to contribute resources to launch their Warfront!
