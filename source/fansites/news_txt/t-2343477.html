Warforged Nightmare Giveaway
Thanksgiving just ended and we are thankful for you! We are giving away some Warforged Nightmare (http://www.wowdb.com/items/112326-warforged-nightmare) mounts to celebrate Thanksgiving.

Using the item that comes in the mail (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/june/wfnmMail.jpg) will give you the Nightmarish Hitching Post (http://www.wowdb.com/items/112324-nightmarish-hitching-post), which can be used to gain a buff that makes all of your mounts look like the Warforged Nightmare for the next 20 minutes.


The giveaway is for 10 x Warforged Nightmare (http://www.wowdb.com/items/112326-warforged-nightmare) mounts.
To enter the giveaway, just reply to this post with what you are thankful for! Make sure to mention if you play on US or EU servers in your reply.
The giveaway will be closed on Monday November 27, 2017 at 11:59 PM EST and winners will be chosen randomly.
Winners will be contacted by PM on the site. There is no email alert by default, you will need to log in and check your PMs (http://www.mmo-champion.com/private.php?folderid=0).
A note will be added to the front page of the site when winners have been contacted.
