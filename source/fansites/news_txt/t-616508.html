WotLK - Heroic Dungeon Loot List
During the past weeks, we mostly focused on raid instances and didn't talk much about Heroic instances. It's time to fix that, in Wrath of the Lich King heroic instances have their own loot table, and now that most (all ?) instances are available in Heroic Mode on beta servers, that's a LOT of items. All these items are ilvl 200 (Same as Naxxramas-10, but rare instead of epic obviously), for the moment we won't sort them by instance because we don't have enough data to do that, but it will eventually happen.

The list is not complete, but most of the items should be here. I'll probably start working on nice lists of loot for each dungeon very soon, you can stop threatening me in mail because I didn't update some of the lists in a long time or kept them spread over a few posts. (Who said Obsidian Sanctum and Eye of Eternity ?)
