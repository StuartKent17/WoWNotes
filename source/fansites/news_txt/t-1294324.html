Ghostcrawler on Tri Spec
More replies today to the Tri Spec tweet (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/3232-Heirloom-Shields-Ghostcrawler-on-Tri-Spec-April-30-Hotfixes-Blue-Tweets-Fan-Art) from yesterday!
All the classes already feel the same thanks to you.
We try to strike a balance between the demands for "I need X tool that the other guy has" and classes still feeling cool. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/329753630137802754))

Specs should be saveable, e.g. equip sets. Keep talents, glyphs, action bars. Limit could then be set to 3, 4, 5 w/e
Yeah, we think an approach like that would take the hassle out of it. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/329754437725859840))

Could you explain the logic behind tri-spec diminishing class uniqueness? more choices = more variety.
You no longer think of your friend as a Feral or a Balance druid. She is just a druid who can fill any role just like any druid. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/329758932774039552))

If you were really serious about this statement, a character would be permanently limited to 2 of the 3 specs.
The intent of letting you remake choices was never supposed to be "I can swap to anything at a moment's notice." (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/329760054200909825))
It was supposed to be "I made a decision that now I really regret and wish I could make over." (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/329760165333200896))
I do worry sometimes that dual spec solved a problem (talent points were a pain to rebuy) that no longer exists. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/329759928753471488))

Blue Tweets
Character
Hey! Sorry if this has been asked before, but there was talk about letting us hide weapon enchant glows iirc. Idea scrapped?
Not scrapped. Just not sure yet on the right design. It gets into the issue of weapon transmog pretty quickly. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/326870634279890944))

I don't understand why rings are unique-equipped, is it too game breaking to have two of the same kind? Any plans to change?
You only get so many gear slots, so chasing the same BiS piece for two just removes some of the gearing options / opportunities. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/326446309424918529))
That's understandable, but going for BiS is completely optional and nothing something all players do, I'm no expert but...
Unfortunately, we find the opposite. Many players expect BiS and even consider the second place item to be "garbage." (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/326453033359794177))

I like the new talent system but it makes leveling kinda unrewarding. Long stretches where you get nothing.
Agree. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/326447222856220672))

As a corollary, that mean healers lacking mitigation should actually be leading "meters", Yet, the opposite is true.
Most players would do well to compare their own performance night to night and stop worrying about what everyone else does. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/327959748706197505))
Either remove the ability of HPS meters/WoL to work,or balance the game around it.Cant change everyone's minds
Neither of those seems like a reasonable response to misinterpretation of data. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/328223580523462656))

Do you feel that the shared achievements have worked out for 10 and 25 man raiding? Any plans for the future?
We think shared is fine, but it would be nice to be able to determine which version someone earned the achievement in. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/327876976977264640))

Eternal Flame blanketing with paladin mastery is so strong. Why not revert the mastery nerf and make EF HoT not stack mastery.
We buffed EF just before release and have regretted it ever since. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/327874313933955072))
Mastery was too strong regardless, but longer-term we don't want EF to be the only choice for Holy. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/327874259718377472))
Could you follow up with a why? EF(in a 25h raid) implies much greater skill then keeping up SS+using LoD

Keep in mind: to drop EF for SS, LoD needs to be viable. It's not good enough atm.
LoD just has to beat WoG, which it does. Eternal Flame is the problem. It lets Holy become a Resto druid and a Disc priest. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/327959047611498496))

Recent Q+A mentioned more with shields - possible future choice for Ret (a la War TG/SMF)? 1h-shield Ret sounds interesting!
I think there is some interesting design space for offensive shield use. The 300s Spartans don't feel like Prot warriors to me. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/327868317043920896))

I think retri pala still needs some special/unique ability to be picked up for "serious" games in pvp. What do you think?
Not crazy about the idea of 34 unique abilities to ensure that every spec gets picked up for PvP. Just feels like an arms race. (Source (https://twitter.com/Ghostcrawler/status/327871859209166848))

An idea I had was to make RoF only generate embers if it hits at least 3 targets. What do you think about that?
