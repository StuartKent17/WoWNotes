Pet Battle Bonus Event
Put your best battle pet teams to the test with this week’s event. To get in on the action, open your Pet Journal (Collections Shift+ P) and click the “Find Battle” button to be match up with an opponent with their own team of well-trained companions.

Look for the following all week long:


Chronicler Shoopa in Zuldazar and in Boralus has a quest for you – The Very Best (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53038-the-very-best). Or, if you forget to pick it up there, you can start it within the Adventure Journal.
Quest requirement: Win 5 pet battles versus other players with a team of three level-25 pets.
Reward: One Ultimate Battle-Training Stone (https://www.wowdb.com/items/122457-ultimate-battle-training-stone).
Passive bonus: All Pet Battle experience gains are increased by +200%


Every Week
The Bonus Events system consists of a rotating schedule of different activities, currently scheduled to run each week beginning on Tuesdays. Each Bonus Event grants a passive bonus to a particular game activity and offers a once-per-event quest with a noteworthy reward for accomplishing a related goal. The in-game calendar can serve as your one-stop reference for the event schedule. The Adventure Guide also offers a direct link to active Bonus Events, allowing you to easily accept any associated quests.

PvP Brawl - Gravity Lapse
Defy the law of gravity in this week’s PvP Brawl: Gravity Lapse!

Every other week, a new PvP Brawl will be available to players looking for something just a little different. Each Brawl will offer a variety of modes, rules, and scenic changes to the usual Battleground gameplay, and you’ll find them in the Group Finder tool (hotkey “I”) in the Player vs. Player tab. Just like Random Battlegrounds, you’ll receive a reward for achieving victory. Here are just a few of the Brawls you can look forward to:

Gravity Lapse


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/april/thumb/gravityLapse.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/april/gravityLapse.jpg)

Gravity getting you down? Step into the Eye of the Storm and be prepared for a whole new en-lighten-ing experience. Every minute, players on the battlefield will find themselves launched into the air, only to gently fall toward the ground again in a perpetual aerial ballet as they try to accrue enough resources to take the win home for their team.


Call to Arms: Stormsong Valley
This week's Call to Arms is Call to Arms: Stormsong Valley (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52782-call-to-arms-stormsong-valley) or Call to Arms: Stormsong Valley (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52957-call-to-arms-stormsong-valley). Head to Stormsong Valley with warmode enabled to kill 10 players of the opposing faction. This will reward you with conquest and 75 reputation for either 7th Legion (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2159-7th-legion) or The Honorbound (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2157-the-honorbound).
