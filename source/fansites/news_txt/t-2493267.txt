Essences Feedback - Healer Essences
Some tuning tweaks that will be on PTR soon, noting here because they’ll be coming in as PTR hotfixes at this point:

Artifice of Time, Minor: Haste/Speed bonus increased 13%
Ever-Rising Tide, Minor: Int bonus increased 20%
Ever-Rising Tide, Major: Duration both active duration, and bonus duration reduced to 8 seconds
Life-Binder’s Invocation, Major: Cooldown reduced to 2.5 minutes
Vitality Conduit, All: Transfer amount increased 15%
Vitality Conduit, Minor: Proc rate increased 25%
Vitality Conduit, Minor Rank 3: Absorb value increased 38%
Lucid Dreams, Minor: Proc rate for mana users reduced 20%




Essences Feedback - Multiple Role Essences
Some additional hotfix balance changes have been made for a few Essences:

Crucible of Flame

Minor effect rank 1 damage and healing values increased by 35%.
Minor effect rank 1 proc chance increased for all healer roles.


Vision of Perfection

Major effect proc chance reduced for Holy Paladin.




Stress Test Has Concluded -- Thank You!
Following a final flurry of sudden-but-inevitable deaths due to bosses spawned in the starting zones and an impressively huge lineup of people in Goldshire, we’ve concluded the Stress Test.

Please feel free to leave your feedback on your experiences in this stress test in our Feedback thread. We’re particularly interested in your experiences after the initial four hours of the test. Please let us know what time-frame your experiences are from when you post feedback.

Again, thank you very much for participating!
