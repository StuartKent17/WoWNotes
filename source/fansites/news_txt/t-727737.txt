Midsummer Festival - Lord Ahune Loot Table
The Midsummer Festival is here and players can now fight Lord Ahune for super easy epics, because we all like super easy epics. You will be teleported to the instance instantly, you then get to kill Lord Ahune once. You will get an epic item from his chest see the loot table and all the members of the group will be rewarded with 2 x Emblem of Frost http://db.mmo-champion.com/i49426 from the Satchet of Chilled Goods http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/54536/satchel-of-chilled-goods/. 

Important - You only get the bag with the 2 x Emblem of Frost http://db.mmo-champion.com/i49426 from the Satchet of Chilled Goods http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/54536/satchel-of-chilled-goods/ once a day, but you can kill the boss as many times as you want if you want to farm the chest. 
Important #2 - The chest only drops cloaks, the bag has a chance to drop rare loot like the Frostscythe and the companion pets.
