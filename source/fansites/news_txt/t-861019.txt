Boubouille2011-03-01, 06:37 PMUpdate Patch 4.1 PTR - Official Notes Update
The Patch 4.1 PTR Notes  have been updated once again with the 2 missing changes from build 13698. 
 Warrior Forums  / Cataclysm Talent Calculator  / Skills/Talents 
Arms

Strikes of Opportunity damage has been increased by 10% to keep stat parity with other strike changes.


Fury

Unshackled Fury now only grants 2 base points of mastery, down from 8. This change offsets the Flurry boost to better control Fury burst damage.



Patch 4.1 on PTRs - Build 13698
A new build will be deployed on test realms today. Most of these changes were already documented in the latest PTR notes update  but keep an eye out for new things!

Achievements Changes
Dungeons & Raids
Cataclysm Dungeon


Spirit Twister  Defeat 20 Twisted Spirits within 15 seconds during the Jin'do encounter in Zul'Gurub on Heroic difficulty.
Ring out!  Defeat Daakara in Zul'Aman on Heroic difficulty without leaving the rectangular turquoise stonework inscribed on the floor in the center of his area.



Reputation


Ambassador of the Horde  now requires you to be exalted with Bilgewater Cartel.
Ambassador of the Alliance  now requires you to be exalted with Gilneas.




Spell Changes
Class Skills
Companions


Lashtail Hatchling  has been added to the game. 


 Death Knight Forums  / Cataclysm Talent Calculator  / Beta Skills/Talents 
Unholy


Raise Ally  has been revamped and is now a battle rez spell - Pours dark energy into a dead target, reuniting spirit and body to allow the target to reenter battle with 20% health and 20% mana. 30 yd range, 50 Runic Power, 10 min cooldown, Instant


Glyph


Inscription: Glyph of Raise Ally  has been replaced by Inscription: Glyph of Death Gate  - Reduces the cast time of your Death Gate spell by 25%.


 Druid Forums  / Cataclysm Talent Calculator  / Beta Skills/Talents 
Feral


Stampeding Roar Bear  now increases movement speed by 60% for 8 sec, up from 40% for 6 sec.
Stampeding Roar Cat  now increases movement speed by 60% for 8 sec, up from 40% for 6 sec.


Restoration


Lifebloom  now costs 11% of base mana, up from 7%. Base healing reduced by 20%, from 2306 to 1847.


Balance


Cyclone  now lasts 5 sec, down from 6 sec.
Starsurge  base damage has been reduced by 20%, from [ 1272 to 1756 ] to [ 1017 to 1405 ]. 
Moonkin Form  now frees the caster of movement impairing effects instead of slowing effects. 


 Priest Forums  / Cataclysm Talent Calculator  / Beta Skills/Talents 
Holy


Body and Soul Rank 2  now increases target's movement speed by 30%, up from 25%.


Discipline


Inner Fire  now lasts until cancelled.
Inner Will  now lasts until cancelled.


 Warlock Forums  / Cataclysm Talent Calculator  / Beta Skills/Talents 
Demonology


Demonic Empowerment  now also removes fear, banish, or horror effects on your Felguard. 


 Warrior Forums  / Cataclysm Talent Calculator  / Beta Skills/Talents 
Protection


Spell Reflection  now has a 25 sec cooldown, up from 10 sec. 
Gag Order  no longer affects Shield Bash. 
Shield Mastery  no longer reduces the cooldown of Spell Reflect. Now causes your Shield Block to also reduce magic damage taken by 8/16/25%.


Fury


Unshackled Fury  now Increases the benefit of abilities that cause or require you to be enraged by 11.2%, down from 44,8%.
Pummel  no longer requires Battle or Berserker Stance. 


Arms


Strikes of Opportunity  now gives a 17.6% up from 16% chance for your melee attacks to instantly trigger an additional melee attack for 100% normal damage. Each point of Mastery increases this chance by 2.2%. Up from 2%
Drums of War  no longer affects Shield Bash, now affects Demoralizing Shout, Intimidating Shout and Challenging Shout.
Juggernaut  now reduces the cooldown of your Charge by 2 sec, down from 3 sec.


Glyph


Inscription: Glyph of Spell Reflection  now reduces the cooldown on Spell Reflection by 5 sec, up from 1 sec.
