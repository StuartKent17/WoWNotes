Tier 8 Sets - Final Model Preview
Tonight, we saw the first preview of the stats of a Tier 8 set on the PTRs, for the moment they're only T7.25 sets with an higher ilvl (and armor).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/march/tier8_warrior_10_tank_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/march/tier8_warrior_10_tank.jpg)

I guess it's a good time to post the final preview of the Tier 8 models, there are actually plenty of changes since the last time I posted screenshots of tier 8. The biggest change is probably that all the "old" tier 8 sets have been upgraded with high-resolution textures. Here are the changes for each class since the last time I posted these sets:

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ideathknights.gif Death Knight - Higher resolution textures.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid - All races added, robe version of the set added.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter - Higher resolution textures.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/images.gif Mage - Higher resolution textures, and finally decided that the pants version of the set might be the final version for players. As of today there isn't any texture for Tier 8 Mage robe in the game files.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin - All races added, robe version of the set added.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest - Higher resolution textures.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue - Higher resolution textures. There are still a few bugs with this set and the green recolor isn't available on male characters, undead females are also unavailable.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman - All races added, robe version of the set added.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock - Higher resolution textures.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior - All races added.

There is also a very high definition screenshot available for each set.

Ignis the Firemaster
Health - 24.4M (Heroic) / ? (Normal)
Scorch (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63476) -- Fires a jet of flame, burning all enemies in front of the caster within 30 yards, dealing 3770 to 4230 Fire damage every half-second for 3 sec. Additionally, the ground is scorched by this effect causing it to catch on fire, dealing 3299 to 3701 Fire damage every second too all enemies within 13 yards. Iron Constructs within this radius will begin to heat up, eventually becoming molten. (10-Man (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=62546) - 2357-2643 Damage)
Flame Jets (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63472) -- Stomps the ground causing geysers of flame to errupt under all enemies. These geysers deal 8483 to 9517 Fire damage, knocking the targets into the air and interrupting any spells being cast for 8 sec. In addition, the targets take 1000 Fire damage per second for 8 sec. (10-Man (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=62680) - 5655-6345 Damage)
Slag Pot (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63477) -- Charges and grabs a random enemy target, tossing them into the caster's slag pot. The target is unable to attack the caster and takes 5000 Fire damage every second for 10 sec. If the target survives, they are imbued with the magical slag, increasing their haste by 150% for until cancelled. (10-Man (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=62717): 3500 damage)

He also activate the Iron Constructs next to him throughout the fight.

Iron Construct
Health - 3.81M (Heroic) / ? Normal
Running them into fire will give them a charge of Heat (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=62343) -- Blasts all nearby Iron Constructs with intense heat, increasing their movement speed by 5 and haste by 5. This effect stacks up to 20 times. If this effect stacks to 20, the affected Iron Construct will become Molten.
Once a construct is Molten (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=62373) he will gain extra haste and deal 4713 to 5287 Fire damage to enemies within 7 yards. Running him into water will destroy it. (10-Man: 2357-2643 Damage)
