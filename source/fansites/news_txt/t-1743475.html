Blue Tweets
Classes
.Why does the Windwalker movespeed aura not stack with the passive portion of Pursuit of Justice?
It's by design. From the Pursuit of Justice tooltip: “This does not stack with most other movement speed increasing effects.” (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/578337646260330497))

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/275-Rogue) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/rogue) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#Q))
the rogue talent Shuriken Toss' auto-attacks only apply lethal poisens? Is this intended?
Yes, only lethal poisons. This was inadvertently left out of the tooltip. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/578334625535115264))

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warlock) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#k))
I still haven't seen any response about the Codex of Xerrath not applying to Glyph of Metamorphosis! On the radar?
We don’t currently have plans to support this particular combination of visual options. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/578336170767060993))

Character / Items
thx for all the heirloom upgrades/tab. Any plans to convert existing plate to str/int like wod add int plate for pallys?
We don’t currently have plans to do that. We like heirlooms being targeted to specific roles. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/578334442877423616))

PvE
is crit chance reduced versus raid bosses?
Crit chance is affected by the target’s level, yes. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/578333770459185152))

PvP
When does the 18sec window start? At the beginning of first cc, end of the 3rd cc? It'd be awesome, if that was specified.
It starts each time the crowd control effect is applied (e.g. the moment the target becomes stunned). (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/578252494360326144))

Misc
Any Chance on Seeing 6.1 Legendary Questline Cinematic on our Garisson like other important Cinematics during lvling ?
We're going to shoot for Patch 6.2 to get this added onto the list. Thanks for the suggestion! (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/578311461732114432))

My bank is getting filled up with in-game books, any plans on implementing a library?
Hmmm, I could see turning all of them into toys at some point, that might be neat. (Muffinus (https://twitter.com/Muffinus/status/578697708006821888))
