Patch 7.2 Hotfixes - April 21
Artifact Challenge

Fixed a bug where some cooldowns were not being reset properly when starting an Artifact Challenge.


Classes

The Artifact traits Master of Combinations, Fury of Ashamane, Warmth of the Phoenix, and Mind Quickening now correctly scale based on level.


Followers

Lady S'theno's Watery Grave ability should now knock enemies up, rather than away.
Fixed an issue preventing a version of Maximillian from having a mount.
Maximillian’s Blessing of Kings should now correctly increase stats.


Items

Rethu's Incessant Courage and Celumbra, the Night's Dichotomy should no longer put you in combat with neutral creatures in certain situations.
Fixed a bug where Riddler’s Mind Worm could be lost if you didn’t loot the item after opening the Gift of the Mind-Seekers.


Services

Transferring a character to a new account should no longer remove access to World Quests.
