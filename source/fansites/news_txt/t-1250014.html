Patch 5.2 - Isle of Giants Updated
A recent PTR build populated the Isle of Giants with many colors and types of dinosaurs. All of them have several million health and drop a few Giant Dinosaur Bones (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/94288-giant-dinosaur-bone), which can be turned in for gold, Mote of Harmony (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/89112-mote-of-harmony), Spirit of Harmony (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/76061-spirit-of-harmony), Spectral Porcupette (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/94190-spectral-porcupette), or a Reins of the Bone-White Primal Raptor (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/94290-reins-of-the-bone-white-primal-raptor#related:reward-of-quest).

Each dinosaur will drop 1 to 9 bones, depending on luck and how much health they have. See the quest tables below for the amounts required for the rewards, but keep in mind this is the PTR and the numbers can change.
