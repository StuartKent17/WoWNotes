Enter once by replying to this news post and telling us what feature or change you would like to see in Patch 6.1 that was not announced in the Patch 6.1 preview (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4590-World-of-Warcraft-in-the-New-Year-Patch-6-1-Heirloom-Changes).
