chaud2017-08-27, 10:31 PMPatch 7.3 - Profession Changes
Patch 7.3 adds new crafted items, materials, upgrade items, and more! 


There are several new crafted items that use new Argus materials, with multiple recipe ranks. 

Rank 1 of the recipes is usually unlocked by completing a quest.
Rank 2 of the recipes are purchased from the Army of the Light vendor for 2,000 gold.
Rank 3 of the recipes drop from a boss in Antorus, the Burning Throne. 

Primal Obliterum  is the new upgrade item

It is crafted from Obliterum  and Primal Sargerite  It can be used to upgrade only the new crafted items. 
Primal Sargerite  is the new Blood of Sargeras , acquired in the same ways as Blood of Sargeras  was. 
There is a new shoulder enchant to obtain Light's Fortune  Boon of the Lightbearer 

Herbalism got a new herb Astral Glory  and pet Fel Lasher 
Mining got a new ore Empyrium  and pick Krokul Mining Pick 
Skinning got a new skin: Fiendish Leather 
Cooking got a new feast: Feast of the Fishes 
There are several turn in quests  for profession materials that grant skill and reputation. 



Alchemy
Alchemists got a new trinket, a potion, and elixirs.


Astral Alchemist Stone  - A new craftable trinket: 

Rank 1 is rewarded from Ascending Alchemy  This quest requires Astral Glory  x 75, Primal Sargerite  x 5, and a Infernal Alchemist Stone 
When you heal or deal damage you have a chance to increase your Strength, Agility, or Intellect by 6,599 for 15 sec. Your highest stat is always chosen. 
Increases the effect that healing and mana potions have on the wearer by 40%. This effect does not stack. 
Can be used as a Philosopher's Stone.

Lightblood Elixir  is rewarded from Limited Supplies 
Tears of the Naaru  is rewarded from Tracking the Trackers 
Astral Healing Potion  and Astral Mana Potion  can be purchased from Alchemist Funen
