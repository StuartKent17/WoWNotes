Patch 8.2 Hotfixes - August 7th, 2019
Dungeons and Raids

Azshara’s Eternal Palace

Za’qul, Harbinger of Ny’alotha

Za’qul’s health reduced by 5.5% on Mythic difficulty.
Dread (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/292963-dread) and Manic Dread (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/302504-manic-dread) should no longer target healers unless not enough other targets are available on all difficulties.
Mind Tether (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/295537-mind-tether) now connects the primary tank to a random target if a second tank is unavailable on all difficulties.
Mind Tether (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/295537-mind-tether) now reflects 100% (was 50%) of the inflicted damage to the Mind Tethered partner on all difficulties.

Crucible of Storms

Crucible of Storms is now correctly available for cross-realm groups on Mythic difficulty.




Items

Azshara's Font of Power (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169314-azsharas-font-of-power) now correctly removes stealth and applies Subterfuge (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/108208-subterfuge) when channeled by a stealthed Rogue.
Ashvane's Razor Coral (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169311-ashvanes-razor-coral) and Leviathan's Lure (https://www.wowdb.com/items/169304-leviathans-lure)'s debuffs now appear on enemy nameplates.
Heart of Azeroth

Fixed an issue causing Last Gift (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/280624-last-gift) (Azerite Trait) to not proc correctly on healing spells cast onto friendly targets.
Fixed a bug causing The Ever-Rising Tide (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/299878-the-ever-rising-tide) (Essence) to give extra stacks of Overcharge Mana to Restoration Druids casting Regrowth in certain situations.



Player versus Player

Notorious Gladiator's Proto-Drake (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/262024-notorious-gladiators-proto-drake) is now correctly displayed in the Mount Journal.


Quests


Fixed an issue that sometimes prevented the Mechagon Reclamation Rig from activating its reset timer.
