Professions Changes
Alchemy

Alchemy: Free Action Potion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/6624/free-action-potion/) now lasts 30 - (Player level - 77) * 3 if the player is above level 77. That's 30 - 8 * 3 = 6 seconds at level 85.
Alchemy: Lifebound Alchemist Stone (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/80508/lifebound-alchemist-stone/) is no longer unique. (Now Unique-Equipped)

Alchemy: Vibrant Alchemist Stone (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/96254/vibrant-alchemist-stone/) is no longer unique. (Now Unique-Equipped)
Alchemy: Volatile Alchemist Stone (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/96252/volatile-alchemist-stone/) is no longer unique. (Now Unique-Equipped)
Alchemy: Living Action Potion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/24367/living-action-potion/) now requires 1 x Mountain Silversage (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/13465/mountain-silversage/) instead of 2 and no longer requires Heart of the Wild (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/10286/heart-of-the-wild/)
Alchemy: Mysterious Potion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/80487/mysterious-potion/) now restores 1 to 30 000 health, up from 1 to 15 000.
Alchemy: Mythical Healing Potion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/80498/mythical-healing-potion/) now restores 18900 to 23100 health, up from 7500 to 12500.
