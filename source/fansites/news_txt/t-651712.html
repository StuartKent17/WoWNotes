World first Val'anyr by Avant Garde
I think we're almost done with Ulduar now! Avant Garde (http://www.avantgardekj.com/) (Kil'jaeden-US) was the first guild to complete Val'anyr, Hammer of the Ancient Kings (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/april/valanyr.jpg) the mace went to Snuggleme (http://www.wowarmory.com/character-achievements.xml?r=Kil%27jaeden&cn=Snuggleme&gn=Avant+Garde), a happy druid.

For your information, the blue bubble around the player is triggered by the proc effect. (See this post (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/12/16903512910-valanyr-hammer-of-the-ancient-kings.html) for more info on it)
