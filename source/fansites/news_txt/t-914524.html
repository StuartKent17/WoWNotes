PvP Mounts - Epic Wolf and Horse PvP Mounts
Two new awesome PvP mounts have been added, the Horde War Wolf (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/70910/horde-war-wolf/) and the Alliance War Steed (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/70909/alliance-war-steed/) are rewards of the Veteran of the Horde II (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/5824/veteran-of-the-horde-ii/) and Veteran of the Alliance II (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/5823/veteran-of-the-alliance-ii/) achievements. There's also a couple of recolors but as far as I know only the first 2 pictures below are used.

PvE Mounts - Fire Hawks
3 different Fire Hawks are currently in the game files but only 2 seem to be implemented/have a known drop location.



Corrupted Fire Hawk (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/97560/corrupted-fire-hawk/) (Purple) is used by Corrupted Egg of Millagazor (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/69230/corrupted-egg-of-millagazor/), a reward of Glory of the Firelands Raider (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/5828/glory-of-the-firelands-raider/)
Pureblood Fire Hawk (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/97493/pureblood-fire-hawk/) (Orange) is used by Smoldering Egg of Millagazor (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/69224/smoldering-egg-of-millagazor/), a rare drop from Ragnaros in the Firelands Raid. (Doesn't seem to be heroic-only so far)
Green Fire Hawk (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/97501/green-fire-hawk/) (Green) is used by Green Fire Hawk Mount (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/69226/green-fire-hawk-mount/) but doesn't seem to be implemented.

PvE Mount - Fire "Raven God" / Flametalon of Alysrazor
The Fire "Raven God" model is the Flametalon (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/101542/flametalon/) mount from Flametalon of Alysrazor (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/71665/flametalon-of-alysrazor/). The mount isn't listed as a loot from Alysrazor in the Encounter Journal for the moment but it's very likely that it will drop from this boss, let's just wait and see if there's an official confirmation in an upcoming PTR build.
