There are a lot of new Mounts coming in Patch 7.3!

Reputation
There are 2 new factions coming in Patch 7.3, Argussian Reach  and Army of the Light 

Argussian Reach  - New Faction in Argus that is unlocked by following the main story line. You can earn reputation with them by completing normal quests, world quests, emissary quests, and invasion quests. They have 6 mounts available at Exalted that are sold by Toraan the Revered  for 10,000g each.


Amethyst Ruinstrider 
Beryl Ruinstrider 
Cerulean Ruinstrider 
Russet Ruinstrider 
Sable Ruinstrider 
Umber Ruinstrider

Raids
There are 4 mounts that are associated with the new raid Antorus, The Burning Throne  These are the Antoran Charhound , Antoran Gloomhound , Violet Spellwing  and the Shackled Ur’zul 


Antoran Charhound  - Rare drop from Hounds of Sargeras Boss Fight.
Antoran Gloomhound  - Complete the achievement Glory of the Argus Raider 
Violet Spellwing  - Complete the quest Blood of the Titan  by looting Blood of the Unmaker  from Argus the Unmaker  on Heroic difficulty or higher.
Shackled Ur’zul  - Drops from Argus the Unmaker  on Mythic difficulty.

The Fierce Gladiator’s Storm Dragon  will be the Gladiator mount for Legion Season 5.

Pond Nettle
There is a new mount from fishing, the Pond Nettle  It is speculated to be another mount similar to the Sea Turtle  in that it is a random chance to drop from pools either on the Broken Isles or Argus.


 


Lucid Nightmare
Sourced from the Puzzler’s Desire, the Lucid Nightmare  is speculated to be another secret mount similar to Riddler’s Mind Worm  or Voidtalon of the Dark Star  Currently it is unknown on how to acquire it.
