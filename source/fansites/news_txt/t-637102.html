Undocumented Changes/Spell Comparison
Please note that the stats of the spells here are taken directly from the game files and might be different from the officiel patch notes. This is a list of the differences between tooltips of classes spells, just don't jump to any conclusion just because a change is here and wait until it reaches the live server.

Most of the changes here were revealed in the latest update of the official PTR notes.

[b]General
Hearthstone (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=6948) cooldown has been reduced from 60 to 30 minutes.

Racial
Gift of the Naaru (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=59547) now also removes 2 harmful magic effects from the friendly target.
Stoneform (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=20594) no longer gives a 8 second immunity to disease, poison, and bleed effects.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ideathknights.gif Death Knight (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=650) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?deathknight))
Blood Strike (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=49930), Heart Strike (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=55262), Obliterate (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=51425) and Scourge Strike (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=55271) had their tooltip changed to say "total damage increased 12.5% per each of your diseases on the target" instead of just "damage increased etc ..." but this is probably just a wording change.
Blood
Abomination's Might (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=53138) now also affects Death Strike.
Unholy
Desecration (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=55670) radius seems to have been lowered from 10 to 7 yards, due to the nature of this change (spell effect changed instead of the spell itself) I strongly suggest that you wait until the PTRs are online before taking it as confirmed
Glyphs
Glyph of Strangulate (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=58618) now reduces the cooldown of Strangulate by 30 sec. (Down from 60 sec)
Glyph of Plague Strike (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=58657) now gives your Plague Strike 20% additional damage. (Down from 60%)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=700) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?druid))
Feral
Maim (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=49802) now lasts 1/2/3/4/5 sec depending on combo points. (Down from 3/4/5/6/7 sec)
Heart of the Wild (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=24894) now increases stamina in Dire Bear form by 2/4/6/8/10%. (Down from 4/8/12/16/20%)
Restoration
Lifebloom (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=48451) now let the Druid regains half the cost of the spell when Lifebloom completes its duration or is dispelled.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=620) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?hunter))
Marksmanship
Viper Sting (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=3034) now Stings the target, draining 4% mana over 8 sec (up to a maximum of 8% of the caster's maximum mana), and energizing the Hunter equal to 300% of the amount drained. Only one Sting per Hunter can be active on any one target.
Auto Shot (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=75) now gives 15% haste to all attacks fired by a Hunter.
Survival
Explosive Shot (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=60053) damage reduced, rank 4 now deals [ 14% of RAP + 386 ] - [ 14% of RAP + 464 ] fire damage. (down from [ 16% of RAP + 428 ] - [ 16% of RAP + 516 ])
Wyvern Sting (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=49012) now has a 1.5 minute cooldown. (Up from 1 minute)
Pet
Pummel (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=26090) (Gorilla) cooldown has been increased to 30 sec (Up from 10 sec.) and silence effect duration lowered to 2 sec. (Down from 4 sec)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=610) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?paladin))
Protection
Divine Protection (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=498) cooldown has been lowered to 3 minutes. (Down from 5 minutes)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=640) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?priest))
Holy
Hymn of Hope (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=60931) has been re-added to the game.
Shadow
Psychic Horror (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64044) (New Skill) - You terrify the target, causing them to tremble in horror for 3 sec and drop all weapons for 10 sec. (16% of base mana, 30 yd range, Instant cast, 1 min cooldown)
Vampiric Touch (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=48160) now cause 680 damage (at Rank 5) if the effect is dispelled.
Shadow Affinity (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=15320) now gives the priest 5% of his base mana when his Shadow Word: Pain or Vampiric Touch spells are dispelled.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=630) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?rogue))
Combat
Shiv (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=5938) changed to add "Neither Shiv nor the poison it applies can be a critical strike."

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=660) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?shaman))
Enhancement
Stormstrike (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=32176) now lasts 12 sec (Up from 10 sec), cooldown lowered to 8 sec (Down from 10 sec)
Unleashed Rage (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=30809) now properly increases the expertise by 3/6/9 instead of the agility.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=680) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?warlock))
Affliction
Drain Soul (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=47855) effect added " Each time the Drain Soul damages the target, it also has a chance to generate a Soul Shard."

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=600) / Talent + Glyph Calc. (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?warrior))
Arms
Charge (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=11578) now stuns the target for 1.5 sec. (Up from 1 sec)
Protection
Last Stand (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=12975) cooldown has been lowered to 3 minutes. (Down from 5 minutes)
Glyphs
Glyph of Last Stand (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=58396) now reduces the cooldown of your Last Stand ability by 1.5 min. (Down from 3 min)
