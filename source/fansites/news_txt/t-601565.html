For EU players who want to use premades:

1. Go to this link (https://www.wow-europe.com/login/login?service=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.wow-europe.com%2Fptr%2F)
2. Log in
3. Open new window, go to this page (https://www.wow-europe.com/login/login?service=http%3A%2F%2Fwow-europe.com%2Fptr%2Fcharacter-copy.html%3F_page%3D1%26premade%3Dtrue) (Took me two try to have it working, but eventually works)
4. Select the premade character, copy. You're done !
