Addon - Can I Mog It?
The Can I Mog It? addon (http://mods.curse.com/addons/wow/can-i-mog-it) adds additional information about appearance collections to item tooltips:


If you have learned it.
If you have learned it, but from a different item.
If you have learned it, but can't transmog it (wrong armor type or too low level).
If you can learn it.
If you can't learn it on this character.
If you can't learn it (necks, trinkets, rings, etc.).

Achievement - Fashionista: Shirt
When looking at the initial data from players that had a Fashionista achievement, we saw that only 20% of the players that had one of the achievements had the Fashionista: Shirt (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/10691-fashionista-shirt) achievement. Complete all of the Fashionista achievements to earn Fabulous (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/10694-fabulous), which rewards the Fabulous (http://www.wowdb.com/titles/477-the-fabulous) title!

We will take a look at progress on these achievements in a week or two, after everyone has had a chance to finish them up.


Thomas Yance (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/18672-thomas-yance) - Patrols the road between Tarren Mill and Southshore in the The Escape from Durnholde. Sells five shirts for <span class="money-copper" alt="c">1</span>
Broxel Goldgrasp (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/33853-broxel-goldgrasp) - Located at the Argent Tournament in Icecrown, selling faction themed shirts for <span class="money-gold" alt="g">25</span>
Karandonna (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/29491-karandonna) - Located in the Threads of Fate shop in Dalaran, selling many shirts from <span class="money-gold" alt="g">20</span> - <span class="money-gold" alt="g">800</span>
Angelique Butler (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/29628-angelique-butler) - Several First Aid vendors sell two shirts that are <span class="money-gold" alt="g">16</span> - <span class="money-gold" alt="g">20</span>
Crafted Shirts (http://www.wowdb.com/items/armor/shirts?filter-source=2) - There are lots of crafted shirts that you can make or buy from the Auction House
Guild Vendors (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs?filter-search=Guild+Vendor) - These vendors sell guild shirts for <span class="money-gold" alt="g">100</span> - <span class="money-gold" alt="g">200</span>. These shirts come with some requirements to be able to purchase them.
