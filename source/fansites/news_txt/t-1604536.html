Glyph of Immediate Truth (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/56416/#18988-18996) Increases the instant damage done by Seal of Truth by 40% 30%, but decreases the damage done by Censure by 50%. Major Glyph.


Raid & Dungeon Abilities

Burning Breath (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/166041/#18988-18996) Breaths a wide line of fire, inflicting 23,750 to 26,250 19,000 to 21,000 Fire Damage. 40 yd range. Instant.
Deafening Screech (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/143343/#18988-18996) Inflicts 250,000 Inflicts 200,000 Physical damage to all enemies and interrupts spellcasting for 2 sec. 100 Mana. 0.7 sec cast. 1.5 sec cooldown.
Imbued Iron Axe (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/162097/#18988-18996) Throws a whirling axe, inflicting 38,000 to 42,000 28,500 to 31,500 Fire damage to nearby enemies. Unlimited range. Instant.
