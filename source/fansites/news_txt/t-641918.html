Achievements Changes
The Ulduar immortal achievements had their description slightly changed ...
Champion of Ulduar (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2903) - Defeat each boss in Ulduar on Normal Difficulty without allowing any raid member to die to that boss at any point during that raid lockout period.
Conqueror of Ulduar (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2904) - Defeat each boss in Ulduar on Heroic Difficulty without allowing any raid member to die to that boss at any point during that raid lockout period.

A few more Ulduar achievements were changed as well.
Iron Dwarf, Medium Rare (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2923) - 2 Minutes time limit removed.
Heroic: Iron Dwarf, Medium Rare (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2924) - 2 Minutes time limit removed.
Minimal Casualties (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2920) has been removed.
Heroic: Minimal Casualties (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2922) has been removed.
Getting Back to Nature (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=2982) - Defeat Freya while she is affected by 25 stacks of Attuned to Nature on Normal Difficulty.
He Feeds on Your Tears (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=3004) - Defeat Algalon the Observer on Normal Difficulty without any raid member dying to Algalon at any point during that raid lockout period.
Observed (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=3037) - Defeat Algalon the Observer on Heroic Difficulty without any raid member dying to Algalon at any point during that raid lockout period.
Supermassive (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=3003) - Now requires closing 3 Black Holes instead of 4.

Kologarn - Boss Page (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=843)
