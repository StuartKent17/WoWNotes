Achievements

The criteria for Azerite Admiral (https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/13125-azerite-admiral), Tell Me a Tale (https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/13127-tell-me-a-tale), and Helping Hand (https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/13132-helping-hand) should now combine progress from multiple characters on the same account. Please log in on every character that has progress toward these achievements to be credited.
