Weekly Event - Burning Crusade Timewalking
Burning Crusade Timewalking is back this week. Don't forget to check out Black Temple Timewalking (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/6690-Black-Temple-Timewalking) during the event!


The weekly quest (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/44164-a-burning-path-through-time) from Archmage Timear (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/111246-archmage-timear) rewards a Accolade of Victory (https://www.wowdb.com/items/140176-accolade-of-victory), Seal of Broken Fate (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1273-seal-of-broken-fate), and Cache of Antoran Treasures (https://www.wowdb.com/items/153504-cache-of-antoran-treasures). Don't forget to keep working towards Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk), Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger), and Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade)!
Reins of the Infinite Timereaver (https://www.wowdb.com/items/133543-reins-of-the-infinite-timereaver) can also drop from any Timewalking dungeon boss.
