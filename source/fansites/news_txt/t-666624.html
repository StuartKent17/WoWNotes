World first Tribute to Insanity (10 Players) by For The Horde
For The Horde (http://www.for-the-horde.org/) only needed a few hours to go through the hard mode of the 10-Man Trial of the Crusader .... without wiping. They are the first guild to complete the A Tribute to Insanity (10 Players) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/3810/a-tribute-to-insanity-10-player/) achievement.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/august/fthtribute_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/august/fthtribute.jpg)

The Tribute Chest loot was :
Swift Horde Wolf (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49046/swift-horde-wolf/) (Just one, if you were wondering)
Drape of the Sunreavers (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/48666/drape-of-the-sunreavers/)
Blood and Glory (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/48699/blood-and-glory/)
Trophy of the Crusade (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/47242/trophy-of-the-crusade/) x 4
