Mischief Pet Giveaway
The Mischief pet is now available on the Blizzard Store (http://blizz.ly/mischiefpet)! 100% of the purchase price will be donated to Make-A-Wish until December 31, 2016.


The giveaway is for 20 x Mischief (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/113527-mischief) pets.
To enter the giveaway, just reply to this post!
The giveaway will be closed on Wednesday, December 7th at 11:59 PM EST and winners will be chosen randomly within five business days.
Winners will be contacted by PM on the site. There is no email alert by default, you will need to log in and check your PMs (http://www.mmo-champion.com/private.php?folderid=0).
A note will be added to the top of the current news post when winners have been PMed.
Winners have 72 hours to respond before another winner will be selected to replace them.
