Legion - Loot Glow Effect
In Legion, bosses with loot remaining on them will have a special glow to visually indicate that there is still loot on the body. This glow has also appeared in several other places during the beta during Fishing, on some quest objects, and other NPCs but now seems to be only on bosses.
