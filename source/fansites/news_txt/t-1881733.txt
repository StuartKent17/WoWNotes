This year the item rewards dropped by the Headless Horseman are item level 670.
New Currency - Spooky Supplies http://www.wowdb.com/items/128658-spooky-supplies

You can purchase decorations for your Garrison and Tricky Treats http://www.wowdb.com/items/33226-tricky-treat with the supplies.
The most useful decoration is Creepy Crawlers http://www.wowdb.com/items/128664-creepy-crawlers, as it spawns the Hallow's End pets in your Garrison to capture. It also spawns Arachnis http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/96323-arachnis, which drops the new toy: Sack of Spectral Spiders http://www.wowdb.com/items/128794-sack-of-spectral-spiders.
Witch's Brew http://www.wowdb.com/items/128663-witchs-brew is also useful, as it adds an NPC that gives you Mysterious Brew http://www.wowdb.com/items/128634-mysterious-brew.

Daily Quests - You can pick up the four new daily quests www.wowdb.com/items/128658-spooky-supplies#related:reward-of-quest;_! in the center of your Garrison. 

All of the quests require you to head to southern Shadowmoon Valley.
One of the quests is a group quest to kill a relatively challenging NPC, the other three are easily soloed.
Coin of Many Faces http://www.wowdb.com/items/128807-coin-of-many-faces has a ~1% drop rate from the mobs you kill for the daily quests. 
You have a ~10% chance to loot Spectral Grog http://www.wowdb.com/items/104316-spectral-grog from the Boneship crew in the area.
The quests reward a total of 5 x Spooky Supplies http://www.wowdb.com/items/128658-spooky-supplies per day.

Ghastly Rat http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/97568-ghastly-rat, Ghost Maggot http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/97569-ghost-maggot, and Spectral Spinner http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/97324-spectral-spinner

Exquisite Costume Set: "Deathwing" http://www.wowdb.com/items/128643-exquisite-costume-set-deathwing is new this year and it can be yours for 200 x Tricky Treats http://www.wowdb.com/items/33226-tricky-treat.
