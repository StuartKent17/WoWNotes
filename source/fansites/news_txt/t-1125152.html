Mists of Pandaria Beta - Dungeon Armor Sets
Today we're continuing the armor sets preview with the Mists of Pandaria dungeon sets! Those armor models will be used on the rare gear you get in 5-man and heroic instances and they will most likely be the armor your characters will wear for a while before you actually clear raids and gear up! It's also a very nice opportunity to try something new on the website!

New website feature - Image Gallery I'm giving another try at the whole image gallery thing! Clicking an image in this post will bring you to a very nice gallery that will let you browse through pictures easily.


You can click the top right icon for a very fancy full screen mode.
The original picture in full resolution is still available through the "Original" link at the bottom right.
Using your left/right arrows keys to navigate between pictures should work, if you don't like clicking.
It should work fairly well, but don't hesitate to post bugs in comments.
