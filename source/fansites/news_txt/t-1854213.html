Patch 6.2.2 - Timewalking Rewards
Patch 6.2.2 adds more rewards to Timewalking! You can see Cupri (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/98685-cupri) in Shattrath (Outland) to purchase these items for Timewarped Badge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge), rewarded from running Timewalking dungeons.

A recent PTR build adjusted the costs, but we haven't had another Timewalking weekend to confirm the new prices and any changes to the drop rate. The latest version of the official notes still says "Players now receive a Timewarped Badge when they complete a Timewalking dungeon". Keep in mind that the drop rate and prices are not final!


Mounts (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/misc/mount?filter-patch=20395&filter-ilvl-min=70) - The Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk) and Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger) cost 5,000 Timewarped Badge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge).

The Ironbound Wraithcharger (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger) model was added to the game in Mists of Pandaria and was originally going to be a shop mount (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/3306-New-Stuff-Coming-to-the-Pet-Store).

Heirloom Upgrades (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/consumables/consumable?filter-expansion=6&filter-quality=128) - You can purchase the heirloom upgrade items for 750 / 1000 badges (Armor) and 900 / 1200 badges (Weapon)
Armor and Weapons (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items?filter-patch=20395&filter-ilvl-min=110&filter-ilvl-max=264&filter-quality=8) - Badge of JusticeTimewarped Badge gear is item level 675 and uses the same models as some Burning Crusade gear.
Reputation Commendations (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/quest?filter-patch=20395&filter-quality=128) - These BoA items grant 500 Reputation with some of the Burning Crusade and Wrath of the Lich King factions.
Pets (http://www.wowdb.com/items/35348-bag-of-fishing-treasures#related:contains-item;_!) - The Bag of Fishing Treasures (http://www.wowdb.com/items/35348-bag-of-fishing-treasures) can contain several different crocodile pets. You can also obtain this bag by completing the Crocolisks in the City (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/11665-crocolisks-in-the-city) daily quest.
Cooking - The Barrel of Fish (http://www.wowdb.com/items/33844-barrel-of-fish) and Crate of Meat (http://www.wowdb.com/items/33857-crate-of-meat) can contain some older Cooking recipes.
