Stage 3 - ?? / ??

Set Bonus http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/109939/item-dragon-soul-legendary-daggers/ - Your melee attacks have a chance to grant Shadows of the Destroyer, increasing your Agility by 17, stacking up to 50 times.
