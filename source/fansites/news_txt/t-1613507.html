Ko'ragh was the only ogre to survive direct exposure to a mysterious relic unearthed by the Highmaul excavations of Nagrand, and was left with a near-complete immunity to all magic. Rumors abound regarding his connection to the Imperator, with some wondering why Ko'ragh would remain subservient to a sorcerer despite his unique gifts.


Overview - Ko'ragh is protected by a Nullification Barrier that absorbs all magic damage taken. When the barrier is removed, he activates a rune that recharges it. While recharging, one player may also absorb some of the rune's energy and receive a Nullification Barrier. As the fight progresses, an increasing amount of Overflowing Energy orbs spill from the rune and float to the ground, exploding when they land. Players who have a Nullification Barrier can catch and absorb Overflowing Energy to mitigate the amount of raid wide damage and prevent a lethal amount of damage from occurring. In Mythic difficulty, two players may enter the rune per phase of Charging.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Damage Dealers - Absorb <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/161242-caustic-energy">Caustic Energy</a> to gain a <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/156803-nullification-barrier">Nullification Barrier</a>.
Avoid <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/161612-overflowing-energy">Overflowing Energy</a> until you have a <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/163134-nullification-barrier">Nullification Barrier</a>. Absorb <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/161612-overflowing-energy">Overflowing Energy</a> when you do have a <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/163134-nullification-barrier">Nullification Barrier</a>.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/healer.png" title="Healer" /> Healers - Focus intense healing on players absorbing <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/161242-caustic-energy">Caustic Energy</a> to allow them to survive the transformation.
<a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/162185-expel-magic-fire">Expel Magic: Fire</a> inflicts heavy raid wide damage and requires dispels to mitigate some of the damage.
<a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/162184-expel-magic-shadow">Expel Magic: Shadow</a> absorbs all healing on the raid until it is removed.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Tanks - Tanks marked with <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/162186-expel-magic-arcane">Expel Magic: Arcane</a> must run away from their allies and avoid the periodic explosions.
Volatile Anomalies will spawn periodically during the charging phase. If they are killed within a <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/161328-suppression-field">Suppression Field</a>, they will not cast <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/163466-destabilize">Destabilize</a>.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Nullification Barrier (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/156803) - Ko'ragh is protected from all magic, absorbing up to 4,020,890 Magic damage.

Breaker's Strength (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/162569) - While Ko'ragh maintains a Nullification Barrier, his damage done is increased by 8% every 10 seconds. This effect stacks. When the barrier expires, Breaker's Strength is removed.
Charging (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/1538) - When the Ko'ragh's Nullification Barrier is removed, he begins to recharge. After 20 sec, the barrier is restored.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Caustic Energy (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/161242) - One player may enter the rune with Ko'ragh, absorbing a portion of its energy. The energy inflicts 32,152 Arcane damage every second and reduces all healing received by 5% for as long as the player remains inside of the rune. This effect pierces all immunities and damage reductions. When the rune powers down, the player receives their own Nullification Barrier, proportional to the amount of time spent inside the rune. The Nullification Barrier received by players can absorb up to 15,000,000 Magic damage. In Mythic difficulty, two players may enter the rune per phase of Charging. In Raid Finder difficulty, five players may enter the rune per phase of Charging.


Suppression Field (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/161328) - Ko'ragh strikes the ground at a player's location, inflicting 57,200 Arcane damage in an 8 yard area. In addition, a field of suppressing energy is created that silences all players and inflicts 34,280 Arcane damage every second.<br/><br/>This effect will also silence Volatile Anomalies.
Volatile Anomalies - While the Rune of Nullification is active, 3 Volatile Anomalies spawn every 8 sec.

Destabilize (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/163466) - Volatile Anomalies explode upon death, inflicting 28,575 Arcane damage to all players.

Expel Magic -

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/magic.png" title="Magic" /> Expel Magic: Fire (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/162185) - Ko'ragh expels Fire magic inflicting 10,725 Fire damage every second for 10 sec. to all players. When Expel Magic: Fire expires, it inflicts 57,200 Fire damage to all allies within 5 yards.

When Expel Magic: Fel expires, it creates a line of fel energy between where the wearer gained this effect and where the effect expired. The fel energy persists for 1.5 min, and inflicts 95,000 Fire damage to all players within it.

After 1.5 sec., the marked location explodes, inflicting 143,000 Arcane damage to all players within 5 yards. In addition, this effect increases Physical damage taken by 100%.
Expel Magic: Frost (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/172747) - Ko'ragh expels an orb of Frost that inflicts 64,350 Frost damage to all players. The orb persists for 20 sec and slows movement speed of all players within 30 yards by up to 85%. The snare effect of Expel Magic: Frost decreases as a player stands farther away from the expelled orb.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/healer.png" title="Healer" /> Expel Magic: Shadow (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/162184) - Ko'ragh expels Shadow magic, inflicting all players with a heal absorption effect that prevents up to 50,000 healing received.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/deadly.png" title="Deadly" /> <img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Overflowing Energy (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/161612) - Arcane energy bleeds from the rune stone, inflicting 150,000 Arcane damage to enemies it comes into contact with. If the energy reaches the ground, it explodes, inflicting 49,500 Arcane damage to all enemies.<br/><br/>This effect pierces all immunities and damage reductions.
Dominating Power (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/163472) - Imperator Mar'gok uses the power of the rune stone to mind control all players that come into contact with Overflowing Energy. This effect grants immunity to all Magic and is removed when the player is brought below 20% health.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/interruptable.png" title="Interruptable" /> Forfeit Power (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/163517) - Players mind controlled by Dominating Power attempt to forfeit 5% of their shield and give Ko'ragh 5% of his shield.





Arthas Costume Hotfix
So after looking into it there are a few hotfixes that will be going out early next week to address the Lich King costume. We won't be testing these fixes until Monday, so until they're applied and listed in the Hotfixes blog these are just current intentions. Assuming it all goes to the current plan: the cost is being adjusted down by half, we're ensuring it is indeed 50 charges (20min per charge), and that it's usable outside of the Hallow's End event period. Keep in mind that when the hotfixes go out the cost shown on the vendor won't change, but the item will indeed be purchasable for the hotfixed amount.

Seems rather silly to price it in such a way you have to choose to either purchase that, or the pets and other things because of there only being about 650 candy thats physically possible to get.
It's intentionally priced as being sort of the 1 item someone would want to go after if they really want to turn into the Lich King every once in a while. It won't be what everyone wants to chase after, and that's ok. However, pets are account-wide of course, and so you can get multiple items if you're participating in the event on multiple characters.

Computers - Setup of the Month
Each month or every 2 months, depending on the hardware evolution, we will post a couple of hardware setups for those of you who are thinking of upgrading their computer!

This month
The current SSD prices (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/october/ssdgraph.gif) are trending slightly downward over the past few months, with most SSDs under $0.50 per gigabyte when on sale.

The GTX 980 and 970 have been released! The 970 is a good value, but it is almost always out of stock.

Depending on how much you want to overclock, you may need to spend a bit more for a better CPU cooler like the NH-D14 (http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002VKVZ1A?ie=UTF8&tag=mmochamp-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B002VKVZ1A).
