Mists of Pandaria Timewalking
Mists of Pandaria timewalking is back! It offers rewards such as Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (https://www.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade).


Visit Mistweaver Xia (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/118828-mistweaver-xia) to purchase timewalking rewards while the event is live
Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (https://www.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade) costs 5000 x Timewarped Badge (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)
Paradox Spirit (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/118063-paradox-spirit) and Infinite Hatchling (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/118060-infinite-hatchling) cost 2200 x Timewarped Badge (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)
Reputation tokens (https://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-patch=23038&filter-search=commendation) (50 x Timewarped Badge (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)) for the Mists of Pandaria factions are also for sale, including friendship items for the Valley of the Four Winds.
Portable Yak Wash (https://www.wowdb.com/items/144393-portable-yak-wash) costs 500 x Timewarped Badge (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)
Adopted Puppy Crate (https://www.wowdb.com/items/144072-adopted-puppy-crate) (1000 x Timewarped Badge (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)) and Chilled Satchel of Vegetables (https://www.wowdb.com/items/143948-chilled-satchel-of-vegetables) (25 x Timewarped Badge (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge)) are also for sale
