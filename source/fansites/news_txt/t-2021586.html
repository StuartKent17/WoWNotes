Frost Death Knight -- Legion Beta Feedback
Hey all. We've seen the discussion about bugs with Frost DK damage (most notably Rime proc chance), and so I wanted to stop by and let you know that we are aware and fixing several bugs with Frost DK damage over the next build or two.

Rime had 3 chances to proc per Obliterate, several new abilities only used your base 5% crit chance, and the Ambidexterity artifact trait was affecting MH damage, among a few other minor bugfixes. Sindragosa's Fury is also getting a tiny buff. These fixes will obviously affect throughput, and we'll be adjusting tuning with them in mind. (Blue Tracker (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/411738-frost-death-knight-legion-beta-feedback/) / Official Forums (http://us.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/20743646087?page=20#393))
