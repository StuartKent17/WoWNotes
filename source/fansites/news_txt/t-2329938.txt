Update 12:10 PM AM PDT: Intro cinematic for World of Warcraft: Battle for Azeroth is being shown!
Update 12:05 PM AM PDT: World of Warcraft: Battle for Azeroth http://worldofwarcraft.com/battleforazeroth is the next expansion. New features include Kul' tiras continent, Zandalar continent, level cap 120, new dungeons and raids, plunder uncharted islands, dominate warfronts, and allied races.
Update 12:04 PM AM PDT: Shadow the Fox plush announced for charity.
Update 12:03 PM AM PDT: World of Warcraft: Classic classic server options announced!
Update 11:59 AM PDT: J Allen Brack has taken the stage for World of Warcraft.
Update 11:51 AM PDT: Darin De Paul voice of Reinhardt introduces Reinhardt cinematic.
Update 11:46 AM PDT: New Overwatch hero: Moira https://playoverwatch.com/en-us/heroes/moira a support hero! She is playable on show floor.
Update 11:44 AM PDT: New Overwatch map: Blizzard World, a Blizzard theme park.
Update 11:41 AM PDT: Jeff Kaplan is taking the stage for Overwatch.
Update 11:38 AM PDT: New Hearthstone single player mode: Dungeon Run is about defeating bosses and collecting loot. Earn new cards for deck but if you lose your deck gets thrown away and you start over. It's completely free.
Update 11:37 AM PDT: Legendary weapons are now cards for all classes in Hearthstone.
Update 11:36 AM PDT: Everyone who logs into Hearthstone gets the Marin the Fox card for free starting Monday. If you are at Blizzcon or have the virtual ticket, you get a golden copy.
Update 11:31 AM PDT: Kobolds and Catacombs https://playhearthstone.com/en-us/expansions-adventures/kobolds-and-catacombs/ is the new Hearthstone expansion.
Update 11:24 AM PDT: Performance based matchmaking, voice chat, new camera, laning improvements, stealth rework and evolved battleground mechanics all coming to Heroes.
Update 11:20 AM PDT: Heroes of the Storm Cinematic showcases new heroes: Hanzo and Alexstraza!
Update 11:19 AM PDT: Kaeo Milker has taken the stage for Heroes of the Storm.
Update 11:15 AM PDT: Starcraft 2 is now free to play on November 14th!
Update 11:11 AM PDT: Recap video is showing the many Esports tournaments that have led to Blizzcon.
Update 11:07 AM PDT: Announcements for games will happen on multiple stages during the opening ceremony.
Update 11:04 AM PDT: Mike Morhaime is taking the stage.
Update 11:02 AM PDT: Opening video shows the passion and experiences of the Blizzard community.
