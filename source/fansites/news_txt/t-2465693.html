PTR Patch 8.1.5 - Hati Customization
Hunters will be reuniting with their favorite wolf from Legion in Patch 8.1.5. On top of this, Hati is receiving a plethora of different customization items!


Charged Sparkstone (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/167800-charged-sparkstone) - Use: Bring out the latent power in Hati's lightning, visually enhancing Hati's abilities. (60 sec cooldown)
Ironspine Protocol (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/167799-ironspine-protocol) - Use: Enhance Hati's size and constitution, allowing mounting. (60 sec cooldown)
Jolt Jerky (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/167797-jolt-jerky) - Use: Feed Hati a special treat, healing a small amount of damage. Sometimes, Hati may become too excited and viciously lick your face, dealing Nature damage. (60 sec cooldown)
Rubber Ball (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/167798-rubber-ball) - Use: Throw the ball for Hati. (60 sec cooldown)
Voltweave Fez (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/167801-voltweave-fez) - Use: Give the fez to Hati. (60 sec cooldown)
