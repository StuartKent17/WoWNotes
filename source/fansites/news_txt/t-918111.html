Spell Changes

Cooking

Cooking: Kibler's Bits (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/43772/kiblers-bits/#14199/14241) now makes your pet bigger for 5 minutes.


Engineering

Engineering: Flintlocke's Woodchucker (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/100587/flintlockes-woodchucker/#14199/14241) is now restricted to hunters.


Mechanics - Mounted

Tarecgosa's Visage (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/101641/tarecgosas-visage/#14199/14241) has been added to the "mounted" spells, but not to the actual mounts. Most likely confirms the theory that the "Tarecgosa Mount" datamined is just a transformation during the questline and not an actual mount.

Wild Quiver (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/76659/wild-quiver/#14199/14241) now has a 16.8% chance to proc, up from 14.4%. Each point of Mastery increases the chance by an additional 2.1%, up from 1.8%.
Careful Aim (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/34483/careful-aim/#14199/14241) now only works on targets above 90% health, up from 80%.


Survival

Black Arrow (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/3674/black-arrow/#14199/14241) damage has been increased by 40%. Now scales from 13.3% of AP, up from 9.5%.
