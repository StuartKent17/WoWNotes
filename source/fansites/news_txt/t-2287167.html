Patch 7.3 - New Factions
Patch 7.3 adds two new factions and some catch up items.


The Armies of Legionfall (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/48641-armies-of-legionfall) faction will offer an emissary quest for the Broken Shore world quests.

Demon's Soulstone (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/153113-demons-soulstone) grants reputation for all of the Broken Isles factions, including Argus factions.
Nathrezim Tome of Manipulation (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/153114-nathrezim-tome-of-manipulation) - Increases reputation gain with the factions of the Broken Isles by 200% for 2 hrs while leveling.
Relinquished Armor Set (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/153005-relinquished-armor-set) - Set a Champion's item level to 880.
Cube of Discovery (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/153127-cube-of-discovery) - Allows you to sometimes find artifact power items when looting enemies while leveling.



Army of the Light
The Army of the Light (https://wow.gamepedia.com/Army_of_the_Light) offers world quests on Argus, with the normal emissary reward as well. Note that the Lightforged Warframe (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/152788-lightforged-warframe) costs <span class="money-gold" alt="g">500,000</span>.
