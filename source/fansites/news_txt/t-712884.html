Icecrown Citadel Buff increased to 15%
The Hellscream's Warsong (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/73819/hellscreams-warsong/) and Strength of Wrynn (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/73825/strength-of-wrynn/) buffs in Icecrown Citadel have been increased to 15%. So far, only 9 guilds killed the Lich King in 25-Man Heroic mode, 8 of them with a 10% buff, and 1 with the 5% buff.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2010/april/buffj_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2010/april/buffj.jpg)

We have increased the potency of the Strength of Wrynn and Hellscream’s Warsong buffs in Icecrown Citadel by an additional 5%. These buffs now increase total health, healing done and damage dealt by 15%. In addition, the number of attempts allotted for all final bosses on Heroic difficulty has been increased to 45.
