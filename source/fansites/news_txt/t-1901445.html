The 11th anniversary celebration of World of Warcraft has begun! Log in between now and November 30 to earn the WoW's 11th Anniversary (http://www.wowdb.com/world-events/566-wows-11th-anniversary) achievement, then check your mail for a special Anniversary Gift Package filled with a fun items to mark the occasion, including:


Celebration Package (http://www.wowdb.com/items/127987-celebration-package) – Increases experience and reputation gains from kills by 11% for 1 hour. (10 second cooldown)
Celebration Wand – Gnoll (http://www.wowdb.com/items/128506-celebration-wand-gnoll) (5 charges)
Celebration Wand – Murloc (http://www.wowdb.com/items/128505-celebration-wand-murloc) (5 charges)
Exquisite Costume Set: "Edwin VanCleef" (http://www.wowdb.com/items/128510-exquisite-costume-set-edwin-vancleef)
Inflatable Thunderfury, Blessed Blade of the Windseeker (http://www.wowdb.com/items/128507-inflatable-thunderfury-blessed-blade-of-the) – Using this item grants you a Feat of Strength. “Did Someone Say...? (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/10335-did-someone-say)” (account-wide)
