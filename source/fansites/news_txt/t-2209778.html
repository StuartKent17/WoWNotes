US Realms - Nether Disruptor
The Nether Disruptor is active on US realms!

Nether Disruptor

Zone-wide buff: Epic Hunter (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/239647-epic-hunter)

Unlocks new world bosses in the Broken Isles. These bosses drop item level 900 loot.
If your profession is maxed before you complete the building, you get Armorcrafter's Commendation (http://www.wowdb.com/items/147451-armorcrafters-commendation) to start on the crafted legendary item questlines (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/6491-Patch-7-2-Live-This-Week!#craftedLegendary).
Purchase a Nether Portal Disruptor (http://www.wowdb.com/items/147775-nether-portal-disruptor) to disrupt Unstable Nether portals around the Broken Shore. This summons a small boss which drops War Supplies, Beacons, and other loot.

This week's buff: Reinforced Reins (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/240985-reinforced-reins) - Interact with Objects while mounted
Potential buffs: Fate Smiles Upon You (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/239968-fate-smiles-upon-you), Netherstorm (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/239969-netherstorm), Reinforced Reins (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/240985-reinforced-reins), Seal Your Fate (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/239967-seal-your-fate)
