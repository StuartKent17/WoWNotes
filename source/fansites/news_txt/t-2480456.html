Noblegarden 2019
Noblegarden (http://www.wowdb.com/world-events/181-noblegarden) has returned to Azeroth from today until April 29th, bringing all of the old goodies from previous years, as well as the ability to transmog to your Noblegarden cosmetics during the holiday and the Cursed Rabbit's Foot (https://www.wowdb.com/items/155905-cursed-rabbits-foot) from opening eggs.


New in 2019

New Toy: Noble Gardener's Hearthstone (https://www.wowdb.com/items/165802-noble-gardeners-hearthstone)

The Noble Gardener's Hearthstone summons a flurry of springtime critters to whisk you away.

Added in recent years is the Noblegarden Bunny (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/33975-noblegarden-bunny) battle pet, sold by Noblegarden Merchant (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/32837-noblegarden-merchant) for 200 x Noblegarden Chocolate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44791-noblegarden-chocolate)
Hunt for Brightly Colored Eggs (http://www.wowdb.com/items/45072-brightly-colored-egg) in starter zone towns to loot Noblegarden Chocolate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44791-noblegarden-chocolate). These eggs are lootable objects that will spawn around the town.
Use Noblegarden Chocolate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44791-noblegarden-chocolate) to purchase three battle pets, a mount, cosmetic items, and more.

To get all three pets you will need 400 Noblegarden Chocolate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44791-noblegarden-chocolate).
To get the mount you will need 500 Noblegarden Chocolate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44791-noblegarden-chocolate).
Mages - Purchase Tome of Polymorph: Rabbit (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44793-tome-of-polymorph-rabbit) for 100 Noblegarden Chocolate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44791-noblegarden-chocolate).
Purchase or hold on to Noblegarden items if you want to have them for transmog during future Noblegarden events (after Patch 7.2.5).

There are two Nobelgarden quests, but neither are necessary to complete.
Unlock the nine achievements required for Noble Gardener (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2798-noble-gardener) using items found in Brightly Colored Eggs (http://www.wowdb.com/items/45072-brightly-colored-egg) or purchased from vendors.
The Noblegarden Egg (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44818-noblegarden-egg) is required to complete Noble Garden (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2420-noble-garden), and it must be purchased from the vendor. It costs 5 Noblegarden Chocolate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44791-noblegarden-chocolate).


Added in 2018 - Cursed Rabbit's Foot
Cursed Rabbit's Foot (https://www.wowdb.com/items/155905-cursed-rabbits-foot) was added in 2018 that can be looted from eggs. It causes your character to periodically spawn eggs on the ground that are smaller and slightly different than the normal Noble Garden eggs.

Hunt for Brightly Colored Eggs
Collect eggs to gather Noblegarden Chocolate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44791-noblegarden-chocolate) and other Noblegarden items.


Horde players can farm eggs in Bloodhoof Village in Mulgore (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/215-mulgore), Brill in Tirisfal Glades (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/85-tirisfal-glades), Falconwing Square in Eversong Woods (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/3430-eversong-woods) and Razor Hill in Durotar (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/14-durotar).
Alliance players can farm eggs in Azure Watch on Azuremyst Isle (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/8275-azuremyst-isle), Dolanaar in Teldrassil (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/141-teldrassil), Goldshire in Elwynn Forest (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/12-elwynn-forest),and Kharanos in Dun Morogh (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/1-dun-morogh).
There are also some eggs outside of Shattrath City (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/3703-shattrath-city).
Eggs contain (http://www.wowdb.com/items/45072-brightly-colored-egg#related:contains-item) the Noblegarden Chocolate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44791-noblegarden-chocolate) currency, necessary for purchasing items from a Noblegarden Vendor (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/32837-noblegarden-merchant), as well as nearly all of the clothing and consumable items sold by the vendors.
Brightly Colored Eggs (http://www.wowdb.com/items/45072-brightly-colored-egg) can also contain: Spring Rabbit's Foot (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44794-spring-rabbits-foot), Blossoming Branch (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44792-blossoming-branch), White Tuxedo Shirt (http://www.wowdb.com/items/6833-white-tuxedo-shirt), Black Tuxedo Pants (http://www.wowdb.com/items/6835-black-tuxedo-pants), Elegant Dress (http://www.wowdb.com/items/19028-elegant-dress), Spring Circlet (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44803-spring-circlet), Spring Flowers (http://www.wowdb.com/items/45073-spring-flowers), Spring Robes (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44800-spring-robes), and Swift Springstrider (http://www.wowdb.com/items/72145-swift-springstrider).
If you're on the daily quest The Great Egg Hunt (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/13480-the-great-egg-hunt), eggs will also contain Brightly Colored Shell Fragment (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44806-brightly-colored-shell-fragment) quest item. Completing this quest awards a Blossoming Branch (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44792-blossoming-branch).

Host Your Own Egg Hunt or Transfer Gold
Noblegarden vendors also sell Poorly Painted Egg (http://www.wowdb.com/items/116357-poorly-painted-egg), Intricately-Painted Egg (http://www.wowdb.com/items/116358-intricately-painted-egg), and Magnificently-Painted Egg (http://www.wowdb.com/items/116359-magnificently-painted-egg) for gold.


Place these eggs on the ground and they will all look exactly the same.
Each egg type is unique, so you can only have one in your inventory at a time.
Each egg can be looted by only one player and will contain a gray-quality item that can be sold to a vendor for 90% of the original egg purchase price.
The eggs can be seen by anyone around you, so they are a useful way to transfer gold between realms or factions (with a 10% loss).

Complete Quests
There is a one time quest and daily quest that you can complete:


One-time Quest: A Tisket, a Tasket, a Noblegarden Basket (H) (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/13503-a-tisket-a-tasket-a-noblegarden-basket) or A Tisket, a Tasket, a Noblegarden Basket (A) (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/13502-a-tisket-a-tasket-a-noblegarden-basket).

Awards an Egg Basket (http://www.wowdb.com/items/45067-egg-basket). You are given an identical basket with which to actually do the quest, so you don't have to spend the Chocolate and actually turn the quest in unless you want the permanent basket.
Just use the basket you were given for the quest to gain a speed buff while farming Brightly Colored Eggs (http://www.wowdb.com/items/45072-brightly-colored-egg).

Daily Quest: The Great Egg Hunt (H) (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/13479-the-great-egg-hunt) or The Great Egg Hunt (A) (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/13480-the-great-egg-hunt).
Collect 10 Brightly Colored Shell Fragments (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44806-brightly-colored-shell-fragment) to receive a Blossoming Branch (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44792-blossoming-branch).
You'll be able to complete this quest while farming eggs, so you can do this quest to get a Blossoming Branch (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44792-blossoming-branch) for the achievement Hard Boiled (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2416-hard-boiled).
You can also find Blossoming Branch (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44792-blossoming-branch) in a Brightly Colored Egg (http://www.wowdb.com/items/45072-brightly-colored-egg).


Unlock Achievements
In order to earn the achievement Noble Gardener (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2798-noble-gardener) (part of What a Long, Strange Trip It's Been (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2144-what-a-long-strange-trip-its-been)) and unlock the Noble (http://www.wowdb.com/titles/155-the-noble) title, you must complete these nine achievements:


Shake Your Bunny-Maker (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2422-shake-your-bunny-maker) - Use Spring Flowers (http://www.wowdb.com/items/45073-spring-flowers) to give bunny ears to level 18 (or higher) female characters of each race.
Blushing Bride (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2576-blushing-bride) - Kiss someone wearing an Elegant Dress (http://www.wowdb.com/items/19028-elegant-dress) while wearing a White Tuxedo Shirt (http://www.wowdb.com/items/6833-white-tuxedo-shirt) and Black Tuxedo Pants (http://www.wowdb.com/items/6835-black-tuxedo-pants).
Chocoholic (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2418-chocoholic) - Eat 100 Noblegarden Chocolate (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44791-noblegarden-chocolate).
Spring Fling (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2497-spring-fling) or Spring Fling (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2419-spring-fling) - Visit each of the four cities listed for your respective faction with your Spring Rabbit (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/32791-spring-rabbit) out and find someone else with a Spring Rabbit (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/32791-spring-rabbit) out for yours to love.
Desert Rose (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2436-desert-rose) - Use Spring Robes (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44800-spring-robes) to plant a flower in the Badlands (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/3-badlands), Silithus (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/1377-silithus), Thousand Needles (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/400-thousand-needles), Desolace (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/405-desolace), and Tanaris (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/440-tanaris).
I Found One! (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2676-i-found-one) - Loot a Brightly Colored Egg (http://www.wowdb.com/items/45072-brightly-colored-egg).
Hard Boiled (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2416-hard-boiled) - Visit Un'Goro Crater's Golakka Hot Springs as a rabbit during Noblegarden. You can't use Blossoming Branch (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44792-blossoming-branch) on yourself, so team up with someone else (with their own Branch) and head out there to change each other into rabbits.
Noble Garden (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2420-noble-garden) or Noble Garden (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/2421-noble-garden) - Hide a Brightly Colored Egg in Silvermoon City (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/3487-silvermoon-city) (Horde) or Stormwind City (http://www.wowdb.com/zones/1519-stormwind-city) (Alliance). To “hide” the egg, place one using Noblegarden Egg (http://www.wowdb.com/items/44818-noblegarden-egg).

Noblegarden springs to life April 22 through April 29. Don your bunny ears and hunt for eggs, eat delectable chocolate, collect toys, pets, and more.

The great feast of Noblegarden has long been celebrated by the races of the Alliance and recently adopted by those of the Horde. During this joyous event, it is customary for the nobles and lords from each race to hide coins, candy, and the occasional treasures within special eggs painted to look like wildflowers. These eggs are then scattered around major cities for the citizenry to find.

When: April 22-29
Where: Visit Azure Watch, Dolanaar, Goldshire, Kharanos, Brill, Razor Hill, Bloodhoof Village, Falconwing Square, or Shattrath City to join in the festivities.
Currency: Noblegarden Chocolates are used as currency during this holiday to purchase garments, mounts, pets, and other special rewards. These can be collected by completing quests and opening the eggs found in the event areas.

Holiday Activities
Daily Quests
The Great Egg Hunt rewards you with money, a small amount of experience, and a Blossoming Branch in exchange for Brightly Colored Shell Fragments gathered during your travels.

Egg Hunting
Search around Azure Watch, Dolanaar, Goldshire, Kharanos, Brill, Razor Hill, Bloodhoof Village, Falconwing Square, or Shattrath City for these (mostly) well-hidden Brightly Colored Eggs. To help you get to them more quickly, you’ll want to purchase an Egg Basket from the Noblegarden merchants and vendors to increase your speed and beat the competition.

Brightly Colored Eggs will often contain delicious chocolate you can eat or use to purchase items from the Noblegarden merchants and vendors including, toys, costumes, and mounts. These eggs can also contain a variety of special items.

Here are some of the items you could either find in Brightly Colored Eggs or purchase from vendors with Noblegarden Chocolates:

New Toy: Noble Gardener's Hearthstone (https://www.wowdb.com/items/165802-noble-gardeners-hearthstone)

The Noble Gardener's Hearthstone summons a flurry of springtime critters to whisk you away.

New Appearances: Blue Spring Circlet (https://www.wowdb.com/items/164922-blue-spring-circlet), Brown Spring Circlet (https://www.wowdb.com/items/164923-brown-spring-circlet), Yellow Spring Circlet (https://www.wowdb.com/items/164924-yellow-spring-circlet)
Mounts: Swift Springstrider (https://www.wowdb.com/items/72145-swift-springstrider)
Pets: Mystical Spring Bouquet (https://www.wowdb.com/items/116258-mystical-spring-bouquet), Spring Rabbit's Foot (https://www.wowdb.com/items/44794-spring-rabbits-foot), Noblegarden Bunny (https://www.wowdb.com/items/141532-noblegarden-bunny)
Costumes and Fun Items: Festival Garments, Blossoming Branch (https://www.wowdb.com/items/44792-blossoming-branch), Egg Basket (https://www.wowdb.com/items/45067-egg-basket), Spring Flowers (https://www.wowdb.com/items/45073-spring-flowers), Noblegarden Egg (https://www.wowdb.com/items/44818-noblegarden-egg).
Consumables: Mages can purchase the Tome of Polymorph: Rabbit (https://www.wowdb.com/items/44793-tome-of-polymorph-rabbit) in exchange for 100 Noblegarden Chocolate (https://www.wowdb.com/items/44791-noblegarden-chocolate).

Noblegarden Finery
Show off your style by strutting your stuff through town while wearing some of the great Noblegarden fineries. These can either be found within Brightly Colored Eggs or purchased from the merchants and vendors.

Achievements
Completing 8 Noblegarden Achievements will reward the title “the Noble” and count toward the What a Long Strange Trip It’s Been meta-achievement.

Spring into action so you don’t miss any of the holiday fun!
