Legion - Loot Glow Effect
In Legion, bosses with loot remaining on them will have a special glow to visually indicate that there is still loot on the body. This glow has also appeared in several other places during the beta (during Fishing, on some quest objects, and other NPCs) but now seems to be only on bosses.

Ghostcrawler Tweets
Ghostcrawler still occasionally talks about WoW. Remember that he no longer works for or speaks for Blizzard.
I think there are 5 ways to achieve game longevity: 1) tons of content (hard!), 2) reusable content (same stuff, different rules)... (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/724741759129919488))
(cont.) 3) procedural content, 4) user-generated content, 5) multiplayer provides different experiences. What am I forgetting? (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/724741856437755904))
What do you mean by procedural content?
The computer makes (or mixes up ) the content, like many Roguelikes (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/724957667177844736))
I don't think it's necessarily one that fully qualifies, but reward structures can be a big deal for me.

a good narrative!
Agreed, but I'd file that under lots of content, or else you exhaust the story too quickly. And it's hard. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/724742383418531840))
Speedruns/achievements maybe?
I meant speedruns under mods, kind of like difficulty adjustments or Halo skulls. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/724747158285471744))
