PTR Patch 3.0.8 - Build 9438 Undocumented Changes
As usual, these changes are from tooltip comparisons and they might not reflect the current state of the game. (But that's just a warning, they're still accurate 99% of the time)
Items
You can now exchange 1 Emblem of Valor (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40753) (25 Man) for 1 Emblem of Heroism (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40752) (10 Man) from the Emblems of Valor Quartermaster. Obviously, it doesn't work the other way.
Greater Inscription of the Gladiator (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/january/greaterinscriptionofthegladiator.jpg) is now available for 10 000 Honor Points -- Permanently adds 30 Stamina and 15 resilience rating to a shoulder slot item. Using the inscription will cause the shoulder slot item to become soulbound.

Item changes
Spark of Life (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=37657) now only proc from healing or damaging spells.
Forge Ember (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=37660) now only proc from healing or damaging spells.
Illustration of the Dragon Soul (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40432) now increase your spell power by 20. (Up from 18, PTR change)

Alchemy
Northrend Alchemy Research (http://www.wowhead.com/?spell=60893) can now be done every 3 days. (Down from 7 days)
Etheral Oil (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/january/etherealoil.jpg) - Make yourself translucent for 5 min
Elixir of Water Walking (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/january/elixirofwaterwalking.jpg) are now craftable.
Transmute: Titanium (http://www.wowhead.com/?spell=60350) now only takes Saronite Bar (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=36913) x 8
Lesser Flask of Resistance -- Increases your resistance to all schools of magic by 50 for 1 hr. Counts as both a Battle and Guardian elixir. This effect persists through death.

The amount of cobalt required for Spiked Cobalt (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/january/spikedcobalt.jpg) and Reinforced Cobalt (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/january/reinforcedcobalt.jpg) items has been increased. If you spend at least 10 seconds eating you will become well fed and gain 40 Hit Rating and 40 Stamina for 1 hour. This delicacy is best served raw and thus requires no cooking fire.
