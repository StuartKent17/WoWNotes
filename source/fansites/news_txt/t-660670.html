Tribute Run Cloaks
These items are the reward for completing A Tribute to Insanity (25 Players) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/3819/a-tribute-to-insanity-25-player/), with an ilvl of 272 in 25-Man they're the most powerful items you can find in game as of today. If you reach the tribute chest in hard mode (without dying) you will be able to loot the high-level cloaks, otherwise you will only get some weapons.
