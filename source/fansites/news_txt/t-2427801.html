Wildstar Developer Carbine Studios Shutting Down
Carbine Studios, the developer behind Wildstar, has shut down according to Kotaku (https://kotaku.com/wildstar-developer-carbine-studios-shuts-down-1828862729). The studio will begin the process to shut down the game. Purchases made after July 1, 2018 in game will be refunded.
