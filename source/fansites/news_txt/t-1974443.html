FEEDBACK: Il'gynoth (Heroic)
Waaaay too little damage going out in P1.
Yeah, we noticed that immediately once the test began. A recent internal playtest on Mythic had way too much raid damage in P1 (compounded by new Mythic mechanics), and that led to raid damage being tuned down across the board. But of course without the Mythic mechanics present, there's not much to heal. Whoops. World of Warcraft Takes To The Streets of Europe
On Tuesday, March 29, a group of urban artists from ARTtitude (https://www.facebook.com/arttitudebook) set their sights on London’s Shoreditch neighborhood to bring a bit of World of Warcraft’s night elf magic to the walls along Great Eastern Street. Check out the video below to see their work come to life.

These night elf murals will remain in London until April 6, after which time the artists will move on to claim Paris for all of orc kind and Amsterdam for the murloc multitude. The Paris invasion will begin with a live performance at the Paris Comics Expo (http://pariscomicsexpo.fr/en/home/) on April 16, with street art installation taking place over the following days.
