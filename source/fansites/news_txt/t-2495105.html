Get Murgle the Red Murloc Plush and Mug July 9!
The Queen of Tides has returned with Rise of Azshara (https://worldofwarcraft.com/news/23014432/), bringing a ton of new experiences to encounter—including many rare creatures—in Battle for Azeroth. One such creature is the elusive rare red baby murloc pet, Murgle. You can find Murgle in Nazjatar’s Bloodfin Village, but you’ll need to summon and defeat King Gakula first. Stirring up a little trouble with the village tadpoles is sure to draw him out.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2019/july/thumb/murgle01.png (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2019/july/murgle01.png)
Murgle in-game pet

Your characters aren’t the only ones who can adopt Murgle, though! You can bring this adorable little murloc home in both huggable plush and mug form. The Murgle Plush and the Red Murloc Mug can be yours as they’ll be available on the Blizzard Gear Store (https://gear.blizzard.com/eu/) worldwide on July 9.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2019/july/thumb/murgle02.png (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2019/july/murgle02.png)
Murgle Mug and Plush

Those attending the Japan Expo (https://www.japan-expo-paris.com/) in Paris July 4-7 will also be able to grab these tadpole-inspired treasures early. You’ll find them along with plenty of other Gear Store merchandise in the on-site Blizzard shop in hall 6 – Stand F716. (Full map)

Battle for Azeroth Dungeon Event
This week's bonus event is the Battle for Azeroth Dungeon Event.


All week long the final boss of each Battle for Azeroth Dungeon will drop an extra piece of loot.
Pick up the quest Emissary of War (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/44171-emissary-of-war) from either your Adventure Journal or in your faction hub. Every Week
The Bonus Events system consists of a rotating schedule of different activities, currently scheduled to run each week beginning on Tuesdays. Each Bonus Event grants a passive bonus to a particular game activity and offers a once-per-event quest with a noteworthy reward for accomplishing a related goal. The in-game calendar can serve as your one-stop reference for the event schedule. The Adventure Guide also offers a direct link to active Bonus Events, allowing you to easily accept any associated quests.
