Weekly Event - Burning Crusade Timewalking
Burning Crusade Timewalking is back this week. Don't forget to check out Black Temple Timewalking (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/6690-Black-Temple-Timewalking) during the event! Black Temple awards base item level 355 gear.


The weekly quest (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/44164-a-burning-path-through-time) from your adventure guide rewards Azerite Power and Cache of Uldir Treasures (https://www.wowdb.com/items/164258-cache-of-uldir-treasures).
You can find Cupri (https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/98685-cupri) in Shattrath.
Don't forget to keep working towards Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk), Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (https://www.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger), and Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade)!
Reins of the Infinite Timereaver (https://www.wowdb.com/items/133543-reins-of-the-infinite-timereaver) can also drop from any Timewalking dungeon boss.

PvP Brawl - Temple of Hotmogu
Enter the Temple of Kotmogu for this fast-paced match. For a whole new twist, players will be able throw the Orb of Power to friendly players in a game of hot potato. Beware, they’ll also gain the debuffs along with the orb so choose wisely. We’re turning things up (to 11) by changing resurrection time to a quick 5 seconds. Who will be able to hold on to the power to claim victory?

Call to Arms: Stormsong Valley
This week's Call to Arms is Call to Arms: Stormsong Valley (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52782-call-to-arms-stormsong-valley) or Call to Arms: Stormsong Valley (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/52957-call-to-arms-stormsong-valley). Head to Stormsong Valley with warmode enabled to kill 10 players of the opposing faction. This will reward you with conquest and 75 reputation for either 7th Legion (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2159-7th-legion) or The Honorbound (https://www.wowdb.com/factions/2157-the-honorbound).

If you capped conquest last week, you can work towards the next reward - a 370 item level Azerite Chest Piece.
