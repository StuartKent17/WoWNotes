Blue Posts
Guild XP "Top 20 Contributors"
There is no concept of "Top 20 contributors" anymore. We changed that functionality many months ago. XP contributed to the guild comes from all members. Guild rep is always available no matter what amount you have contributed to the guild. (Source (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/t/26435397268/leveling-speed/))

Guild Rep/XP not awarded in dungeons on beta?
This data did not make the last patch. This functionality should be live in the next build. (Source (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/t/26859266660/guild-rep-xp-not-awarded-in-dungeons/))

Guild System in Patch 4.0.1
The new guild system UI will be active in the pre-patch but guild experience and guild achievements will be disabled until Cataclysm launch. (Source (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/t/26560868398/when-will-it-go-live/))
