Winged Guardian Arrives in Pet Store
The Winged Guardian is now available from the pet store!



US Price - $25

A new arrival has just landed in the Blizzard Pet Store: the majestic Winged Guardian flying mount, a titan creation designed to protect Azeroth’s most ancient secrets. As with the Celestial Steed, this Guardian of the Titans will carry you across Azeroth as fast as your riding skill will take you. Head over to the Pet Store (http://us.blizzard.com/store/browse.xml?f=c:5,c:33#p1) to see this new addition and other World of Warcraft companions, and check out the FAQ (http://us.blizzard.com/support/article.xml?locale=en_US&articleId=29845) for more information.
