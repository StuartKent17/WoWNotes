WotLK Mounts Update - Green Proto-Drake
A new drop location has been added to the mounts list (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=824). The Green Proto-Drake (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/november/greenprotodrake.jpg) is actually a (very?) rare drop from the Mysterious Egg (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=39878) rewarded by The Oracles (http://www.wowwiki.com/Oracles) in Sholazar Basin. I guess the choice between them and the Frenzyheart Tribe (http://www.wowwiki.com/Frenzyheart_Tribe) will be much easier now. Spirit Beasts and additional pets
We wanted to have a difficult to get pet for those hunters who enjoyed the challenge of it in the old days. We wanted to make sure the Spirit Beast was not absolutely the best pet for that reason. Mind Flay "bug"
If I had any more information, I would give it. We changed the way Mind Flay worked, which introduced the problem you referenced. It is not technically a bug, but it's still annoying and we'd like to get it fixed. We're not sure if rebuilding the spell is the right answer or if there are other things we can do.
