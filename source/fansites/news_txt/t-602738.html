Druids
Vengeful Gladiator's Refuge (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/october/druid_refuge2.jpg) (healing set) has been changed, all MP/5 bonuses are modified into +spirit. (Old stats available here (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/october/s3/druid-refuge.jpg))



Hunters
There is now a Mortal Strike effect (Healing done on the target reduced by 50%, Duration 10 seconds) on Aimed Shot. This is a physical effect.
The min range to shot has been reduced from 8 yards to 4 yards. The "dead zone" is now pretty much removed.



Paladins
[spell=31879] now increase the critical strike change of all judgements capable of a critical hit by 3% and reduces threat caused by all actions by 6%. (For each level)
[spell=20193] now increase both melee and spell hit %.



Rogues
[spell=18429] now also applies to Backstab.
[spell=36554] range changed to 8-25 yards. The 20% damage bonus now applies to any ability.
[spell=26864] changed from +10 to +36 damage, max charges has been reduced to 10, duration remains the same. (15 seconds)



Warriors
[spell=20505] provides 2% dodge reduction at rank 2 instead of the 4 promised a few days ago.



Engineering
New models available for some random items (paper toys and a rocket chicken, new icon for flying mounts and hula doll), the flying mount model has been updated to reflect the difference between the epic and normal version of the mount. (Note that the rotor is still here in game for both mounts, it's just a bug from wowmodelviewer)

Guild banks
Guild banks are available in most capital cities (including Scryers and Aldor banks) ! Each tab can contain 98 items and you can purchase up to 6 of them.
1st tab : 1 gold
2nd tab : 10 gold
3rd tab : 100 gold
4th tab : 1 000 gold
5th tab : 5 000 gold
6th tab : 10 000 gold

Everything is updated in real time (you might have to wait a few seconds to update settings). One interesting feature undocumented in the patch notes is a permission allowing people to take only X stacks from a tab per day.
