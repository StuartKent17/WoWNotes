PTR 4.0.6 Patch Notes - January 24 Update
The PTR Patch 4.0.6 Patch Notes (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/149669/public-test-realm-patch-406-notes-updated-jan-24) have been updated once again with a couple of interesting changes to dungeons and classes.

Guilds

The guild reward Armadillo Pup (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/89670/armadillo-pup/) now requires revered faction instead of exalted.
The guild faction requirement on the Guild Herald (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/92398/guild-herald/) has been lowered to Revered reputation.


Dungeons & Raids

Level-85 dungeon bosses in Lost City of the Tol'Vir, Grim Batol, and Halls of Origination now drop 30 Justice Points each when killed.
The daily Random Dungeon reward on normal difficulty has been increased to 140 Justice Points, up from 70.
Players no longer need to discover Cataclysm dungeon entrances in order to access them via the Dungeon Finder.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/270-Druid) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..druid) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1117-Druid-Cataclysm))

Rejuvenation (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/774/rejuvenation/) is now trained at level 3, down from level 8. In addition, its mana cost is now 20% of base mana.


Balance

Moonkin Form (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/24858/moonkin-form/) now grants 15% damage reduction instead of increased armor. In addition, shapeshifting in or out of this form now breaks roots. Note this is not stated in the tooltip.


Feral

Blood in the Water (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/80319/blood-in-the-water/): Timing on this effect has been improved so that Ferocious Bite (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/22568/ferocious-bite/) will immediately refresh Rip (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/1079/rip/), rather than be slightly delayed (which gave a chance for the Rip to expire despite being refreshed).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/273-Paladin) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..paladin) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1670-Paladin-Cataclysm))

Divine Light (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/82326/divine-light/) mana cost has been increased by approximately 10%.
Flash of Light (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/19750/flash-of-light/) mana cost has been increased by approximately 10%.
Holy Light (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/635/holy-light/) mana cost has been increased by approximately 10%.
Lay on Hands (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/633/lay-on-hands/) now causes Forbearance (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/25771/forbearance/) on the target. It used to only cause it when cast on the paladin. This was an old design from when Divine Protection (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/498/divine-protection/) caused Forbearance and the paladin didn't want to prevent a tank from using their defensive cooldown.
Lay on Hands cannot be a critical effect and will not be affected by most abilities which modify healing (such as Beacon of Light (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/53563/beacon-of-light/)). In addition, Lay on Hands is no longer on the global cooldown.


Holy

Aura Mastery (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/31821/aura-mastery/): This ability no longer increases the benefit granted by Crusader Aura (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/32223/crusader-aura/).


Protection

Eternal Glory (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/87164/eternal-glory/): The chance to not consume Holy Power from this talent now also applies to not consuming the clearcasting effect generated by Divine Purpose (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/86172/divine-purpose/).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/274-Priest) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..priest) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1123-Priest-Cataclysm))

Mind Blast (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/8092/mind-blast/) now does 18% more damage than Mind Spike (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/73510/mind-spike/).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/275-Rogue) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..rogue) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1122-Rogue-Cataclysm))
Combat

Blade Flurry (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/13877/blade-flurry/) is now a toggle that can be turned off by pressing the button again. In addition, this ability no longer has a fixed duration, but will go on cooldown for 10 seconds after being canceled (similar to Stealth (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/1784/stealth/)). In addition, Blade Flurry now reduces energy generation by 30% while active, up from 20%.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/276-Shaman) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..shaman) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1124-Shaman-Cataclysm))

Healing Rain (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/73920/healing-rain/) now has a 40-yard radius, up from 30, to be consistent with other shaman heals.


Elemental

Elemental Overload (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/77222/elemental-overload/) (Mastery) procs of Lightning Bolt (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/403/lightning-bolt/) or Chain Lightning (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/421/chain-lightning/) can now generate Rolling Thunder (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/88764/rolling-thunder/) charges.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..warlock) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1125-Warlock-Cataclysm))

Devour (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/19505/devour-magic/) (Felhunter) cooldown has been increased to 20 seconds, up from 8. Devour is no longer able to be auto-cast.


Affliction

Soul Swap (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/86121/soul-swap/) can no longer be dispelled (the aura given to the warlock between swapping).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/278-Warrior) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..warrior) / Beta Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1676-Warrior-Cataclysm))
Fury

Single-Minded Fury (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/81099/single-minded-fury/) bonus has been increased to 20%, up from 15%.


Items

The PvP Balance druid 4-piece set bonus effect (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/46832/sudden-eclipse/) has been increased to 20 Solar Energy and 13 Lunar Energy, up from 5 and 13. The effect will also now be more intelligent at choosing whether to give the druid Lunar or Solar Energy (it checks the Balance Power to determine which to give depending on which energy is higher at the time).


Quests & Creatures
Tol Barad

Rusty Rifles will now spawn more frequently at Farson Keep.
Players should now always receive 6 daily quests in Tol Barad Peninsula as intended, rather than sometimes only getting 5.
