Irae AoD kills Yogg-Saron with one Keeper
Irae AoD (http://www.irae-guild.de/) (EU-Mug'thol) managed to kill Yogg-saron with the assistance of only one keeper (Heroic: One Light in the Darkness (http://www.wowhead.com/?achievement=3163)), it definitely looks like the hard mode kill without any keeper is possible. They also got a hard mode loot (ilvl 239) from Yogg-Saron for this kill.
