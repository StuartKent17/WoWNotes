Crushing Slam https://www.wowdb.com/spells/262004 - Slams the ground, inflicting Physical damage and knocking the player back. In addition, the target's threat is cleared.
Coalesced Essence https://www.wowdb.com/spells/261597 - Creates an orb of swirling spirits. Touching the orb causes it to explode, inflicting 21 Shadow damage to the target, knocking them back.
Consuming Spirits https://www.wowdb.com/spells/261632 - Causes random players to become suffused with energy that bursts forth inflicting 7 Shadow damage to allies within 8 yards every 2 sec for 6 sec.
Terror Wail https://www.wowdb.com/spells/261552 - Inflicts 37 Shadow damage to all enemies in a cone in front of the caster, causing them to flee in fear for 3 sec.
