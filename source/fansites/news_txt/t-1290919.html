Patch 5.3 PTR Notes Update - April 22
Lots of interesting changes today, with the highlights below:


Experience needed to increase from level 85 to level 90 has been reduced by 33% (How it will work (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/259121-53-mop-xp-lowered-please/#post173))
Reduced the number of Lesser Charms of Good Fortune needed for the Mogu Runes of Fate weekly quest to 50, down from 90.
Shado-Pan Assault belts (http://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-req-faction-1=1435&filter-req-faction-2=1&filter-slot=64) now require Friendly reputation to purchase, down from Revered.
Shado-Pan Assault chest pieces (http://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-req-faction-1=1435&filter-req-faction-2=1&filter-slot=32) now require Honored reputation to purchase, down from Revered.


General

Experience needed to increase from level 85 to level 90 has been reduced by 33%.


Classes

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/270-Druid) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/druid) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#s)

General

Tranquility (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/740) now targets 12 raid members (up from 5) each time it heals when used in a 25-player instance. This change also applies to players using the Symbiosis (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/110498) version of Tranquility (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/740).

Restoration

Ironbark (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/102342) now has a cooldown of 60 seconds, down from 2 minutes.
Swiftmend (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/81269) area-of-effect component now heals injured allies within 10 yards, up from 8 yards.
Wild Mushroom: Bloom (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/102792) now heals all allies within 10 yards (up from 8 yards) for 100% more, and includes bonus healing from Rejuvenation (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/774) overhealing.


Item Sets

Restoration PvP 4-piece set bonus now reduces the cooldown of Ironbark (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/102342) by 30 seconds (down from 90 seconds).




http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/271-Hunter) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/hunter) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#M))

General

Revive Pet (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/982) now has a cast time of 4 seconds, down from 6 seconds.




http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/imonks.gif Monk (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/341-Monk) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/monk) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#o))

Mistweaver

Revival (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/115310) now has a healing cap of 15 raid members (up from 6) when used in a 25-player instance.




http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/273-Paladin) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/paladin) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#I))

General

Devotion Aura (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/31821) can now be used while the Paladin is silenced.

Glyphs

Glyph of the Battle Healer (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/81956) now heals a nearby injured friendly target, excluding the Paladin, for 20% of damage dealt while Seal of Insight (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/20165) is active, down from 30%.




http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/274-Priest) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/priest) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#U))

Glyphs

Glyph of Smite (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/42416) no longer causes the additional 20% damage dealt by Smite (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/585) to transfer into Atonement (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/81749).

Discipline

Atonement (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/81749) now heals nearby friendly targets for 90% of the damage dealt, down from 100%.
Penance (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/47666) now deals 10% less damage, but healing done is increased by 10%.

Holy

Divine Hymn (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/64843) now targets 12 raid members (up from 5) each time it heals when used in a 25-player instance.

Shadow

Vampiric Embrace (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/15286) now has a healing cap of 15 raid members (up from 6) when used in a 25-player instance.




http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warlock) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#k))

General

Demonic Gateway (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/111771) cast time has been reduced to 4 seconds, down from 5 seconds, gains its first charge after 5 seconds (down from 13 seconds), and gains a new charge every 10 seconds (down from 15 seconds). The summoned gateway has 100% of the summoning Warlock's health, 100% of the Warlock's resilience, and can now be attacked and killed by enemy players.

Item Sets

Warlock PvP 2-piece set bonus no longer reduces cooldown on Unending Resolve (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/104773) and have been replaced by the former 4-piece set bonus where the Warlock's Twilight Ward (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/6229) absorbs damage from all magical schools.
Warlock PvP 4-piece set bonus now increases damage dealt by Agony (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/131737), Corruption (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/131740), Doom, Immolate (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/348), and Unstable Affliction (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/30108) by 10%.




Pet Battles

General

Pandaren Spirit Pet Supplies now has a chance to drop a family specific rare Battle-Stone.

Battle Pet Quests

A new achievement and reward has been added for characters that complete every Pet Battle daily quest in a single day.
Protection for bad luck streaks have been added to Battle-Stones from pet supplies bags and wild battles. Each bag or battle that does not provide a stone has a progressively better chance to award a stone to the player.



Raids, Dungeons, and Scenarios

Raids

Bonus Roll

Reduced the number of Lesser Charms of Good Fortune needed for the Mogu Runes of Fate weekly quest to 50, down from 90.

Terrace of Endless Spring

Players no longer need to have defeated Grand Empress Shek'zeer to enter the Terrace of Endless Spring in Normal difficulty.

Throne of Thunder

Primordius
The Genetically Unmodified Organism achievement has been redesigned. It now requires players to defeat Primordius while none of the players in the raid received a harmful mutation.


Dungeons

Completing the Challenge mode daily quest now awards a Heroic Cache of Treasures with a chance to contain an epic item!
