Patch 6.2.2 - Timewalking Rewards
Patch 6.2.2 adds more rewards to Timewalking! You can see Cupri http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/98685-cupri in Shattrath Outland to purchase these items for Timewarped Badge http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge, rewarded from running Timewalking dungeons. 

A recent PTR build adjusted the costs, but we haven't had another Timewalking weekend to confirm the new prices and any changes to the drop rate. The latest version of the official notes still says "Players now receive a Timewarped Badge when they complete a Timewalking dungeon". Keep in mind that the drop rate and prices are not final!
