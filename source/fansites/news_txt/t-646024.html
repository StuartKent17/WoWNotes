<font size="3" style="line-height: 1.3em;">Ulduar - Hard Mode Gear List</font>
Today we&#039;ve got one of the most important update to the Ulduar loot list, pretty much all the items from the hard mode encounters are now available. Yes, we also have the hard mode rewards from Yogg-Saron, Mimiron or others unbeaten encounters. Please note that <span style="color: red;">Items do not display socket bonuses, this is a known bug</span>

As of today, only the items from Algalon are missing, but the quest rewards have been discovered (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?topic=51931.0). A new screenshot is also available for <span style="color: #ff8000;">Val&#039;anyr, Hammer of the Ancient Kings</span> (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/april/valanyr.jpg).
