Patch 7.2 adds two new tiers of Order Hall Traits. Tier 7 costs 20000 x Order Resources (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/1220-order-resources) and Tier 8 costs 30000 x Order Resources (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/1220-order-resources).

You can browse all of the new traits on the WoWDB PTR site (http://ptr.wowdb.com/orderhall/talents?sort=-position).


Tier 7 Column 1 (http://ptr.wowdb.com/orderhall/talents/483-guardians-of-the-kirin-tor) - New troop type that has a random Ability and can counter either Minions, Spells or Hazards, in addition to +30% success chance.
Tier 7 Column 2 (http://ptr.wowdb.com/orderhall/talents/484-roster-of-champions) - Increase the number of Champions you can have active at once by 1.
Tier 8 Column 1 (http://ptr.wowdb.com/orderhall/talents/485-relic-empowerment) - All new Relics are able to grant a Rank increase to a second Artifact Trait.

PSA: Be Careful with your Essences
It's no different then any other daily quest. Once you get the set number of items and turn in the quest the next time you pick up the quest the overflow isn't there..it's reset.
I can see this being an issue in a few situations, specifically around Nighthold progression and going over the 50 "cap" by a moderate amount during your normal raid time.

We will send this feedback over to the developers and see what's going on.

I think it's typical for quests like this to work in this way, but in this case, when looking at how raids progress and LFR tiers and such, it seems people could end up saving 70-80 at a time without realizing it which could be devastating.
