Feline Familiar, Creepy Crate and Little Wickerman
The new Feline Familiar (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/70908/feline-familiar/) companion pet and Little Wickerman (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/70722/little-wickerman/) are available from vendors for 150 x Tricky Treat (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/33226/tricky-treat/) each!

It also seems that a new quest chain is available in Stormwind/Orgrimmar Inns, completing it will reward you with a nice Creepy Crate (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/71076/creepy-crate/)!
