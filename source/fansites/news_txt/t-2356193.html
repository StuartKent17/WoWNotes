7.3.5 PTR - Mythic Plus Battle Resurrections
Combat resurrections in Mythic+ dungeons in Patch 7.3.5 will function similarly to the way they currently do in raids.


Combat resurrections start with 1 charge for the group and gain a new charge every 10 minutes.
Raise Ally (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/61999-raise-ally), Rebirth (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/20484-rebirth), Soulstone (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/20707-soulstone) and Hunter battle resurrections all share the same charges.
You can accumulate a total of 5 charges at a time, however, it would take 40 minutes to reach this point.

7.3.5 PTR - Ogmot Journal
Ogmot the Mad (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/132591-ogmot-the-mad) is a new rare elite ogre in Silithus that drops Ogmot's Dream Journal (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/156852-ogmots-dream-journal). This journal contains 10 pages that detail the events of a nameless person who deals with the whispers of the Old Gods. The drop was originally discovered by wilder_people (https://www.reddit.com/r/wow/comments/7kpbuk/new_foreshadowing_riddles_found_in_journal_on_735/)!
