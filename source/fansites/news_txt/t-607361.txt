Boubouille2008-05-04, 01:06 AMUpdate - Added a few more unconfirmed loot to the Loot Table. 

Tonight on the EU-Vek'nilash server, SK-Gaming http://www.sk-gaming.com/wow/ managed to get the world first kill of M'uru, the fifth boss of Sunwell Plateau. Only Kil'jaeden remains but sadly, we'll have to wait for the opening of the last door of the instance in a few weeks.
