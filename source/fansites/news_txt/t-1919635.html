Artifact Wepaons
Will our artifacts be able to unlock additional relic slots at some point?
Artifacts start with 2 relic slots and unlock a 3rd shortly after reaching 110. We're not planning any additional slots. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/675118010940702720))

PvP
does this mean tank specs will be viable in PvP also?
We're looking into ways to make tanks fun - both to play as, and to play against - in PvP. It's a tough challenge. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/675119169545875456))

why not just make racials unavailable for pvp?
We feel racials are an important part of what defines your character, and should come into PvP with you. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/675119332217790470))

Transmogrification
Rumor from alpha is clothies can unlock cloth transmog but plate cannot. Intended behavior?
Intended. Plate wearers unlock plate, mail wearers unlock mail, etc. Weapons must be equippable by that character. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/675118823566147584))

If I unlocked the CM armor during MOP on one warlock, if I make another will I be able to XMOG it on him with the new system?

How do you keep your team motivated when deadlines loom and setbacks start to/have happened? How do you boost morale?

Hey, GC, how hard is it to take a wow boss mechanic, like Shadow Crash, and make it a player ability? Is it technically hard?
If it's tied to the room geometry (target this x,y coordinate) then it can be hard. A lot of class abilities started with bosses. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/673143612075458560))

I understand, do u think redesigning classes every xpac is a good idea? I despise having to relearn the specs I play esp. 1/2
I like class change. I was sad in WoD when the direction was minimal change. But having to relearn is the big risk. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/673144132601184256))

what is a Devs definition of "Balance" equal everything or as close as balanceable?
Depends on devs and projects. Usually it's something like decisions of what / how to play aren't driven by raw power. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/674042467474214913))

Systems designers distinguish between "balanced" and "balanceable" and focus more on the latter since it's a lot harder. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/673989369959698432))
how do unbalanceable things form? i.e. by what general mechanism does a system/mechanic become unbalanceable?
Things are balanceable if they have tuning knobs. A switch with on/off lacks tuning knobs. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/674042966801956864))
An ability that is "kill next target" doesn't scale with target health or power, so you lack an ability to tune it. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/674043220888698880))

like why make it balancable if you don't then use that work to try achieve balance.
Totally. Balance is the end goal and the most important in the end. We just talk about the hard part which is balanceable. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/673997803002003456))
Sadly, what isn't balance-able is community perception. Once they believe something is bad, it's hard to change that view.
That's true, but players don't owe use endlessly trying something to see if it's finally fixed yet. (OccupyGStreet (https://twitter.com/OccupyGStreet/status/674248424988917760))
