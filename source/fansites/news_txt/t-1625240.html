Warlords of Draenor - Garrisons: Barracks
The Barracks (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/28-barracks) takes up a Large plot which can also be used for a Mage Tower / Spirit Lodge (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/39-mage-tower), Gnomish Gearworks / Goblin Workshop (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/164-gnomish-gearworks), Stables (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/67-stables), or Dwarven Bunker / War Mill (http://www.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/8-dwarven-bunker). Once upgraded to Tier 3, your garrison will have two Large plots available.

Abilities
This building is good for leveling followers quickly, having more active followers, having a bodyguard in Draenor, and some garrison visual changes.


Level 1 - Unlocks patrol missions. Patrol missions have higher than normal follower experience rewards.
Level 2 - Allows a follower with the bodyguard trait to accompany you as a guardian in Draenor zones.
Level 3 - Increases your follower limit by 5 and grants access to racial guards and banners.



Level 3 Unlock
The Level 3 version of this building is unlocked by completing the Patrolling Draenor (http://beta.wowdb.com/achievements/9523-patrolling-draenor) achievement, which requires you to complete 50 garrison patrol missions.


Bodyguard
The Level 2 Barracks will give you a bodyguard in Draenor that will assist you in combat. There are currently five followers that have the necessary Bodyguard ability (http://www.wowdb.com/garrison/abilities/231-bodyguard).

Each follower has their own reputation with three levels of 10k reputation each. Killing mobs in Draenor will give you ~11 reputation per kill. Abilities are unlocked at the second level of reputation, followed by perks at the third.

Warlords of Draenor - Garrisons: Mage Tower / Spirit Lodge
The Mage Tower / Spirit Lodge (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/39-mage-tower) takes up a Large plot which can also be used for a Barracks (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/28-barracks), Gnomish Gearworks / Goblin Workshop (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/164-gnomish-gearworks), Stables (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/67-stables), or Dwarven Bunker / War Mill (http://www.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/8-dwarven-bunker). Once upgraded to Tier 3, your garrison will have two Large plots available.


Abilities
This building is good for quick travel around Draenor and a combat perk.


Level 1 - Allows the collection of ogre waystones from Ogres on Draenor. These waystones can be used to power an Ogre Waygate of your choice on Draenor. Also occasionally triggers Runes of Power during combat.
Level 2 - Unlocks access to a second Ogre Waygate for a total of 2.
Level 3 - Unlocks access to a third Ogre Waygate for a total of 3.



Level 3 Unlock
The Level 3 version of this building is unlocked by completing the Finding Your Waystones (http://beta.wowdb.com/achievements/9497-finding-your-waystones) achievement, which requires you to collect 500 Ogre Waystones (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/117491-ogre-waystone) from killing Ogres in Draenor.


Waygates
Waygates are one-way (Ogre Waygates) portals to specific points in Draenor, with a maximum of three open at once with a Level 3 building.

Activating a Waygate (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/173820-activating-waygate) costs 25 Ogre Waystone (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/117491-ogre-waystone) and Deactivating a Waygate (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/173818-deactivating-waygate) costs 10 Ogre Waystone (http://beta.wowdb.com/items/117491-ogre-waystone).

You can find a map of the waygate locations in Draenor below, or find a more detailed location on the Ancient Waygate Protector's page for each zone: Frostfire Ridge (http://beta.wowdb.com/npcs/89235-ancient-waygate-protector), Shadowmoon Valley (http://beta.wowdb.com/npcs/89236-ancient-waygate-protector), Gorgrond (http://beta.wowdb.com/npcs/89234-ancient-waygate-protector), Talador (http://beta.wowdb.com/npcs/89233-ancient-waygate-protector), Spires of Arak (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/89232-ancient-waygate-protector), Nagrand (http://beta.wowdb.com/npcs/89230-ancient-waygate-protector).

Abilities
This building is good for playing with inventions of limited usefulness.


Level 1 - Unlocks a personal engineer that creates a powerful invention each day. Exceptional work!

Patch 6.0.3 Hotfixes - November 5
Racials

Dwarven Stoneform ability should no longer cause the character to break out of stealth.


Classes

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ideathknights.gif Death Knight (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/269-Death-Knight) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/death-knight) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#Y))

General

Outbreak (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/77575) modified by Glyph of Outbreak (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/43826) should no longer incorrectly consume Runic Power if the attack misses.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/271-Hunter) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/hunter) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#M))

General

Explosive Trap (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/13813) modified by Glyph of Explosive Trap (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/42908) should now correctly knock enemies away from the trap instead of away from the Hunter.
Freezing Trap (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/1499) should now trigger correctly when a stealthed character walks over it.

Survival

Serpent Sting (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/1978) should now be correctly applied to targets even if they move more than 40 yards away when struck by Arcane Shot (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/3044).

Hunter Pets

Tenacity Pet Specialization: Blood of the Rhino now reduces the pet's physical damage taken by 15% instead of increasing armor by 20%.
Tenacity Pet Specialization: Great Stamina now increases the pet's health by 60% (up from 12%).
Carrion Bird: Bloody Screech can now be set to autocast.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/274-Priest) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/priest) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#U))

Discipline

Divine Aegis (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/47515) should now correctly factor in the increase from Might of the Mountain racial passive.
Holy Nova (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/132157) healing has been decreased by 15%.

Talents

Divine Star (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/110745) healing or damage (depending on Specialization) has been increased by 15%.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warlock) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#k))

General

DoomGuard (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/115295) should no longer run out of energy from casting Doom Bolts.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/278-Warrior) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warrior) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#E))

Talents

Sudden Death (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/29725) is now 2.5 RPPM (real procs per minute increased by Haste) instead of a 10% chance to activate from autoattacks.


Quests

Just Around the Corner (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/12819-just-around-the-corner): Characters should now be able to interact with Sparksocket's Tools.
The Brothers Bronzebeard (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/13417-the-brothers-bronzebeard): Brann's Flying Machine has been made to be more sturdy and should no longer be destroyed too quickly.
The Fall of Neferset City (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/28520-the-fall-of-neferset-city): Dark Pharaoh Tekahn should now spawn in correctly, allowing players to complete the quest.
The Strength of Tortolla (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/25915-the-strength-of-tortolla): The quest can now be completed by characters with the PvP flag enabled.
Truce? (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/11989-truce): Characters should now be able to interact with Dull Carving Knife.


World Environment

Fixed an issue where Zidormi may send players to an incorrect phase in the Blasted Lands.
Timeless Isle

Resolved an issue where Mist-Covered Treasure Chest (http://www.wowdb.com/objects/220908-mist-covered-treasure-chest) could sometimes be empty.


Dungeons, Raids, and Scenarios

Terrace of Endless Spring

Protectors of the Endless: Protector Kaolan's Defiled Ground ability now deals less damage.

Heart of Fear

[Requires a realm restart.] Garalon: Destroying Garalon's Legs should now correctly damage Garalon and resolved a situation where players facing one of the legs may incorrectly receive an error message that they're not.

Dungeons

Siege of Niuzao Temple: Players should now be able to complete the achievement, Where's My Air Support?
Well of Eternity: Players should now be able to complete the achievement, That's Not Canon!

Challenge Mode

Once a Challenge Mode has started, characters can no longer change specializations, talents, or glyphs.


PvP

Resolved an issue where some eligible characters did not receive a Prideful Gladiator's Cloud Serpent.


Battlegrounds and Arenas

Battlegrounds

[Requires a realm restart.] Characters should now receive a popup notifying them with the amount of bonus Honor that was awarded at the end of a Random Battleground match.

Arenas

Priests entering an Arena match while in Spirit of Redemption form should no longer die during the Arena Preparation phase.


Professions

Mixology should be working correctly doubles the duration of flasks and elixirs once more.


Items

[Requires a realm restart.] Adjusted a number of item enhancements from earlier expansions that were providing a larger than intended bonus.
Adjusted a number of potions from the Cataclysm expansion that were providing a larger than intended bonus.
Crocolisk Au Gratin should now correctly provide a buff to Haste and Stamina instead of Expertise and Stamina.
Fisherman's Feast now provides a more reasonable buff to Stamina.
Formula: Enchant Ring - Striking no longer has a use effect as the formula it teaches no longer exists.
Haunted Memento's Haunted effect is now a buff that can be removed by the character.


Bug Fixes

Resolved an issue where items transmogrified into The Horseman's Horrific Hood or The Horseman's Sinister Slicer could revert back to their original state.
Resolved an issue where boosting characters could cause them to forget spells that have been taught by items.

Celebrate World of Warcraft’s 10th Anniversary With Us!
Blizzard is marking the 10-year anniversary of World of Warcraft’s launch with awesome events in three major cities across Europe. If you can make it to central Paris, London, or Berlin on November 22 you’ll have the chance to attend and be part of the celebration. You’ll be able to mix with fellow players, well-known fan sites, and Blizzard staff, take part in fun activities including WoW dance and costume contests, win cool prizes, have unique photo opportunities to show your WoW pride, and much more… Of course, there will also be drinks and birthday cake!

Warlords of Draenor Irvine Signing Event
While you can’t get a much bigger Warlords of Draenor launch and signing celebration than BlizzCon, we’ll be holding a local signing event the night of the release of the expansion for those of you who live around the Orange County area. We’ll have over 100 developers on-hand to sign your box or piece of Warcraft memorabilia and help celebrate the beginning of a new adventure through The Dark Portal.

Where:
GameStop, Irvine Spectrum Center
71 Fortune Dr. #856
Irvine, CA 92618

When:
Wednesday, November 12, starting at 10:00 a.m. PST


Once the store opens at 10 a.m., pre-orders can be finalized, and a line can begin forming.
When GameStop opens, product may be purchased and wristbands are distributed when pre-orders are finalized.
Early arrivals should form a line on the left side of the GameStop front door.


Thursday, November 13, 12:01 a.m. PST

World of Warcraft: Warlords of Draenor available for pick-up (final sales receipt required).
Blizzard developer signing tables open.
NOTE: To try to ensure everyone can get home to play the game as soon as possible, we’ll only be signing 1 item per attendee.


If you pre-ordered at GameStop at the Irvine Spectrum Center:
1. Pay for and finalize your pre-order at the GameStop in Spectrum Center any time after 10:00 a.m. on Wednesday, November 12 and pick up your wristband.
2. After you have your wristband, join your fellow WoW players in-line to the left side of the front door of the store (left side when facing the front door). As the line grows, please be sure that it does not block the entrance to any other stores.

If you pre-ordered at a different GameStop:
Your pre-order will not be accessible from a different store, but can be refunded any time before or after the event in the store where you placed the order. When you arrive at the GameStop location at the Spectrum Center:
1. Purchase your copy of World of Warcraft: Warlords of Draenor at the GameStop store between 10:00 a.m. and midnight.
2. After you have your wristband, join your fellow WoW players in line to the left side of the front door of the store (left side when facing the front door). As the line grows, please be sure that it does not block the entrance to any other stores.

If you have not pre-ordered World of Warcraft: Warlords of Draenor with GameStop:
No pre-order? No problem! Purchase your copy of the World of Warcraft: Warlords of Draenor standard or Collector’s Edition at the GameStop located at the Irvine Spectrum Center. Supplies are limited, so be sure to stop by the store as soon as you arrive to make your purchase and get your receipt and event wristband.

If you purchased a digital version:
You are still welcome to join your fellow friends in the World of Warcraft community to get an item of your choice signed by the development team. Please be sure to obtain your event wristband beginning at 7:00 p.m. on November 12. NOTE: To try to ensure everyone can get home to play the game as soon as possible, we’ll only be signing 1 item per attendee.
If you do not wish to have your copy signed:

No problem! At midnight, GameStop will host a separate distribution for those who want to get their copy, get home, and begin playing.

We’ll see you there!
