Spell Comparison
Not a lot of new changes here if you already went through the patch notes update. 

Items
Item - Shaman T10 Elemental 2P Bonus  - Your Lightning Bolt and Chain Lightning spells reduce the remaining cooldown on your Elemental Mastery talent by 2 sec. Old - Your Lightning Bolt spell reduces the remaining cooldown on your Elemental Mastery talent by 1 sec.
Item - Shaman T10 Elemental Relic Shocks  - The periodic damage from your Flame Shock spell grants 44 haste rating for 30 sec. Stacks up to 5 times. Old - Your Earth Shock, Flame Shock, and Frost Shock spells grant 73 haste rating for 30 sec. Stacks up to 3 times.
Item - Death Knight T10 DPS Relic Obliterate, Scourge Strike, Death Strike  - Your Obliterate, Scourge Strike, and Death Strike abilities grants 73 Strength for 15 sec. Stacks up to 3 times. Old - Stacked up to 5 Times
Item - Icecrown 25 Heroic Caster Trinket 1 Base  - Your harmful spells have a chance to increase your spell power by 121 and an additional 121 every 2 sec for 20 sec.

Racial
Will of the Forsaken  now shares a 45 sec cooldown with other similar effects.

 Paladin
Holy
