I wanted to clear this up since it is understandably causing some confusion. All pet tiers cost 3 points to advance. There are no talents that cost 5 points. In the beta some tooltips may say 5 but only 3 is enforced. Most importantly this means that there are not talents that are available only to BM hunters. They can get more talents - that's all. Furthemore, the exotic pet abilities while interesting are just the same kind of unique abilities that every pet family gets, like web or prowl. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=8402821333&postId=84017653348&sid=1#0))



Achievements
The Tuskarmageddon achievement requiring you to kill 1 000 000 tuskars was a joke achievement. It has been removed. The name was transferred over to the Tuskarr reputation achievement. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=8429964839&postId=84292368651&sid=1#3))
The bug preventing to complete the Hellfire Ramparts achievements when killing Vazruden the Herald will be fixed in the next build. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=8429983343&postId=84292958610&sid=1#1))
An achievement might be added for the completion of the Shartuul Event in Ogri'la. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=8430363518&postId=84292958586&sid=1#2))

Keep reporting anything like this. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=8202600899&postId=82015837300&sid=1#5))

WotLK - Upcoming Beta Classes Changes V2
The official talent calculator (http://www.worldofwarcraft.com/info/classes/talent-index/wrath.html) has been updated with what seems to be the upcoming changes to the Wrath of the Lich King beta. Below is a list of changes (we might have missed a few things)

We posted a first draft of these changes a few days ago but an updated version feels necessary because of a few details :
According to feedbacks, Ice Lance not being mentioned in the Arctic Reach tooltip was a known bug and it might not have been changed.
The mana cost of Druids forms might have not been changed, official site doesn't seem to support % of base mana for spell costs and even on the BC Talent Calculator (http://www.worldofwarcraft.com/info/classes/druid/talents.html), the fixed value of mana is here. Please stop reporting changes to mana costs for all the classes (especially Warlocks or Priests), this is most likely a bug.
Death Knight Frost Tree changes from saturday have been added to the list.

Will of the Necropolis now increases your expertise by 3/6/9 instead of 4/8/12
Blood Gorged now only affects melee damage



Frost
Improved Icy Touch now increase the damage of your Icy Touch by 10/20/30/40/50% and its damage against Frozen Targets by 20/40/60/80/100% (Old version : Reduces the cooldown of your Icy Touch spell by 1/2/3/4/5 sec and increases its critical strike damage bonus by 10/20/30/40/50%)
Nerves of Cold Steel has been moved from Tier 3 to Tier 2.
Annihilation has been moved from Tier 6 to Tier 3.
Aneurysm has been moved from Tier 8 to Tier 7.
Merciless Combat has been moved from Tier 8 to Tier 6.

Unholy
