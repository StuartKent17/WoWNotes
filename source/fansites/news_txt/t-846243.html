Patch 4.0.6 on live realms this week
Patch 4.0.6 will be deployed on live realms this week and it's time for a recap of the changes with a couple of highlights. You can find the retail patch notes below, they were massively updated compared to the PTR notes (more categories, bug fixes, etc ...) and I strongly suggest that you read them, even if you checked the PTR notes before.


Flasks Crafting Requirements Changes
Alchemy
All the flasks had their reagents requirements changed and now requires 8 Volatile Life (up from 6) and 8 of each Herb (down from 12)

Patch 4.0.6 changes the price of all the Archaeology junk items and makes the profession slightly more profitable. You won't make thousand of golds every hour but at least it won't feel like you're completely wasting your time!

Also, Survey (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/80451/) has a 3 sec cooldown now, down from 5 sec.
