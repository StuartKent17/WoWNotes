Ranked PvP Item Levels
Now that BFA Season 1 is underway, we’ve seen a number of questions about the item levels of various rewards and reward tiers. Here’s an outline of the current item levels you’ll see when you receive rewards from ranked PvP:


Tier, Rating, End of Match iLvl, Weekly cache iLvl, Weekly Azerite item iLvl
Unranked, 0 – 1399, item level 340, item level 355, item level 355
Combatant, 1400 – 1599, item level 350, item level 365, item level 355
Challenger, 1600 – 1799, item level 360, item level 370, item level 370
Rival, 1800 – 2099, item level 365, item level 375, item level 370
Duelist, 2100 – 2399, item level 370, item level 380, item level 385
Gladiator, 2400+, item level 375, item level 385, item level 385

Some reminders:
End of match rewards are occasional.
For your weekly Azerite item Conquest turn-in, see Marshal Gabriel in the Salt and Shanty Inn near the Ashvane Docks in Boralus or Xander Silberman at Mugambala in Zandalar.
You’ll loot your weekly cache reward from a chest in that area.
The weekly cache items can Warforge/Titanforge.

Now go strike down your enemies!

Battle for Azeroth Item Level Rewards Breakdown
With the additional information on PvP reward item levels, we now know what item level ranges there are for all of the reward sources.


Dungeons (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7884-Battle-for-Azeroth-Live-Today!#bfadungeons)

Normal Dungeons - Item Level 310
Heroic Dungeons - Item Level 325
Mythic Dungeons - Item Level 340
Mythic + Dungeons

End of Mythic + Cache - Item Level 345 - 370 based on Mythic + Difficulty
Weekly Cache

Basic Gear - Item Level 355 - 380 based on highest difficulty achieved
Azerite Armor - Item Level 340 - 385 based on highest difficulty achieved



Reputations (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7884-Battle-for-Azeroth-Live-Today!#reputations)

Honored Reputation Cloaks - Item Level 320
Revered Reputation Gear- Item Level 335
Exalted Reputation Gear - Item Level 350 - 355

Island Expeditions - Item Level 325
World Drops - Item Level 350
Profession Gear (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7884-Battle-for-Azeroth-Live-Today!#professions) - 355 (Can be upgraded to 385)
World Quest Rewards - Scales up to Item Level 330 based on personal item level
Warfronts

Warfront Win Gear - Item Level 340
Arathi Highlands Rare Gear - Item Level 340
Warfront Weekly Cache Reward - Item Level 370
Arathi Highlands World Boss - Item Level 370

World Bosses (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7855-Battle-for-Azeroth-World-Boss-Guide-Loot-and-Locations) - Item Level 355 (Kul Tiras) and Item Level 370 (Warfronts)
Raiding (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7944-Uldir-Boss-Guides-World-of-Warcraft-Battle-for-Azeroth-Raid)

Uldir Raid Finder - Item Level 340
Uldir Normal - Item Level 355
Uldir Heroic - Item Level 370
Uldir Mythic - Item Level 385

PvP

Unranked (0 – 1399) - Item Level 340 (End of Match), Item Level 355, (Weekly Cache), Item Level 355 (Weekly Azerite Item)
Combatant (1400 – 1599) - Item Level 350 (End of Match), Item Level 365, (Weekly Cache), Item Level 355 (Weekly Azerite Item)
Challenger (1600 – 1799) - Item Level 360 (End of Match), Item Level 370, (Weekly Cache), Item Level 370 (Weekly Azerite Item)
Rival (1800 – 2099) - Item Level 365 (End of Match), Item Level 375, (Weekly Cache), Item Level 370 (Weekly Azerite Item)
Duelist (2100 – 2399) - Item Level 370 (End of Match), Item Level 380, (Weekly Cache), Item Level 385 (Weekly Azerite Item)
Gladiator (2400+) - Item Level 375 (End of Match), Item Level 385, (Weekly Cache), Item Level 385 (Weekly Azerite Item)
