Patch 8.2 Hotfixes - July 25th, 2019
Dungeons and Raids

Azshara’s Eternal Palace

Fixed an issue that could cause players to charge under the world while swimming in the Eternal Palace.
The door to Za'qul no longer opens until nearby enemies have been defeated.
The Queen’s Court

Fixed a bug where sometimes Frenetic Charge https://www.wowdb.com/spells/300550-frenetic-charge would not damage targets in certain situations in all difficulties.

Queen Azshara

Cyranus should no longer throw his Charged Spear https://www.wowdb.com/spells/298425-charged-spear if he is defeated just before throwing it.
Changed the magic school of Charged Spear https://www.wowdb.com/spells/298425-charged-spear from Arcane to Nature for all difficulties.
Reduced the duration of Arcane Vulnerability https://www.wowdb.com/spells/302999-arcane-vulnerability to 5 seconds was 7 seconds on Mythic difficulty.


Mythic Keystone Dungeons

Beguiling Emissaries are no longer affected by Sanguine https://www.wowdb.com/affixes/8-sanguine.
Void Touched Emissaries are now immune to Distract https://www.wowdb.com/spells/1725-distract.



Items

Fixed a bug that caused Relinquished Azerite Spaulders https://www.wowdb.com/items/169337-relinquished-azerite-spaulders tokens to not generate Mantle of Ceremonial Ascension https://www.wowdb.com/items/158344-mantle-of-ceremonial-ascension or Mantle of Fastidious Machinations https://www.wowdb.com/items/158304-mantle-of-fastidious-machinations.
Fixed a bug which could cause Logic Loop of Synergy https://www.wowdb.com/items/169156-logic-loop-of-synergy to not trigger on several healing spells such as Atonement https://www.wowdb.com/spells/81749-atonement or Prayer of Mending https://www.wowdb.com/spells/33076-prayer-of-mending.
Heart of Azeroth

Arcane Heart https://www.wowdb.com/spells/303006-arcane-heart

There is now a confirmation prompt when a Rank 3 Anima of Life and Death Essence is created, as a check to confirm you’re in the intended Loot Specialization. 




Player versus Player

Harbinger's Inscrutable Will https://www.wowdb.com/items/167867-harbingers-inscrutable-will now deals 50% damage against player targets.


Quests

Fixed an issue where the Mechagon Reclamation Rig sometimes did not reset where it was previously completed.


World Quests

Bloodlust and other similar effects should no longer move runes offscreen during "Runelocked Chest https://www.wowdb.com/quests/56022-runelocked-chest".
"Vicemaul https://www.wowdb.com/quests/51508-vicemaul" can now be completed while the world quest "Naga Attack! https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53773-naga-attack" is active in the Timbered Strand in Drustvar.



King Mechagon Soloed by Rextroy
Rextroy https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCf8BuPKoB2xLIlVgqQfZgEw managed to solo King Mechagon in Operation: Mechagon!
