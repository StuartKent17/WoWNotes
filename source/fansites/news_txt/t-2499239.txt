Patch 8.2 Hotfixes - July 25th, 2019
Dungeons and Raids

Azshara’s Eternal Palace

Fixed an issue that could cause players to charge under the world while swimming in the Eternal Palace.
The door to Za'qul no longer opens until nearby enemies have been defeated.
The Queen’s Court

Fixed a bug where sometimes Frenetic Charge  would not damage targets in certain situations in all difficulties.

Queen Azshara

Cyranus should no longer throw his Charged Spear  if he is defeated just before throwing it.
Changed the magic school of Charged Spear  from Arcane to Nature for all difficulties.
Reduced the duration of Arcane Vulnerability  to 5 seconds was 7 seconds on Mythic difficulty.


Mythic Keystone Dungeons

Beguiling Emissaries are no longer affected by Sanguine 
Void Touched Emissaries are now immune to Distract 



Items

Fixed a bug that caused Relinquished Azerite Spaulders  tokens to not generate Mantle of Ceremonial Ascension  or Mantle of Fastidious Machinations 
Fixed a bug which could cause Logic Loop of Synergy  to not trigger on several healing spells such as Atonement  or Prayer of Mending 
Heart of Azeroth

Arcane Heart 

There is now a confirmation prompt when a Rank 3 Anima of Life and Death Essence is created, as a check to confirm you’re in the intended Loot Specialization. 




Player versus Player

Harbinger's Inscrutable Will  now deals 50% damage against player targets.


Quests

Fixed an issue where the Mechagon Reclamation Rig sometimes did not reset where it was previously completed.


World Quests

Bloodlust and other similar effects should no longer move runes offscreen during "Runelocked Chest ".
"Vicemaul " can now be completed while the world quest "Naga Attack! " is active in the Timbered Strand in Drustvar.



King Mechagon Soloed by Rextroy
Rextroy  managed to solo King Mechagon in Operation: Mechagon!
