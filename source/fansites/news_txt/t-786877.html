Enchanting in Cataclysm Preview
It's time for a short preview of what to expect from Enchanting in Cataclysm!

1. Weapon Enchantments
Most of the weapon enchantments in Cataclysm are procs, Mighty Agility (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/95471/enchant-2h-weapon-mighty-agility/) being the only passive stat boost. Also, some of them have very impressive visual effects.
