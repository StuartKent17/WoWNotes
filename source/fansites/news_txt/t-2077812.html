Addon - World Quest Tracker
World Quest Tracker (https://mods.curse.com/addons/wow/world-quest-tracker) is a handy addon that makes seeing what world quests are up easier:


Shows all quests and rewards (including amounts) on the Broken Isles map.
Changes quest icons to the reward icon on individual zones.
Shows world quests on the flight path map, allowing you to show them all or just tracked quests.
Quests that are new since the last time you looked at the map are highlighted.

Patch 7.1 - Trial of Valor: Guarm
Guarm is the second boss in the Trial of Valor raid.


<iframe width="900" height="506" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/3NHWg4HVnzA" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Helya's vicious three-headed hound stands guard at the gates of Helheim. Bred to require neither comfort nor sleep, Guarm remains ever-watchful for any invaders who come to challenge the reign of his cruel mistress.


Overview - Guarm's Guardian's Breath (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/227573-guardians-breath) unleashes three elements: Fire, Nature and Shadow. Guarm gains a stack of Frothing Rage (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/228174-frothing-rage) for every player not hit by one of his breath attacks.

Taking damage from Guardian's Breath (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/227573-guardians-breath) will result in a debuff: Fiery Phlegm (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/227539-fiery-phlegm), Salty Spittle (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/227566-salty-spittle) or Dark Discharge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/227570-dark-discharge). Gaining any two of these is deadly.

Periodically Guarm will become more mobile and aggressive, casting Roaring Leap (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/227883-roaring-leap) andHeadlong Charge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/227816-headlong-charge).

When Guarm casts Guardian's Breath (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/227573-guardians-breath), stand grouped with allies within either Fiery Phlegm (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/227539-fiery-phlegm), Salty Spittle (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/227566-salty-spittle) or Dark Discharge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/227570-dark-discharge). Don't mix the elements!
Move out of Guarm's way when he performs a Headlong Charge (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/227816-headlong-charge).

Do you think Legion or the intended purpose of Legion can fix the problems that Blizzard had in WoD like content drought
I have no insider knowledge or anything. I think the biggest difference is they are saying they are no longer trying to do the expansion a year thing, so hopefully they can put all of their effort into making sure players never go too long without something new.

Even back when I was there, we tried to ship expansions faster, which sometimes meant focusing less on the current expansion in order to shift all resources to the newer one. But then when we missed our dates, it just meant players had those long droughts. I think it's great that rather than being stubborn, the team is trying a different, and very player-focused strategy.
