chaud2019-03-12, 08:28 AMPatch 8.1.5 - Brawler's Guild Updates
Patch 8.1.5 updates the Brawler's Guild for the latest expansion. Enemies of the opposite faction in Zandalar and Kul Tiras now drop invitations, and a new vendor named A. Shady occasionally has them for sale in Orgrimmar and Stormwind

Location: Brawl’gar Arena in Orgrimmar or Bizmo’s Brawlpub in the Deeprun Tram
Entry Requirements: Level 120 and either a Blood-Soaked Invitation or Brawler’s Pass

Brawler's Guild Quest Chain
There is a sizable quest chain that has you solve a murder in the Brawler's Guild, eventually leading to a mount reward!


Bruce https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/166724-bruce - Reward for completing the Brawler's guild quest chain.



https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2019/march/thumb/bruce.jpg https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2019/march/bruce.jpg
https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/june/mvbar900.jpg


This quest starts off with finding Quackenbush https://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/68363-quackenbush dead on the Brawler's Guild floor. 
Inspecting the scene starts off the quest Murder at the Brawlpub https://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/55002-murder-at-the-brawlpub which eventually has you meet another investigator who gives you the followup quest Put Up Your Dukes https://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/55003-put-up-your-dukes.
The first suspect starts off simple enough but eventually as you progress the suspects will start to have ties to the Old Gods. Quest chains usually involve interacting with suspects and completing brawls or tasks around the brawl arena to progress the chain. You sometimes have to fight specific bosses using cards such as Challenge Card: Lord Sylysthrel https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/164935-challenge-card-lord-sylysthrel.
Eventually, you will take part in a trial during the quest The Brawlpub Trial https://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/55020-the-brawlpub-trial. 
Events take a sudden turn and eventually you discover that Bizmo the owner of the Brawler's Guild is actually in Area 52 and the one at the guild is an ethereal in disguise! He gives you Mysterious Challenge Card https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/164936-mysterious-challenge-card that gives you access to fight the imposter etheral, Xan-Sallish https://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/145782-xan-sallish, who is the murderer and is working for the ethereal Shadreen https://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/133198-shadreen!
