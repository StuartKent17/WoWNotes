Starcraft 2 Commemorative Bundle
In appreciation for John "Totalbiscuit" Bain and his family's contributions to Starcraft 2, Blizzard is releasing a commemorative bundle (https://starcraft2.com/en-us/news/22481041) of cosmetics for the game. 100% of the profits will go to the Bain family as well as from any future sales of John's Announcer Pack. The bundle will be available this week and is a limited time item, becoming unavailable once it sells out or by December 31, 2018.
