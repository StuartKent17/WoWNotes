Patch 4.3.2 Hotfixes - February 23
General

A completed character race change should no longer clear your active quests.
Multiple hunters attacking the same target should now see the proper benefit from Noxious Stings.


Dungeons and Raids

All players identified as tanks in Raid Finder are now automatically labeled as assistant leaders.
Firelands

Lava Wielder's Lava attack should now hurt players in the area of the visual effect.


Items

The Blast of Corruption effect from Rathrak, the Poisonous Mind will no longer hit creatures that are very far away when used with the Fire Nova ability.
Ruthless Gladiator's Footguards of Alacrity (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/70358/ruthless-gladiators-footguards-of-alacrity/), Ruthless Gladiator's Boots of Cruelty (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/70348/ruthless-gladiators-boots-of-cruelty/), and Ruthless Gladiator's Boots of Alacrity (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/70351/ruthless-gladiators-boots-of-alacrity/) have received a +25 Stamina increase.
Cataclysmic Gladiator's Armwraps of Accuracy (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/73528/cataclysmic-gladiators-armwraps-of-accuracy/) and Cataclysmic Gladiator's Armwraps of Alacrity (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/73529/cataclysmic-gladiators-armwraps-of-alacrity/) have received a +22 Stamina increase.
Cataclysmic Gladiator's Waistband of Accuracy (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/73532/cataclysmic-gladiators-waistband-of-accuracy/), Cataclysmic Gladiator's Waistband of Cruelty (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/73533/cataclysmic-gladiators-waistband-of-cruelty/), Cataclysmic Gladiator's Boots of Alacrity (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/73683/cataclysmic-gladiators-boots-of-alacrity/), Cataclysmic Gladiator's Boots of Cruelty (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/73684/cataclysmic-gladiators-boots-of-cruelty/), and Cataclysmic Gladiator's Footguards of Alacrity (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/73726/cataclysmic-gladiators-footguards-of-alacrity/) have received a +20 Stamina increase.


Quests and Creatures

The quest Heeding the Call (http://db.mmo-champion.com/search/all/Heeding_the_Call/) is no longer available to players.



Blue Posts
Ressing in Arena
Resurrections in arena is something that has been around since it was implemented. You have 10 seconds, excluding haste, to reach your target and interrupt or bring them into combat. If they are able to gain those precious few moments out of combat then I can only congratulate them on some very solid play. I recommend that you do your utmost to stay in range of your enemy and prevent them from casting their resurrection spell. (Blue Tracker (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/217948-ressing-in-arena/) / Official Forums (http://eu.battle.net/wow/en/forum/topic/3389170953?page=1#14))


PayPal Now Available on European Battle.net
We’re pleased to announce that PayPal is now available to use as a Battle.net payment option here in Europe.

This means that you can now sync up your existing PayPal account with your Battle.net account, in order to purchase Blizzard Entertainment products and services directly through Battle.net.

You can currently use the PayPal payment option to purchase World of Warcraft paid services and digital versions of select Blizzard Entertainment games, including Diablo II, StarCraft II and World of Warcraft expansions.

For more information on Europe’s new PayPal payment option, please check out our PayPal FAQ (http://eu.battle.net/support/article/paypal-faq).
