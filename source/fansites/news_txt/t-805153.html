Cataclysm is going live THIS WEEK!
Cataclysm is going live this week! Players will finally be able to explore the new regions, dungeons, raids, fly in Azeroth, learn Archaeology, and do lot of awesome stuff! Thanks to Simca (http://www.mmo-champion.com/members/545200), Marlamin (http://www.mmo-champion.com/members/622014), Elyaan (http://www.mmo-champion.com/members/573739-Elyaan) and Vjeux who pretty much saved my ass and helped me finish this news post on time.

New Dungeons
7 New dungeons have been added to the game: Blackrock Caverns (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1055-Blackrock-Caverns), Grim Batol (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1608-Grim-Batol), Halls of Origination (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1060-Halls-of-Origination), The Stonecore (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1057-The-Stonecore), Throne of the Tides (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1062-Throne-of-Tides), Vortex Pinnacle (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1061-Vortex-Pinnacle), Lost City of the Tol'vir (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/595-Lost-City-of-the-Tol-vir). Also, two old dungeons have been fully revamped and are now available in a new lvl 85 heroic version: The Deadmines (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/627-The-Deadmines-Cataclysm) and Shadowfang Keep (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/605-Shadowfang-Keep-Cataclysm).
