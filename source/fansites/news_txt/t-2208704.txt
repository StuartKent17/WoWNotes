Heroes of the Storm is getting a big update, including a new progression system, Loot Chests, a new Battleground, new heroes, and more. Check out the Heroes of the Storm wiki http://heroesofthestorm.gamepedia.com/Heroes_of_the_Storm_Wiki and our launch guide http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2208631-Heroes-2-0-Launch-Guide. 


Progression - Player Level and Hero Level caps removed. Leveling now rewards gems, chests, and more. 
Loot Chests - Purchase or earn loot chests that can contain all of the things you used to buy with gold and other new cosmetics. 
Mega Hero Bundles - Log in from launch to May 22, 2017 to earn enough gems to purchase one of the new Mega Hero Bundles. 
New Map - Hanamura http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2203970-Hanamura-map is the new map, adding a payload mechanic to Heroes!
New Heroes - Genji http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2203961-Genji-Ranged-Assassin-Overwatch-Hero and D.Va http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2208733-D-Va-Unknown-Warrior-Overwatch-Hero?p=45497071#post45497071 are the new heroes, jumping into the Nexus right from Overwatch. 
Nexus Challenge 2.0 - Play 5 games with a friend http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2208735-Nexus-Challenge-2-0 each week below to earn rewards:

Week 1 April 25 - Earn Oni Genji Skin, Portrait, and Spray in Overwatch and Orochi Hovercycle Mount, Oni Genji Banner, Portrait, and Spray in Heroes.
Week 2 May 2 - Officer D.Va Skin in Overwatch and Busan Police Hovercycle Mount in Heroes.
Week 3 May 9 - Officer D.Va Portrait and Spray in Overwatch and Overwatch Nexagon Mount, Officer D.Va Banner, Portrait, and Spray in Heroes. 
Week 4 May 16 - 10 Loot Boxes in Overwatch and 10 Loot Chests including 1 Rare in Heroes.
