Boss info
Health - 13M (25-Man) / 4M (Normal)

During the latest PTR testing phase, the boss was fairly bugged and there isn't much we know about it. Razorscale fly over the area and players starts the event by talking to the dwarves here. As soon as the event starts Dwarves will start repairing the turrets here while the players deal with the trash.

Once the turrets are fixed, the dwarves use them to bring Razorscale down to let players engage her. She uses the following abilities:
Wing Buffet (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=62666) - Knocks back enemies in a 35 yards radius around the caster.
Flame Breath (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64021) - Inflicts 17500 to 22500 Fire damage to enemies in a cone in front of the caster.
Flame Buffet (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64023) - Increases the Fire damage an enemy takes by 1500 for 1 min.
Devouring Flame (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=63816) - Spits a Lava Bomb at an enemy, inflicting 8788 to 10212 Fire damage and deals 8788 to 10212 Fire damage every second to anyone staying within 6 yards of the explosion. (25 seconds duration)
