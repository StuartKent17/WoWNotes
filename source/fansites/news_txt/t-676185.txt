Gunship Airbattle US PTR Testing
The Gunship Airbattle will be available for testing on US PTRs tonight. A list of abilities will be available shortly! 

The goal of this encounter is to board the enemy gunship and reach the top of the citadel before the other faction. You will be helped by the leader of your faction and will be able to control turrets and board the enemy ship to take down the enemy crew.
