Patch 5.4 - Darkmoon Faire Updates
The Darkmoon Faire is back today and with it comes Moonfang (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/71992-moonfang), a new boss. Moonfang has a low respawn time, can tag be tagged by anyone, and will give you an extra 10 tickets (http://www.wowdb.com/items/105891-moonfangs-pelt#related:starts) on your first kill. Be sure to enable the WoWDB addon (http://www.curse.com/addons/wow/wowdb-profiler) to help us get drop rates faster!

Hundreds of constant minor tweaks erode class diversity. Ppl would complain less frequently if you changed less.

By making them poor. Any spell for which the game chooses the targets for you should lose to smart player decisions
