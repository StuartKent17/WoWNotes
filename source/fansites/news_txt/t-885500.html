Patch 4.1 - PTR Build 13860
A new build will be deployed on test realms today! This build is a Release Candidate (marked as "Release") and it means that the patch will hit the live servers within the next few weeks. (As I said, last week of April/first week of May is very likely)

Professions

Master Crafter (http://db.mmo-champion.com/achievement/5035/#13850/13860) now requires the guild to 500 epic items, down from 1,500.
We're Gonna Need A Bigger Boat (http://db.mmo-champion.com/achievement/5274/#13850/13860) now requires the guild to fish 25,000 fish, down from 50,000.
The Pen is Mightier (http://db.mmo-champion.com/achievement/5457/#13850/13860) now requires the guild to create 2,500 Glyphs, down from 25,000.
