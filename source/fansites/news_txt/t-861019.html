Update Patch 4.1 PTR - Official Notes Update
The Patch 4.1 PTR Notes (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/160430/patch-41-public-test-realm-notes-updated-march-1) have been updated once again with the 2 missing changes from build 13698.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/278-Warrior) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..warrior) / Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1676-Warrior-Cataclysm))
Arms

Strikes of Opportunity damage has been increased by 10% to keep stat parity with other strike changes.


Fury

Unshackled Fury now only grants 2 base points of mastery, down from 8. This change offsets the Flurry boost to better control Fury burst damage.



Patch 4.1 on PTRs - Build 13698
A new build will be deployed on test realms today. Most of these changes were already documented in the latest PTR notes update (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/2193-Patch-4.1-Notes-February-28-Update) but keep an eye out for new things!

Achievements Changes
Dungeons & Raids
Cataclysm Dungeon


Spirit Twister (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/5759/spirit-twister/#13682/13698) Defeat 20 Twisted Spirits within 15 seconds during the Jin'do encounter in Zul'Gurub on Heroic difficulty.
Ring out! (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/5760/ring-out/#13682/13698) Defeat Daakara in Zul'Aman on Heroic difficulty without leaving the rectangular turquoise stonework inscribed on the floor in the center of his area.



Reputation


Ambassador of the Horde (http://db.mmo-champion.com/achievement/762/#13682/13698) now requires you to be exalted with Bilgewater Cartel.
Ambassador of the Alliance (http://db.mmo-champion.com/achievement/948/#13682/13698) now requires you to be exalted with Gilneas.

Protection


Spell Reflection (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/23920/spell-reflection/#13682/13698) now has a 25 sec cooldown, up from 10 sec.
Gag Order (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/12958/gag-order/#13682/13698) no longer affects Shield Bash.
Shield Mastery (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/84608/shield-mastery/#13682/13698) no longer reduces the cooldown of Spell Reflect. Now causes your Shield Block to also reduce magic damage taken by 8/16/25%.


Fury


Unshackled Fury (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/76856/unshackled-fury/#13682/13698) now Increases the benefit of abilities that cause or require you to be enraged by 11.2%, down from 44,8%.
Pummel (http://db.mmo-champion.com/spell/6552/#13682/13698) no longer requires Battle or Berserker Stance.


Arms


Strikes of Opportunity (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/76838/strikes-of-opportunity/#13682/13698) now gives a 17.6% (up from 16%) chance for your melee attacks to instantly trigger an additional melee attack for 100% normal damage. Each point of Mastery increases this chance by 2.2%. (Up from 2%)
Drums of War (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/12963/drums-of-war/#13682/13698) no longer affects Shield Bash, now affects Demoralizing Shout, Intimidating Shout and Challenging Shout.
Juggernaut (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/64976/juggernaut/#13682/13698) now reduces the cooldown of your Charge by 2 sec, down from 3 sec.


Glyph


Inscription: Glyph of Spell Reflection (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/64302/glyph-of-spell-reflection/#13682/13698) now reduces the cooldown on Spell Reflection by 5 sec, up from 1 sec.
