Patch 3.3 - PTR Build 10623 Items
A few items were added to the latest PTR build, we didn't see a lot of them for the moment but hey ... better than nothing.

Crafted Items
A few crafted items introduced with Patch 3.3 finally have some stats! They're ilvl 264 and patterns can be bought with Primordial Saronite (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49908/primordial-saronite/) from The Ashen Verdict (http://db.mmo-champion.com/f/1156/the-ashen-verdict/).

Pattern: Draconic Bonesplinter Legguards (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49965/pattern-draconic-bonesplinter-legguards/)
Pattern: Lightning-infused Leggings (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49962/pattern-lightning-infused-leggings/)
Plans: Legplates of Painful Death (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49971/plans-legplates-of-painful-death/)
Plans: Pillars of Might (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49973/plans-pillars-of-might/)
Plans: Puresteel Legplates (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49969/plans-puresteel-legplates/)

Inscription Techniques
Blizzard finally introduced Inscription patterns and you won't have to run your Northrend Inscription Research for the 3094309th time. For the moment, the 3 new glyphs recipes are sold by the Inscription Supplies NPC in Dalaran.

Technique: Glyph of Eternal Water (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/50166/technique-glyph-of-eternal-water/)
Technique: Glyph of Rapid Rejuvenation (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/50167/technique-glyph-of-rapid-rejuvenation/)
Technique: Glyph of Quick Decay (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/50168/technique-glyph-of-quick-decay/)
