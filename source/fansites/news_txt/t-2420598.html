Battle for Azeroth Island Expeditions Guide
Battle for Azeroth brings a new Island Expeditions feature. Taking what the team liked from Mists of Pandaria's scenarios and stepping it up, these expeditions take a team of three players to uncharted islands across the Great Sea in search of Azerite against advanced AI opponents.


The first team to collect a certain amount of Azerite wins.
Each island is randomly generated and so there is always something unique and new to find.
The enemy AI does not behave like normal scripted creature AI. They will fight in a much more unpredictable way.
Island Expeditions are role agnostic, meaning that they do not require a Tank, Healer and DPS in order to succeed.
Difficulties include Normal, Heroic and Mythic.
There is also a PvP mode where the enemy AI is replaced with 3 players of the opposite faction.

How to Unlock Island Expeditions
You will unlock the ability to queue for Island Expeditions by progressing your War Campaign. At one point you will receive a quest that places you in a solo scenario on a small island and has you go through the ropes of what a basic Island Expedition entails. Completing this will unlock the ability to queue for Island Expeditions on all characters on your account provided they are 110 or above.


Where to Queue for Unlock Island Expeditions
Once you unlock Island expeditions you can queue for them at the Expedition Map in the following locations:


Alliance - Boralus Harbor
Horde - Port of Zandalar

Island Expedition Difficulty Modes
Island Expeditions have 4 difficulty modes:


Normal - Collect 6,000 Azerite.
Heroic - Collect 9,000 Azerite. Requires item level 295.
Mythic - Collect 12,000 Azerite. Requires item level 315.
PvP - Requires item level 295 and a group of three to queue.


Replayability of Island Expeditions
Islands are a unique experience every time you queue up for one. There are countless different themes, enemies and environments you can come across and it's random each time you start one. There will also be different puzzles and obstacles to face every time you visit.

Island Expeditions UI
When you click on the map you will see your weekly Azerite quest progress as well as the three active Islands for the week. A drawing next to the island maps will showcase what type of enemy is found on that island. You can also select the difficulty before queuing up. Mousing over the difficulty will show you the reward for completion.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/july/thumb/islandsque.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/june/islandsque.jpg)


Map
The Island map behaves like most other games with Fog of War. Things that are close enough to see for one of your group members are updated on the map. It displays various objectives on the island including Azerite node locations, events, quests and the location of the enemy team. There is also an Azerite Detector timer that counts down and when it runs out, all sources of Azerite will be displayed on the map.


Blue diamond shapes mark an Azerite source.
Skulls mark a mini-boss that rewards Azerite.
X symbols mark an Azerite source in an area that hasn't been explored yet.
Faction symbols mark the enemy faction players on the map.
Both factions have a ship icon that mark the initial spawn point.

Enemy AI
Battle for Azeroth will feature new NPC AI for Island Expeditions. This AI is not scripted and will not behave in the way a normal creature usually does. This new AI will behave more like actual players taking part in PvP would. Examples include polymorphing or sapping your healer while you are engaged in a boss fight. This AI also is well aware of important map objectives and will constantly be giving you a run for your money as you race to complete objectives. This AI can make strategic decisions as well. They may stop what they are doing to come disrupt the objective you are working on or to steal your Azerite. Different opponents will also have distinct personalities and unique behaviors. An example would be Sneaky Pete, a rogue who likes to disrupt healers.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/july/thumb/islandai.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2018/july/islandexpeditions03.jpg)


Island Buffs
There are several buffs that can be found on islands:


Azerite Energy (https://www.wowdb.com/spells/265703-azerite-energy) - These globes come from more powerful enemies on the island and are needed for Give Me The Energy (https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/13126-give-me-the-energy)
Shrines - Similar to the shrines on Timeless Isle, you can find these across the islands, allowing for a single player to utilize the buff.
Other Items - You can find other buff items around the island that give you a one time use ability or food that heals you.



Island Consumables
A seafaring vendor shows up at the docks each day to provide unique buffs and consumables to use on the island at the cost of Seafarer's Dubloons (https://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1710-seafarers-dubloon) earned from expeditions. These power ups tend to fill in role gaps. Examples include buffs to make an Arms http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior more tanky or another consumable that allows a http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/images.gif Mage to heal.

Island Expeditions are going to be the most lucrative form of gaining Azerite power. While the Azerite you gain during the match goes towards your faction, you receive some for yourself in many different ways.


Once a week you may complete Azerite for the Horde (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53435-azerite-for-the-horde) or Azerite for the Alliance (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53436-azerite-for-the-alliance) for a hefty chunk of Azerite.
A winning bonus that is more lucrative based on the difficulty.
Rare quest item rewards from drops such as Unscarred Black Scale (https://www.wowdb.com/items/163477-unscarred-black-scale) during an expedition.
PvP mode also rewards 50 conquest points in addition to Azerite.
