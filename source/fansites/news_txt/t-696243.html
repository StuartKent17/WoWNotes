World First Glory of the Icecrown Raider (10) by Exorsus
A few hours ago, the russian guild Экзорсус (http://exorsus.ru/) (EU-Ревущий фьорд) succesfully completed all the achievements required for Glory of the Icecrown Raider (10 Player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4602/glory-of-the-icecrown-raider-10-player/) and were rewarded with brand new Bloodbathed Frostbrood Vanquisher (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/72808/bloodbathed-frostbrood-vanquisher/) 310% Flying mounts. Congratulations to them!
