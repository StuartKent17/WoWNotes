Blizzard Entertainment is inviting the top four World of Warcraft 3v3 teams in the U.S. to compete professionally for a grand cash prize of $10,000. Each member of the winning team will also receive the recently announced XPS M1730 World of Warcraft Edition notebook. Dell's four team 3v3 invite tournament will begin at 2 PM PST.

That tournament will be a best-of-five round-robin tournament.
