World of Warcraft's 5th Anniversary
World of Warcraft was released on November 23, 2004 and today was the beginning of the 5th anniversary in-game. Anyone logging in game during the next few days will get the WoW's 5th Anniversary (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4400/wows-5th-anniversary/) achievement and a cute Onyxian Whelpling (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49362/onyxian-whelpling/)!
