US PTR Testing - Festergut
Festergut, one of the 3 bosses of the Plague wing of Icecrown Citadel will be available for testing on US PTRs tonight, starting 04:00PM PDT. Stay tuned for more information, streams, and all the funny stuff. (Note that only non-heroic modes are available for testing, it probably won't look really hard and it is intended)
