chaud2019-01-24, 11:36 PMGlory of the Dazar'alor Raider World First
Glory of the Dazar'alor Raider https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/13315-glory-of-the-dazaralor-raider has been completed by the Achievements Discord https://discord.gg/achievements! The achievement rewards the Dazar'alor Windreaver https://www.wowdb.com/items/166539-dazaralor-windreaver mount, one of the three pterrordax mounts available in Battle of Azeroth.

If you're looking for a group to complete the achievement and get the Dazar'alor Windreaver https://www.wowdb.com/items/166539-dazaralor-windreaver mount, then you can join the Achievements Discord https://discord.gg/achievements! You can also opt into roles to receive notifications of achievement runs, which makes forming groups much easier.
