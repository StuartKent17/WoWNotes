chaud2019-01-24, 11:36 PMGlory of the Dazar'alor Raider World First
Glory of the Dazar'alor Raider  has been completed by the Achievements Discord ! The achievement rewards the Dazar'alor Windreaver  mount, one of the three pterrordax mounts available in Battle of Azeroth.

If you're looking for a group to complete the achievement and get the Dazar'alor Windreaver  mount, then you can join the Achievements Discord ! You can also opt into roles to receive notifications of achievement runs, which makes forming groups much easier.
