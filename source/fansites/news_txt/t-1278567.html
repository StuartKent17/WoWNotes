Hearthstone - WoW TCG Game Announced
Blizzard just announced Hearthstone, the online Free to Play WoW TCG Game! More details to come. You can start talking about it in our new Hearthstone forum (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/344-Hearthstone-WoW-TCG-Online-Game)!


Over 300 cards with the initial launch
Earn card packs by playing, or purchase them
Basic and Expert cards
Disenchant cards you don't want to receive dust, which is used to craft new cards.
