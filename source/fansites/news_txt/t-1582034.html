Rotation Depth and Talents
We’ve changed the combat rotations of each Hunter specialization (primarily through Ability Pruning) with the goal of them being a platform to build upon with talents. To facilitate this, we’ve modified several talents to add additional rotational depth. Hunters had many talent options which added abilities to their rotation, but most of them had quite simple usage, with little interaction with your rotation or other abilities. Now, there should be more of a variety; still some simple talents, but also some talents that create a more engaging combat rotation.

Barrage (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/120360) now has a 20 second cooldown (down from 30 seconds), costs 60 Focus (up from 30 Focus), and its damage has been increased by 100%.
Murder of Crows has been changed. Its duration, cost, and cooldown have been reduced by 50%. It no longer has a shorter cooldown when used on low health targets. Instead, its cooldown is reset if the target dies.
Fervor (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/82726) has been replaced with a new passive ability, Steady Focus (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/53224).

Steady Focus (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/53224) increases Focus Regeneration by 50% for 10 seconds after using Cobra Shot (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/77767) or Steady Shot (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/56641) twice in a row, or after using Focusing Shot.


Survival Changes
Apart from rotational changes through Ability Pruning, changes for Survival Hunters mostly center on Lock and Load (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/56343). We changed how Lock and Load (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/56343) is triggered to increase the value of Multistrike, provide gameplay depth and the ability to pool damage more. Also note the new passive ability, Survivalist, which increases multistrike chance by 10%, in order to give Survival Hunters some baseline Multistrike chance.

(Line moved to Ability Consolidation and Refinement.) Black Arrow (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/3674) now deals 60% more damage, inflicts Shadow damage every 3 seconds (up from every 2 seconds), has a duration of 18 seconds (down from 20 seconds), and triggers Lock and Load (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/56343) on Multistrike hits.
(Line moved to Ability Consolidation and Refinement) Lock and Load (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/56343) effects have been incorporated into Black Arrow (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/3674) and have been redesigned.

Lock and Load (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/56343) now causes the next Explosive Shot (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/53301) to not trigger its cooldown, but no longer causes it to be free or reset the ability's current cooldown. This effect can stack up to 5 times.

Survivalist is a new passive ability learned by Survival Hunters at level 10.

Survivalist increases Multistrike damage by 20% the chance to Multistrike by 10%, and causes the Hunter to gain 15% health over 10 seconds after killing a target.

Raid Utility

Healthstone's cooldown will now not reset until the player leaves combat. Healing from this ability is now a flat amount (instead of a % of maximum health) and can no longer be a Critical Effect.


Affliction Changes

Haunt (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/48181) now increases periodic damage by 30% (down from 35%).


Demonology Changes
We also tweaked the design of Master Demonologist, in order to solve some edge cases, and simplify and clarify its effects.

Mastery: Master Demonologist (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/77219) has been slightly changed. It now passively increases all damage by 6% regardless of form. While in Metamorphosis (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/103958), it further increases the damage of Touch of Chaos (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/112089), Chaos Wave, Doom, Immolation Aura, and Soul Fire (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/6353) while by 12%.

Seasoned Soldier (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/12712) now increases damage by 10% (down from 15%).


Talent Changes

Staggering Shout (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/107566) has been removed and replaced with 3 new talents that vary by specialization.

Protection: Heavy Repercussions: Shield Slam (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/23922) deals 50% additional damage while Shield Block (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/2565) or Shield Charge is active.


Enrage and Deep Wounds
With the introduction of one of the level-100 Warrior Talents, which deals Fire damage, We also expanded Enrage (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/13046) to affect all damage, not just Physical damage, and increased its duration slightly so that it can cover burst windows like Colossus Smash (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/86346) adequately. We're also making a change to the design of Deep Wounds (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/115768), to try to limit is effectiveness in PvP, without hurting its effectiveness in PvE.
