chaud2016-07-17, 12:42 AMPatch 7.0.3 Live This Week
Patch 7.0.3 goes live this week, but some of the content is not going to be available until a later date. To avoid confusion, we are only covering the content that is live for the patch release. We will recap the rest of the content closer to it being live.


What is Launching This Week?
Lots of things! The bigger changes are listed in sections below, but there are also smaller things:


One more character slot has been added. 
Many shirts are becoming Bind on Equip , so be sure to unequip them if you don't want them to be bound to you.
Many items have been converted to toys. 
Classes that can use Mail and Plate no longer graduate to a higher armor level, they just start with the ability to use their armor type.
All creatures and NPCs can now be tapped by up to 5 other characters.
The Stranglethorn Fishing Extravaganza has taken a temporary hiatus.
Set bonuses for item sets from expansions prior to Warlords of Draenor have been deactivated, though the text describing their former function still remains.
Draenor Perks have been removed from the game.
The Mass Resurrection  guild perk has been removed.



What's Not Coming This Week?
The events leading up to the launch of the expansion will be released throughout the coming weeks.


All of the Legion content: leveling, the Broken Isles, order halls, dungeons, raids, Legion items, and artifact weapons.
Burning Legion Invasions  and Demon Hunter Early Access Launching around mid August, no later than August 17
The Battle on the Broken Shore
A new PvP Season.
PvP Honor Talents  except for Gladiator's Medallion

Stat, Talent, and Glyph Changes
Major Glyphs are gone and the rules for swapping talents have changed: 

Stats

Multistrike, Spirit, and Bonus Armor have been removed. 
Spell Power has been removed from weapons and converted into Intellect. 


Talents

Dual spec is gone! You can swap between all of your specs now. 
Changing specs will be free, and change your actions bars and optionally transmog. 
Changing talents will be free in a city or your Order Hall.
Changing talents out in the field will require a reagent crafted by Inscription. 
Group Reagent: Codex of the Clear Mind 
Solo Reagent: Tome of the Clear Mind 


Glyphs

The Glyph UI and Major Glyphs are going away Charred Glyph  
Minor Glyphs will be applied directly to spells now and consumed on use. 



Class Trials
Legion allows you to try a class before boosting it with a short tutorial and the artifact acquisition quests.


  


Updated Class Models
Druids received some form updates and Warlocks have new demon models.


       

        


Transmogrification and Wardrobe Changes
Blizzard has a nice overview of the Wardrobe system here  


You can now hide your shoulders!
You can transmog weapon enchants, shirts, and tabards. 
You need to visit a Transmogrification NPC to hide your cloak, helm, or shoulders now. 
Old quest rewards and items you already have should be automatically added to your wardrobe when the patch is live. 
If one color of an appearance comes from multiple items, you just need to collect one of them.
To collect an appearance, you need to be able to equip the item, it must be your primary armor type, and it must be obtainable and equippable by your class. 
Don't forget to grab Arsenal: Draenor Challenger's Armaments  if you are eligible. 



  

  


PvP Changes
The PvP system received a large overhaul for Legion, allowing for easier and better balancing. 


Honor Points and Conquest Points were removed. 
Items now cost Mark of Honor , obtained from Battlegrounds, Arenas, and Skirmishes.
The 5v5 Arena bracket has been retired. "Arena Master " achievement has been converted into a Feat of Strength.
Winning a Battleground with characters below level 110 now awards a Crate of Battlefield Goods  Players on the opposing team also have a chance to receive a crate with an increasing chance based on team score at the end of a match. The crate contains a piece of armor appropriate for the characterâ€™s level and specialization with a chance for the crate to contain additional rewards such as weapons or relics.
PvP stat templates  are active.



UI Changes
Lots of changes are coming to the UI, so we have compiled them in a separate post. 


  


Dungeon and Raid Changes
There are a few changes coming to Hellfire Citadel, but the mounts and heirloom trinkets will be around for a little longer.:


Tuning is ongoing  to ensure that Hellfire Citadel remains slightly easier than it was before the patch. 
Many of the trinkets have been nerfed. 
Loot from Normal difficulty and higher is part of the new upgrade system, with a chance to be randomly upgraded some multiple of 5 item levels. 
You can still obtain the Grove Warden , Ironhoof Destroyer , and Felsteel Annihilator , they change with the expansion launch.
You can still obtain the heirloom trinkets  that scale to 110 from Mythic dungeons until the expansion launches.
The ability to start the legendary quest line has been removed, but you can keep working on it if you already are on the quest.
Normalized gold from Legacy raids prior to Warlords of Draenor. Heroic difficulty Cataclysm Raids now award less gold than before, while raids from Mists of Pandaria and Wrath of the Lich King will now award more gold.



Gold and Garrison Changes
A few changes are coming to Garrisons in this patch:


Some Garrison follower upgrades such as Goredrenched Weaponry  now sell for significantly less. 
Treasure Hunter  is replaced by Extreme Scavenger 
The especially rewarding missions that reward gold, such as Blood, Gold, and Regret  are being changed to reward something else, such as Apexis Crystals, Oil, Primal Spirit , or other non-gold rewards.
Felblight  is now sold at the Primal Trader in your garrison for 25 x Primal Spirit 
Big Crate of Salvage  will no longer be rewarded, existing crates will be unaffected. You can still get transmog gear 
Large Crate of Salvage  replaces the old crate. 
The number of Pet Charms  rewarded by quests have been halved. 
The Garrison Resource cost of many items on the Trader NPC at the Trading Post have been increased. 
The amount of Garrison Resources rewarded by the random dungeon quest has been reduced. 
The maximum amount of gold that can be held at once has been increased to 9,999,999 gold. Guild bank maximum has also been increased to 9,999,999 gold.
Potion of Luck  and Greater Potion of Luck  no longer have an effect.



Professions

WoWDB Addon Data Collectionwowdbaddons
Quickly populating the database with new additions is done with the help of many players. The more people that contribute data, the more accurate and quickly we can update the database. 

To help out, just click here curse://enable-wowdb-upload if you have Curse Client already installed. 

When you are playing the addon will gather data and save it. When you are done playing, Curse Client will see the game close and upload the collected data from the addon.
