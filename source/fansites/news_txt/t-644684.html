Hodir Hard Mode Kill by Inner Sanctum
The buff to hard mode encounters (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?topic=50576.0) didn't scare Inner Sanctum (http://www.innersanctum.eu/). Hodir was killed in Hard Mode tonight at 02:35AM CET!


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/april/hodiris.jpg (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?topic=50705)

Loot
Nothing new here sadly, the 2 epic items dropped by Hodir were discovered on PTRs a few weeks ago. The raid also got a Fragment of Val'anyr (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=855) and 2 x Tier 8 Breastplate Tokens (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=852)
