Patch 8.1.5 Hotfixes - March 21st, 2019
Achievements

The Dinner Impossible https://www.wowdb.com/achievements/1785-dinner-impossible is now again possible to achieve in Arathi Basin.


Allied Races

Fixed an issue that prevented Kul Tiran characters from using the ferry system in Boralus if they skipped the Battle for Lordaeron scenario or used a Level 110 Character Boost.


Brawler's Guild

Players who purchase a Rumble Card should now be automatically added to the queue for the upcoming rumble.
Fixed an issue that prevented players from receiving credit for "Solving the Mystery https://www.wowdb.com/quests/53935-solving-the-mystery" after killing a mystery boss.
Fixed an issue that prevented Mindbreaker Gzzaj https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/117102-mindbreaker-gzzaj from dealing damage to Alliance players when casting Mind Break https://www.wowdb.com/spells/233212-mind-break.
Fixed a bug that prevented players from being awarded victory if a player defeated Ro-Shambo https://www.wowdb.com/npcs/70749-ro-shambo playing the Rock, Paper, Scissors game.
