Boubouille2009-01-15, 01:21 AMUpdate - Almost forgot to mention that the Talent Calculator  is now updated with the latest build from the Patch 3.0.8 PTRs. Glyphs will be updated shortly as well. 

PTR 3.0.8 - Build 9464
The test realms have been updated to a new build. It doesn't look like there is a lot of changes here but some of them are really interesting. 

New installer and launcher interfaces
The patch installer  and launcher  have been updated with a new interface.


   

 

Undocumented Changes
Undocumented changes are merely comparison of tooltips between the new version and the previous one. Changes made to spells aren't always reflected in tooltips and vice versa. Keep in mind that it's a test server and things might changes before it hits the live servers.
Items
Libram of Reciprocation  now has a chance to increase critical rating. Old - Haste rating

Enchanting
Enchant Weapon - Lifeward  - Permanently enchant a melee weapon to sometimes heal the wielder when striking in melee. Old - healed for 140 to 160, it might just be a tooltip change and the healing could be the same as before
Enchant Weapon - Titanguard  Permanently enchant a melee weapon to increase Stamina by 50. Down from 75

Engineering
Scrapbot  now let you repair your equipment.

 Death Knight Skills List  / Talent + Glyph Calc. 
Blood
Vampiric Blood  now Temporarily grants the Death Knight 15% down from 20% of maximum health and increases the amount of health generated through spells and effects by 35% down from 50% for 20 sec. After the effect expires, the health is lost.

 Druid Skills List  / Talent + Glyph Calc. 
Balance
Celestial Focus  doesn't affect Starfall anymore. 

 Hunter Skills List  / Talent + Glyph Calc. 
Survival
Lock and Load  now has a 3/7/10% chance to proc on periodic damage. Up from 2/4/6%
Tranquilizing Shot  cooldown has been increased from 6 to 8 seconds. Mana cost decreased from 12% of base mana to 8%.

 Paladin Skills List  / Talent + Glyph Calc. 
Glyph
Glyph of Turn Evil  now also increases the cooldown of the spell by 8 sec.

 Rogue Skills List  / Talent + Glyph Calc. 
Assassination
Ambush  now awards 2 combo points. Up from 1
Lethality  now only increase the damage of of all combo moves that do not require stealth.
