Freya 10-Man Hard Mode Zerged
Some of you probably remember the video of Sartharion 10-Man killed with 3 Drakes in 76 Seconds (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?topic=47540.0). Something Diabolical (http://www.somethingdiabolical.org/) (US-Fizzcrank) decided to try the same strategy on Freya (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?topic=64626.0) and succesfully killed her just by zerging the encounter with 150 stacks of Attuned to Nature.
