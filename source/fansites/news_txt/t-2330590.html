Blizzcon 2017 - Day 1 Recap
Saturday starts off with the CodeCraft - Under the Hood with Blizzard Engineering panel at 10 AM PST / 1 PM EST, followed by DesignCraft - Building Blocks of Level Design panel at 11:30 AM PST / 2:30 PM PST.

The Art of World of Warcraft panel is at 12:45 PM PST / 3:45 PM EST and The World of Warcraft - Live Q&A is at 4 PM PST / 7 PM EST. Muse will close out the show!

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/july/wowmini.png" style="margin-bottom:-2px; margin-right:0px; vertical-align:bottom" /> World of Warcraft
A new expansion (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7117-BlizzCon-2017-World-of-Warcraft-What-s-Next) and Classic servers were announced! Submit your questions now (http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2330249-Submit-Questions-for-the-Developers) for the live Q & A tomorrow!


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/submitQuestion.jpg (http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/2330249-Submit-Questions-for-the-Developers)


World of Warcraft: Classic Servers (https://youtu.be/TcZyiYOzsSw) announced.
World of Warcraft: Battle for Azeroth (https://worldofwarcraft.com/battleforazeroth) is the next expansion.
Allied Races (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7120-Battle-for-Azeroth-Allied-Races) - Horde: Nightborne, Highmountain Tauren and Zandalari Trolls / Alliance: Void Elves, Lightforged Draenei and Dark Iron Dwarves.
New gameplay features and social systems (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7118-World-of-Warcraft-%E2%80%93-Gameplay-and-Systems-Deep-Dive) will make it easier to play together.
7.3.5 will start the old world zone scaling as well as introduce the new battleground: The Seething Shore.
Shadow the Fox is now also a charity plush for purchase.
