Tier 14 Sets
Tier 14 gear is here, and you can find it all on WoWDB ! Here is a recap of the set bonuses, as a few have changed since April !

 Death Knight Forums 

You can find all of the set pieces on WoWDB 
Item - Death Knight T14 Blood 2P Bonus  - Reduces the cooldown of your Vampiric Blood ability by 20 sec.
Item - Death Knight T14 Blood 4P Bonus  - Increases the healing received from your Death Strike by 10%.
Item - Death Knight T14 DPS 2P Bonus  - Your Obliterate, Frost Strike, and Scourge Strike deal 10% increased damage.
Item - Death Knight T14 DPS 4P Bonus  - Your Pillar of Frost ability grants 5% additional Strength, and your Unholy Frenzy ability grants 10% additional haste.
