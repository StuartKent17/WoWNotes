Armory Stats - Legendary Quest Line Achievements
This weekend a thread http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/1766514-Legendaries-aren-t-very-legendary-if-20-of-the-raiding-population-is-carrying-them talking about an old quote from the Dragonwrath drop rate changes has been pretty popular on the forum, so we are doing another update on the progress of the legendary quest line.

This time we are looking at the percentage of overall players that have the achievements, the percentage that have done some Highmaul at least the first wing that have the achievements, and the percentage that have done some Blackrock Foundry at least the first wing that have the achievements. Unsurprisingly, the players that raid more are more likely to have progressed on the quest line.

The data used today is a sample of 6.8 million characters from 3.2 million accounts. We also did this at the end of 2013 for Mists of Pandaria, which you can see here http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/3644-Armory-Stats-Legendary-Quest-Achievements-Blue-Tweets-IV-Contest-Voting.
