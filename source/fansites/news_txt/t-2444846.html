Battle for Azeroth Dungeon Event
This week's bonus event is the Battle for Azeroth Dungeon Event.


All week long the final boss of each Battle for Azeroth Dungeon will drop an extra piece of loot.
Pick up the quest Emissary of War (https://www.wowdb.com/quests/44171-emissary-of-war) from either your Adventure Journal or in your faction hub. Completing this quest rewards Cache of Uldir Treasures (https://www.wowdb.com/items/164257-cache-of-uldir-treasures) which contains a piece of heroic Uldir loot and a large sum of Azerite power.
