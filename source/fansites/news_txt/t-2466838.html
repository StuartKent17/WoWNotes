PTR Patch 8.1.5 - Raiding with Leashes VI: Pets of Pandaria
Patch 8.1.5 will add another raiding with leashes achievement, Raiding with Leashes VI: Pets of Pandaria (https://ptr.wowdb.com/achievements/13469-raiding-with-leashes-vi-pets-of-pandaria), this time for rare pets collected from Pandaria raids. Collecting all of these pets earns you Happiness (https://ptr.wowdb.com/items/167057-happiness), which teaches Happiness (https://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/150385-happiness).
