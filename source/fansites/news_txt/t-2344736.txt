Legendary Items and Upgrades
With the Antorus, the Burning Throne release, you can upgrade your legendary items to item level 1000 by completing another collection quest and all legendary items obtained from now on will drop at item level 1000.


Collect 50 x Wakening Essence  for A Titanic Effort 
This quest rewards 1 x Awoken Titan Essence , which upgrades a Legion legendary item from any item level to item level 1000. 
In addition to all of the regular sources of Wakening Essence , The Cursed Shard  rewards 10 x Wakening Essence 
Insignia of the Grand Army  is a new legendary item that is rewarded from The Death of a Titan  clearing Antorus. 
Aman'Thul's Vision  is a new legendary item that is dropped by Argus. It does not count towards the legendary item equip cap.
