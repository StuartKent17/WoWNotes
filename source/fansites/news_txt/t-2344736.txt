Legendary Items and Upgrades
With the Antorus, the Burning Throne release, you can upgrade your legendary items to item level 1000 by completing another collection quest and all legendary items obtained from now on will drop at item level 1000.


Collect 50 x Wakening Essence http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1533-wakening-essence for A Titanic Effort http://www.wowdb.com/quests/49473-a-titanic-effort
This quest rewards 1 x Awoken Titan Essence http://www.wowdb.com/items/154879-awoken-titan-essence, which upgrades a Legion legendary item from any item level to item level 1000. 
In addition to all of the regular sources of Wakening Essence http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1533-wakening-essence, The Cursed Shard http://www.wowdb.com/quests/49474-the-cursed-shard rewards 10 x Wakening Essence http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1533-wakening-essence
Insignia of the Grand Army http://www.wowdb.com/items/152626-insignia-of-the-grand-army is a new legendary item that is rewarded from The Death of a Titan http://www.wowdb.com/quests/49015-the-death-of-a-titan clearing Antorus. 
Aman'Thul's Vision http://www.wowdb.com/items/154172-amanthuls-vision is a new legendary item that is dropped by Argus. It does not count towards the legendary item equip cap.

WoWDB Addon Data Collection
Quickly populating the database with new additions is done with the help of many players. The more people that contribute data, the more accurate and quickly we can update the database.

To help out, just click here curse://enable-wowdb-upload if you have the Twitch Desktop App already installed.

When you are playing the addon will gather data and save it. When you are done playing, Twitch will see the game close and upload the collected data from the addon.
