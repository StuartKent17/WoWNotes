Mounts

Armored Razzashi Raptor (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/96491/armored-razzashi-raptor/#13623/13682) Summons and dismisses a rideable Armored Razzashi Raptor. 1.5 sec cast
Beryl Fire Hawk (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/97501/beryl-fire-hawk/#13623/13682) Summons and dismisses a rideable Beryl Fire Hawk. This is a flying mount. 1.5 sec cast
Crimson Fire Hawk (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/97493/crimson-fire-hawk/#13623/13682) Summons and dismisses a rideable Crimson Fire Hawk. This is a flying mount. 1.5 sec cast
Flameward Hippogryph (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/97359/flameward-hippogryph/#13623/13682) Summons and dismisses a rideable Flameward Hippogryph mount. This is a flying mount. 1.5 sec cast
Savage Raptor (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/97581/savage-raptor/#13623/13682) Summons and dismisses a rideable Savage Raptor. 1.5 sec cast
Swift Zulian Panther (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/96499/swift-zulian-panther/#13623/13682) Summons and dismisses a swift Zulian panther. 1.5 sec cast




We don't really have a lot of information on drop locations but we know a few things:



Savage Raptor (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/97581/savage-raptor/#13623/13682) will be a WoW TCG reward but doesn't have any model for the moment.
Amani Dragonhawk (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/96503/amani-dragonhawk/) is also included in this patch and is also supposed to be a WoW TCG Reward but doesn't look like it has any final model either.
Nightsaber Cub (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/96819/nightsaber-cub/#13623/13682) will be a WoW TCG rewards but other versions of the panther cubs will be available through questing/rare drops apparently.
