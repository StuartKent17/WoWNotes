General
Some players may notice improved data load times when newly entering the world and after zoning into new areas.
Character/NPC load times have been decreased, and now should appear faster. You’re not hearing voices, there really are people in Shattrath!



Bug Fixes
Felspine the Greater in Shadowmoon Valley will no longer evade when engaged.
Guttripper in Nagrand will no longer evade when engaged.
Players will now be correctly credited with their arena points if they purchase an arena item during weekly arena calculations.
Using a castsequence macro on a target moving out of range will no longer cause it to stop working.



WoW PaperIdol Beta
We've got another "model viewer" link today, this one is a very early beta and you might want to contribute to the project if you've got a few good ideas when it comes to designing signatures and things like that.

By going on http://www.grumpycoder.net/signatures/generate.html (http://www.grumpycoder.net/signatures/generate.html) you will be able to create a signature and an avatar based on your in game character model, however there are a few restrictions :
Your face/hair style/hair color/facial hair style can't be gathered from your armory profile, and you'll have to use the "Customization" sub menu if you want the avatar to exactly match your ingame character.
Same problem with the hide helm/cloak. (It will probably be added to the customization menu soon)
It's an early beta, you can only get a very basic avatar or signature for the moment.



It seems that it was released a few days ago, and I figured out it might be a good idea to give it some support because we could definitely get something nice for the community out of this. If you've got any feedback or suggestion related to this tool please use Grumpycoder.net forums (http://forum.grumpycoder.net/index.php/board,1.0.html)

Your signature/avatar will be available through a link that look like : http&#58;//grumpycoder.net/signatures/**************************/signature.jpg (avatar.jpg for the avatar, obviously). If for some reason there is something bugged with it you can replace avatar.jpg/signature.jpg with taint.txt to manually refresh your character data.
