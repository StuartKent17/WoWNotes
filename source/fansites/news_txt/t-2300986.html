New Pet - Shadow
Shadow (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/123650-shadow) was hotfixed into the game tonight, with the In-Game Shop listed as the source. This pet changes colors, shifting between Shadow and Light.

It does not yet appear on the in-game shop or website.
