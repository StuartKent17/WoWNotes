Auriaya PTR Testing
Auriaya (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/march/auriaya.jpg) will be tested on EU PTRs tonight at 19:00 CET. Stay tuned for streams, loot, and boss abilities!


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/march/auriaya2_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/march/auriaya2.jpg)

Auriaya
Health - 16,734M (25-Man) / 3,137M (10-Man)
Sonic Screech (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64688) - A Sonic Shockwave that deals 185000 to 215000 Shadow damage to all enemies in it's path. The Shockwave damge is split between all targets in it's path. (2.5 Sec cast time)
Sentinel Blast (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64679) - Inflicts 5500 Shadow damage and increases Shadow damage taken by 100% for 5 sec. Stacks up to 5 times.
Horrifying Screech (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64386) - The screech strikes fear into the hearts of all nearby enemies, causing them to flee in horror for 5 sec.
She also spawns a wave of panther adds (~20 adds) with 13k Health in 25-Man and 3k Health in 10-Man every now and then.

Add - Feral Defender
This add spawns about 1 minute after the beginning of the fight, it starts the fight with 9 stacks of Feral Essence (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64455).
Health - ? (25-Man) - 200k (10-Man)
Feral Essence (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64455) -- Each essence increases the damage dealt by the Feral Defender by 50%. The Feral Defender can revive himself at the cost of one of his Feral Essences.
Feral Pounce (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64669) -- The Feral Defender pounces the target stunning them for 4 sec and inflicting 602 to 698 Shadow damage every 1 sec.
Seeping Feral Essence (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64675) -- The Seeping Essence of the Feral Defender Inflicts 9000 Shadow damage every 1 sec. This is a void zone-like effect where the Feral Defender spawns and dies.

Panther Adds - Sanctum Sentry
There are 4 adds roaming around the central room with Auriaya in 25-Man, 2 in 10-Man.
Health - 557k (25-Man) - 335k (10-Man)
Strength of the Pack (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?spell=64381) - The presence of other members of the pack increases damage done by 50% to all members of the pack within 20 yards. (Stackable, you have to spread out the panthers to get rid of this buff)
