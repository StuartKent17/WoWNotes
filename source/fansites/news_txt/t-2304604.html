Patch 7.3 - Orix the All-Seer
Patch 7.3 added Orix the All-Seer (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/128134-orix-the-all-seer) and wants you to collect Intact Demon Eyes (http://www.wowdb.com/items/153021-intact-demon-eye) for various rewards!

Head to 60, 45 in Antoran Wastes and jump off the bridge to find his cave. You can click an All-Seer's Focus (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/128151-all-seers-focus) to get the Agent of the All-Seer (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/254174-agent-of-the-all-seer) buff that allows you to collect the eyes. Be careful, as the buff causes you to take 100% additional damage!

Build of the Month - September 2017
Each month or so we will post a couple of hardware setups for those of you who are thinking of upgrading their computer!

All of the parts on this page are Amazon affiliate links. When you purchase your parts through them it helps support the site!

This month
The current SSD prices (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/september/ssdgraph.gif) are fairly flat right now, with most SSDs under $0.50 per gigabyte when on sale.

Depending on how much you want to overclock, you may need to spend a bit more for a better CPU cooler like the NH-D15 (http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00L7UZMAK?ie=UTF8&tag=mmochamp-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B00L7UZMAK).

Unless you are doing a lot of video rendering or streaming, you do not need the 7700K and would get more performance from spending that money elsewhere.

Make sure to keep your AMD (http://sites.amd.com/us/game/downloads/Pages/downloads.aspx) and Nvidia (http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us) drivers up to date.

Don't forget to visit the Computer Forum (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/290-Computer) for any extra questions!
