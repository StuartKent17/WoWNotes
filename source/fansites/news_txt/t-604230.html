Blizzcast isn't here to give new information
A lot of players have been disappointed by yesterday's Blizzcast, here is a small explanation.
Thanks for the feedback! I'm sorry if there was some hype from somewhere that this was going to be providing huge exclusive information, that just isn't the case. In fact, it's quite likely that the next BlizzCast won't even mention World of Warcraft. The podcast itself is intended to be "a glimpse of what it is like to make the games you love from their (employees) perspective", and not necessarily as a way to make announcements or press releases. Most "big news" is usually given as exclusives to magazines or websites, of course there are quite a lot of people that don't already know that information, and in those cases the podcast will provide some new info on Sunwell, if it doesn't for you then hopefully you enjoyed the other parts.

Also, to our credit, we'll be able to pump these out a little quicker in the future so there won't be such a gap between information releases and the BlizzCast episodes if they do contain some tidbits.


US Realm - Free character transfer
US Realms are getting their free transfers too, with a lot more servers involved than the one posted for the european players yesterday.

We will be offering free character moves from selected high population realms to lower populated realms, assisting in population dispersal. The ability to move a character on the selected source realms will become available on Thursday, January 10 at 3:00 PM PST, and are scheduled to run until Thursday, January 17 at 3:00 PM PST.

In the event that a realm meets our transfer goals before the scheduled end date, the transfers to that realm will be disabled. We highly recommend that if you are planning to transfer that you do so at your earliest convenience. No characters will be eligible for these free moves once the transfers have been closed.

Once the transfers open you can begin your transfer by visiting the Character Move page located here: https://www.worldofwarcraft.com/account/character-move.html (https://www.worldofwarcraft.com/account/character-move.html)

The realms chosen consist of source realms with the highest sustained queues, and destination realms with the lowest population. The numbers gathered are directly from our data, and depict the realms in most need of these transfers. We will be continuing to monitor all realms and open additional free transfers in the future where necessary.

Other blue posts
More bug reports, and a small information about Death Knights.
Deathknights races
Developpers don't currently plan on limiting racial choice for when you create your Deathknight. You don't turn your character into a Deathknight, but instead create a new one. (Basically, you could perfectly create a Gnome Deathknight even if your main character is a Draenei) (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=3773337274&postId=38812315126&sid=1#23))

Chat bubbles disappearing when the interface is hidden
Ever since the 2.3 patch, chat bubbles disappear when you turn the interface off. This was changed intentionally, but in a future patch there will probably be a new option that will allow you to turn them back on. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=3881820060&postId=38815420539&sid=1#1))

Playing in Arenas / Hated reputation with the Steamwheedle Cartel
Some arena NPCs are intended to be on goblin factions and hostile to Bloodsail Buccaneers, but there should be at least one vendor and one arena master that is friendly to everyone. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=3773367014&postId=38813018329&sid=1#4))

Blade's edge mountain eating/drinking interruptions
The bug causing people to stand up in Blade's Edge Mountains and interrupting their eating/drinking was fixed but has been reintroduced with the 2.3.2 patch, devs are re-investigating the cause. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=3881780067&postId=38813016894&sid=1#2))

Undead female weapons misaligned.
In 2.3.2 when the weapons on an undead female are unsheathed, the mainhand weapon will angle downward, while the offhand will point straight ahead, parallel to the ground. This should be fixed in an upcoming patch. (Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=3881819045&postId=38813017192&sid=1#1))
