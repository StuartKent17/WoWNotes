Spell Comparison
Tier 8 Sets
Item - Druid T8 Balance 2P Bonus (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/64818/) - Increases the bonus granted by Eclipse for Starfire and Wrath by 7%. (Down from 15%)

Tier 9 Sets
Item - Shaman T9 Elemental 4P Bonus (Lava Burst) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/67228/) - Your Lava Burst spell causes your target to burn for an additonal 10% of your spell's damage over 6 sec. (Down from 20%)

Tier 10 Sets
Item - Druid T10 Balance 4P Bonus (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/70723/) - Your critical strikes from Starfire and Wrath cause the target languish for an additional 7% of your spell's damage over 4 sec. (Up from 5%)

Blizzard Entertainment at Dreamhack Winter!
We’re pleased to announce that Blizzard Entertainment will be attending Dreamhack Winter 2009, the world’s largest computer festival, which is taking place from 26th to 29th November in Jönköping, Sweden.

Dreamhack attendees will be able to visit the Blizzard booth to take part in various competitions and contests, and also to play both StarCraft II and World of Warcraft: Cataclysm.

You can find out more about Dreamhack Winter 2009 at http://www.dreamhack.se/dhw09 .

We look forward to seeing you there!
