Boubouille2007-08-02, 09:20 PMBlizzCon Character Copies Available - 8/2/2007

BlizzCon attendees are now able to copy up to four level 70 characters to an event realm. Attendees will have the chance to experience new content at the event using their own level 70 characters or a pre-made character. Those wishing to make copies may do so at the character copy page , which will be available until the end of BlizzCon on August 4, 2007.


BlizzCon Live Coverage - 8/2/2007

Good news for fans who didn't make it to BlizzCon this year but still would like to check out the event: Giga  will be providing live coverage from BlizzCon in the form of a live video stream. Coverage will include the StarCraft, Warcraft III and World of Warcraft Arena tournaments, and much more. You will need to download the Octoshape plug-in  to access the stream.
