Arena Season 2 Stats
New season is now scheduled for 19th June.
Ilvl went from 123 to 136, there isn't much change to the stats, you just get more "normal" stats, most of the set bonuses don't change and "special" bonuses on some parts aren't upgraded. For the moment, only one set stats are available.

New Quests/Events
Ezra's quest (http://www.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2007/june/ezraquest.jpg) is in the patch. (Full story here (http://blogs.ocregister.com/gadgetress/archives/2007/05/wishing_for_war.html) and here (http://www.ocregister.com/ocregister/money/article_1702706.php))

L70ETC has been added to the game, to play a 4 minutes long song in the Darkmoon Faire.
The symbol on the drums is "L70ETC," which stands for Level 70 Elite Tauren Chieftain. This is the same band that did the "The Power of the Horde" metal track in WarCraft 3: The Frozen Throne. They were called Level 10 Elite Tauren Chieftain then, and changed their name to Level 60 Elite Tauren Chieftain when WoW came out. They also played Blizzcon the last year it was held.
