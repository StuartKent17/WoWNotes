Rob Pardo Working With Unity
You may remember that Rob Pardo left Blizzard last summer (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4131-Rob-Pardo-Leaving-Blizzard-Entertainment) and appears to have been enjoying his break judging by his Twitter feed (https://twitter.com/Rob_Pardo). It looks like he is going to spend some time working with Unity as a Creative Advisor (http://blogs.unity3d.com/2015/04/22/rob-pardo-world-tour/), making a global tour to meet with developers. Blizzard used the Unity engine to create Hearthstone. Both GamesBeat (http://venturebeat.com/2015/04/22/world-of-warcrafts-lead-designer-joins-unity-as-creative-adviser-and-kicks-off-a-world-tour/) and gamesindustry.biz (http://www.gamesindustry.biz/articles/2015-04-22-rob-pardo-partners-with-unity) have interviews with Pardo about his new role.
