Are windwalker monks really supposed to have 30+ keybinds in warlords? I just counted the skills and the prunning did nothing to us
They had the lowest number of active buttons in the game. (holinka (https://twitter.com/holinka/status/518547754059317249))

PvE
Are the normal 5-mans in the beta supposed to be incredibly difficult to do in heroic raid gear, or is it just me?
Your gear is being scaled to the bare minimum for the zone. Which dungeon(s) did you find to be too challenging? (WatcherDev (https://twitter.com/WatcherDev/status/518930836142710784))

Are the insanely long loot tables for dungeon bosses intended ?
Personal loot in dungeons means every boss needs to have at least something for every spec. (WatcherDev (https://twitter.com/WatcherDev/status/519226747082924032))

PvP
Why don't you guys make a PvP talent tree to a whole different one from PvE separated, so they don't interfere for more balance?
We already do so many thing to tune the game separately. A second talent tree sounds like a complexity nightmare. (holinka (https://twitter.com/holinka/status/518559025190174720))

Reputation / Questing
Does WoD have hidden/alternative quests that reveal themselves after having a convo between an npc?
Quests are there to guide you, so we don't generally hide them. WoD has more "discover-able" content than any expac, though. (_DonAdams (https://twitter.com/_DonAdams/status/518633542142525443))

Does WoD give players choices that alter (at least slightly) the story while questing? Makes people pay attention to the story.
The building you choose to construct at your garrison outpost in each zone can lead to different quests in some cases. (_DonAdams (https://twitter.com/_DonAdams/status/518633846682554368))

Do WoD have optional (or interactions) that are morally driven rather than reward driven? (Saving a person in a fire for nothing)
We tend to create interactions that have a mechanical purpose (objective credit, buff) or add flavor. (_DonAdams (https://twitter.com/_DonAdams/status/518635251568893952))
If you save a person from a fire, we feel like we should reward you for that, in addition to you feeling morally good about it. (_DonAdams (https://twitter.com/_DonAdams/status/518635353742143488))

Is there any open-world non-raid reason to group in WoD? Do you think there should be? Is that supported gameplay?
We have some group quests while leveling up, and several of the max-level outdoor areas are group content. (WatcherDev (https://twitter.com/WatcherDev/status/518867353992691712))

In Diablo 2 there are random shrines that spawn to add flavor to the gameplay. I always wondered why WoW doesn't have this...
Timeless Isle had these! (Muffinus (https://twitter.com/Muffinus/status/519184466406031361))
Will we see any random shrines like these in Warlords?
There are tons of useful buff items scattered around Draenor/endgame POIs, not shrines this round, different/new stuff (Muffinus (https://twitter.com/Muffinus/status/519192381531619330))

Garrisons
Is the barn supposed to make cooking obsolete or is this an oversight? Garrisons shouldn't be more important than the characters.
Your shipments are limited, and not everyone will have the barn, how many feasts does your raid need? (Muffinus (https://twitter.com/Muffinus/status/518554112724828161))

Any chance we can Have prisoners of enemy factions in our garrison? i'd love to have gnome slaves and goblin overseers please
This was going to be a feature of the barracks jail at one point, maybe we'll revisit it in the future with some fun questgivers (Muffinus (https://twitter.com/Muffinus/status/518554230672850944))

some of us would rather have a "collect them all" than "research which follower comes from where in advance"
You can see them in your follower list (uncollected followers show up as grey but searchable), but yes that is a concern. (Muffinus (https://twitter.com/Muffinus/status/518937946612043777))

What if we want to collect those other followers though!
Considering ways to allow you to gain them in the future. (Muffinus (https://twitter.com/Muffinus/status/519020478879789056))
