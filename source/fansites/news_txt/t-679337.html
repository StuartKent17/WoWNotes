Icecrown Citadel
It's a Trap! (10 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4533/) and It's a Trap! (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4609/) have been removed from the game.

Pit of Saron
Don't Look Up (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4525/) now requires you to complete the achievement on the first try.


Spell Changes

Miscellaneous
Item - Shadowmourne Legendary (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/71903/) has been changed once again - Your weapon swings have a chance to drain a Soul Fragment granting you 40 strength. When you have acquired 10 Soul Fragments you will unleash Chaos Bane, dealing 1900 to 2100 shadow damage split between all enemies within 8 yards.

Engineering
Iceblade Arrow (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/72953/) has been added to the game - Creates Iceblade Arrow (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/52021/iceblade-arrow/) x 1000. / Reagents: Crystallized Shadow (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/37703/) x 2
Shatter Rounds (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/72952/) has been added to the game - Creates Shatter Rounds (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/52020/shatter-rounds/) x 1000. / Reagents: Crystallized Earth (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/37701/) x 2

Protection
Divine Intervention (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/19752/) no longer clears the effect of Exhaustion and Sated on target. (Important - The official notes were changed to : This ability now also removes Exhaustion or Sated from a target if the recipient is out of combat when the effect end)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue
Asssassination
Improved Poisons (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/14117/) now increases the chance to apply Deadly poison to your target by 4/8/12/16/20%. (Up from 2/4/6/8/10%)

Poisons
Deadly Poison (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/57973/) now has an additional effect - Once stacked to 5 times, each application of Deadly Poison also causes the poison on the Rogue's other weapon to apply.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock
Affliction
Improved Drain Soul (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/18372/) now reduces the threat caused by your Affliction spells by 10/20%. (Up from 5/10%)

Destruction
Destructive Reach (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/17918/) now reduces the threat caused by Destruction spells by 10/20%. (Up from 5/10%)

Items
Shaman Tier-9 4-Piece Set Bonus (Elemental): This set bonus now does an additional 10% of Lava Burst’s initial damage over time instead of increasing the size of the Lava Burst.
