Pets

Tear Armor (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/50498/#15739-15752) weakens the armor of the target by 4% for 30 sec. Stacks up to 3 times.

Cauterize (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/86949/#15739-15752) has a 2 minute cooldown.


Fire

Pyroblast (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/11366/#15739-15752) DoT damage remains the same, but is spread out over 18 sec instead of 12 sec.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/imonk.gif Monk (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/341-Monk))

Legacy of the Emperor (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/115921/#15739-15752) now costs 50 Energy / 6% of Base Mana, down from 60 Energy / 6% of Base Mana.


Talents

Rushing Jade Wind (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/116847/#15739-15752) no longer allows you to move at 100% speed while using Spinning Crane Kick after you cast Rushing Jade Wind.
Zen Sphere (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/124081/#15739-15752) had a tooltip fix to show damage at 460 + 65% of AP.


Brewmaster

Avert Harm (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/115213/#15739-15752) now works on nearby party and raid targets rather than all friendly targets.
Breath of Fire (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/115181/#15739-15752) had the bonus damage from when Dizzying Haze is on the target increased by 355%.
Summon Black Ox Statue (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/115315/#15739-15752) passive now absorbs 197.1% of AP + 50 damage, rather than 14,232.


Mistweaver

Renewing Mist (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/115151/#15739-15752) now also generates 1 Chi.


Windwalker

Legacy of the White Tiger (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/116781/#15739-15752) had the energy power cost reduced to 50, down from 60.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/274-Priest))

Cascade (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/120785/#15739-15752) is now instant cast, down from 3 sec. Range reduced to 40 yd from 100.
Flash Heal (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/2061/#15739-15752) now costs 6.6% of Base Mana, up from 6%.
Halo (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/120696/#15739-15752): Creates a ring of Shadow energy around you that quickly expands and grows in power, up to 30 yds away. Deals up to (20,216 + 195.0% of SP) Shadow damage to enemies, and up to 0 healing to allies, with the greatest effect at 25 yds. 100 yd range. Instant.
Halo (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/120692/#15739-15752): Creates a ring of Holy energy around you that quickly expands and grows in power, up to 30 yds away. Deals up to (20,216 + 195.0% of SP) Holy damage to enemies, and up to 0 healing to allies, with the greatest effect at 25 yds. 100 yd range. Instant.
Prayer of Mending (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/33076/#15739-15752) now costs 3.9% of Base Mana, up from 3.5%.
Renew (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/139/#15739-15752) now costs 2.9% of Base Mana, up from 2.6%.
Shadowfiend (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/34433/#15739-15752) no longer grants this bonus effect for Shadow: Your Mind Flay critical strikes reduce the remaining cooldown of this ability by 10 sec.


Talents

Archangel (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/109147/#15739-15752) now has a 2 minute cooldown for the Dark Archangel portion of the spell.
Mindbender (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/123040/#15739-15752) no longer grants this bonus effect for Shadow: Your Mind Flay critical strikes reduce the remaining cooldown of this ability by 10 sec.



Discipline

Meditation (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/95860/#15739-15752) now allows 40% of your mana regeneration from Spirit to continue while in combat, up from 25%.
Penance (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/47540/#15739-15752) now costs 3.4% of Base Mana, up from 3.1%.


Holy

Divine Hymn (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/64843/#15739-15752) no longer has a maximum of 20 heals.


Holy & Discipline

Greater Heal (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/2060/#15739-15752) now costs 6.6% of Base Mana, up from 6%.
Heal (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/2050/#15739-15752) now costs 2.2% of Base Mana, up from 2%.
Holy Fire (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/14914/#15739-15752) is now a Holy & Discipline Specialization spell.


Shadow

Devouring Plague (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/2944/#15739-15752) had the healing component reworked: Also heals the caster for 1% of their maximum health when it deals direct damage, and 0.30% of their maximum health when it deals periodic damage. Damage and healing increased based on the number of Shadow Orbs consumed.

Affliction

Drain Soul (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/1120/#15739-15752) now causes double the normal damage and your periodic damage spells tick 50% (was 100%) faster if the target is at or below 20% health.
Malefic Grasp (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/103103/#15739-15752): Now your periodic damage spells tick 50% faster while afflicted by Malefic Grasp, down from 100%.
Unstable Affliction (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/30108/#15739-15752) base damage was increased by 100%.
