Plagueworks unlock next week!
The next raid wing of the Icecrown Citadel will unlock next week and will give players a chance to fight:
Rotface http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=899
Festergut http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=892
Professor Putricide http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=898, your new favorite boss if you like Futurama http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9PStC-LNZ2s.

Important - You only have 10 attempts to kill Professor Putricide because of the Icecrown Citadel Gating http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=911. The number was changed from 5 to 10 since the original announcement 

It also means that people working on Shadow's Edge http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49888/shadows-edge/ will finally have a chance to get Festergut's Acidic Blood http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/50226/festerguts-acidic-blood/ and Rotface's Acidic Blood http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/50231/rotfaces-acidic-blood/ to complete The Sacred and the Corrupt http://db.mmo-champion.com/q/24545/the-sacred-and-the-corrupt/.
