The Sha of Fear has corrupted the grand empress of the mantid. Her increasingly erratic and paranoid commands have driven the Klaxxi--the keepers of mantid culture--to take aggressive action. Because they are few in number, the Klaxxi are reluctantly willing to accept outside help in destroying the empress and purging their race of the sha's negative influence.

Garalon

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Furious Swipe (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/122735) - Garalon performs a Furious Swipe to targets in a large cone in front of him, inflicting 800,000 Physical damage. If Garalon fails to hit at least 2 targets with his Furious Swipe, he gains Fury.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Fury (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/122754) - Fury increases Garalon's damage dealt by 10% and movement speed by 10% for 30 sec. This effect stacks.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/deadly.png" title="Deadly" /> Crush (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/122774) - Garalon crushes his foes, inflicting 100,000 Physical damage to all players. Any player within 12 yards receives an additional 800,000 Physical damage as Garalon's body slams down upon them. Garalon's massive bulk also knocks down all players for 2 sec. Garalon crushes foes whenever he detects their presence beneath him. In Normal Difficulty, Garalon also crushes foes when Pheromones passes to a new target. In Heroic Difficulty, Garalon periodically crushes foes.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Pheromones (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/123092) - Garalon surrounds a player with powerful pheromones, fixating on the target. Every 2 sec. the Pheromones inflict 20,000 Nature damage and Pungency on the target, then spawn a Pheromone Trail. Pheromones pass to allies in close proximity.

Pungency (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/123081) - Exposure to Garalon's Pheromones renders the player extra pungent, increasing Pheromone damage by 10% for 1.25 min. This effect stacks.
Pheromone Trail (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/123120) - The Pheromone Trail on the ground inflicts 25,000 Nature damage to players within 4 yards.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Garalon's Legs - Players attack Garalon's legs separately from his body.

Weak Point - Close proximity to Garalon's leg allows attackers to see weak points, increasing their damage dealt to the leg by 100%.
Broken Leg (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/122786) - Breaking one of Garalon's legs damages Garalon for 3% of his maximum health and reduces Garalon's movement speed by 15%. This effect stacks up to 4 times.
Mend Leg (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/123495) - Garalon periodically mends one of his broken legs, restoring it to full health.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/heroic.png" title="Heroic" /> Damaged (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/67323) - In Heroic difficulty, Garalon gains the Damaged effect upon reaching 33% remaining health.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Pheromones (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/123092) - While Garalon has the Damaged effect, Garalon ignores the fixate effects of Pheromones.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Melee Attack - While Garalon has the Damaged effect, Garalon uses his normal melee attack and gains a dramatic speed increase.

Beta Class Balance Analysis
One slight correction to something we said a while back:
White attacks are still ‘one roll’ (Miss Dodge Parry Block Glance Crit Hit), and yellow attacks are still ‘two roll’ (Miss Dodge Parry Block, Crit). The only change to that is that Block against players is an additional separate roll now.
Correction: Block is always a separate roll, not just on players. So white attacks are technically two rolls (Miss Dodge Parry Glance Crit Hit vs. Block NonBlock), and yellow attacks are technically three rolls (Miss Dodge Parry Hit vs. Block NonBlock vs. Crit NonCrit). However, please take care in discussing this, since "two roll" has historically meant that crit is rolled separately, which is NOT the case for white attacks.

From the calculator I'm assuming this [healer pushback] is 70 percent right?
Yes.
