Patch 7.3.2 PTR Build Deploy - 10/17/2017
Greetings all,
PTR realms will be coming down so we can deploy a new 7.3.2 build. No ETA for today's push but I'll update this thread if things look like they're going to take longer than usual (~3-4 hours).

I'll leave this thread open for discussion so long as conversations remain civil and adhere to forum guidelines.

Thanks for your patience everyone!

Friday, October 20th

Varimathras - Mythic Antorus, the Burning Throne
13:00 PDT (16:00 EDT, 22:00 CEST)

14:00 PDT (17:00 EDT, 23:00 CEST)

Hope's End - Raid Finder Antorus, the Burning Throne

As always, this testing schedule is very fluid and subject to the realities of a PTR environment. We might have to change the time of a testing session, change the bosses being tested, or cancel a test entirely, due to bugs, server hardware issues, etc. Keep an eye on this forum for the latest information, and thank you in advance for testing and providing feedback.


Weekly Event - Burning Crusade Timewalking
Burning Crusade Timewalking is back this week.


The weekly quest (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/44164-a-burning-path-through-time) from Archmage Timear (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/111246-archmage-timear) rewards a Accolade of Victory (http://www.wowdb.com/items/140176-accolade-of-victory), Seal of Broken Fate (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1273-seal-of-broken-fate), and Cache of Fel Treasures (http://www.wowdb.com/items/147519-cache-of-fel-treasures). The cache can contain tier set pieces.
You can find Cupri (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/98685-cupri) in Shattrath.
Don't forget to keep working towards Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (http://www.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk), Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (http://www.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger), and Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade)!
Reins of the Infinite Timereaver (http://www.wowdb.com/items/133543-reins-of-the-infinite-timereaver) can also drop from any Timewalking dungeon boss.
Head to Black Temple with a 10-30 player premade group to take on the bosses that drop item level 900 loot.
