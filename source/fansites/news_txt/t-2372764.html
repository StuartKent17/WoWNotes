Patch 7.3.5 Hotfixes - January 31
Characters

All accounts now have 16 character slots available per realm (was 12).
The Mountaineer (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255658-mountaineer) racial ability now correctly grants Highmountain Tauren 1% Versatility.
Fixed a bug that prevented a small number of players from being able to complete the initial quests for the Nightborne.
The Befuddle ability cast by Confusion is now more player friendly.


Events

The duration of the Kirin Tor Tavern Crawl has been extended by an additional 24 hours.


Items

The Spear of Rethu (http://www.wowdb.com/items/131733-spear-of-rethu) can now be transmogrified at any level.


Professions

Tailoring

[After next realm restart] Players now relearn Silkweave Gloves (Rank 1) when using the relearning book for Tailoring.



Quests

Mountaineer's Ale for the Wetlands quest "The Stolen Keg" can now be looted by multiple players.


Sounds

Fixed a bug that was preventing some addon sounds from playing correctly.



Kirin Tor Tavern Crawl Micro-Holidaykttc
The Kirin Tor Tavern Crawl (http://ptr.wowdb.com/world-events/646-kirin-tor-tavern-crawl) is a micro-holiday that sends players to different taverns around the world.


You can join the currently active tavern from a new NPC in Dalaran (Tipsi Wobblerune (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/119045-tipsi-wobblerune) or Anebria Sunwine (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/119088-anebria-sunwine))
After some time the group moves along to the next Tavern. You can press the last call button or take the portal to move on.
If you get left behind on the tavern tour, you can catch up by speaking to the mage in the current tavern you are in.
Each location has drink and food that you can interact with. The food will grant a well fed buff.
There are also additional NPCs in each tavern enjoying the holiday.
You can click on the discarded packages to apply one of many unique hat models for four hours.
