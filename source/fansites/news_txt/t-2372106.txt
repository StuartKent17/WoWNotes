Bestowed with moose horns by their ancestor Huln https://wow.gamepedia.com/Huln_Highmountain, the Highmountain Tauren https://wow.gamepedia.com/Highmountain_tauren tribes have united together after pushing the Legion out of their lands. With the threat gone, these Tauren have decided to ally with the Horde and the rest of their kind to fight the Alliance.

Unlocking the Highmountain Tauren
To unlock the ability to make a Highmountain Tauren, the following achievements must be completed on one character on your account. Once done, you can make a Highmountain Tauren on any server that you have a 110 character provided you have purchased Battle for Azeroth.


Ain't No Mountain High Enough http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/10059-aint-no-mountain-high-enough - Complete the Highmountain storylines
Exalted with The Highmountain Tribe http://www.wowdb.com/factions/1828-highmountain-tribe

Racials
The Highmountain Tauren racial abilities give you damage reduction, increased mining, increased versatility, a knockdown, and a chance to loot more meat and fish. 


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_RuggedTenacity.jpg https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_RuggedTenacity.jpg https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_PrideofIronhorn.jpg https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_PrideofIronhorn.jpg https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_Mountaineer.jpg https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_Mountaineer.jpg https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_BullRush.jpg https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_BullRush.jpg https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_WasteNotWantNot.jpg https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_WasteNotWantNot.jpg https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Achievement_AlliedRace_HighmountainTauren.jpg 


Rugged Tenacity http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255659-rugged-tenacity - Reduces damage taken by 1.8% of stamina.
Pride of Ironhorn http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255655-pride-of-ironhorn - Mining skill increased by 15 and allows you to mine faster.
Mountaineer http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255658-mountaineer - Increases your Versatility by 1%.
Bull Rush http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255654-bull-rush - Charges forward for 1 sec, knocking enemies down for 1.5 sec. 2 minute cooldown. 
Waste Not, Want Not http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255656-waste-not-want-not - You have a chance to loot additional meat and fish. 



Mounts
The Highmountain Tauren racial mount is the Highmountain Thunderhoof http://www.wowdb.com/items/155662-highmountain-thunderhoof. It is usable by all Horde races upon completion of Allied Races: Highmountain Tauren http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/12245-allied-races-highmountain-tauren.
