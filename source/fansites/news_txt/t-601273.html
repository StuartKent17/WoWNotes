Method Interview
Method will be interviewed on WoW Radio (http://www.wcradio.com)'s flagship show 'Blue Plz!' on Sunday 13th of May about their Lady Vashj kill and end-game experiences. Join them for a musical preshow at 17:30 servertime (Paris time) (11:30 EST) with the live show kicking off at 18:30 servertime (12:30 EST).
The interview is 60 minutes long and will include a full run down of everything Vashj related, all the bugs, and tons of drama + general opinions on end-game raiding.

Arena (Via WoWinsider (http://www.wowinsider.com/))
The Arena System Q&A #2 (http://forums.wow-europe.com/thread.html?topicId=285038857&pageNo=1&sid=1#1) was updated on the official forums, we now have the list of the titles that players will be able to chose at the end of the season based on their ranking.
Q: What ranks can we earn?
A: There will be 4 titles total for players to earn. The ranking needed for each Arena title is as follows: (Rankings are based by Battlegroup):
% - .5% : Gladiator (and Armoured Nether Drake sent in mail)
.5% - 3% : Duelist
3% - 10% : Rival
10% - 35% : Challenger
