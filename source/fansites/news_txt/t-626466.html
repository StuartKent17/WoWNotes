Boubouille
2008-12-13, 12:50 AM
A new build has been deployed on PTRs, the servers are now live. This post will most likely be updated.

Updates
18:30 CET - Relic of Ulduar (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=42780) aren't soulbound anymore. Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/reliculduarsoulbound.jpg)
16:30 CET - Small update on the new stats of Feral staves (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/feralstaffcomparison.jpg), http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druids can now see the Feral AP value given by all weapons. (Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/fishingpoledruid.jpg))
12:30 CET - Many item changes added, new Sigils, Titansteel Guardian (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/titansteelguardian308.jpg) new stats, and also a few change to the Sons of Hodir. Professions changes slightly updated as well.
11:00 CET - All Feral Druid Staves have been changed, you can check the comparison of the new/old stats for most of the WotLK feral staves (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/feralstaffcomparison.jpg).
10:00 CET - Let's start the updates! New epic rewards now properly labelled as Lake Wintergrasp rewards, prices added.

Items
Professions
You can now buy Forest Emerald (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=36933), Sky Sapphire (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=36924), Twilight Opal (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=36927), Monarch Topaz (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=36930), Autumn's Glow (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=36921), Scarlet Ruby (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=36918) for 10 x Emblem of Heroism (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40752) each.

Misc. / Sons of Hodir
The Iron Boot Flask (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=43499) now costs 250 Relic of Ulduar (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=42780), you might want to buy yours now just in case...
You can now use Relic of Ulduar (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=42780) to level your reputation with the Sons of Hodir. Hodir's Tribute (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/hodirstribute.jpg) is a repeatable quest and will give you 250 (275 as a human) reputation for each turn-in.

Equipment
Loop of the Kirin Tor (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/9355kirintor.jpg) and Ring of the Kirin Tor (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/9355kirintor.jpg) have been added for people who weren't fully satisfied by their Band of the Kirin Tor (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40586) or Signet of the Kirin Tor (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40585)
Titansteel Guardian (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/titansteelguardian308.jpg) now gives 457 Spell Power. (Down from 490)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ideathknights.gif Death Knight
Sigil of Haunted Dreams (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40715) now gives Your Blood Strike and Heart Strikes a chance to grant 173 critical strike rating for 10 sec. (Old - Haste Rating)
Sigil of the Unfaltering Knight (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/newsigils308.jpg) can now be bought for 15 x Emblem of Heroism (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40752)
Sigil of Arthritic Binding (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/newsigils308.jpg), Sigil of the Wild Buck (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/newsigils308.jpg) and Sigil of the Frozen Conscience (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/newsigils308.jpg) can now be bought for 30 x Venture Coin (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=37836)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid
All Feral Druid Staves have been changed, you can check the comparison of the new/old stats for most of the WotLK feral staves (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/feralstaffcomparison.jpg). Druids can now see the Feral AP value given by all weapons. (Screenshot (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/fishingpoledruid.jpg))

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin
Libram of Reciprocation (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40706) now gives Your Judgement of Command spell a chance to grant 173 critical strike rating for 10 sec. (Old - Haste Rating)


Professions changes
Alchemy
Casting time for transmutation of Eternal Life/Fire/Air/Shadow/Earth/Water/Might has been decreased from 25 to 5 seconds.
Alchemy: Lesser Flask of Resistance (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/lesserflaskofresistance.jpg) has been added - Increases resistance to all schools of magic by 50 for 1 hr. Counts as both a Battle and Guardian elixir. This effect persists through death.

Blacksmithing
Plans: Titanium Plating (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/9355newenchants.jpg) - Permanently attach titanium plating to a shield, increasing block value by 40.

Cooking
The recipe for the Fish Feast (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=43015) is now available for Dalaran Cooking Award (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=43016) x 5.

Enchanting
Formula: Enchant Weapon - Titanguard (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/9355newenchants.jpg) - Permanently enchant a melee weapon to increase Stamina by 75. Requires a level 60 or higher item.
Formula: Enchant Bracer - Major Stamina (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/9355newenchants.jpg) - Permanently enchant bracers to increase Stamina by 40. Requires a level 60 or higher item.

Engineering
The duration of Hyperspeed Accelerators (http://wotlk.wowhead.com/?spell=54999) has been increased from 8 to 10 seconds.

Jewelcrafting
Jewelcrafting: Icy Prism (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/icyprism.jpg) has been added to the game and is most likely the Northrend version of Brilliant Glass (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=35945)

Leatherworking
Windripper Leggings (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/9355windripper.jpg) and Windripper Boots (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/december/9355windripper.jpg) have been added to the game.


Lake Wintergrasp
New epic rewards have been added to Lake Wintergrasp, they can be bought with Wintergrasp Mark of Honor (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=43589).

Spell Comparison
This list is obtained by comparing spell data between the latest PTR version and the current live version, PTR patches are sometimes pushed live with outdated data and aren't 100% reliable. (Well, that's why they're TEST servers)
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/deathknight.jpg Death Knight (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=650) / Talent Calc. (9095) (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?deathknight))
Frost
Icebound Fortitude now reduces all damage taken by 20%. (Up from 15%)
Frost Presence now increases armor contribution from items by 80%. (Up from 75%)

Blood
Dancing Rune Weapon now costs 40 Runic Power. (Down from 50)

Glyphs
Glyph of Unbreakable Armor - Unbreakable Armor grants an additional 15% armor. (Old - Unbreakable Armor grants an additional 5% parry chance but no longer increases your Strength.)
Glyph of Frost Strike - Reduces the cost of your Frost Strike by 8. (Old - Your Frost Strikes have a 10% chance to Freeze the target for 8 sec.)
Glyph of Rune Strike - Increases the critical strike chance of your Rune Strike by 10%. (No longer increases runic power cost)
Glyph of Obliterate - Your Obliterate strikes for 20% additional weapon damage. (No longer decrease bonus damage per disease)
Glyph of Vampiric Blood - Increases the duration of your Vampiric Blood by 10 sec. (Old - Your Vampiric Blood also heals you for 3% of your total health.)
Glyph of the Ghoul - Your Ghoul receives an additional 40% of your Strength and 40% of your Stamina. (Old - Your Ghoul receives an additional 20% of your Strength.
Glyph of Death Grip - When you deal a killing blow that grants honor or experience, the cooldown of your Death Grip is refreshed. (Old - Increases the cooldown of Death Grip by 10 sec but stuns targets for 1 sec.)
Glyph of Rune Tap - Your Rune Tap heals yourself for an additional 10% of the effect, and also heals your party for 10% of their maximum health. (Old - Didn't benefit the DK and only healed the party)
Glyph of Death Strike - Increases your Death Strike's damage and healing by 20%. (Old - Your Death Strike's damage and healing increase by 2% for every 5 runic power you currently have. The runic power is not consumed by this effect.)
Glyph of Raise Dead - Your Raise Dead spell no longer requires a reagent. (Old - Your Ghoul receives an additional 20% of your Strength)

Items
Sigil of Haunted Dreams (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40715) now gives Your Blood Strike and Heart Strikes a chance to grant 173 critical strike rating for 10 sec. (Old - Haste Rating)

Glyphs
Glyph of Serpent Sting - Increases the duration of your Serpent Sting by 6 sec. (Up from 3 sec)

Pet
Spirit Strike (Spirit Beast) now deals its damage over 6 seconds, down from 10.

Frost
Frostbite doesn't reduce the cast time of Frostfire Bolt anymore. (See this post (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=13392250928&pageNo=1&sid=1#9))

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/24x24/paladin.jpg Paladin (Skills List (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=610) / Talent Calc. (9095) (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?paladin))
Glyph
Glyph of Flash of Light - Your Flash of Light has an additional 5% critical strike chance. (Old - Your Flash of Light heals for 50% less initially, but also heals for 140% of its inital effect over 12 sec.)
Glyph of Holy Light - Your Holy Light grants 10% of its heal amount to up to 5 friendly targets within 20 yards of the initial target. (Up from 10 Yards)
Glyph of Hammer of Wrath - Reduces the cost of Hammer of Wrath by 100%. (Old - Increases the range on Hammer of Wrath by 5 yards.)

Items
Libram of Reciprocation (http://www.wowhead.com/?item=40706) now gives Your Judgement of Command spell a chance to grant 173 critical strike rating for 10 sec. (Old - Haste Rating)

Assassination
Envenom Rank 4 and 3 had their tooltip fixed and now show proper the proper damage bonus. (eg. Rank 4 now deals + 216/432/648/864/1080 additional damage for each dose, up from 148/296/444/592/740. Bonus damage from AP remains unchanged)

Enhancement
Mental Quickness now increases your spell power by an amount equal to 23/46/70% of your agility. (Old - 10/20/30% of your attack power, this is temporary and it will be reverted to attack power according to this post (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/28/13393781372-why-the-change-to-mental-quickness.html))

Restoration
Healing Way doesn't stack up to 3 times anymore and now increases the effect of subsequent Healing Wave spells on the target by 18%. (Up from 6%)
Improved Water Shield has been changed to - You have a 33/66/100% chance to instantly consume a Water Shield Orb when you gain a critical effect from your Healing Wave or Riptide spells, and a 19.8/39.6/60% chance when you gain a critical effect from your Lesser Healing Wave spell.

Glyph
Glyph of Windfury Weapon - Increases the chance per swing for Windfury Weapon to trigger by 5%. (Old - The attack power bonus on the additional attacks granted by Windfury Weapon is increased by 40%.)
Glyph of Fire Nova Totem - Reduces the cooldown of your Fire Nova Totem by 3 seconds. (Old - Increases the radius of Fire Nova Totem's effect by 2 yards.)
Glyph of Fire Elemental Totem - Reduces the cooldown of your Fire Elemental Totem by 10 min. (Up from 4 minutes)
Glyph of Astral Recall - Cooldown of your Astral Recall spell reduced by 7.5 min. (Up from 2.5 min)
Glyph of Renewed Life - Your Reincarnation spell no longer requires a reagent. (Old - All stats increased by 5% for 1 min when you Reincarnate.)

Glyphs
Glyph of Bloodrage - Reduces the health cost of your Bloodrage ability by 100%. (Up from 50%)
Glyph of Cleaving - Increases the number of targets your Cleave hits by 1. (Old - Reduces the rage cost of Cleave by 5.)
Glyph of Mortal Strike - Increases the damage of your Mortal Strike ability by 10%. (No longer reduces the healing penalty)
Glyph of Bloodthirst - Increases the healing you receive from your Bloodthirst ability by 100%. (Up from 20%)
Glyph of Whirlwind - Reduces the cooldown of your Whirlwind by 2 sec. (Old - Increases the number of targets your Whirlwind ability hits by 1.)
Glyph of Victory Rush - Your Victory Rush ability has a 30% increased critical strike chance against targets above 70% health. (Old - 90% health)
Glyph of Rending - Increases the duration of your Rend ability by 6 sec. (Up from 3 sec)
