Allied Races - Nightborne
Thalyssra (https://wow.gamepedia.com/First_Arcanist_Thalyssra) and the Nightborne managed to quell their addiction to the Nightwell (https://wow.gamepedia.com/Nightwell) that had plagued them inside their protective barrier in Suramar (https://wow.gamepedia.com/Suramar) for over 10,000 years. When the Legion came, the leaders of their race made a pact that created civil war. Now liberated, the Nightborne have found common ground with the Horde and plan to fight for them.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/thumb/ar11.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/ar11.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/thumb/ar12.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/ar12.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/thumb/ar13.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/ar13.jpg)


Unlocking the Nightborne
To unlock the Nightborne Allied Race, the following achievements must be completed on one character on your account. Once done, you can begin a short questline to actually unlock the race. You will then be able to make a Nightborne on any server that you have a 110 character provided you have purchased Battle for Azeroth.


Insurrection (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/11340-insurrection) - Complete the Insurrection story in Suramar.
The Nightfallen (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/10778-the-nightfallen) - Exalted with The Nightfallen (http://www.wowdb.com/factions/1859-the-nightfallen)

Racials
The Nightborne racial abilities give you inscription skill, magical damage, mail access, a damaging snare, and reduced magical damage taken.


https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_AncientHistory.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_AncientHistory.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_ArcaneAffinity.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_ArcaneAffinity.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_Cantrips.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_Cantrips.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_MagicalResistance.jpg (https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Ability_Racial_MagicalResistance.jpg) https://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/november/Achievement_AlliedRace_Nightborne.jpg


Ancient History (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255663-ancient-history) - Inscription skill increased by 15.
Arcane Affinity (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255665-magical-affinity) - Increases magical damage 1%.
Cantrips (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255661-cantrips) - Conjure up an Eldritch Grimoire, allowing you mail access for 1.5 minutes.
Arcane Pulse (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/260364-arcane-pulse) - Deals 5000 Arcane damage to nearby enemies every 2 seconds and snares them by 50%. Lasts 20 sec.
Arcane Resistance (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/255664-arcane-resistance) - Reduces magical damage taken by 1%.
