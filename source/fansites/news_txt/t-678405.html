Achievements
Feats of Strength have been added to the game, Invincible (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2009/october/flyinghorse_updated.jpg) is now a confirmed drop from Arthas!

Icecrown achievements have been updated and do not look like placeholders anymore.

Full House (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4611/) - Defeat Lady Deathwhisper with at least five different types of Cultists active at the time of her demise in 25-player mode.
I'm on a Boat (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4612/) - Claim victory in the Gunship Battle without any raid member visiting the enemy gunship more than once in 25-player mode.
I've Gone and Made a Mess (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4613/) - Defeat the Deathbringer before Mark of the Fallen Champion is cast three times in 25-player mode.

The Orb Whisperer (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4617/) - Defeat the Blood Council without anyone in the raid taking more than 22,000 spell damage in a single hit.
Once Bitten, Twice Shy (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4618/) - Defeat the Blood Queen once as a vampire and again without becoming a vampire in 25-player mode.
Portal Jockey (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4619/) - Enter every portal spawned by Valithria Dreamwalker before healing her to full health in 25-player mode.
All You Can Eat (25 player) (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/4620/) - Defeat Sindragosa in 25-player mode without any member of your raid receiving more than 4 stacks of Mystic Buffet.
