PvP
what's the purpose of making people start over with prestige levels? Why not just continue gaining xp/prest after 50?
We want the decision to Prestige to be optional, but not trivial. If everyone does it, it's not nearly as "Prestigious." (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/677296609340723200))

Stay true to the idea of a tank being hit; make Taunt give penalties for not hitting tank, such as reduced damage dealt.
We don't think the fantasy of "I get hit" works well in PvP. Prefer to work on the "I'm on the front line" fantasy instead. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/677564696514310144))

Curious how you landed on 0.1%/ilvl. What was the target goal? Is 0.2% or 0.5%/ilvl too much?
It's based on the full spectrum of item levels in the tier. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/677564898881089537))

Looks fun!Will stat nullification(reallocation) activate upon entering pvp combat in open world or only in instance pvp?
Only in instanced PvP. (WarcraftDevs (https://twitter.com/WarcraftDevs/status/677565040728236032))
