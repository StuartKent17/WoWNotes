Professions changes
Fishing
The casting time of most items Aquadynamic Fish Attractor, etc ... applied to your Fishing pole has been lowered from 5 to 2 seconds.

Alchemy
Elixir of Shadow Power http://www.wowhead.com/?item=9264 now last 1 hour. Up from 30 minutes
Arcane Elixir http://www.wowhead.com/?item=9155 now last 1 hour. Up from 30 minutes
Elixir of Firepower http://www.wowhead.com/?item=6373 now last 1 hour. Up from 30 minutes

Enchanting
The following enchant have been changed from "Enchant Weapon" to "Enchant 2H Weapon"
Enchant 2H Weapon - Massacre - Permanently enchant a two-handed weapon to increase attack power by 110. Requires a level 60 or higher item.
Enchant 2H Weapon - Greater Savagery - Permanently enchant a two-handed weapon to increase attack power by 85. Requires a level 60 or higher item.
Enchant 2H Weapon - Scourgebane - Permanently enchant a two-handed melee weapon to increase attack power against Undead by 140. Requires a level 60 or higher item.


Glyph Changes
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter 
Glyph of Aspect of the Monkey - While Aspect of the Monkey or Aspect of the Dragonhawk are active, each time you dodge you gain 30% increased movement speed for 6 sec. This speed does not stack with other movement speed increasing effects. Old - Didn't include Aspect of the Dragonhawk

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin 
Glyph of Crusader Strike - Reduces the mana cost of your Crusader Strike ability by 20%. Old - Your Crusader strike deals 20% more damage when your target is incapacitated or stunned.
