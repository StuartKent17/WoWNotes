Class Adjustments - February 6
With realm maintenance in each region, starting tomorrow (February 6), we plan to apply a few class tuning changes via hotfix. Details below.

Classes

Beast Mastery
All damage increased by 2.5%.
Serpentstalker Guise 4-piece bonus now causes Kill Command to reduce the cooldown of Aspect of the Wild by 3 seconds (was 2.5 seconds).

Fire All damage increased by 3%.

https://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/277-Warlock) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/warlock) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#k) / Artifact Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/artifact-calculator#lAAAAAAAA) / PvP Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/pvp-talent-calculator#k))

Affliction Corruption, Agony damage reduced by 6%. Long Dark Night of the Soul now increases healing you receive from Drain Soul by 3% per rank (was 10%).

Player versus Player
All of the above class changes are offset in PvP situations.

Allied Race Reputation
With the release of Allied Races, we wanted to take a quick look at ways to earn reputation for each of the factions required to unlock a race.


When the Darkmoon Faire (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/6665-Darkmoon-Faire-Guide) is in town, ride the carosuel for WHEE! (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/46668-whee) or purchase a Darkmoon Top Hat (http://www.wowdb.com/items/93730-darkmoon-top-hat)
Take advantage of the upcoming World Quest Bonus Event (http://www.wowdb.com/world-events/592-world-quest-bonus-event) for 50% more reputation from world quests
Argus rare mobs have a very low chance to drop Demon's Soulstone (http://www.wowdb.com/items/153113-demons-soulstone)


Lightforged Draenei
Unlocking the Lightforged Draenei (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7367-Battle-for-Azeroth-Allied-Races-Lightforged-Draenei-Preview) requires Exalted with Army of the Light (http://www.wowdb.com/factions/2165-army-of-the-light).


Complete the Argus quest line, world quests, and Order Hall missions that reward insignias (http://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-search=army+light+insignia).
Complete weekly quests that reward 1000 reputation each: Fuel of a Doomed World (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/48799-fuel-of-a-doomed-world) and Invasion Onslaught (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/49293-invasion-onslaught)


Void Elves
Unlocking the Void Elves (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7363-Battle-for-Azeroth-Allied-Races-Void-Elves-Preview) requires Exalted with Argussian Reach (http://www.wowdb.com/factions/2170-argussian-reach).


Complete the Argus quest line, world quests, and Order Hall missions that reward insignias (http://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-search=Argussian+insignia).
Complete weekly quests that reward 1000 reputation each: Fuel of a Doomed World (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/48799-fuel-of-a-doomed-world) and Invasion Onslaught (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/49293-invasion-onslaught)


Nightborne
Unlocking the Nightborne (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7362-Battle-for-Azeroth-Allied-Races-Nightborne-Preview) requires Exalted with The Nightfallen (http://www.wowdb.com/factions/1859-the-nightfallen).


Complete the Suramar story: Insurrection (http://beta.wowdb.com/achievements/11340-insurrection)
Complete world quests and Order Hall missions that reward insignias (http://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-search=nightfallen+insignia).
Complete the Withered Army Training scenario
Complete the daily Feeding quests (http://www.wowdb.com/quests?filter-patch=20740&filter-xp-val=0&filter-xp-op=3&filter-money-val=0&filter-money-op=3&filter-search=Feed)


Highmountain Tauren
Unlocking the Highmountain Tauren (https://www.mmo-champion.com/content/7365-Battle-for-Azeroth-Allied-Races-Highmountain-Tauren-Preview) requires Exalted with Highmountain Tribe (http://www.wowdb.com/factions/1828-highmountain-tribe).


Complete story quests, world quests, and Order Hall missions that reward insignias (http://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-search=highmountain+insignia).
Do the Legion Invasion quests when Highmountain is up
You can find insignias (http://www.wowdb.com/items?filter-search=highmountain+insignia) in treasure chests around the zone
