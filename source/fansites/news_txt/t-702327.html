World first Shadowmourne by Juggernaut
A few minutes ago, Modk (http://www.wowarmory.com/character-sheet.xml?r=Mal%27Ganis&n=Modk), a Death Knight Juggernaut (http://www.juggernautguild.com/) (US-Mal'ganis) was the first player to complete the questline to rebuild the legendary Shadowmourne (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/49623:3368:3518:3518:3518/shadowmourne/). Congratulations to one lucky DK, and a very supportive guild!


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2010/march/jugg_modk_shadowmourne_small.jpg (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2010/march/jugg_modk_shadowmourne.jpg)

According to Modk, the axe behaves exactly like in the video I posted a while back, except that the proc was slightly changed in a later version of the game, see Item - Shadowmourne Legendary (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/71903/item-shadowmourne-legendary/).
