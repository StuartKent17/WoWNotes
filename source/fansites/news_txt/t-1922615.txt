Winter Veil 2015
If you haven't done so already, check out all of the updates to Winter Veil! 


Completing the new daily quests in your garrison will reward 5 x Merry Supplies  every day.
The Snow Mound  around the daily quest area have a 5% chance to contain Grumpling 
Izzy Hollyfizzle  will sell you different things for your Merry Supplies 

There are 5 different decorations again this holiday, with Little Helpers  arguably being the most useful, as you get the Winter Wondervolt machine in your garrison.
You can have multiple decorations up for a holiday at a time and they even work after the holiday is over, so be sure to collect them all!
Crashin' Thrashin' Killdozer Controller  is a new toy, it also costs 5 x Merry Supplies 
Savage Gift  can contain 300 x Garrison Resources , 300 x Oil , 1000 x Apexis Crystal , Elixir of the Rapid Mind , Medallion of the Legion , Frozen Arms of a Hero , or Minion of Grumpus 

You're a Mean One...  rewards Smokywood Pastures Special Gift , which has a small chance to contain Winter's Grasp  or Flames of Ragnaros 
Iron Armada  is new this year, requiring you to collect this year's gift, Crashin' Thrashin' Killdozer Controller  from the vendor, and other Crashin' Thrashin' Controllers from Tanaan Jungle rare spawns.
Smokywood Pastures Gift Pack  has a 20% chance to contain Fizzy Apple Cider  
Miniature Winter Veil Tree  is now item level 670.

Legion - Increased View Distance
One of the nice features in Legion is the significantly increased maximum view distance. In the comparison that we included, you can see that the view distance is increased, but objects no longer load as far away. This may not be intended, as the Environment Detail setting appears to have no effect on object loading distance right now.
