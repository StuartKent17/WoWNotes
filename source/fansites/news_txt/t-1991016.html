Legion - Elerethe Renferal
Elerethe Renferal (http://wow.gamepedia.com/Elerethe_Renferal) is one of the bosses in the Emerald Nightmare raid.


<iframe width="900" height="506" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/K_vsV1zU5M4?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Elerethe, once a mighty druid and adept shapeshifter, died believing she was betrayed by those she trusted. Her soul now resides in the nightmare, damning her to an existence of fear, confusion, and hatred.


Overview - As the fight progresses, Elerethe swaps between two nightmare forms that determine what abilities she uses. During Spider form, Elerethe uses <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/210242-web-of-pain">Web of Pain</a> on the tanks and on a random pair of dps. Damage received by your partner is reflected back to you. During Roc form, Elerethe uses <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/210948-dark-storm">Dark Storm</a> that creates a safe space on another platform then flies to it. Until the raid reaches that safe space, they take damage. If Elerethe ever walks onto the web, she gains <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/222063-web-walker">Web Walker</a> and will proceed to destroy anyone in her path. In Mythic difficulty, Elerethe activates an additional ability depending on what platform she is currently fighting on.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Damage Dealers - During Spider form, stand apart from the raid during <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/215443-necrotic-venom">Necrotic Venom</a> to place venomous pools.
During Roc form, place <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/210850-twisting-shadows">Twisting Shadows</a> away from the raid.
Kill Venomous Spiderlings.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/healer.png" title="Healer" /> Healers - During Spider form, heal players connected by <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/210242-web-of-pain">Web of Pain</a> .
During Roc form, <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/210948-dark-storm">Dark Storm</a> pulses damage to the raid until they reach the eye of the storm.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Tanks - During Spider form, remain within 20 yards of your partner during <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/210242-web-of-pain">Web of Pain</a>.
During Roc form, taunt the boss off of the tank that was just hit by <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/215582-raking-talons">Raking Talons</a>.
Swap off of the adds when gaining too many stacks of <a href="http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/210229-dripping-fangs">Dripping Fangs</a>.

Elerethe Renferal -

Nightmare Spawn (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/218629) - Continuously summons egg sacs around her location. Egg sacs that have not been popped when Gathering Clouds has begun, release Skitterling Spiderlings inside.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Venomous Spiderling - Venomous Spiderlings appear from the web during the encounter.

Dripping Fangs (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/210229) - Dripping fangs poison the current target and inflict 10,750 Nature damage every 1 sec. This effect stacks.
Venomous Pool (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/213123) - A venomous pool forms, inflicting 250,500 Nature damage every 1 sec.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Swarm (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/214450) - Elerethe draws strength from her minions, increasing her damage dealt by 50% and attack speed by 70% when they are close. This effect stacks.

Surging Egg Sacs - Running into these egg sacs releases a swarm of Skittering Spiderlings.

Skittering Spiderling - These tiny spiders burst forth and attack in numbers when released from their egg sacs.

Spider Form (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/200666) - Takes the form of a nightmare spider.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Web of Pain (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/215288) - Elerethe connects two targets with a web of pain. 100% of damage done to the linked source is reflected to you as Nature Damage.<br/>Reflected damage is increased after the targets seperate beyond 20 yards from each other.<br/>Additionally, targets that run through the beam are inflicted with 187,500 Nature damage and knocked down.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Feeding Time (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/212364) - Elerethe calls her children down for dinner.
Vile Ambush (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/214348) - Drops from above onto her prey, inflicting 3,570,000 Physical damage and knocking back all targets. Damage decreases the further targets are from the point of impact.
Necrotic Venom (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/215443) - Hurls necrotic poison at random enemies that inflicts 46,450 Nature damage every 1 sec and places a debuff on the target that inflicts 50,000 Nature damage to nearby allies each tick.<br/>Also, each pulse leaves behind a venomous pool.
Tangled Webs (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/217983) - Quickly covers the ground with a tacky web, reducing enemy movement speed by 40% and concealing the locations of venomous pools. The web last 1.2 min.

Roc Form (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/35200) - Takes the form of a nightmare roc.

Gathering Clouds (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/212707) - The boss flings quills at all targets for 179,000 Physical damage every 1 second and pushes everyone back.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/important.png" title="Important" /> Dark Storm (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/210948) - Conjures a dark storm and flies to another platform. The storm inflicts 35,700 Shadow damage every 1 sec until targets enter the eye of the storm.

Eye of the Storm (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/211127) - Within the eye, targets are protected from the dark storm.
Foul Winds (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/211137) - Increases damage of the next Dark Storm by 75%. This effect stacks.

Twisting Shadows (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/210864) - The boss causes shadow tornadoes to form on random enemies. After 10 sec tornadoes appear at the enemy destinations that will pull nearby targets into them, inflicting 71,450 Shadow damage every 1 sec for 4 sec.
Razor Wing (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/210547) - The bird buffets its wings, hurling razor feathers that inflict 607,500 Shadow damage to targets in a cone in front of the boss.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Raking Talons (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/215582) - Rakes her talons across her current target, inflicting 1,430,000 Shadow damage.<br/>The boss repeats this cast multiple times in rapid succession.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Violent Winds (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/218124) - Elerethe channels violent winds at her target, pushing them away and inflicting 500,000 Shadow damage every 1 sec to enemies in a line in front of her. This effect increases the damage taken from Violent Winds by 12% per stack. <br/>The amount of force pushing the targets away is reduced for each player in the effect.

Wind Burn (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/218519) - Elerethe channels violent winds at her target, pushing them away and inflicting 500,000 Shadow damage every 1 sec to enemies in a line in front of her. This effect increases the damage taken from Violent Winds by 12% per stack. <br/>The amount of force pushing the targets away is reduced for each player in the effect.
