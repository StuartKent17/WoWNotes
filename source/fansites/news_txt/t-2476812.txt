Brawler's Guild

Fixed an issue that prevented players from completing the "The Brawlpub Trial https://www.wowdb.com/quests/55020-the-brawlpub-trial".


Quests

Fixed an issue that prevented "A Nose for Ptrouble https://www.wowdb.com/quests/50801-a-nose-for-ptrouble" from being completed when Naga World Quests were active.
Warriors will once again be able to use the Dalaran Crater portal while completing the Arms Legion Artifact quest line to earn Strom'kar, the Warbreaker https://www.wowdb.com/items/128910-stromkar-the-warbreaker.
Fixed a bug that caused players to get stuck in the combat phase for "Turning the Tides https://www.wowdb.com/quests/54722-turning-the-tides"
Fixed an issue where Alliance players who had not yet completed certain story quests in Warfang Hold could not complete the faction assault world quest "Ale Intent https://www.wowdb.com/quests/54647-ale-intent".
