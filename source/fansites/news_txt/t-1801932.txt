Patch 6.2 - Garrison Campaign Rewards
Patch 6.2 expands upon the garrison campaign with a new quest line. Everyone with a Tier 3 garrison can and will want to complete at least part of this new line, as it unlocks and rewards several useful things. You will see Yrel, Gul'dan, Vol'jin, Varian and other notable characters.


Unlocks the Shipyard  and Naval missions
Unlocks your faction's hub in Tanann Jungle
Rewards some Garrison Resources  and Oil 
Rewards some reputation with Hand of the Prophet  / Vol'jin's Headhunters 
Rewards several Baleful items: Baleful Treads , Baleful Tunic , Baleful Gauntlets , Baleful Girdle 
Rewards an Empowered Apexis Fragment , allowing you to create a 695 Baleful item
Rewards an epic follower: Oronok Torn-heart  Demonic Knowledge 
Rewards the Shard of Cyrukh  Be sure to loot it from the Forgotten Shard of the Cipher  after finishing The Cipher of Damnation
