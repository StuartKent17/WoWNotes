Achievements Changes
Player vs. Player
Tol Barad

Tol Barad Veteran  now requires players to win 25 battles in Tol Barad, down from 100.



Spell Changes

 Shaman Forums  / Talent Calculator  / Skills/Talents 
Elemental

Shamanism  now gives Lightning Bolt, Chain Lightning, and Lava Burst an additional 36% benefit from spell power, up from 32%.


Alchemy

Alchemy: Draught of War  now restores 22500 to 27500 health and 9250 to 10750 mana, up from 7200 to 8800 health and 7200 to 8800 mana.
Alchemy: Mighty Rejuvenation Potion  now restores 9000 to 11000 mana and health, up from 7200 to 8800. Now requires 1 x Volatile Life 
Alchemy: Mysterious Potion  now restores 1 to 50000 health and 1 to 20000 mana, up from 1 to 30000 health and 1 to 15000 mana.
Alchemy: Mythical Healing Potion  now restores 22500 to 27500 health, up from 18900 to 23100. No longer requires Volatile Life , now requires 1 x Twilight Jasmine , down from 2. 
Alchemy: Mythical Mana Potion  now requires 1 x Whiptail , down from 2.
