Small update on leveling
A lot of people have been reporting lower experience requirements from level 60 to 70, this is an intended change !
Yes, we're planning on speeding up leveling in the 60-70 range. Keep the feedback coming. We are still adjusting it. Let us know what levels you are in what areas in this bracket.


Halls of Stone - Loot Table
Finally, we've got the first items dropping from the Halls of Stone (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=796) in Storm Peaks. This instance is tuned for LVL 77 to 79 players and will probably offer the first alternatives to Sunwell loot. The first weapon we discovered, Unrelenting Blade (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/wotlk/items/rare/rareitem_065.jpg) reaches 117 DPS and is bind on equip ! as a comparison [item=34329] drops on Kil'jaeden and goes "only" as high as 114.2 DPS.
