It's All About Pepe: New In-Game Outfits and Plush
You can finally find Pepe!


<div class="gallery" id="gallery-pepeloc">http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/thumb/pepem01.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/pepem01.jpg) http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/thumb/pepem02.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/pepem02.jpg) http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/thumb/pepem03.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/pepem03.jpg) http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/thumb/pepem04.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/pepem04.jpg) </div>

There’s something about Pepe that has everyone all aflutter, and today we have a bunch more reasons for you to love him.

First, we’ve added a Pepe plush to the Blizzard Gear Store*! He’s soft, squishy, loveable, and can clip onto a hat or hairband (or pretty much anywhere else) to go with you anywhere Pepe wants to go.

Get your own Pepe now! (http://blizz.ly/1GEOw84)


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/thumb/pepe01.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/pepe01.jpg) http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/thumb/pepe02.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/pepe02.jpg) http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/thumb/pepe03.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/pepe03.jpg)

But wait—there's more!

Pepe's Brand New Clothes
Several new in-game outfits for Pepe are also now available for you to track down. Collecting them all will reward the I Found Pepe! (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/10053-i-found-pepe) achievement. We’ve written some clues—and more than a couple of bad rhymes—to get you started on the right path to locating each one. (We’re not even a little sorry for it. Screenshots below not indicative of his in-game location. Good luck!)

Knight Pepe – Talador
Sir Pepe is a knight, you see, but every warrior needs a rest. You’ll find him half-asleep right now near a pile of amythest.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/thumb/pepe04.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/pepe04.jpg)

Ninja Pepe – Gorgrond
What killed these iron hordies, you thought you knew—but alas, you were wrong. Turns out it was the stealthy Ninja Pepe all along.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/thumb/pepe05.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/pepe05.jpg)

Pirate Pepe – Spires of Arak
Pirate Pepe flew down south to visit his feathered friends. You’ll find him there relaxing in some arrakoa ru-ins.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/thumb/pepe06.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/pepe06.jpg)

Viking Pepe – Nagrand
This Pepe will be easy for anyone to spot. Viking Pepe always thought his dancing moves were hot.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/thumb/pepe07.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2015/october/pepe07.jpg)

Join the celebration of our Pepe overlord! Share your Pepe love and screenshots with the @Warcraft (http://twitter.com/warcraft) Twitter using the hashtag #IFoundPepe all day today.
