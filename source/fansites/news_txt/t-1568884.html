Warlords of Draenor - Garrison Stables
One of the buildings you can put on a large plot in your garrison is the Stables (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/65-stables#related:buildings). The stables will show off mounts that you have set as favorite mounts, allowing them to wander around to different stacks of hay.

There are five other buildings (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/65-stables#related:same-plot) that can go in large plots, with a total of two plots available at garrison level 3. As with every other building, there are three building tiers, which give these bonuses:


Tier 1 - Enables the capture and training of special mounts on Draenor.
Tier 2 - You may remain mounted while interacting with objects in outdoor Draenor zones.
Tier 3 - Increases mount speed in Draenor by 20%.

Warlords of Draenor Beta Patch Notes Update - August 8
Raid Utility Balance

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/images.gif Mage

Amplify Magic is a new spell available to Mages.

Amplify Magic amplifies the effects of helpful magic, increasing all healing received by 10% 12% for all party and raid members within 100 yards, and lasts 6 seconds with a 2-minute cooldown.


Self-Sustainability

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior

Victory Rush (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/34428) and Impending Victory (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/103840) now heals for 25% of maximum health (up from 15%).




Class Changes

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ideathknights.gif Death Knight
Ability Consolidation and Refinement

Death Coil (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/47541) now costs 30 Runic Power (down from 40 Runic Power), and has a 40 yard range for both hostiles and allies.


Blood Changes

Rune Tap (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/48982) has been redesigned. It now reduces all damage taken by 50% for 3 seconds. It also now has 2 charges, with a 40-second recharge time.

Will of the Necropolis (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/81164) has been redesigned. It now automatically triggers an immediate, free Rune Tap (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/48982) when you take damage that reduces you below 30% health. This effect cannot occur more often than once every 30 45 seconds.

Veteran of the Third War (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/50029) now increases your critical strike chance, Multistrike chance, haste, and Stamina by 10% (up from 9% to Stamina only), reduces the chance for attacks to be parried by 3%, increases the damage of Death Strike (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/49998) by 100%, and grants 1 Runic Power per second while in combat.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid
Ability Pruning

Barkskin (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/22812) is now available to Balance, Guardian, and Restoration Druids. and no longer provides pushback protection, but its base cooldown has been reduced to 30 seconds.
Mangle (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/33878) (Bear Form (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/5487)) now naturally applies the Infected Wounds (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/48484) snare effect for Feral and Guardian Druids.
Rake (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/1822) now also stuns the target for 4 seconds if used while stealthed, and is only available to Feral Druids.


Ability Consolidation and Refinement

Cat Form (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/768) now increases movement speed by 30% (up from 25%).
Infected Wounds (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/48484) has been removed and its effects have been incorporated into Mangle (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/33878) and Shred (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/5221) for Feral and Guardian Druids.
Shred (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/5221) no longer requires the Druid to be behind the target (See also: Facing Requirements.) and is now available to all specializations. Shred (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/5221) now deals 35% more damage in addition to having double the normal chance to critically strike while stealthed. Additionally, Shred (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/5221) now naturally applies the Infected Wounds (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/48484) snare effect for Feral and Guardian Druids.
Thick Hide (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/16931) has been removed and its effects have been incorporated into Bear Form (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/5487).

Bear Form (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/5487) now increases armor by 330% 600% for all Druid specializations, and no longer increases Haste and Critical Strike from items by 50%, but instead causes Haste to reduce the global cooldown

Tiger’s Fury now lasts 8 seconds (up from 6 seconds) and is usable during Berserk (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/106952).


Guardian Changes

Maul (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/6807) now costs 20 Rage (down from 30 Rage).
Mangle (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/33878) now generates 10 Rage (down from 15 Rage).
Lacerate (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/33745) now generates 2 5 Rage, has no cooldown (down from a 3-second cooldown), and the periodic bleed effect triggers every 1 second (down from 3 seconds).
Thrash (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/106830) now generates 1 2 Rage every time it deals direct or periodic damage, has no cooldown (down from a 6-second cooldown), but no longer has a chance to reset Mangle (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/33878) cooldown.


Restoration Changes

Living Seed (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/48500) now heals for 50% of the critical heal (up from 30%).


Talents

Nature’s Vigil, while active, increases single-target damage and healing caused by healing spells by 30% 16% (up down from 25%), and all single-target damage spells also heal a nearby friendly target for 40% 35% of the damage done (up from 25%).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter
Marksmanship Changes

Bombardment (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/35110) now reduces the cost of Multi-Shot (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/2643) by 25 Focus (up from 20 Focus).
Chimera Shot (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/53209) has been renamed Chimaera Shot. It is now a two-headed shot that hits two targets, costs 35 Focus (down from 45 Focus), and deals Frost or Nature damage, but no longer heals the Hunter.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/images.gif Mage
Arcane Changes

Arcane Missiles (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/5143) can now accumulate 3 charges (up from 2 charges).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/imonks.gif Monk
Brewmaster Changes

Dizzying Haze (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/115180) now has no Energy cost, but no longer causes the target's melee attacks to sometimes misfire.
Elusive Brew (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/115308) now increases dodge chance by 35% (up from 30%).
Stance of the Sturdy Ox (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/115069) now increases Stamina by 40% (up from 20%).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin
Ability Consolidation and Refinement

Hand of Sacrifice (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/6940) is now off global-cooldown.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest
Ability Consolidation and Refinement

Holy Fire (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/14914) now lasts 9 seconds (up from 7 seconds).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/irogues.gif Rogue
Ability Consolidation and Refinement

Burst of Speed (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/108212) now costs 25 20 Energy (up from 15 Energy).
Envenom (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/32645) now increases poison chance by 30% (up from 15%).
Venomous Wounds (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/79134)’s effects have been merged into baseline Rupture (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/1943) for Assassination Rogues. It no longer triggers from Garrote (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/703) and now always triggers when Rupture (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/1943) deals damage (up from a 75% chance).


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman
Ability Consolidation and Refinement

Echo of the Elements (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/108283) has been redesigned. The Shaman's spells and abilities have a chance to trigger Echo of the Elements (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/108283), causing their next short-cooldown spell or ability to not trigger a cooldown.

Elemental: It may be used on Earth Shock (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/8042), Flame Shock (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/8050), Frost Shock (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/8056), Earthquake (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/61882), or Lava Burst (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/51505).
Enhancement: It may be used on Earth Shock (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/8042), Flame Shock (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/8050), Frost Shock (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/8056), Fire Nova (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/1535), Lava Lash (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/60103), or Stormstrike (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/17364).
Restoration: It may be used on Unleash Life (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/73685), Purify Spirit (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/77130), or Riptide (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/61295).

Lava Lash (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/60103) now spreads Flame Shock (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/8050) to up to 6 nearby enemies (up from 4 enemies).


Elemental and Enhancement Changes

Feral Spirit (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/51533)’ damage has been increased by 300% 100%.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarlocks.gif Warlock
Ability Pruning

Flames of Xoroth (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/120451) has been removed.
Drain Life is no longer available to Destruction Warlocks.

Harvest Life has been replaced with a new talent called Searing Flames for Destruction Warlocks.

Searing Flames reduces Ember Tap's Burning Ember cost by 50%, and increases its healing by 50%.



Demonology Changes

Demon pets controlled by Demonology Warlocks now deal 20% more damage.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior
Talent Changes

Piercing Howl (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/12323) has been removed and replaced with a new talent, Sudden Death (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/29725).

Sudden Death (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/29725) causes auto-attack hits to have a 10% chance to make the next Execute (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/5308) free and useable on any target, regardless of health level. These Execute (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/5308) do not consume additional Rage to deal additional damage.
Piercing Howl (http://beta.wowdb.com/spells/12323) is now available to all Fury Warriors as a baseline ability.

Warlords of Draenor hands-on with the live beta featuring updated character models and the intro experience for the upcoming expansion.
