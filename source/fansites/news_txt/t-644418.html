Knock Knock Knock on Wood by Inner Sanctum
Inner Sanctum (http://innersanctum.eu) just completed Heroic: Knock, Knock, Knock on Wood (http://ptr.wowhead.com/?achievement=3187), the hard mode version of Freya (+ 3 Adds). That's the 2nd Hard Mode encounter they beat this week (the first one was Thorim (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?topic=49671.0)).

The Hall of Fame (http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?page=864) has been updated.

Loot
