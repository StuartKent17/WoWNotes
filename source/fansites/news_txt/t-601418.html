Today was the first day of the European Arena Tournament finals in Hamburg. I'll update this post with the latest results as fast as possible. The ranking layout was highly inspired from the one on Team-AAA.com (http://www.team-aaa.com/)

Update 16:00 : Actually it seems that http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/flags/se.gif SK-Gaming won the tournament. (Want to buy german translators next time, thanks.)
Update 14:15 : http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/flags/se.gif SK-Gaming won the 2nd round.
Update 13:50 : http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/flags/se.gif Log (Curse) won the first round of the finals (3-1).
Update 12:50 : http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/flags/se.gif Log (Curse) won the Lower Brackets finals against http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/flags/europeanunion.gif Nashwan. The last match will be http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/flags/se.gif Log vs http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/flags/se.gif SK-Gaming.

Final Ranking
#1 - http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/flags/se.gif SK-Gaming - 3000$ + Qualified for the Blizzcon.
#2 - http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/flags/se.gif Log (Curse) - 2000$ + Qualified for the Blizzcon.
