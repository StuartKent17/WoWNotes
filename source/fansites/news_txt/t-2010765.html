Warcraft Movie - Box Office Update
The Warcraft Movie continues to do well internationally, with Deadline posting (http://deadline.com/2016/06/warcraft-conjuring-2-x-men-apocalypse-ninja-turtles-jungle-book-international-box-office-weekend-results-1201771160/) updated numbers:

It also set the record for fastest to 1B rmb, beating the old record holder, Furious 7.
Warcraft also set a new record for IMAX showings, making $20M and passing the old $17M record held by Ultron.
The international total is $261M and domestic is $24M, for a total of $286M so far.

Build of the Month - June 2016
Each month or so we will post a couple of hardware setups for those of you who are thinking of upgrading their computer!

This month
Important: The GTX 1080 ($700) (http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B01FWI7J7G?ie=UTF8&tag=mmochamp-20) and GTX 1070 ($450) are here. Unfortunately stock is very limited, resulting in expensive cards if you can find them. New cards from AMD are also on the way. Nicer versions of the 1080 and 1070 are also coming soon, with a lower price and better coolers. If you aren't in a hurry, it is worth waiting for price drops and new cards.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2016/june/nvidiaBenchmarks.png?v2 (https://www.techpowerup.com/reviews/NVIDIA/GeForce_GTX_1070/21.html)

The current SSD prices (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2016/june/ssdgraph.gif) are fairly flat right now, with most SSDs under $0.50 per gigabyte when on sale.

Depending on how much you want to overclock, you may need to spend a bit more for a better CPU cooler like the NH-D15 (http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00L7UZMAK?ie=UTF8&tag=mmochamp-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B00L7UZMAK).

Unless you are doing a lot of video rendering or streaming, you do not need the 6700K and should spend that money elsewhere.

Make sure to keep your AMD (http://sites.amd.com/us/game/downloads/Pages/downloads.aspx) and Nvidia (http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us) drivers up to date.

Don't forget to visit the Computer Forum (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/290-Computer) for any extra questions!
