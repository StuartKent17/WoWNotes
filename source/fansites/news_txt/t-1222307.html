Cinder Kitten Charity Drive for Superstorm Sandy Coming Soon
Support the Red Cross’s Superstorm Sandy Response (https://www.redcross.org/donate/index.jsp?donateStep=2&itemId=prod10002) relief efforts and adopt a Cinder Kitten when this fiery feline arrives in the Blizzard Store later this year. Your support will enable the Red Cross to provide shelter, food, emotional support, and other assistance to survivors of disasters like Superstorm Sandy with 100% of the $10 USD adoption fee during this charity drive going toward this worthy cause.

Patch 5.0.5 Hotfixes - Nov 6
Classes
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/imonks.gif Monk (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/341-Monk) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/monk) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#o))

The base healing of Chi Torpedo (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/124040) has been increased, but it now scales less efficiently with attack power.
Keg Smash (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/121253) now deals progressively less damage when it strikes more than 5 targets.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipriests.gif Priest (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/274-Priest) / Skills (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/class-abilities/priest) / Talent Calculator (http://www.wowdb.com/talent-calculator#U))

Rapture (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/47536) now returns mana equal to 200% of the Priest's total Spirit (was 150%).
The shield generated by Divine Aegis (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/47515) now absorbs 50% of the amount healed (was 30%).


Creatures

Monstrous Plainshawks (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/59641-monstrous-plainshawk) are now more peace loving.
Springtail Trappers (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/59513-springtail-trapper), Springtail Burrowers (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/59514-springtail-burrower), and Springtail Thumpers (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/66229-springtail-thumper) are now marginally less annoying.


Items

The Malevolent Gladiator's Ripper (http://www.wowdb.com/items/85128-malevolent-gladiators-ripper) can now be purchased for the correct number of Conquest Points.


Dungeons & Raids
Heart of Fear

The Raid Finder version of Garalon (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/63191-garalon) is now tuned more appropriately for Raid Finder groups. His health has been significantly reduced, and Furious Swipe (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/122735-furious-swipe) now deals significantly less damage.
Wind Lord Mel'jarak's (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/62397-wind-lord-meljarak) health has been reduced slightly in the 10 player normal mode version of this encounter.
The Sha of Fear's (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/60999-sha-of-fear) Ominous Cackle (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/119593-ominous-cackle) will no longer sometimes send players to the same platform twice in a row.
Grand Empress Shek'zeer (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/62837-grand-empress-shekzeer) will no longer become confused about what phase of the battle she's in.
Imperial Vizier Zor'lok (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/62980-imperial-vizier-zorlok)

Visions of Demise (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/124862-visions-of-demise) now inflicts damage immediately once cast.
The Imperial Vizier can no longer be attacked until all the foes in the Oratorium are slain.
Zealous Bug (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/64405-zealous-bug) will no longer spawn in the Raid Finder version of this encounter.

Amber-Shaper Un'sok (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/62511-amber-shaper-unsok)

Volatile Amber pools will now properly eject more bolts of Volatile Amber in the 25 player versions of this encounter.
The health of Amber-Shaper Un'sok, Living Amber, and Amber Monstrosities has been increased in Heroic mode.
The Amber Explosion (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/122402-amber-explosion) cast by transformed players now does the correct amount of damage when striking more than 10 targets.


Mogu'shan Vaults

Elegon's Total Annihilation (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/129711-total-annihilation) will no longer penetrate immunity effects in the normal mode version of this encounter.


Bug Fixes

Wiping on Imperial Vizier Zor'lok or defeating the enemies preceding him in the Oratorium will no longer sometimes cause players to become raid locked to the Heart of Fear.
Tooltips for Crystallized Dread (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/89873-crystallized-dread), Crystallized Horror (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/89882-crystallized-horror), and Crystallized Terror (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/89881-crystallized-terror) have been updated.
The speed increase provided by the Druid ability Stampeding Roar (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/106898-stampeding-roar) no longer stacks with the speed increases provided by Cat Form or Travel Form.
Battleground speed power ups will no longer sometimes incorrectly stack with player movement speed increases.
Mana Tea (http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/115294-mana-tea) channeling will no longer sometimes be interrupted when friendly players consume the Monk's Healing Spheres.
