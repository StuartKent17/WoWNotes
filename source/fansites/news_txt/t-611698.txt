Patch Note Update Additions
The official patch notes http://blue.mmo-champion.com/1/8765581972-wrath-of-the-lich-king-beta-patch-notes.html have been updated with a bunch of undocumented changes we didn't report yet. 
General
Spell casting and spell channelling pushback has been changed to the following:
When casting a spell:
The first and second hit will add .5 secs each to the cast time.
All hits after the second will have no effect.



When channelling a spell:
The first and second hit reduces current duration by 25% of total duration each.
All hits after the second will have no effect.

Blood
Blood Strike damage is now only increased by the death knight's diseases
Pestilence now spreads to 3 targets instead of 2 but isn't affected by attack power anymore. 



Frost
Frost Strike now causes 100% of weapon damage instead of 50%, the amount of additional frost damage has been increased greatly 252.5 to 416 for rank 5. The ability doesn't do double damage to Frozen targets anymore and now costs 50 Runic Power. 
Howling Blast now deals double damage to targets infected with Frost Fever instead of triple damage to Frozen targets.
Obliterate now causes 50% additional damage for each of the death knight's diseases on the target. Cost changed from 1 Blood 1 Frost to 1 Unholy 1 Frost
Icy Touch now infects the target with Frost Fever. 
Empower Rune Weapon now activates all your runes and generate 25 runic power. Cooldown changed from 3 minutes to 5 minutes.
Chains of Ice spell cost changed from 1 Unholy 1 Frost to 1 Frost. 
Mind Freeze doesn't deal damage anymore. 



Unholy
Corpse Explosion cost changed from 60 Runic Power to 1 Unholy.
Death Coil cost changed from 40 Runic Power to 50 Runic Power. 
Death Strike now deals 60% weapon damage plus 200 and heals the death knight for 100% of the damage done for each of his hor her diseases on the target.
Death and Decay damage has been increased but isn't affected by attack power anymore. Targets aren't diseased anymore, now produces a high amount of threat. Cost changed from 2 Unholy to 1 Unholy 1 Blood 1 Frost. 
Plague Strike now deals 60% weapon damage and infects the target with Blood Plague, a disease dealing Shadow Damage over time. The strike removes one heal over time effect from the target.
Unholy Blight damage increased 76 to 152 for Rank 4 and now inflicts Blood Plague. Persists for 1 sec per 5 runic power instead of 10 
Anti-Magic Shell now lasts 5 seconds instead of 3.

Build 8770 - Professions Update
New bonus skills have been added to tailoring and alchemy. 

Blacksmithing
Socket Bracer
Permanently add a socket to your bracers. Requires a level 60 or higher item.

Socket Gloves
Permanently add a socket to your gloves. Requires a level 60 or higher item.

New items
See also Titanium Belt Buckle http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/august/titaniumbuckle.jpg and the other new epic weapons / armors in the Build 8770 Changes http://www.mmo-champion.com/index.php?topic=12440 post.

Tailoring
Lightweave Embroidery
Embroiders a subtle pattern of light into your cloak, giving you a chance to cause an additional 800 to 1000 Holy damage when you damage an enemy with a spell.
You can only embroider your own cloak and embroidering your cloak will cause it to become soulbound.

Darkglow Embroidery
Embroiders a magical pattern into your cloak, giving you a chance to restore 300 mana when you cast a spell.
You can only embroider your own cloak and embroidering your cloak will cause it to become soulbound.

Swordguard Embroidery
Embroiders a magical pattern into your cloak, causing your damaging melee attacks to sometimes ignore 1000 of your target's armor.
You can only embroider your own cloak and embroidering your cloak will cause it to become soulbound.

Herbalism
Wild Growth
Embrace the natural energies surrounding you, healing you for 2000 over 15 sec. 300/480/720/900/1200/2000 Depending on Rank. Instant, 5 min cooldown
