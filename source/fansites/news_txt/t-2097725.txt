chaud2016-10-24, 04:48 PMPatch 7.1 Live This Week
Patch 7.1 goes live this week! It brings the Karazhan dungeon, Trial of Valor raid, a continuation of the Suramar story, new pets, mounts, achievements and much more! Keep in mind that Trial of Valor will not be open for another two weeks.

Karazhan - Mythic Dungeon
We will Return to Karazhan  in Patch 7.1! An updated version of Karazhan with some new encounters has been added as a Mythic dungeon. Don't worry though, the old version of Karazhan is sticking around so you can keep trying for Fiery Warhorse's Reins 


  

Quests

Attunement will primarily involve Mythic dungeon content  Source 
Master of the House  and The Tower of Power  are two new Karazhan quests.


Items

Loot ranges from item level 855 to 860, with 875 loot from the final boss. 
The Violet Seals have returned ! They come in an item level 850, 855, 860, and 875 version. The 875 version has the Karazhan teleport  
The chest items in Karazhan have a Chest Armor Bonus  that interacts with 
There are two trinkets with effects that work together: Mrrgria's Favor  and Toe Knee's Promise 
An updated version of Ruby Slippers  has been added: Astonishingly Scarlet Slippers 
There is a new item set: Wine-Stained Mantle  and Amulet of the Last Guardian 
Two new mounts come from Karazhan: Midnight's Eternal Reins  and Smoldering Ember Wyrm 
You can complete a quest line to acquire Moroes  as a follower. He has the Dinner is Served  ability. 
The Blazing Ember Signet  toy drops from Nightbane. 



Karazhan Exterior Updated
The Legion is invading the area of Deadwind Pass around Karazhan. 


      


Blood of Sargeras Trader
Visit Illnea Bloodthorn  in Dalaran to trade in your Blood of Sargeras  for trade goods and Order Resources  


  


PvP Changes
Patch 7.1 makes several changes to PvP:


Honor talents are no longer lost when you Prestige More 
The amount of Artifact Power you will earn from a PvP victory is displayed in the UI 
You will earn extra Honor and Artifact Power for your first win of the day in all unrated and rated PvP activities
Rated and Unrated Battleground wins will reward a piece of gear. Losses have a chance to reward gear based on the score.
Skirmish and Arena victories will have an increased chance to drop gear.
The item level of PvP gear now more closely mirrors the type of item level progression you see from World Quests
Each rated PvP bracket 2v2, 3v3, Rated Battlegrounds has a weekly quest that rewards items based on your best rating from the previous week. 
The quests for Arena brackets will require 10 wins, and the quests for the Rated Battleground bracket require three wins.
Below 2000, the quests reward Gladiator appearance gear and above 2000 they reward Elite appearance gear. 



  


Nagrand Arena Visual Update
Nagrand Arena has received visual updates in this patch! Gameplay should still be the same. 


      


Quick Join
Patch 7.1 allows you to join friends that are in the queue for different activities. You select a role, ask to join, and then are in the queue with them! 


  


Return to Alcaz Isle
Alcaz Isle  has been invaded by Dr. Weavil and the Drudge Gnomes! 


The island is now full of strong sentries and NPCs, as well as Dr. Weavil himself!
You won't be able to fly there, as the island has anti-air guns that will dismount you.
Dr. Weavil drops the Big Red Raygun  toy
He also has Machine Fluid  and Drudge Fluid  for your pets
Three new mechanical spiders to tame: Copper Arachnodrone , Silver Arachnodrone , and Golden Arachnodrone 
These spiders require the Electrified Key  from Dr. Weavil
A new color of mechanical guard dog to tame: Model GU-4RD-3R

Other Additions
There are lots of smaller new things in this patch: 

Quests

The Helarjar World Quests are unlocked with the A Call to Action  quest chain, granting access to new world quests
Illidan's story continues, with The Hunt for Illidan Stormrage  and Soul Prism of the Illidari  

Lingering Soul Fragment  will take some time to collect, as you need 80 and there is a weekly cap of 10.
There are some interesting details in the quest descriptions!

Something's Not Quite Right... 


Toys, Pets, and Mounts

Several new toys  were added
Many new battle pets  were added, along with the Raiding with Leashes IV: Wrath of the Lick King  achievement and Stardust 
Four falcosaur pets  were added, complete with a quest chain  that eventually leads to access to the falcosaur mounts: Predatory Bloodgazer , Viridian Sharptalon , Snowfeather Hunter , Brilliant Direbeak 


Achievements

Completing Murky quests rewards a new achievement: No Shellfish Endeavor 
Completing the PvP world quests 20 times each rewards a PvP mount: Free For All, More For Me 
A new legacy achievement: Sul'thraze the Lasher , requiring Sul'thraze the Lasher 


UI / Addons

The slider for max camera zoom was removed from the UI options. You can increase it with "/console cameraDistanceMaxZoomFactor 2.6"
Patch 7.1 will remove some the ability for addons to access information about player position. 

You can see the full list of changes here 
You can read about the impact this will have on DBM here 

The Broken Isles loading screen has had a slight change 



Talon's Vengeance
Talon's Vengeance  is a new faction in Patch 7.1. It isn't clear how it is unlocked, but many of the rewards are PvP related. One item earned from PvP kills grants Mark of Prey , increasing your reputation with Talon's Vengeance.

Falcosaur Pets and Mounts
Four Falcosaur pets and mounts were added in this patch! You can find more details on Quintessence's blog 


  

Falcosaurs

For each of the quests below, go to the world quest, kill the Matriarch, and then find the orphaned hatchling. 
Be sure to have the correct food in your inventory or the hatchling won't interact with you.

Bloodgazer Swarm! Azsuna  - Requires Azsunian Grapes  and rewards Bloodgazer Hatchling 
Sharptalon Swarm! Val'sharah  - Requires Dried Bilberries  and rewards Sharptalon Hatchling 
Direbeak Swarm! Stormheim  - Requires Pungent Vrykul Gamalost  and rewards Direbeak Hatchling 
Snowfeather Swarm! Highmountain  - Requires Smoked Elderhorn  and rewards Snowfeather Hatchling 

After you have acquired the pet, you will need to level it to 25 , do world quests with him , pet battles , buy things , do dungeons , raids , and more!

The flight path map has been changed to show only the path to your destination in Patch 7.1, how many world quests are up in a zone, as well as showing non world quests on the map. Currently the flight points aren't lining up correctly with the Broken Isles map.


    


Auction House Sorting Improved
The Auction House can now sort by price per unit, helping with the varying stack sizes. 


  


Mythic+ Dungeon Filter
The group finder will now allow you to list Mythic and Mythic+ groups separately. 


  


Fire Mage: Hidden Flame Orbs
Fire mages can talk to Archmage Melis  to hide the floating flame orbs of Felo'melorn  in Patch 7.1.


    


Mage - Updated Portal and Teleport Icons
Some Mage armor teleports and portals now have a new, unique icon.


              


Reputation Improvements
We will be easing a couple of linked features with 7.1.

First, Court of Stars and The Arcway will now unlock account-wide once each individual dungeon is completed once on an account.

Second, World Quests will no longer have the "friendly" reputation unlock requirement for secondary/alt characters. Once you hit 110 on an alt, you will immediately receive a quest to speak with Khadgar to receive your Flight Master's Whistle and unlock World Quests, as long as you've already unlocked World Quests on your main.


New Music
Patch 7.1 adds some new music, including music for Karazhan, Winter's Veil, and Diablo's Anniversary.


  


Action Camera
The Action Cam should be returning in Patch 7.1, meaning addons like dynamiccam  might work once again.


  


New Icons
Lots of new icons were added in this patch!


  


New Strings
New strings in this patch include references to the system that allows you to join friends that are queuing for something. 


  


WoWDB Addon Data Collection
The migration to the new Curse App  continues with improved addon management support and guild servers.

Quickly populating the database with new additions is done with the help of many players. The more people that contribute data, the more accurate and quickly we can update the database.

To help out, just click here curse://enable-wowdb-upload if you have Curse App already installed.

When you are playing the addon will gather data and save it. When you are done playing, Curse App will see the game close and upload the collected data from the addon.
