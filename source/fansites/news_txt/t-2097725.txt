PvP Changes
Patch 7.1 makes several changes to PvP:


Honor talents are no longer lost when you Prestige More http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/765977-honor-talents-will-not-reset-71/#post19
The amount of Artifact Power you will earn from a PvP victory is displayed in the UI 
You will earn extra Honor and Artifact Power for your first win of the day in all unrated and rated PvP activities
Rated and Unrated Battleground wins will reward a piece of gear. Losses have a chance to reward gear based on the score.
Skirmish and Arena victories will have an increased chance to drop gear.
The item level of PvP gear now more closely mirrors the type of item level progression you see from World Quests
Each rated PvP bracket 2v2, 3v3, Rated Battlegrounds has a weekly quest that rewards items based on your best rating from the previous week. 
The quests for Arena brackets will require 10 wins, and the quests for the Rated Battleground bracket require three wins.
Below 2000, the quests reward Gladiator appearance gear and above 2000 they reward Elite appearance gear.

Reputation Improvements
We will be easing a couple of linked features with 7.1.

First, Court of Stars and The Arcway will now unlock account-wide once each individual dungeon is completed once on an account.

Second, World Quests will no longer have the "friendly" reputation unlock requirement for secondary/alt characters. Once you hit 110 on an alt, you will immediately receive a quest to speak with Khadgar to receive your Flight Master's Whistle and unlock World Quests, as long as you've already unlocked World Quests on your main.

WoWDB Addon Data Collection
The migration to the new Curse App https://www.curse.com/games/wow/upgrade continues with improved addon management support and guild servers.

Quickly populating the database with new additions is done with the help of many players. The more people that contribute data, the more accurate and quickly we can update the database.

To help out, just click here curse://enable-wowdb-upload if you have Curse App already installed.

When you are playing the addon will gather data and save it. When you are done playing, Curse App will see the game close and upload the collected data from the addon. 


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2016/october/curseAppWowdb.png https://mods.curse.com/addons/wow/wowdb-profiler
