New Plush and In-Game Pet: Meet Squirky!
We’ve added a new member to the murloc family. Meet Squirky (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/113440-squirky)! This cuddly bit of sunshine will mrgrrgrgllll straight into your heart, both as a cuddly plush and as an in-game pet you can capture and take along on your adventures.

Squirky Murloc Plush
“Off the coast of Azsuna lies Seabreak Isle, home to the wild and secretive Squigglefin tribe. We’ve heard rumors of intrepid explorers who’ve successfully domesticated young Squigglefin, called ‘Squirkies,’ but no one’s sure if it can actually be done. Grab a Squirky plush and find out for yourself! Measures 7” tall, made of polyester.”


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/may/thumb/squ01.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/may/squ01.jpg)

Purchase your Squirky plush today (http://blizz.ly/squirkyplush)!

Squirky Battle Pet
Looking to add to your in-game pet collection? Be prepared to face dangers untold just off the coast of Azsuna on Seabreak Isle. These elite members of the Squigglefin tribe aren’t going to be happy about your visit to their little home, but if you’re lucky, you’ll be able to find Squirky among them. Summon your veteran pet battle team into action, and make him a part of your own band of merry battle pets.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/may/thumb/squ02.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2017/may/squ02.jpg)

Good luck, collectors!


Breaching the Tomb
The next step of Breaching the Tomb (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/11546-breaching-the-tomb) is available now. Head to the Broken Shore to pick up Take Out the Head (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/46250-take-out-the-head) from Maiev, rewarding 3000 x Nethershard (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1226-nethershard). Bonus Event - Cataclysm Timewalking
Cataclysm Timewalking (http://www.wowdb.com/world-events/587-timewalking-dungeon-event) is back this week.


The weekly quest (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/44166-a-frozen-path-through-time) from Archmage Timear (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/111246-archmage-timear) rewards a Accolade of Victory (http://www.wowdb.com/items/140176-accolade-of-victory), Seal of Broken Fate (http://www.wowdb.com/currencies/1273-seal-of-broken-fate), and Cache of Nightborne Treasures (http://www.wowdb.com/items/140150-cache-of-nightborne-treasures). The cache can contain tier set pieces.
You can find Kiatke (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/101759-kiatke) in Stormwind and Orgrimmar. Her unique items include Tosselwrench's Mega-Accurate Simulation Viewfinder (http://www.wowdb.com/items/133542-tosselwrenchs-mega-accurate-simulation-viewfinder) and Gurboggle's Gleaming Bauble (http://www.wowdb.com/items/133511-gurboggles-gleaming-bauble).
Don't forget to keep working towards Reins of the Eclipse Dragonhawk (http://www.wowdb.com/items/129923-reins-of-the-eclipse-dragonhawk), Bridle of the Ironbound Wraithcharger (http://www.wowdb.com/items/129922-bridle-of-the-ironbound-wraithcharger), and Yu'lei, Daughter of Jade (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/87775-yulei-daughter-of-jade)!
Reins of the Infinite Timereaver (http://www.wowdb.com/items/133543-reins-of-the-infinite-timereaver) can also drop from any Timewalking dungeon boss.
