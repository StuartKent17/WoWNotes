Darkmoon Faire Races
This month brings some more races to the Darkmoon Faire, in addition to the other Patch 6.1 changes (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4748-Darkmoon-Faire-Race-and-Changes-Archaeology-Fix-Blue-Tweets-Method-Interview-DLC). The first race uses the same course as last month, allowing you to earn Rocketeer: Gold (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/9764-rocketeer-gold) and the Everlasting Darkmoon Firework (http://www.wowdb.com/items/122119-everlasting-darkmoon-firework) fairly easily.

The other two races use a new course that is more challenging. These reward Big Rocketeer: Gold (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/9805-big-rocketeer-gold) (Race MiniZep Controller (http://www.wowdb.com/items/122125-race-minizep-controller)) and Big Race Roadhog (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/9799-big-race-roadhog) (Checkered Flag (http://www.wowdb.com/items/122128-checkered-flag)). Keep in mind that the Checkered Flag (http://www.wowdb.com/items/122128-checkered-flag) only has 50 charges and that is the more difficult of the two achievements to complete. This can be done solo (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tnuuNVr9K2U) if RNG lines up just right or with the help of a friend (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wCQXSjxs4v4).

The new course has new powerups as well. Everything other than the speed boots and coins rotates through the buffs, so they are constantly changing. There are two new buffs, one being a rocket that shoots you up in the air and the other being a small blue portal that rushes you ahead like Disengage. You can collect coins to power your final ability. There is also a new obstacle on this course, mini-zeppelins. These will shoot you and slow you down if you get too close to them.
