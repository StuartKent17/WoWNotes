Patch 8.2 PTR - New Faction Rewards
Patch 8.2 adds two new faction specific reuptations, and one neutral reputation to earn rewards from!

Waveblade Ankoan
Waveblade Ankoan (https://wow.gamepedia.com/Waveblade_Ankoan) are an Alliance only faction of Ankoan that has been fighting back against the naga in Nazjatar. Alliance players must earn Revered for part of Battle for Azeroth Pathfinder, Part Two (https://ptr.wowdb.com/achievements/13250-battle-for-azeroth-pathfinder-part-two).

Prismatic Manapearl (https://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/1721-prismatic-manapearl) are earned from chests, rares, and some world quests in Nazjatar.
