BlizzCon 2014 In-Game Goodies Sneak Peek
BlizzCon loot explosion! It’s time to take a sneak peek at this year’s BlizzCon in-game goodies for World of Warcraft, StarCraft II, Diablo III*, Hearthstone: Heroes of Warcraft, and the upcoming Heroes of the Storm**—they’re yours whether you’re attending the show in person or watching from home with the Virtual Ticket (http://bit.ly/Z8Qui4).

World of Warcraft – Grommloc Pet
Watch your foes flee in terror before this delightfully dreadful baby murloc masquerading as Grommash Hellscream, legendary leader of the Warsong clan.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/september/thumb/E1QJMIPMT5WE1411171884394.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/september/E1QJMIPMT5WE1411171884394.jpg)

Hearthstone: Heroes of Warcraft – Blizzard 2014 Card Back
Infuse your deck with Blizzard spirit by equipping this commemorative card back, forged to resemble the compass design emblazoned in Blizzard’s courtyard.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/september/thumb/16GX45BXL8Z41411171973614.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/september/16GX45BXL8Z41411171973614.jpg)

StarCraft II – Portraits & Decal
Deck out your profile with a pair of portraits, including Hierarch Artanis and another to-be-revealed icon, and prep your army for battle across multiple universes with a Heroes of the Storm–themed decal.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/september/thumb/3CJTE942NTB21411172027631.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/september/3CJTE942NTB21411172027631.jpg)

Diablo III – Warlords of Draenor Weapon Transmogrifications
Outfit your heroes with six weapon transmogrification appearances (BlizzCon® 2014 In-Game Goodies Sneak Peek) inspired by World of Warcraft: Warlords of Draenor—one for each of Diablo III: Reaper of Souls’ classes.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/september/thumb/0PELYF4JH8381411172074030.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/september/0PELYF4JH8381411172074030.jpg)

Heroes of the Storm – Nexus Mount and Portrait
Rush into the storm atop the ethereal Nexus Charger, and embrace the unknown with the mysterious Nexus Brawler portrait.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/september/thumb/I1DNBUVN7RRN1411172126040.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/september/I1DNBUVN7RRN1411172126040.jpg)

This year’s epic collection of in-game goodies will be available to use after BlizzCon begins. Head to the BlizzCon Virtual Ticket page to order or learn more.

*Diablo III content for Windows/Mac version only.

**Heroes of the Storm content will be available once you get access to the game; note that ordering a Virtual Ticket does not guarantee a spot in the alpha or beta tests.
