Achievements
Raids & Dungeons
Looking For Multitudes  now properly rewards Perky Pug 

Icecrown Citadel
It's a Trap! 10 player  and It's a Trap! 25 player  have been removed from the game.
Boned  - Defeat Lord Marrowgar without any raid member remaining impaled for more than 8 seconds. Old - Defeat Lord Marrowgar without anyone taking damage from Bone Cyclone

Pit of Saron
Don't Look Up  now requires you to complete the achievement on the first try.


Spell Changes

Miscellaneous
Item - Shadowmourne Legendary  has been changed once again - Your weapon swings have a chance to drain a Soul Fragment granting you 40 strength. When you have acquired 10 Soul Fragments you will unleash Chaos Bane, dealing 1900 to 2100 shadow damage split between all enemies within 8 yards. 

Engineering 
Iceblade Arrow  has been added to the game - Creates Iceblade Arrow  x 1000. / Reagents: Crystallized Shadow  x 2
Shatter Rounds  has been added to the game - Creates Shatter Rounds  x 1000. / Reagents: Crystallized Earth  x 2

 Paladin
Protection
Divine Intervention  no longer clears the effect of Exhaustion and Sated on target. Important - The official notes were changed to : This ability now also removes Exhaustion or Sated from a target if the recipient is out of combat when the effect end

 Rogue
Asssassination
Improved Poisons  now increases the chance to apply Deadly poison to your target by 4/8/12/16/20%. Up from 2/4/6/8/10%

Poisons
Deadly Poison  now has an additional effect - Once stacked to 5 times, each application of Deadly Poison also causes the poison on the Rogue's other weapon to apply.

 Warlock
Affliction
Improved Drain Soul  now reduces the threat caused by your Affliction spells by 10/20%. Up from 5/10%

Destruction
Destructive Reach  now reduces the threat caused by Destruction spells by 10/20%. Up from 5/10%
