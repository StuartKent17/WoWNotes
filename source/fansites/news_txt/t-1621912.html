Warlords of Draenor - Garrisons: Barn
The Barn (http://www.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/133-barn) takes up a Medium plot which can also be used for a Lumber Mill (http://www.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/138-lumber-mill), Gladiator's Sanctum (http://www.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/161-gladiators-sanctum), Trading Post (http://www.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/111-trading-post) or Inn / Tavern (http://www.wowdb.com/garrison/buildings/36-trading-post). Once upgraded to Tier 3, your garrison will have two Medium plots available.

Abilities
This building is good for stockpiling leather, meat, fur, and Savage Blood (http://www.wowdb.com/items/118472-savage-blood) for crafting epic armor and weapons.


Level 1 - Allows you to capture clefthoof, elekk, wolves, and talbuk for leather and fur. Used for leatherworking and tailoring recipes.
Level 2 - Allows you to capture boars and riverbeasts for rare meats used to create the most powerful food.
Level 3 - Allows you to capture elite beasts to harvest for savage blood, used in all epic crafted armor and weapons.



Level 3 Unlock
The Level 3 version of this building is unlocked by completing the Master Trapper (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/9565-master-trapper) achievement. Unlocking Level 3 will give you access to the Pygmy Cow (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/87257-pygmy-cow). The Glass of Warm Milk (http://www.wowdb.com/items/120309-glass-of-warm-milk) can spawn on the first or second floor of the barn.


http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/november/thumb/cowPet.jpg (http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2014/november/cowPet.jpg)

Work Orders
The Barn allows you to capture animals in traps, which gives you caged animals (http://www.wowdb.com/items/misc/other?filter-search=caged). You can then trade 1 of these animals for leather, fur, food, and the chance for Savage Blood (http://www.wowdb.com/items/118472-savage-blood).

For an added bonus, you can get a follower with the Skinning (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/abilities/62-skinning) ability to work in the Barn. Currently Goldmane the Skinner (http://www.wowdb.com/garrison/followers/170-goldmane-the-skinner-goldmane-the-skinner) is the only follower with the ability accessible to everyone. You can find him in Nagrand (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/34807-vignette-goldmane-the-skinner) by killing Bolkar the Cruel (http://www.wowdb.com/npcs/80080-bolkar-the-cruel). You can use Goldmane's Cage Key (http://www.wowdb.com/items/111863-goldmanes-cage-key) from Bolkar to release him (http://www.wowdb.com/quests/35596-feline-friends-forever) and claim him as your follower.

Dark Ranger Velonara / Hulda Shadowblade (http://www.wowdb.com/garrison/followers/453-dark-ranger-velonara-hulda-shadowblade) is the other follower with the Skinning (http://beta.wowdb.com/garrison/abilities/62-skinning) ability, unlocked by completing the Standing United (http://www.wowdb.com/quests?filter-search=Standing%20United) quest in Spires of Arak.

Nerdlords of Draenor - Mannequin Army
Self-proclaimed videogame nerds Jesse Cox and Wowcrendor have gathered a few of their friends to storm the frontlines of Draenor in an extension of their popular Cox n’ Crendor podcast (https://www.youtube.com/user/Coxncrendorpodcast) and animated short (https://www.youtube.com/user/Coxncrendor) series. You can catch their display of skill, comic agility, and utter madness in new episodes each week leading up to the November 13 launch of Warlords of Draenor.

Nerdlords of Draenor finds Cox and Crendor jumping onto the beta realms and exploring various aspects of the new expansion with a different guest each episode. The duo will also be releasing animated shorts capturing some of the best moments from their escapades—with a few artistic liberties taken.

Episode 2 features Cox and Crendor teaming up with special guest SmoothMcGroove (https://www.youtube.com/SmoothMcGroove) to enter the Dark Portal and scramble to disrupt the Iron Horde forces in Tanaan Jungle. Watch as these three unlikely heroes are imperiled in a savage past, desperately attempting to stake their claim as Nerdlords of Draenor.
