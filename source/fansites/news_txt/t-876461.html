Poseidus and Reins of Poseidus on PTR 4.1
ChaosStorm posted on the forums earlier today (http://www.mmo-champion.com/threads/875900-Got-it!-Reins-of-Poseidus-%28-PTR-%29) and confirmed that the Reins of Poseidus (http://db.mmo-champion.com/i/67151/reins-of-poseidus/) now drop from Poseidus in the Abyssal Depths (Vashj'ir). According to his post the mount is usable in other water environment, the mount has a 270% speed in Vashj'ir and 108% speed in other areas.
