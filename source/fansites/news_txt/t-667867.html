Recent In-Game Fixes - 09/09/09
Listed below are recent fixes we have applied to the game. Keep in mind that some of these changes may not be active until after the realm has been restarted.

09/09/09
Icehowl is now be tauntable on both 10- and 25-player heroic difficulties.
In the Trial of the Crusader Anub’arak encounter, the frost patches created by destroying frost spheres will now last longer and should disappear only through the encounter’s mechanics and not by themselves.
