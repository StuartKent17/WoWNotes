Patch 4.1 PTR Notes - March 9 Update

General

A dead player can now be resurrected by targeting them using the Party or Raid Frame even if they have released. No more hunting for corpses.


Dungeon Finder

The Dungeon Finder now attempts to avoid putting damage-dealing classes with the same armor type in a group.


PvP

Arathi Basin (http://www.wowpedia.org/Arathi_Basin)

Flags should now cap in 7 seconds, down from 8.

The Battle for Gilneas (http://www.wowpedia.org/Battle_for_Gilneas)

Flags should now cap in 7 seconds, down from 8.

Druids now innately have 100% pushback protection from damage while channeling Tranquility.


Restoration

Malfurion's Gift (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/92364/malfurions-gift/) now reduces the cooldown of Tranquility (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/740/tranquility/) by 2.5/5 minutes.


Druid Bug Fixes

The troll druid models for Flight Form (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/33943/flight-form/) and Swift Flight Form (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/40120/swift-flight-form/) has slimmed down to be more in scale with other druid flight forms.


http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ihunters.gif Hunter (Forums (http://www.mmo-champion.com/forums/271-Hunter) / Cataclysm Talent Calculator (http://www.wowtal.com/#k=..hunter) / Skills/Talents (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/1118-Hunter-Cataclysm))
Pets

Bloodthirsty (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/53187/bloodthirsty/) no longer generates Happiness.
Carrion Feeder (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/54044/carrion-feeder/) no longer restores Happiness.
The Feed Pet (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/6991/feed-pet/) ability now instantly heals 50% of the pet's health. Cannot be used in combat. Requires diet-appropriate food.
Guard Dog no longer causes Growl (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/2649/growl/) to generate additional Happiness.
The Happiness/Pet Loyalty System has been removed. Hunters will no longer have to manage Happiness for their pets, and the previous damage bonus for pets being happy will now be baseline for all tamed pets.


Glyphs

Glyph of Mend Pet (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/58301/glyph-of-mend-pet/) is now Glyph of Greater Proportion, which increases the size of the pet slightly.


Hunter Bug Fixes

Distracting Shot (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/20736/distracting-shot/) and Multi-Shot (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/2643/multi-shot/) are now properly 40-yard range.
Multi-Shot (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/2643/multi-shot/) now properly has a 1-second global cooldown.

In addition, the stacking effect of Arcane Blast now increases the damage done by Arcane Explosion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/1449/arcane-explosion/), and Arcane Explosion does not consume that effect.
Arcane Explosion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/1449/arcane-explosion/) damage has been increased by 13%.
Arcane Missiles (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/5143/arcane-missiles/) damage has been increased by 13%.
Blizzard (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/10/blizzard/) damage has been increased by 70%.


Arcane

Arcane Barrage (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/44425/arcane-barrage/) damage has been increased by 13%.


Fire

Combustion (http://db.mmo-champion.com/s/11129/combustion/) no longer has a global cooldown.
