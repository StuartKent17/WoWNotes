US Downtime
To compensate players for any inconvenience caused by the downtime experienced as a result of this week's 48 hour extended maintenance, we will be issuing a 48-hour time extension to all players who have accounts that are currently active.

Source (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=965512861&pageNo=1&sid=1#0) (with a list of concerned realms)

I know this post is a few days old, but I guess it will make some people happy to know that Blizzard loves them. But sometimes, love is not enough, and it seems that some realms like Elune (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=1134163498&sid=1&pageNo=1) are unplayable since the upgrade. As you can see in the post (http://forums.worldofwarcraft.com/thread.html?topicId=1134163498&sid=1&pageNo=1), Blizzard is trying to find the problem but according to the last reply they didn't identify anything yet.
