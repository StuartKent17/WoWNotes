The Deaths of Chromie Scenario
Chromie needs your help to stop her death! In this solo scenario, you will defeat eight different attacks on Chromie throughout time before it is too late. Rewards include Timewarped Badges (http://ptr.wowdb.com/currencies/1166-timewarped-badge), the Timelord (http://ptr.wowdb.com/titles/510-timelord) title, and transmog gear.


Head to Wyrmrest Temple to find Chromie.
You will have to run this scenario a number of times to farm reputation to unlock Chromie's talents, unlock the Chronoportals, and learn how to beat it in time.
You have 15 minutes (plus whatever bonus time you can collect) to stop all eight attacks on Chromie.
Completing the scenario in time rewards the Chromie themed armor set transmog.
You will have an item level of 1000 and player level of 112.
You will have to do a slow clear of each Dragonshrine once to unlock the other four situations that Chromie needs to be saved from.
The chests that Chromie will reveal on the map with a talent are also present without the talent, so keep an eye out for them.

Chromie's Talents
Chromie has seven tiers of talents, with a higher reputation level needed for each one. Tiers 3-7 take time to research and each tier must be researched before moving on to the next. You can chain run the scenario to farm reputation, allowing you to reach max reputation far before you will be able to research all of the talents. You will be able to swap between talents instantly after researching that tier.

Sands of Time
There is a chance to find Sands of Time (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151493-sands-of-time) on enemy corpses, as quest rewards, and from chests. The item allows you to choose from one of two rewards to make the scenario a little bit easier. Generally the priority for your choice should be Keepsake Box or Bronze Drake, Stolen Time, followed by the rest. Currently damage reduction isn't very necessary, even for cloth casters.

Keep in mind that once a choice is presented, it doesn't change. This can be useful in giving you a little bit of control over the rewards. For example, if you have only cleared one Dragonshrine and get a Bronze Drake as an option, go ahead and clear two more before selecting the drake so that you can use it on the far away final shrine. The same goes for getting a Keepsake box near the end of one of the Chronoportals. Don't make the choice until after you complete that Chronoportal, otherwise you could get one for the current portal you are almost finished with!

Dragonshrines
After completing the tasks to reveal the boss at each Dragonshrine, you will be able to head directly to the boss's location in future runs of the scenario, saving significant time.


Emerald Dragonshrine

Kill Emerald Skytalon (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/122548-emerald-skytalon) to gather 24 x Emeraldine Plume (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151168-emeraldine-plume)
Kill Emerald Lasher (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/122549-emerald-lasher) to gather 30 x Twisted Fiber (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151167-twisted-fiber)
Build 3 x Nightmare-Catcher (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151166-nightmare-catcher), enter the Emerald Nightmare, kill the Dream Tormentor (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/123458-dream-tormentor) that are attacking the ancients.
Head to the skeleton's claw by the water to reveal Thalas Vylethorn (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/122875-thalas-vylethorn)

Azure Dragonshrine

Kill Leyline Elemental (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/122552-leyline-elemental) and Ley Wyrm (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/122551-ley-wyrm) to gather 50 x Darkened Scrap of Vellum (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151141-darkened-scrap-of-vellum)
Head to the eastern floating platform to reveal the Void Gargantuan (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/122553-void-gargantuan)

Ruby Dragonshrine

Kill the undead leaders at the entrances to the Dragonshrine to gather Soul Crystal Fragment (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/150939-soul-crystal-fragment).
Head to the space under the tree to reveal the Talar Icechill (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/122874-talar-icechill)

Obsidian Dragonshrine

Say hi to Wrathion outside.
Fight your way to the back of the cave to kill Charredbone Goliath (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/122528-charredbone-goliath)
Head outside by the entrance to reveal Zorathides (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/122871-zorathides)




Chronoportals
There are four Chronoportals that are unlocked after you complete one of the corresponding Dragonshrines for the first time.


Mount Hyjal

Run up to the injured Chromie, fight off the waves of attackers, defeat the Fiery Behemoth (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/123021-fiery-behemoth)
If you have a Brimstone Beacon (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151547-brimstone-beacon), you can skip right to the boss.

War for Andorhal

Pick up the quest from Chromie and then head to the Alliance side of the battlefield to kill NPCs. Useful NPCs are marked with a star on the mini-map.

Alliance Force-Commander (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/124072-alliance-force-commander) - Drops Sands of Time (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151493-sands-of-time)
Gatzin Zapwrench (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/124095-gatzin-zapwrench) - Drops Lightning Absorption Capsule (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151369-lightning-absorption-capsule), which is necessary to take down the cannon.
Potionmaster Donovan (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/124094-potionmaster-donovan) - Drops Experimental Alchemy Reagent (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151368-experimental-alchemy-reagent), which is necessary to take down the cannon
Other named NPCs can drop Military Explosives (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151370-military-explosives), which take 15% health off of the cannon

Take down the plating and electrical shield on the cannon, then use the explosives to destroy it.
If you have Cenarion Circle Documents (http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/151549-cenarion-circle-documents) you can skip killing the cannon.

The Well of Eternity

Mount up and ride along the edge of the well down the path to the portal. You will have to kill anything you pulled before being able to take the portal, including the Felhound that gets sent chasing you.
Mount up, follow the path back towards where you normally enter the instance. Don't dismount until you come across Grolethax (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/123721-grolethax). Be sure Chromie is next to you and around the corner against the invisible wall to line of sight the caster NPCs.
After killing everything to drop combat, take the portal out. If you don't drop combat, sometimes you are stuck in combat and can no longer mount for the rest of the scenario.

Culling of Stratholme

This is an ideal Chronoportal to be able to skip, as it can take up to seven minutes without speed buffs.
Head into the city and pick up Smoke, Meat, and Pretty Flowers (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/47744-smoke-meat-and-pretty-flowers) from Emery Neill (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/123781-emery-neill)
Purchase all of the special scenario items from the shop vendors. You will probably make two trips the first time through, as the followup quest (http://ptr.wowdb.com/quests/47746-holing-up) sends you to buy other things from the same shops.
Take the Town Hall key, run up to the hidden bookcase, and run through the streets along the normal instance path. Be sure to pull the casters so they aren't pulled by Chromie and kill her.
Kill Nezar'Azret (http://ptr.wowdb.com/npcs/123836-nezarazret), free Chromie, and move on!
