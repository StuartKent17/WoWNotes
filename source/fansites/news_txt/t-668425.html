Patch 3.2.2 Notes Update
The Patch 3.2.2 Notes update were updated once again before the week-end, below are the latest additions since the last time I posted them.
http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ideathknights.gif Death Knight
Unholy
Bone Shield: This ability now has 3 charges instead of 4. Cooldown reduced to 1 minute.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/idruids.gif Druid
Feral
Infected Wounds: The debuff generated by this talent no longer stacks and instead causes the full effect with a single application.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ipaladins.gif Paladin
Protection
Ardent Defender: This talent now reduces damage taken below 35% health by 7/13/20% instead of 10/20/30%.
Hammer of the Righteous: The damage from this ability is now considered physical instead of Holy. The threat generated by the ability has been increased such that it will continue to do approximately the same threat it did when it was Holy damage. (Removed)

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/ishamans.gif Shaman
Elemental
Shamanism: Your Lightning Bolt and Chain Lightning spells gain an additional 3/6/9/12/15% and your Lava Burst gains an additional 4/8/12/16/20% of your bonus damage effects.

http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/icons/iwarriors.gif Warrior
Protection
Critical Block: This talent now grants a 20/40/60% chance to block double the normal amount instead of 10/20/30%.

Professions
Inscription
Added a recipe for Runescroll of Brilliance. This item grants an intellect buff equal to 80% of the highest Arcane Intellect rank to all players in the raid. Does not stack with other intellect scrolls, Arcane Intellect, or Arcane Brilliance. (Removed)

Glyphs
Glyph of Bone Shield: This glyph now grants 1 additional charge instead of 2.
