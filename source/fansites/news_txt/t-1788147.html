One of the original eredar who betrayed the draenei of Argus to take his place as the sinister hand of the dark titan Sargeras, Archimonde the Defiler commands the military might of the Burning Legion. He arrives in Draenor, reaching across space and time, to ensure that the invasion of Azeroth proceeds as planned, and that Gul'dan does not fail his Legion masters.


Overview - Archimonde assaults players with the might of the Burning Legion, gaining new abilities each time his health is reduced by 15%, eventually summoning <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/182225-rain-of-chaos">Rain of Chaos</a> to destroy Draenor. When a player is launched into the air by <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/183817-shadow-burst">Fel Burst</a>, their allies must catch them to avoid fatal damage. As Archimonde <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/185590-desecrate">Desecrates</a> the land, players must protect themselves with the <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/183943-light-of-the-naaru">Light of the Naaru</a>. When Archimonde reaches 40% health remaining, he begins to cast <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/186961-nether-banish">Nether Banish</a>, sending players to the Twisting Nether.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Damage Dealers - Destroy Doomfire Spirits quickly before they create too much <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/182826-doomfire">Doomfire</a>.
<a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/184265-wrought-chaos">Wrought Chaos</a> inflicts heavy damage if aimed towards other players.
<a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/184931-shackled-torment">Shackled Torment</a> inflicts lethal raidwide damage if mutliple shackles are broken at once.
Infernal Doombringers will begin to cast <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/187078-hellfire">Hellfire</a> if not killed quickly.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/healer.png" title="Healer" /> Healers - <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/185590-desecrate">Desecrate</a> inflicts increasing raidwide damage to players without <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/183943-light-of-the-naaru">Light of the Naaru</a>.
<a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/183864-shadow-blast">Shadow Blast</a> significantly increases Shadow damage taken by <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/183828-death-brand">Death Brand</a>.
<a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/184931-shackled-torment">Shackled Torment</a> inflicts heavy raidwide damage each time it is broken.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Tanks - <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/183828-death-brand">Death Brand</a> inflicts massive initial damage and periodic damage until Hellfire Deathcallers are killed.
<a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/186961-nether-banish">Nether Banish</a> sends tanks to the Twisting Nether along with any other players within 10 yards.
