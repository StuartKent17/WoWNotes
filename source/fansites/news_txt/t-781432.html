Achievements Changes

Guild Achievements

Heroic: Deadmines Guild Run (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/5141/) now properly requires you to defeat Vanessa VanCleef.


General

To All the Squirrels Who Cared for Me (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/5548/) - The Cataclysm has brought more critters to Azeroth. You must /love them too.


Quests

Glutton for Punishment (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/5448/) is gone and has been replaced by 3 achievements.
Glutton for Fiery Punishment (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/5448/) - In Archmage Xylem's Trial of Fire in Azshara, gain 10 stacks of Fire Dancing without taking damage.
Glutton for Icy Punishment (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/5546/) - In Archmage Xylem's Trial of Frost in Azshara, gain 20 stacks of Essence of Ice without taking damage.
Glutton for Shadowy Punishment (http://db.mmo-champion.com/a/5547/) - In Archmage Xylem's Trial of Shadow in Azshara, trap 20 Weeping Souls without taking damage.
