Khor, Hammer of the Guardian Hotfix
Khor, Hammer of the Guardian (https://www.wowdb.com/items/163119-khor-hammer-of-the-guardian) has been hotfixed to have a significantly lower item level because it was intended to be a transmog item. Khor, Hammer of the Corrupted (https://www.wowdb.com/items/160679-khor-hammer-of-the-corrupted?bonusIDs=1477,4798) is the version intended for use.
