Raid Unlock Schedule
To help you plan your escapades into the heart of this dangerous new place, we’ve broken down Highmaul’s unlock schedule for you.


December 2, 2014 - Highmaul opens with Normal and Heroic difficulties accessible.
December 9, 2014 - Mythic difficulty and the first wing of Raid Finder Kargath, Butcher, Brackenspore unlock.
December 16, 2014 - Raid Finder Wing 2 Tectus, Twin Ogron, Ko’ragh unlocks.
January 6, 2015 - Raid Finder Wing 3 Imperator Mar’gok unlocks.





Two World Bosses - Tarlna and Drov now Spawn!
The first two world bosses will start spawning as Highmaul opens up. These bosses will both drop 650 gear.

Seal of Tempered Fate
Seal of Tempered Fates  are the new bonus roll item for this first raid tier. This time around there are a few ways you can obtain your weekly cap of 3. If you have your level 3 Dwarven Bunker / War Mill  you will get a free Seal each week. There are also two rare Follower missions that may grant you one.

The main way to obtain these will be via the following weekly quests. You can mix and match between the four types. However, the more you buy into the same type each week, the more it will cost you.
