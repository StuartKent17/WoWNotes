New Season 4 Item discovered - Satin Robe and Hood
Looks like someone pushed the wrong button again, the Brutal Gladiator's Satin Robe (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/april/brutalgladiatorsatinrobe.jpg) and the Brutal Gladiator's Satin Hood (http://static.mmo-champion.com/mmoc/images/news/2008/april/brutalgladiatorsatinhood.jpg) have been activated on a server (EU-Arathor), it's the result of an error made by a GM who gave the wrong item to someone (and as I write this news, it's probably already fixed).
