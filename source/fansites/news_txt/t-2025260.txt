The movie is up to $430M worldwide now.
When doing motion capture, up to nine orcs could be displayed in real time, superimposed over the actors on the monitors and through the lens for the camera operators.
Baby Thrall was one of the hardest characters to get right, designed for maximum cuteness with the right amount of Orc.
ILM worked on the movie in some capacity from Comic-Con 2013 to April 2016.
Over 1,000 people were working on the movie effects at one point.
