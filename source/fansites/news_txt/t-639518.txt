New mounts
Gladiator mounts are Frostwyrms! 
Coralshell Turtle has been renamed to Sea Turtle - Summons and dismisses a rideable Sea Turtle. This mount can't move very quickly on land, but she's a great swimmer.
Deadly Gladiator's Frostwyrm  - Summons and dismisses a rideable Deadly Gladiator's Frostwyrm mount. This is an extremely fast mount. This mount can only be summoned in Outland or Northrend.
