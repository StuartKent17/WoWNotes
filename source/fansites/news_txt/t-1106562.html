Blood

Guardian

Skills

Call Beast (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/120679/#15464-15508): New. Summons a powerful wild animal to assist you in combat for 12 sec. Each time the animal deals damage, you will gain 2 focus. 1 Frost, 1 Unholy, 30 yd range, Instant, 30 sec cooldown.

Lynx Rush (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/120697/#15464-15508): New. Your pet rapidly charges from target to target, attacking 9 times over 4 sec, dealing its normal attack damage to each target. 100 yd range, Instant, 2 min cooldown.
Powershot (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/109259/#15464-15508): Now does 25% weapon damage to all enemies in between you and the target, down from 100%.


Beast Mastery

Focus Fire (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/82692/#15464-15508): No longer has a 15 sec cooldown. Now restores 10 Focus (was 4) to your pet and increases your ranged haste by 8% (was 3%).
Frenzy (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/19623/#15464-15508): Your pet has a 40% chance to gain 15% increased attack speed after attacking with a Basic Attack, lasting for 20 sec and stacking up to 5 times.


Survival

Black Arrow (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/3674/#15464-15508): No longer has a 30 sec cooldown. Now does 110% Weapon damage, up from 85%. DoT damage increased to 4660, up from 3580. Wording removed: "Lock and Load cannot trigger from the damage done to these additional targets."
Lock and Load (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/56343/#15464-15508): Now has a 10 sec cooldown. No longer works with Explosive Trap.
Trap Mastery (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/63458/#15464-15508): No longer works with Black Arrow.
