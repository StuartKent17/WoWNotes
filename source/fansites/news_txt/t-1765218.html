Kilrogg Deadeye, Warlord of the Bleeding Hollow, seized control of his clan after performing a ritual to learn of his death. With this knowledge, he led his clan fearlessly on countless campaigns, marching toward the glorious end he desires. Now, with demon blood coursing through his veins, Kilrogg awaits you in Hellfire Citadel, awaiting the moment of his death, or yours.


Overview - Kilrogg's <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/182428-vision-of-death">Visions of Death</a> transports players to the moment of their doom. The more demons a player kills during this phase, the greater stacks of <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/180718-undying-resolve">Undying Resolve</a> they receive. Failure to send anyone to this phase results in raid damage. As the fight progresses, Kilrogg calls for his clansmen to drink the fel blood within the chamber. If these Salivating Bloodthirsters reach the blood, they transform into Hulking Terrors.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Damage Dealers - Blood Globules that reach Kilrogg Deadeye damage the raid.
Fel Blood Globules that reach Kilrogg damage the raid and heal Kilrogg.
Enter <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/182428-vision-of-death">Visions of Death</a> and kill demons to gain stacks of <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/180718-undying-resolve">Undying Resolve</a>.
Kill Salivating Bloodthirsters before they reach the blood pool.
Interrupt the Hulking Terror's <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/183917-rending-howl">Rending Howl</a>.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/healer.png" title="Healer" /> Healers - Heal players affected by <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/180372-heart-seeker">Heart Seeker</a> to keep them alive.
<a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/180224-death-throes">Death Throes</a> pulses damage to the raid.
Enter <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/182428-vision-of-death">Visions of Death</a> and kill demons to gain stacks of <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/185563-undying-salvation">Undying Salvation</a>.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Tanks - Prevent Hulking Terrors from dying next to allies.
Tank swap off of Hulking Terrors to prevent gaining too much <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/182159-fel-corruption">Fel Corruption</a> and losing control to <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/180313-demonic-possession">Demonic Possession</a>.
Use active mitigation defensive abilities to counter <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/180199-shred-armor">Shred</a> or else suffer from <a href="http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/180200-shredded-armor">Shredded Armor</a>.

Kilrogg Deadeye -

Fel Corruption (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/182159) - Throughout the fight, Fel Corruption is inflicted onto the raid. As Fel Corruption increases on a target, it inflicts increasing damage over time. At 100 Fel Corruption, the target is dominated by the Burning Legion.

Demonic Possession (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/180313) - Fel Blood courses through your veins, binding your will to The Burning Legion!

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/tank.png" title="Tank" /> Shred Armor (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/180199) - The caster's next attack shreds the target's armor for great Physical damage. Victims of this attack are affected with Shredded Armor.<br/><br/>Shreds the victim's armor, causing them to receive 24% more damage for each application of Shredded Armor.<br/><br/>This effect will not apply to tanks using their active mitigation.
<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/healer.png" title="Healer" /> Heart Seeker (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/180372) - Kilrogg hurls a knife at your heart, inflicting 200,000 Physical damage on impact and an additional 23,600 Physical damage every 1 sec. for 10 sec.

<img src="http://media.mmo-champion.com/images/news/2012/march/dps.png" title="Dps" /> Blood Globule - Summoned next to a player affected by Heart Seeker. Immediately begins making its way towards Kilrogg.
