MLG Columbus WoW 3v3 Tournament - Day 1
Tonight starts the eight team World of Warcraft Round Robin tournament in Colombus as the top teams from all over the United States plus Ensidia from Europe compete for $15,000 in cash and prizes.

Scoreboard - Gotfrag.com (http://www.gotfrag.com/wow/story/44238/)

Details
3v3 WoW Arena
8 Team Invitation Only Tournament
Deadly Gladiator Gear (PvP) and Tier 7.5 Gear (PvE)
Round Robin to Double Elimination Bracket
Over $15,000 in cash prizes
Every match streamed live at GotFrag and MMO-Champion.com
3 days of non-stop action live from Columbus, Ohio
Official Rules - http://www.mlgpro.com/pro-circuit/2009/index2.php#wow (http://www.mlgpro.com/pro-circuit/2009/index2.php#wow)

2nd Place - $4,500 US
3rd Place - $2,250 US
