Heroic Anub'arak World 2nd Kill by Stars
Stars (http://www.stars-guild.com/) (Crystalspine Stinger - Taiwan) defeated Anub'arak in Heroic Mode a few hours ago and is the 2nd guild in the world to beat this encounter.

The hard modes were unlocked a day after the european servers ... but I will leave the comments to them.

MMO-Champ Talent Calculator Updated
The Talent Calculator (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/) has been updated with a few extra features to make it ... well, less lame. Feel free to report any bugs, suggestions, or complain for no reason in comments.

Shorter URLs
The URLs have been shortened drastically, people shouldn't hate you as much as before when you post one on forums.

Old - http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?deathknight=23050205300033032310231013500000000000000000000000000000023023033100301000000 00000000000&glyph=100825060504&version=10192
New - http://talent.mmo-champion.com/?deathknight#_CJEBpX09Xxw45,dQJOe3,10192

Developers Note - It didn't seem like a good idea to keep the URL compression secret, if you want to get a look at the code download mmoc-talent-v2-encoder-decoder.zip (http://talent.mmo-champion.com/mmoc-talent-v2-encoder-decoder.zip) and check the Readme to understand how it works.

Build Selection
You can now select the version of the game from the menu at the bottom right of the screen, Live and current PTR versions are clearly labeled to make your life even easier.

Import and Export
You can now Import and Export talent calculators from/to the WoWArmory, the old MMO-Champion URL, and even Wowhead for those of you who want to leave us. However keep in mind that we cannot detect the version of the build you will import from non MMO-Champion calculators.
