Darkmoon Faire Races
The Darkmoon Faire is back again, bringing us the final race, in addition to the other Patch 6.1 changes (http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4748-Darkmoon-Faire-Race-and-Changes-Archaeology-Fix-Blue-Tweets-Method-Interview-DLC). The Powermonger is the new mount this time around.

The first race uses the smaller course, allowing you to earn Powermonger: Gold (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/9785-powermonger-gold) and the Gaze of the Darkmoon (http://www.wowdb.com/items/122120-gaze-of-the-darkmoon).

The second race uses the big course, allowing you to earn Big Powermonger: Gold (http://www.wowdb.com/achievements/9817-big-powermonger-gold) and the Darkmoon Cannon (http://www.wowdb.com/items/122124-darkmoon-cannon). The Cannon is not a toy, so it will take up an additional inventory slot.

The Powermonger mount jumps to powerups (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/152725-jump-to-powerup). It also has a Battery (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/153026-battery) ability that absorbs the next powerup you hit in the next 4 seconds. This powerup can then be used later by using the Battery ability again. The Overwrite (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/152985-overwrite) ability allows you to overwrite a nearby powerup with the stored one, but requires the currency powerup. It isn't necessary to earn the achievements. The final ability, Summon Aggrobots (http://www.wowdb.com/spells/153503-summon-aggrobots), will taunt the mini-zeppelins, allowing you to pass by without getting stunned.

The trick this month is to jump to springs and use the battery to absorb the spring right before you land on it. This allows you to use the spring to jump twice, once when you hit it and once again with the stored jump. You also can store a rocket before the big race to jump up the cliff to the first checkpoint. This can be accomplished by starting a race, storing your rocket, running back, abandoning the quest, and starting again.
