Patch 3.1 - Season 6 and Wintergrasp PvP Gear Compilation
Raiders aren't the only people getting their share of new items in patch 3.1, a lot of items were also added as PvP rewards! Below is a list of the new Wintergrasp rewards (they're basically upgraded versions of the old ones, same prices) and a preview of Season 6 Gear. All the S6 gear isn't available yet, the list below is an almost-complete loot table of Emalon the Storm Watcher, the new version of Archavon.

Also, don't forget to read Arena Season 6 – Upcoming Changes (http://blue.mmo-champion.com/11/15864860497-arena-season-6--upcoming-changes.html) if you plan to play Arenas in 3.1!
