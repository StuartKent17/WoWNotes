Blizzcon Day 2
Live Stream
You can use the stream on giga.de (http://www.giga.de/2/live/free/).
If it doesn't work install the Octoshape plugin (http://www.octoshape.com/plugin/get.asp) and use http://127.0.0.1:6498/ms/asf/EPGIGA.giga350/Octoshape?MSWMExt=.asf (http://127.0.0.1:6498/ms/asf/EPGIGA.giga350/Octoshape?MSWMExt=.asf) in windows media player. (Yes, I know, it's a 127.0.0.1 URL but it works, Octoshape knows what to do with it)

Schedule
The events that you might want to watch are (if two events are listed for the same hour, you won't be able to see both on the stream, obviously) :
10:30 AM - 11:30 AM : Professions & Items Panel
10:30 AM - 11:30 AM : Cinematics Panel
12:00 PM - 01:00 PM : WoW Film
01:00 PM - 02:00 PM : PvP Panel
02:30 PM - 03:30 PM : WoW Classes
03:00 PM - 05:00 PM : 5vs5 Tournament Finals (See PvPScene.com (http://www.pvpscene.com))
05:30 PM - 06:30 PM : Lore & Quest Panel
05:30 PM - 06:30 PM : Art Panel
07:30 PM - 09:30 PM : L70ETC + Video Games Live Concert

You can check this site (http://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/city.html?n=783) to know what time it is in Anaheim.

Utgarde Keep Preview
WoWinsider.com (http://www.wowinsider.com/2007/08/03/first-impressions-wrath-of-the-lich-king-and-utgarde-keep/) posted a small preview of the Utgarde Keep (only playable in the press room). There isn't any major news (but it's still worth the reading) but there is a very interesting information here.
"We could not use our flying mounts in Northrend. But Blizzard has said they will be able to be used there, they just weren't in the build we played (and it made finding that darn keep even harder)."
