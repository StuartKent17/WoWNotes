Darkmoon Faire Races
The Darkmoon Faire is back again, bringing us yet another race, in addition to the other Patch 6.1 changes http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/4748-Darkmoon-Faire-Race-and-Changes-Archaeology-Fix-Blue-Tweets-Method-Interview-DLC. The Wanderluster is the new mount this time around, with an additional achievement: Darkmoon Like the Wind http://ptr.wowdb.com/achievements/9819-darkmoon-like-the-wind.

The first race uses the smaller course, allowing you to earn Wanderluster: Gold http://ptr.wowdb.com/achievements/9792-wanderluster-gold and the Attraction Sign http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/122126-attraction-sign. 

The second race uses the big course, allowing you to earn Big Wanderluster: Gold http://ptr.wowdb.com/achievements/9811-big-wanderluster-gold and the Darkmoon Gazer http://ptr.wowdb.com/items/122121-darkmoon-gazer. The Gazer is not a toy, so it will take up an additional inventory slot. 

The Wanderluster mount jumps to hazards http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/153397-jump-to-hazard rather than gates, allowing you to jump to the wires and be immune for 4 seconds. It has the old ability that allows you to pull powerups to you. It also has a new ability http://ptr.wowdb.com/spells/152875-danger-zone that gives you a shield that protects you from the mini-zeppelins. If they hit you while the shield is up, you get a speed boost, starting at 100 and stacking up to 150.

The trick this month is to use the shield or other speed boosts at the start of the course to get a faster start. This can be accomplished by starting a race, getting your speed boost, running back, abandoning the quest, and starting again.
